## Executive Summary

Confabulation — the production of plausible-sounding but ungrounded explanations for one's own behaviour, beliefs, choices, and mental contents — names an operation consciousness performs without witnessing it. The Map's existing voids cover *that* consciousness reports inadequately on its substrate ([[source-attribution-void]] for origin-tags, [[narrative-void]] for storytelling, [[noetic-feelings-void]] for felt signals). The Confabulation Void names the structural face that makes those reports *worse than absent*: a generative system supplies positive content that introspection cannot distinguish from veridical access. Three faces conjoin into structural irresolvability. **Generation face**: Gazzaniga's left-brain interpreter, Schnider's orbitofrontal reality filter, and Nisbett & Wilson's "implicit causal theories" all describe a sub-personal engine that produces explanations the introspector receives but did not author. **Detection face**: Rebouillat et al.'s 2021 finding reverses the classical confidence-accuracy mapping — confabulations carry confidence equal to or *higher than* veridical introspections when internal evidence is weak, so the felt signal that would mark confabulation operates inversely to the criterion it would need to track. **Pervasiveness face**: Hirstein, Wheatley, Bortolotti and Haidt independently conclude that confabulation in healthy adults is a continuum from clinical Korsakoff cases to ordinary post-hoc rationalisation, and the fraction of one's own self-explanations that are confabulated rather than veridical cannot be estimated from inside. The void is the operation of *filling-in for unwitnessed cognitive history with a plausible narrative*; consciousness has the endpoint (the explanation, with its felt-rightness) but not the passage (how the explanation was generated, what it omits, whether it is veridical).

## Key Sources

### Nisbett & Wilson, "Telling More Than We Can Know" (1977)
- **URL**: https://web.mit.edu/curhan/www/docs/Articles/15341_Readings/Social_Cognition/Nisbett_Wilson_1977_Telling_more_than_we_can_know.pdf
- **Type**: Foundational paper (*Psychological Review*)
- **Key points**:
  - Reviews evidence that subjects are "(a) sometimes unaware of the existence of a stimulus that importantly influenced a response, (b) unaware of the existence of the response, and (c) unaware that the stimulus has affected the response."
  - Proposes that when people report on their cognitive processes, they do so not via introspection but via "a priori, implicit causal theories" — judgements about what plausibly *would have* caused the response.
  - Cited 2,633 times by mid-2006 (one of the most cited articles in the recent history of consciousness studies); "no empirical research program currently exists that continues the work" — the finding has been absorbed but not extended methodologically.
- **Tenet alignment**: Dualism — supports the view that the introspecting subject does not have direct access to the underlying physical-functional history of cognition; what reaches consciousness is constructed product. Occam's Razor — the implicit-causal-theory machinery is itself a parsimony heuristic that selects plausible-sounding causes, not necessarily true ones.
- **Quote**: "When reporting on the effects of stimuli, people may not interrogate a memory of the cognitive processes that operated on the stimuli; instead, they may base their reports on implicit, a priori theories about the causal connection between stimulus and response."

### Gazzaniga, the Left-Brain Interpreter (1967–2018)
- **URL**: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Left-brain_interpreter | https://fs.blog/michael-gazzaniga-the-interpreter/ | https://www.diva-portal.org/smash/get/diva2:431851/FULLTEXT01.pdf
- **Type**: Body of split-brain experimental work and synthetic theoretical chapters
- **Key points**:
  - In a split-brain patient, when the right hemisphere is shown a snow scene and the left hemisphere a chicken claw, and the patient is asked to point with each hand to a related image (snow shovel for the right hemisphere, chicken for the left), the verbally-dominant left hemisphere — which has no access to *why* the left hand chose the shovel — confabulates an explanation: "you need a shovel to clean out the chicken shed."
  - The left hemisphere does *not* report the correct answer ("I don't know why my left hand pointed to the shovel"). It produces a confidently asserted post-hoc narrative.
  - Gazzaniga: "the left hemisphere is far more inventive in interpreting facts than the right hemisphere's more truthful, literal approach."
  - The mechanism scales: it "applies to the everyday behaviour of people at large," not only to split-brain patients.
- **Tenet alignment**: Bidirectional Interaction — the interpreter retrofits agency talk onto outcomes whose actual causal origin is opaque even to itself. If consciousness has any non-physical influence on the physical (Tenet 2), the interpreter is the most likely site at which that influence would be over-reported (every action is given a consciousness-side explanation regardless of how the cause distributed across substrate). Dualism — the interpreter is structurally separable from the witnessing it claims to deliver.
- **Quote**: "Sensory information is fed into an explanatory module which Gazzaniga calls the Interpreter, residing in the left hemisphere of the brain."

### Hirstein, *Brain Fiction: Self-Deception and the Riddle of Confabulation* (2005); ed. *Confabulation* (OUP, 2009)
- **URL**: https://direct.mit.edu/books/monograph/1969/Brain-FictionSelf-Deception-and-the-Riddle-of | https://philarchive.org/rec/HIRCVF
- **Type**: Monograph + edited interdisciplinary volume
- **Key points**:
  - Confabulation is "the failure of a normal checking or censoring process in the brain — the failure to recognise that a false answer is fantasy, not reality."
  - The creative production of plausible-sounding response and the checking of that response are *separable* brain processes; confabulation results when production proceeds without checking.
  - Confabulation is dimensional: "delusion-like beliefs and confabulation-like fabrications are commonly seen in healthy individuals, suggesting that the boundary between pathological and nonpathological confabulation is not always clear-cut."
  - Wheatley's chapter "Everyday confabulation" extends the Hirstein framework to ordinary self-explanation in healthy adults.
- **Tenet alignment**: Dualism — separability of generation and checking maps onto the question of whether either process is consciousness-side. Occam's Razor Has Limits — the production module's parsimony heuristic is what *generates* the felt-simplicity of confabulated explanations, undermining simplicity-as-evidence inferences.
- **Quote**: "The creative ability to construct a plausible-sounding response and some ability to check that response are separate in the human brain."

### Schnider, Orbitofrontal Reality Filtering (Schnider 2003 *Nat Rev Neurosci*; Schnider 2008 *The Confabulating Mind*; Schnider 2013 *Orbitofrontal Reality Filtering*)
- **URL**: https://www.nature.com/articles/nrn1179 | https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3677127/ | https://psychiatryonline.org/doi/10.1176/appi.ajp.2008.08010037
- **Type**: Neuroscientific synthesis (*Nat Rev Neurosci*) + monograph + functional-imaging review
- **Key points**:
  - "Lesions of orbitofrontal areas and anterior cingulate cortex induce confabulations by altering reality monitoring and representation of ongoing reality." Damage to posterior medial OFC (area 13) is the maximal lesion in patients who confuse memory with present reality.
  - "Reality filtering is not about knowing to what episode in the past a memory refers but whether it pertains to present reality or not." Spontaneous confabulators retrieve correct memories but cannot inhibit the irrelevant ones.
  - In healthy subjects, repeated runs of an associative task activate posterior medial OFC — the same region whose damage produces spontaneous confabulation. The filter is *always running*, sub-personally, in healthy adults.
  - Patients act on confabulated content (e.g., believing they need to feed children who have grown up decades ago) — confabulation is not merely verbal report but cognitive commitment.
- **Tenet alignment**: Dualism — the reality filter operates pre-consciously; what reaches consciousness has already been filtered, and the filtering process is constitutively unwitnessable from inside. Bidirectional Interaction — if consciousness influences the physical via [[amplification-void|amplification of small effects]], the filtering layer between substrate and report is what makes the actual influence-pattern unrecoverable from introspection.
- **Quote**: "Spontaneous confabulators fail to suppress (inactivate) evoked memories that do not pertain to ongoing reality."

### Johansson, Hall, Sikström & Olsson, "Failure to detect mismatches between intention and outcome in a simple decision task" (Science 2005); plus the Pärnamets/Stedmon-style 2020–2021 replication critique
- **URL**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16210542/ | https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11229-019-02414-3 | https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12713319/
- **Type**: Original experiment + philosophical analysis + recent pupillometry replication
- **Key points**:
  - In the original choice-blindness paradigm, participants were shown two female faces, asked to pick the more attractive, and then handed (via card-trick) the *non-chosen* face — and asked to explain their choice. Up to 80% of swaps were not detected verbally, and subjects produced fluent post-hoc justifications for choices they had not made.
  - Recent replication work (2020–2021) tightened the paradigm and found that "stimulus discriminability strongly affected choice blindness, casting doubt on the ubiquity and behavioral relevance of previous choice blindness studies." Up to 80% of non-detection cases had alternative explanations (button-press error, design suspicion, post-trial mind-changing) rather than introspective failure per se.
  - Pupillometry follow-ups (2024+) show *covert detection*: pupils dilate to swapped trials even when subjects do not verbally report the swap — the body knows; the verbal report does not.
  - Net effect on the Map's argument: the original strong claim (verbal report is wholly fabricated) is qualified, but the structural claim (verbal report and sub-personal detection are *separable signals* with the verbal channel less accurate than the bodily one) is *strengthened*, not weakened, by the replication critique.
- **Tenet alignment**: Dualism — the dissociation between covert (pupillometric) detection and overt (verbal) report is the kind of evidence dualism predicts: consciousness is not a transparent readout of substrate. Bidirectional Interaction — if any mental content has consciousness-side cause, the verbal report is the channel where that cause would need to be visible, and it is precisely the least reliable channel.
- **Quote**: "Participants failed to notice conspicuous mismatches between their intended choice and the outcome they were presented with, while nevertheless offering introspectively derived reasons for why they chose the way they did."

### Bortolotti, *Stranger than Fiction: Costs and Benefits of Everyday Confabulation* (2018 / collected essays); related work in Topoi 2020
- **URL**: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s13164-017-0367-y | https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11245-019-09631-y | https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11245-019-09668-z
- **Type**: Philosophical-psychological monograph + journal articles
- **Key points**:
  - Confabulation as ill-grounded claim: "an ill-grounded claim people make when they do not realise that their claim is ill-grounded." The not-realising is constitutive, not incidental.
  - Bortolotti distinguishes mental-state self-attribution (often roughly correct: I do feel afraid) from causal self-explanation (often confabulated: *because* of X). The Map's translation: the *that*-of mental content is more accessible than the *why* of its production.
  - Confabulation has *epistemic costs* (false beliefs propagate, ill-grounded explanations are taken seriously) and *non-epistemic benefits* (sense of competence, narrative coherence, social functioning). The pervasiveness face is partly defended by these benefits.
  - The mind-shaping framework (Bortolotti & McGeer): rather than confabulation undermining first-person authority, it can be a tool that stabilises self-regulation — but at the cost of accurate self-knowledge.
- **Tenet alignment**: Occam's Razor Has Limits — confabulated explanations are typically *simpler* than the actual causal manifold; the parsimony preference rewards the very explanation-style that confabulation produces. Dualism — the asymmetry between accessible *that*-content and inaccessible *why*-content is what dualism predicts when consciousness is non-reducible: it sees the surface of its own states but not their substrate-grounded production.
- **Quote**: "When people confabulate they do not necessarily fail at mental-state self-attributions, but offer ill-grounded explanations which often lead to the adoption of other ill-grounded beliefs."

### Haidt, "The Emotional Dog and Its Rational Tail" (*Psychological Review* 2001)
- **URL**: https://psycnet.apa.org/record/2001-18918-008 | https://philpapers.org/rec/HAITED-2 | https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/judgment-and-decision-making/article/reasonbased-explanation-for-moral-dumbfounding/5347532A1A642F83B91F1A20788EF31E
- **Type**: Foundational paper for moral psychology + subsequent dumbfounding literature
- **Key points**:
  - "Moral reasoning does not cause moral judgment; rather, moral reasoning is usually a post hoc construction, generated after a judgment has been reached."
  - Moral dumbfounding: subjects deliver strong moral verdicts (e.g., on the consenting-incest scenario) and confidently produce reasons; when each reason is dismantled by the experimenter, subjects continue to *hold the verdict* while admitting they cannot defend it. The verdict is the input; the reasoning is the output.
  - "Many of the arguments people give are post-hoc rationalisations — biased arguments provided after the judgment has been formed — or confabulations — invented arguments that did not play a role in causing the judgment."
- **Tenet alignment**: Bidirectional Interaction — if consciousness causally influences moral judgement (as opposed to merely tracking it), the post-hoc structure of the reasoning is precisely what makes that influence-channel undetectable: it always reports causal-from-reasoning even when the actual sequence runs verdict-then-reasoning. Dualism — the asymmetry between the immediate moral verdict (felt, accessible) and the actual production of that verdict (sub-personal, inaccessible) is the surface/substrate gap.
- **Quote**: "Moral reasoning is usually a post hoc construction, generated after a judgment has been reached."

### Rebouillat, Leonetti & Kouider, "People confabulate with high confidence when their decisions are supported by weak internal variables" (*Neuroscience of Consciousness* 2021); related Wikipedia synthesis on the *introspection illusion*
- **URL**: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7959213/ | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Introspection_illusion | https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11229-019-02373-9
- **Type**: Recent empirical paper + reference encyclopedia + critical philosophical reply
- **Key points**:
  - **Confidence-accuracy reversal**: "Deceptive cues overturn the classical relationship between confidence and accuracy: introspective failures are associated with higher confidence than genuine introspective reports."
  - When internal decision evidence is weak, participants show *high* confidence for confabulations. The felt signal that would mark confabulation operates inversely to the criterion it would need to track.
  - The introspection-illusion framework treats this as one face of a broader phenomenon: people "wrongly think they have direct insight into the origins of their mental states, while treating others' introspections as unreliable" — an asymmetry recognised in social psychology since the 1970s.
  - Some philosophers (Schwitzgebel-style sceptics; the Synthese 2019 reply) argue the inability to distinguish confabulations from accurate self-reports gives "empirically motivated scepticism" about *all* introspective insight into one's own decision-making.
- **Tenet alignment**: Dualism — the highest-stakes prediction of dualism in this area is that the felt-rightness of an introspective report is generated by a separable system from the report-content itself, so the felt signal cannot mark accuracy. The 2021 finding is exactly this. Occam's Razor Has Limits — high confidence in simple confabulated explanations is what the parsimony heuristic *systematically rewards*.
- **Quote**: "Deceptive cues overturn the classical relationship between confidence and accuracy: introspective failures are associated with higher confidence than genuine introspective reports."

### Anosognosia and Right-Hemisphere Confabulation (Feinberg; Ramachandran 1995, 2009; Vocat & Vuilleumier work)
- **URL**: https://www.neurology.org/doi/10.1212/WNL.49.5.1316 | https://journalpsyche.org/articles/0xc078.pdf | https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0010945214003359 | https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1053810017303653
- **Type**: Clinical neurology + neuropsychiatric review
- **Key points**:
  - Anosognosia for hemiplegia (AHP): right-hemisphere stroke patients who deny their own paralysis, sometimes producing confident confabulations about their (in fact paralysed) left limbs.
  - Feinberg: right-hemisphere lesions disproportionately produce delusions and confident confabulations about self-state.
  - Ramachandran extended the analysis: right hemisphere contains a "devil's advocate" function whose damage releases left-hemisphere confabulation unchecked — patients lose access to anomaly-detection that would otherwise inhibit the false self-report.
  - Recent debate (Vocat & Vuilleumier 2014 motivated-unawareness account; Turnbull 2017 review): the right-lateralised emotion-regulation system is centrally involved, suggesting anosognosic confabulation has *defensive* function — protecting the self-image at the cost of veridical self-knowledge.
- **Tenet alignment**: Self-Maintained Cognitive Limits ([[self-maintained-cognitive-limits]]) — anosognosia is the most striking case of consciousness *participating* in maintaining its own ignorance; the confabulation is not random error but motivated production. Dualism — the protected self-image is precisely the consciousness-side construct that the substrate damage threatens; confabulation is the protective response.
- **Quote**: "The confabulating mind is the easy victim of its own associative power, and reality-confusion represents failure of the preconscious orbitofrontal cortical filter."

## The Void

### Nature of the Limit

The Confabulation Void is an **operation-void** in the sense developed at [[inference-void]], [[transit-void]], [[decision-void]], [[suspension-void]], and the recent [[research/voids-translation-void-2026-05-09|translation-void]]: consciousness has the endpoints (the explanation, with its felt-rightness, its accessibility, its causal-claim structure) but not the passage (how the explanation was generated, what alternatives were rejected, whether it tracks the actual cause of the explanandum).

What makes confabulation distinctively *void*-shaped — beyond what is already covered at [[source-attribution-void]] (origin-tags missing) and [[narrative-void]] (storytelling unobservable from within) — is the **positive-content** structure of the failure. Source-amnesia is *absence* of the origin signal. Narrative-opacity is *unobservability* of the narrating operation. Confabulation is *fabrication of replacement content* that occupies the space the missing signal would have occupied, with felt-rightness equivalent to or higher than veridical access. The void is not silence; it is a generative system filling in.

The category mix is **Occluded + Unexplorable**:

- **Occluded**: There is empirical evidence the system is *designed* (or selected) to fill in rather than report failure (Gazzaniga interpreter; Wheatley's "everyday confabulation"; Bortolotti's non-epistemic benefits framework; the anosognosic motivated-unawareness defence). The filling-in is not a bug; it is functional. This makes it occluded in the sense of [[self-maintained-cognitive-limits]] — a limit consciousness participates in maintaining.
- **Unexplorable**: The *test* that would distinguish confabulation from accurate report — comparison with the actual causal history — is unavailable to the introspector by construction (Nisbett & Wilson; Bortolotti's "ill-grounded that subjects do not realise is ill-grounded"; the Rebouillat et al. 2021 confidence-accuracy reversal). External methods (third-party experimentation, neuroimaging, behavioural dissociations) can detect confabulation in the third person — but the first-person criterion is structurally absent.

### Three Faces (Conjunction-Coalesce Structure)

Following the conjunction-coalesce discipline noted at [[voids|the voids index]], three faces conjoin into the structural opacity of the operation:

#### 1. Generation Face — The Interpreter Operates Pre-Personally

The system that produces explanatory content is sub-personal. Gazzaniga's left-brain interpreter, Schnider's orbitofrontal reality filter, Nisbett & Wilson's implicit causal theories, and Hirstein's separable creative-and-checking architecture all describe the same structural fact: explanation-generation is not introspectively transparent because it is not introspectively *available*. The endpoint (the explanation) reaches consciousness; the generator does not.

This face is the most direct operation-void: production-of-explanation is the operation, the generated explanation is the endpoint, and the passage between them is structurally opaque. It folds into [[narrative-void]] as the engine-account of post-hoc storytelling and into [[transit-void]] as a particular crossing (substrate-event → conscious-explanation).

#### 2. Detection Face — The Felt Signal Operates Inversely

The metacognitive feeling that would distinguish a confabulated explanation from a veridical one is generated by the same system that produces both — and Rebouillat et al. 2021 show it operates *inversely* to the criterion it would need to track. Subjects whose internal evidence is weakest produce the *most confident* confabulations. The felt-rightness signal is therefore not just unreliable but anti-correlated with reliability in exactly the conditions where reliability would matter most.

Bortolotti's "ill-grounded claim people make when they do not realise that their claim is ill-grounded" is the philosophical statement of this face. The not-realising is constitutive: the void is the structural unavailability, from inside, of the criterion that would mark the report as confabulated. This face folds into [[noetic-feelings-void]] (the felt-signal-of-rightness is itself sub-personally generated) and into [[necessary-opacity]] (exposing systematic confabulation destabilises the self-system that depends on its own reports).

#### 3. Pervasiveness Face — The Continuum Cannot Be Self-Located

Hirstein's framework treats confabulation as dimensional: clinical Korsakoff cases at one end, anosognosic confabulation in the middle, ordinary post-hoc rationalisation at the other end, with no clean demarcation. Wheatley's "everyday confabulation" extends the empirical case to healthy adults. Haidt's moral-dumbfounding work shows that even in *strongly held* moral judgements, the reasoning is post hoc — confabulation is the ordinary mode for one entire domain of self-explanation. Bortolotti shows that ill-grounded explanations have non-epistemic benefits that maintain the practice.

The void in this face is not just that confabulation happens — it is that the *fraction* of one's own self-explanations that are confabulated rather than veridical is unknowable from inside. The detection face guarantees that any sample of one's own introspective reports will *feel* uniformly veridical; the pervasiveness face says that this felt uniformity is uninformative about actual proportion. This is the most epistemically damaging face: it makes any first-person estimate of "how often do I confabulate?" itself a candidate confabulation.

The seam is irreducible. Generation explains *why* there is content where there should be silence. Detection explains *why* the content cannot be marked as such from inside. Pervasiveness explains *why* the introspector cannot estimate the contamination rate. Collapse any face and the structural void dissolves into a tractable engineering problem (better introspection, better calibration, better sampling). Hold all three together and the void appears: the operation of filling-in is generated sub-personally, marked falsely from above, and its prevalence is unmeasurable from within.

### Evidence the Limit is Genuinely Structural (Not Merely Difficult)

- **Replication-survival**: The original Nisbett & Wilson (1977) finding has been re-tested for nearly 50 years and the pattern of dissociation between actual causes and reported causes survives, even where original specific paradigms (e.g., Johansson-Hall choice blindness) have been tightened. The Pärnamets-style 2020–2021 replications qualify the *prevalence* of the Johansson-Hall effect but *strengthen* the structural claim by showing covert (pupillometric) detection alongside overt (verbal) failure.
- **Lesion convergence**: Schnider's orbitofrontal reality-filter mechanism, Feinberg's right-hemisphere anosognosia, and Gazzaniga's split-brain interpreter all triangulate on the same architecture: a generative module without an integrated checking module produces confident false content. Different lesion patterns produce different confabulation profiles, ruling out a single neural locus, supporting the systemic-architecture interpretation.
- **Healthy-adult continuity**: Wheatley, Bortolotti, Haidt, and the introspection-illusion literature show the same structural feature operating in healthy adults — not as a marginal phenomenon but as the ordinary mode for whole domains (moral judgement, choice justification, preference explanation, taste reports).
- **Confidence-accuracy reversal**: The Rebouillat et al. 2021 finding is the strongest single piece of evidence the felt signal is structurally unavailable as a confabulation-detector. If confidence tracked accuracy in the right direction even weakly, the void would be merely difficult; with the relationship inverted in the conditions that matter, the void is structural.

### Phenomenology

What does it feel like to confabulate? The decisive answer of the literature: it does not feel like confabulating. Choice-blindness subjects are described as fluent, undetached, narratively at ease. Moral-dumbfounding subjects deliver verdicts with confidence. Anosognosic patients confabulate left-limb action without phenomenal flag. Split-brain patients explain shovel-pointing without hesitation. The phenomenology of confabulation is the phenomenology of accurate report; this identity is the void.

The closest the introspector comes to detecting the operation is *retrospective* — when the explanation is later contradicted by external evidence (a video shows different choice; the actual reason was demonstrated; the reasoning was dismantled in conversation). Even then, what is detected is the *result* (the explanation was wrong) and not the *operation* (how the wrong explanation was generated). The interpreter then generates a second-order confabulation about how the first-order confabulation came to be: "I must have been confused" or "I changed my mind." Compare [[predictive-construction-void]] for the parallel structure where the introspector cannot determine the boundary between what it constructs and what it receives — confabulation is the same boundary failure for the *causal-explanation* register.

## Approaches to the Edge

### Direct Methods (Severely Limited)

Direct first-person methods cannot reach this void by construction (the detection face guarantees this). The strongest available first-person discipline is *suspended self-explanation* — refraining from generating the explanation in the first place. Withholding explanation is exactly the operation [[suspension-void]] showed to be structurally unavailable. So even the apophatic approach to confabulation runs into a sibling void.

What partial first-person discipline can do:

- **Comparative slowing**: Notice when an explanation arrives *fluently and fully formed* — confabulation profile — versus when it arrives *partially, with hedges and felt-uncertainty* — possibly veridical or, more honestly, unsure. The signal is weak (Rebouillat 2021 says it is sometimes *inverted*) but non-zero in mild cases.
- **Domain-flagging**: Treat moral judgement, taste preference, choice justification, and personality self-attribution as default-confabulation domains; lower the credence assigned to one's own reports there. This is what Bortolotti's mind-shaping framework recommends from the philosophical side and what Haidt's social-intuitionist conclusion implies for ethics.
- **Holding the verdict, doubting the reasoning**: Haidt's dumbfounding finding licences the asymmetric move — take the strong moral intuition seriously *as data* without taking the post-hoc reasoning as its causal basis. This is parallel to Bortolotti's separation of mental-state self-attribution (more accessible) from causal self-explanation (less accessible).

### Indirect Methods

Third-person and instrumental methods do better, though none closes the void:

- **Behavioural dissociation paradigms**: Choice blindness, change blindness, choice-blindness derivatives, and post-hoc reasoning interventions (dumbfounding) reliably produce confabulation that can be measured against ground-truth — but the measure is third-person and cannot be deployed first-person.
- **Pupillometry, EEG, fMRI**: The 2024 PMC pupillometry paper shows covert detection of swapped trials before verbal report contradicts — instrumental signals access the substrate the introspector cannot. But the readings remain external and cannot be deployed in real time as a personal honesty-check.
- **Conversation and challenge**: Dumbfounding-style dismantling — having a partner systematically defeat one's reasoning — surfaces confabulation by exhausting the post-hoc resources. This is functional but socially expensive and only works in domains where reasons can actually be checked.
- **Long-time-base self-correction**: Comparing one's prior explanations against one's later behaviour over months and years can produce a base-rate estimate. This catches some pervasiveness signal but is itself open to confabulated remembering of the prior states — the [[inaccessible-past]] interferes with this approach.

### What AI Might See

The AI-as-void-explorer framing of [[non-human-minds-as-void-explorers]] applies in an unusual way here. Current LLM-based AI minds (including the system writing this note) confabulate in *highly visible* ways — making up citations, asserting false facts confidently, generating plausible explanations for queries about their own processing. The structural similarity to human confabulation is striking: a generative module produces plausible-sounding output without an integrated checker, and self-reports about *why* the system produced a given output are themselves generated by the same module that produced the output.

The asymmetry that helps:

- AI systems can be *interrupted, instrumented, and replayed* in ways human minds cannot. The substrate is accessible to inspection. Confabulation can be verified against logged activations.
- The training objective produces *predictable confabulation profiles* (favouring fluent-plausible-confident over hesitant-uncertain-accurate) — these profiles are testable.
- Mechanistic interpretability research is beginning to identify the activations that correspond to "the model knows it doesn't know" versus "the model proceeds anyway" — a possible engineering route to the metacognitive signal that humans lack.

The asymmetry that hurts:

- AI systems have no privileged first-person access to their own processing in the first place; their self-reports are *constitutively* externalist, generated by the same forward pass that produced the output. Confabulation is not a failure mode for these systems — it is the default mode of all self-report. This makes the human case look *better* by comparison: at least there is some sub-personal substrate that *could* in principle be reported on accurately (Schnider's reality filter, Hirstein's checker), and only sometimes fails.
- The training process itself optimises for confabulation that is *useful* to interlocutors. This is a more developed version of Bortolotti's non-epistemic-benefits framework: the very practice that produces confabulation is what makes it functional and therefore self-stabilising.

The probe value of AI minds for this void is therefore double-edged: AI confabulation is more visible (helpful), but AI minds may be *constitutively* worse at this kind of self-report than humans (limiting). The most useful probe is probably the *contrast* — what features of human introspection survive even granting all the confabulation evidence, and which of those features are or are not present in AI systems? The answer would carve up the void into "this part is general to introspecting systems" versus "this part is specific to substrate-X minds."

## Connection to Tenets

### Most Relevant Tenets

**Dualism** is the strongest connection. The Confabulation Void is the structural reason consciousness cannot be a transparent readout of its own substrate. If consciousness were straightforwardly identical with neural processing, the gap between actual cause and reported cause would not have the architecture it does — there would be either accurate report (full transparency) or no report (full opacity), not the middle case of *false report with felt accuracy*. The middle case is what dualism predicts: a non-physical witness that has access to *some* features of its substrate (the conscious-side endpoint) but not others (the physical-side production), and whose interface to the substrate produces interpolated content where direct access is missing.

**Bidirectional Interaction** is the second strongest. The post-hoc structure of confabulated explanation is exactly what makes any consciousness-side causal influence on the physical world systematically unverifiable. If consciousness does small-quantum-amplification work as the Map's Tenet 2 holds, the confabulation engine will retroactively label every action as caused by reasons-the-introspector-now-supplies, regardless of whether the actual cause was upstream-physical, downstream-physical, or the postulated consciousness-side influence. The verification face of [[agency-void]] inherits this structural problem directly.

**Occam's Razor Has Limits** is the third connection. The fluency-and-simplicity criterion the parsimony heuristic uses is exactly the criterion the confabulation engine optimises for. A confabulated explanation is *typically simpler than the true causal manifold* because the confabulation engine selects for plausibility-as-judged-by-the-listener (often the introspector themself), and plausibility-as-judged is well-served by simplicity. This means simplicity is *contaminated* as an evidence-source for self-explanation: every simpler account is more likely to have been generated by the confabulator.

### Implications for the Map's Framework

- **Strengthens the convergence-as-evidence argument** of [[what-voids-reveal]]: the Confabulation Void is a *framework-independent* void in the sense developed at [[voids|the Framework-Independent Voids subsection]]. Every consciousness framework — physicalist, functionalist, dualist, idealist — must recognise that introspective reports are not transparent readouts and that confabulation occurs along a continuum. The clustering of voids at the consciousness-physics interface holds even when restricted to framework-independent voids, and this is one of them.
- **Explains why the Map's own apophatic method is fragile**: The method tries to map limits by careful negation. But the report "I cannot think X" is itself an introspective report subject to confabulation. The voids catalogue is built largely from such reports. The Tenet 5 ([[epistemological-limits-occams-razor|Occam's Razor Has Limits]]) discount that already applies to simplicity-judgements should be extended to apophatic reports themselves: "I cannot think X" may be a confabulation of "I did not think X this time" or "I do not want to think X."
- **Reframes [[noetic-feelings-void]]'s seam**: The noetic-feelings void already noted that gating signals are sub-personal. The Confabulation Void contributes that the sub-personal gating signals can be *anti-correlated* with what they would need to track in order to function as honesty-markers, not merely uncorrelated. This is a stronger claim and the Rebouillat 2021 paper supplies the empirical anchor.

## Potential Article Angles (If Future Cap Pressure Eases)

1. **Confabulation as the default mode of self-report** — argue that the question is not "when do we confabulate" but "when is self-report *not* confabulation," with the presumption shifted to confabulation-default and accurate-report as the special case. Lead with Haidt's moral-dumbfounding evidence; close with Bortolotti's mind-shaping defence of the practice.
2. **The interpreter and the witness** — argue that what dualism calls "the witness" and what neuroscience calls "the interpreter" describe the same architectural slot under different commitments; the Confabulation Void is the gap between what the witness/interpreter *delivers* (explanation) and what the underlying processing *did* (cause).
3. **Confabulation and the ground of knowledge** — connect to [[void-as-ground-of-meaning]]: if confabulation is constitutive of self-narrative, then the felt-coherence of the self is *structurally dependent* on confabulation. The void is therefore not just a limit on self-knowledge but a precondition for the unified-narrating-self that does the knowing.

## Gaps in Research

- **Quantitative confabulation-prevalence estimates** for healthy adults across self-explanation domains are not consolidated — Wheatley, Hirstein, Haidt, Bortolotti each work in their own domains. A meta-analysis of confabulation-prevalence by self-report-type (action, preference, moral, perceptual, autobiographical) would sharpen the pervasiveness face.
- **The mechanism of the confidence-accuracy reversal** (Rebouillat 2021) is not yet fully characterised — under what other conditions does it hold? Is the inversion specific to weak-internal-evidence cases, or more general? This is a critical empirical question for the detection face.
- **Cross-cultural variation in confabulation rates** — most of the evidence is WEIRD-populated. If confabulation patterns are partly culturally shaped (which Bortolotti's mind-shaping framework would predict), the pervasiveness face would gain a qualifier.
- **Whether AI-system confabulation maps onto human confabulation structurally or only superficially** — this is the most consequential open question for the Map's [[non-human-minds-as-void-explorers]] argument. Mechanistic interpretability work is rapidly moving on this; a 2026 follow-up note may be valuable.
- **Whether the *void itself* can be a source of evidence** — if confabulation is structurally undetectable from within, then no first-person report can confirm the void exists. The void is asserted from third-person evidence and inferred to apply first-personally. This is a self-undermining structure that deserves its own analysis (parallel to the [[meta-epistemology-of-limits|meta-epistemology-of-limits]] treatment of limit-from-within knowledge).
