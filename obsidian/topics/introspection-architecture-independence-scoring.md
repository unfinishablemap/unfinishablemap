Per-cluster independence scoring is the open methodological programme [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|the taxonomy of voids]] names at line 172: for each void family, audit which sources supply *despite-commitments* observation (their framework does not predict the signature) versus *because-prediction* observation (their framework does), with patient-population, cross-species, and historically-isolated-tradition evidence as the structural defences against [[common-cause-null|the common-cause null]]. The catalogue's first worked exhibit was [[the-surplus-void]], retracted in May 2026 from independent-evidence framing to framework-internal-coherence calibration. The present article is the second, scoring the four-void introspection-architecture sub-cluster (narrative, source-attribution, noetic-feelings, confabulation) and comparing its profile with the surplus-void's. The exhibit is calibration, not proof.

## The Sub-Cluster Audited

The introspection-architecture sub-cluster names the cluster of voids documenting what the [[cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit|2026-05-15 audit]] calls *the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate* — the architectural fact that introspection delivers reconstructed outputs whose generating substrate is structurally inaccessible. Four voids constitute the cluster, each capturing a different face of the same architectural feature:

- [[narrative-void]] — the *unobservability of storytelling*: the narrator mechanism that produces autobiographical and explanatory content cannot itself be inspected from the conscious side.
- [[source-attribution-void]] — *origin-tag failure*: the architecture that tags a piece of content with its source (perception versus memory versus imagination, self versus other) does not deliver source-tags directly to consciousness; they are reconstructed.
- [[noetic-feelings-void]] — *gating-signal opacity*: the felt-rightness and felt-familiarity signals that gate cognition are produced by feed-forward heuristic machinery consciousness cannot inspect.
- [[confabulation-void]] — *positive-content fabrication*: where direct access is missing, the architecture produces interpolated content that does not feel different from veridical content.

[[self-opacity]] is the broader methodological frame within which the four-void cluster sits — the source of the constitutive-thesis vocabulary and the tenet-routing spine the cluster's apex synthesis will adopt.

## The Apex's Four Criteria

[[apex/taxonomy-of-voids]] at line 162 cites the four criteria from [[apophatic-cartography]] (consolidated and operationalised at the dedicated [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria]] concept page) for treating cognitive failure as data about genuine limits rather than current ignorance:

1. **Signature specificity.** The cluster must produce a *specific* failure signature, not generic mystery — different voids produce different signatures.
2. **Structured persistence.** The failure must persist across time and across competing frameworks.
3. **Cross-observer convergence.** Independent observers (traditions, methodologies, substrates) must produce the same failure signature.
4. **Framework independence.** The failure must persist when the motivating framework is abandoned — functionalism, identity theory, illusionism, and global-workspace theory must all leave the signature intact.

The voids-circularity discount operates on criterion (4) most directly. A cluster whose framework-independence is high earns most of its inferential force; a cluster whose convergence runs primarily through the framework's own definitions earns substantially less.

## Empirical Anchors

The sub-cluster cites the following empirical sources. Citations are to the primary scientific or philosophical sources, with the cluster's vocabulary attached.

**Gazzaniga's split-brain interpreter** (Gazzaniga 1985; Gazzaniga & LeDoux 1978). Callosotomy patients shown a stimulus only to the right hemisphere produce a left-hemisphere narrative explaining behaviour the left hemisphere did not initiate. The shovel/chicken example is canonical: the right hemisphere sees a snow scene and prompts the left hand to select a shovel; the left hemisphere, which saw a chicken claw and selected the chicken, then explains the shovel as "for cleaning out the chicken shed." The narrator runs in real time without access to its own input. Cited centrally by `narrative-void` and `confabulation-void`.

**Schnider's orbitofrontal reality filter** (Schnider 2003, 2008). Patients with orbitofrontal damage confabulate when memories of past events are not filtered against their current-relevance status; the filter is preconscious and its failure is invisible to the patient. Schnider's account is mechanism-specifying rather than merely descriptive — it identifies a substrate whose function is to apply a source-tag (this memory belongs to *now* versus *not-now*) and demonstrates the architecture's necessity from its damage signature. Cited centrally by `confabulation-void` (Generation face).

**Nisbett & Wilson 1977.** "Telling more than we can know" reported that subjects asked to explain their choices in stocking-evaluation, choice-of-distractor, and similar paradigms produced confident causal explanations whose content the experimental manipulation showed to be wrong. The authors' diagnosis was that subjects had "little or no direct introspective access" to higher-order cognitive processes and were applying "implicit, *a priori* causal theories" instead. Cited by `narrative-void`, `source-attribution-void`, `confabulation-void`, and `self-opacity` (the stockings study appears in all four).

**Johansson & Hall 2005.** Choice blindness: when a card or photograph subjects had selected was covertly substituted for a different one, most subjects accepted the substituted choice and produced detailed justifications for selecting it. The 2005 result has been narrowed by 2020–2021 replication work distinguishing concurrent from retrospective measures, and strengthened by pupillometry showing autonomic recognition of the swap even when verbal reports do not flag it. Cited by `narrative-void`, `source-attribution-void`, `confabulation-void`, and `self-opacity`, with `source-attribution-void` and `confabulation-void` carrying the calibrated 2020s-literature framing.

**Coutinho et al. 2021.** The metacognition literature has long shown that subjective confidence partially tracks accuracy; the 2021 study and its sister findings sharpen the picture by identifying conditions under which felt-rightness anti-correlates with accuracy — confidence increases as performance degrades in the relevant regime. The anti-correlation claim is conditional on the experimental regime and is not a universal inversion of confidence-accuracy correlation. Cited centrally by `confabulation-void` (Detection face); not cited elsewhere in the cluster.

**Hirstein on the neuroscience of confabulation** (Hirstein 2005). Hirstein develops a framework treating confabulation as the output of an editing-and-selection system whose failure modes are observable across anosognosia, Capgras delusion, anomic responses, and split-brain. Convergent with Schnider's at the mechanism level and broader at the population level.

**Wheatley on hypnotic-suggestion confabulation** (Wheatley & Haidt 2005). Subjects hypnotised to feel disgust at a specific word produced moral judgements about scenarios containing the word and offered post-hoc reasons whose phenomenology did not differ from non-hypnotic moral reasoning — a controlled-induction parallel to the spontaneous-confabulation results.

**Haidt on moral dumbfounding** (Haidt 2001). Subjects maintained moral judgements about contrived harmless-violation scenarios after the experimenter neutralised every supporting reason they offered. The phenomenology of being-morally-certain was unaffected by the dissolution of the supporting reasons.

Wegner's I-Spy paradigm on confabulated agency, Pronin's introspection illusion, and Johnson, Hashtroudi & Lindsay 1993 on source monitoring supplement the central anchors without changing the population-type profile.

## Population-Type Profile

Sorting the anchors by what *kind* of evidence they supply isolates the population channels that can support or fail to support the common-cause null's discharge.

**Patient-population channel.** Gazzaniga split-brain, Schnider orbitofrontal patients, Hirstein's anosognosia and Capgras cases. The introspective architecture of these populations *differs* from neurotypical introspective architecture — that is the patient-population channel's distinctive force: the convergence cannot be running *only* through neurotypical shared machinery, because the populations under test exhibit architectural divergences (callosal disconnection, orbitofrontal lesion) that the standard machinery does not share. Patient-population evidence is the [[common-cause-null|common-cause null]]'s preferred test class.

**Healthy-adult experimental channel.** Nisbett-Wilson, Johansson-Hall, Coutinho et al., Wheatley-Haidt hypnotic confabulation, Haidt moral dumbfounding, Wegner I-Spy, Pronin introspection illusion. These are within-neurotypical paradigms; the shared introspective architecture is what they all run on. The channel cannot discharge a common-cause objection that locates the convergence's shared upstream *in* the neurotypical introspective architecture — but it can supply repeated, methodologically diverse demonstrations that the architecture produces the signature under varied probes.

**Cross-tradition channel.** Buddhist *kleshas* literature on motivated reasoning supplies a candidate independent tradition — the *kleshas* (afflictive mental states whose operation distorts cognition without the agent's awareness) are a structurally parallel *observation*, made in a vocabulary articulated before any modern psychology of confabulation. The historical-isolation condition the [[common-cause-null|common-cause null discipline]] specifies for treating cross-cultural evidence as a substantive discharge is only *partially* satisfied: pre-modern origin is necessary but not sufficient, and the *kleshas* tradition developed in dialogue with Sāṃkhya, Yoga, and Brahmanical philosophies of mind, so the strong reading of historical isolation is not earned. Two further calibration marks apply. First, the *kleshas* taxonomy was developed to support practice, not to investigate cognitive architecture, and its categories map only partially onto the sub-cluster's faces. Second, the Buddhist diagnosis is anti-substantialist — the operation of afflictive mental states is read as evidence that *no fixed witness lies behind the introspective stream* and that what looks like bounded access is constructed witnessing without a witness. The cluster's framing is the opposite: the architectural feature reveals a *bounded* but architecturally real witness whose access to its substrate is incomplete. The structural parallel therefore lives at the empirical surface and *not* at the architectural reading the cluster builds from that observation. Treating the *kleshas* as cross-observer agreement on the bounded-witness framing would appropriate the tradition's data while inverting its conclusion; the honest framing is cross-observer agreement on the empirical signature paired with metaphysical divergence on the diagnosis. The contemplative parallel is cited as illustrative empirical convergence rather than independent confirmation of the architectural reading.

The 2026-05-16 cross-tradition audit ([[topics/cross-tradition-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|the dedicated channel article]], with full source survey in [[research/cross-tradition-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-2026-05-16|the underlying research notes]]) extends the parallel to Advaita, Sufi, and Stoic sources at calibration-grade weight under the same metaphysical-inversion discipline. The strongest single architectural parallel is the Sufi *khawāṭir* taxonomy (Muḥāsibī, 9th c.; Ghazālī, 12th c.) to [[source-attribution-void]]: incoming thoughts are catalogued by non-self-evident source, *presupposing* that source-tags are not delivered transparently to consciousness; despite-commitments status moderate-to-high for the architectural claim. The strongest single despite-commitments observation is Stoic *propatheia* (Seneca, 1st c.) — involuntary first movements forced the cognitive theory of emotion to accommodate a sub-assent affective layer it had not predicted, mirroring the cluster's [[noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings void]]. Advaita's *adhyāsa* and *antaḥkaraṇa* doctrine supply the most architecturally elaborate parallel and invert metaphysically as the *kleshas* did: Advaita reads the architecture as constructed witnessing against unbounded Brahman, against the cluster's bounded-but-real-witness reading. Historical-isolation problems (Pyrrho/Buddhism via Beckwith 2015 contested; Sufism/Vedānta after the 3rd century Hijri; Stoic-CBT inheritance via Ellis and Beck as a partial common-cause channel for the modern anchors) discount the channel from full strength to *low-moderate* — slightly higher than the *kleshas*-only framing, not load-bearing against the strongest common-cause null.

**Cross-species channel.** Partially fillable in proxy form, not in its direct verbal-report form: introspection's signature is a discrepancy between first-person *verbal* report and underlying machinery, and most non-human animals do not produce verbal reports. The 2026-05-15 audit ([[topics/cross-species-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|the dedicated channel article]]; full survey in [[research/cross-species-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-2026-05-15|the underlying research notes]]) catalogues the substrate-level proxies — Kepecs's rat orbitofrontal confidence-coding (2008-2020) and Crystal's rodent source-monitoring extending the architectural feature to substrates distant from human prefrontal cortex; Clayton's corvid what-where-when caching (1998; 2024-25 revisit) supplying a cross-clade case in avian pallium; Hampton's macaque metamemory the more proximate primate confirmation — with the Le Pelley associative-learning critique and Carruthers's interpretive-sensory-access framework constraining despite-commitments grade. The most architecturally distant cross-observer evidence currently available runs not through animals but through LLM introspection (Vogel 2025; Lindsey et al. 2025-26), where mechanistic interpretability supplies independent ground truth and architectural distance from biological introspection is maximal. Neither the cross-tradition nor the cross-species channel updates discharge the strongest common-cause null — they expand methodological diversity in channels structurally unable to do so, leaving the patient-population channel load-bearing.

## Despite-Commitments vs Because-Prediction

The [[common-cause-null|common-cause-null discipline]] at its line 86 instructs each anchor to be classified by whether the source's generating framework *predicts* the signature (because-prediction) or *does not predict* it and reports it anyway (despite-commitments). The convergence's load-bearing force concentrates in despite-commitments observations.

The classification operates on the *observation at the time it was made*, not on whatever framework the same author later constructed to explain it. Every successful research programme generates a framework that accounts for the very findings that motivated it; if a finding were reclassified as because-prediction once its mature framework predicts it retrospectively, the despite-commitments class would collapse. The discipline's purpose is to test whether the *signature* was anticipated by the *generating model at the time of observation*, which is what the classification below tracks.

**Despite-commitments anchors.** Nisbett-Wilson 1977's cognitivist framework did not predict the result; the field treated the finding as anomalous for a decade before absorbing it. Johansson-Hall 2005's paradigm produced a result the standard rationality-and-choice models did not predict. Coutinho et al. 2021 found confidence-inversion in regimes that contradict standard metacognition models predicting monotonic accuracy-tracking. Schnider's orbitofrontal mechanism and Hirstein's broader framework were both identified through patient-population dissociation no extant model predicted. Gazzaniga's split-brain confabulation *observations* (1978) are despite-commitments with respect to the pre-1978 cognitivist default — even though Gazzaniga's *interpreter framework* (1985 onwards) was developed *to account for* those observations and is therefore not in the despite-commitments class with respect to its own later confirmations. These six observation-instances are *despite-commitments* with respect to the cognitive-psychology default expectation of veridical introspective access.

**Because-prediction anchors.** Haidt's moral-dumbfounding framework and Wheatley's hypnotic-suggestion work were conducted within the social-intuitionist programme whose central claim *is* that moral judgement precedes and motivates rational justification. Gazzaniga's interpreter framework, once articulated, generated subsequent predictions that further patient-population work then confirmed; those later confirmations sit in this class with respect to the interpreter framework specifically (the original observations remain in the despite-commitments class above). These are not common-cause failures — the predictions were earned by independent pre-experimental commitments — but they are *not* in the despite-commitments class either. They are because-prediction confirmations: the framework predicted the signature, the experiment found it, and the convergence with the rest of the cluster reflects the framework's antecedent claim rather than independent triangulation.

**The split as load-bearing, decomposed by channel.** Six observation-instances are despite-commitments and two are because-prediction on the corrected classification (the headline count depends on how the Gazzaniga, Wheatley, and Haidt borderline cases are split between observation and framework). But the headline count is *not* the licensing count for the cluster's structural claim against the strongest common-cause null. As the population-type profile admits, healthy-adult-channel paradigms cannot discharge a common-cause objection whose proposed shared upstream is the neurotypical introspective architecture itself; despite-commitments status does not rescue an anchor from a common-cause objection it is structurally unable to address. The cluster's discharge of that strongest null concentrates in the *patient-population* despite-commitments anchors — Schnider and Hirstein — two anchors, not five or six. The healthy-adult despite-commitments anchors (Nisbett-Wilson, Johansson-Hall, Coutinho) supply methodological diversity within a channel whose structural defence does not extend to the strongest null. The honest accounting: two anchors license the structural claim against the strongest common-cause null; four further despite-commitments observations supply methodological-diversity force within a channel that does not discharge it; two anchors sit in the because-prediction class. The cluster is large enough to license the structural claim where the patient-population channel licenses it, but it is *not* triangulated across many traditions, and the headline numbers should not be read as the licensing count against the strongest null.

## Scoring Against the Four Criteria

**Signature specificity.** The sub-cluster passes this criterion in a strong form. The four faces produce four genuinely different positive contents — story, origin-tag, verdict-feeling, fabricated content — and the [[cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit|2026-05-15 audit]] of the cluster found the differentiation to be substantive rather than nominal. Confabulation-void's anti-correlation finding is a face-specific signature that does not collapse into the others. The methodological discipline complementary to [[apex/conjunction-coalesce|conjunction-coalesce]] (gap-preservation between voids when adjacent failure signatures are deliberately distinct) applies here: the four faces have not been coalesced into a single void, and the audit confirms that adjacent voids are deliberately differentiated rather than reflecting curation bias.

**Structured persistence.** Strong on the empirical signature, with a calibration mark on the theoretical-persistence claim. The Nisbett-Wilson finding has persisted under nearly fifty years of attempts at resolution, including Ericsson & Simon's 1980 protocol-analysis counter-movement and the 2010s neural-correlate research programmes (which qualified scope but did not dissolve it). Schnider's mechanism has been refined but not dissolved by 2010s-2020s patient-population work. The empirical signature is stable across the cognitivist, embodied-cognition, and predictive-processing programmes. The theoretical persistence is more uneven: predictive-processing accounts (Clark, Hohwy) *predict* introspective limitations as features of hierarchical-Bayesian inference rather than as architectural opacity around a bounded witness, and therefore absorb the signature without requiring the void-framing the cluster builds from it. The signature persists; the void-framing does not survive translation into predictive-processing vocabulary as cleanly as into cognitivist or embodied-cognition vocabulary.

**Cross-observer convergence.** Mixed. The convergence is strong within the cognitive-psychology and neuropsychology programmes; moderate at the contemplative-tradition boundary (Buddhist *kleshas*, with the 2026-05-16 Advaita/Sufi/Stoic extension at calibration-grade weight only); weak at the cross-cultural-philosophy boundary; partially fillable in proxy form at the cross-species boundary (Hampton, Kepecs, Crystal, Clayton, with Le Pelley/Carruthers critiques constraining despite-commitments grade). The cluster cannot claim the full force of cross-observer triangulation; what it has is patient-population substrate-divergence within the human species plus methodological-diversity within healthy-adult experimentation. This remains the largest single calibration mark against the cluster's surface convergence claim.

**Framework independence.** Strong, but with a specific exception that the [[voids-circularity-discount|voids-circularity discount]] requires the article to name. The patient-population evidence is generated by physiology, not by the Map's framework; the experimental paradigms are produced by experimental protocols and instruments, not by the Map; the signature itself (reconstruction-not-readout) is an architectural-level claim that survives translation into functionalist, identity-theoretic, illusionist, and global-workspace vocabulary. What the framework supplies is the *significance* of the signature for the consciousness-matter question — the move from *introspection is reconstructive* to *introspection's reconstructive character indicates a bounded conscious witness with limited access to its substrate*. That second move is framework-shaped; the first is not. The cluster's framework-independence score is therefore high for the architectural finding and lower for the interpretive significance.

## Comparison with the-Surplus-Void Scoring

[[the-surplus-void|The surplus void]] is the catalogue's first worked exhibit. Scoring on the same four criteria, post-2026-05-11 retraction: signature specificity moderate; structured persistence strong (since Wallace 1870); cross-observer convergence weak (Wallace, Chalmers, Levine work in philosophically continuous traditions; the zombie argument and the explanatory gap share methodological lineage); framework independence weak (identification under dualism-friendly methodology). The present cluster scores higher on cross-observer convergence (patient-population evidence plus partial cross-tradition channel) and substantially higher on framework independence (the architectural finding survives translation across programmes), comparable on structured persistence, somewhat higher on signature specificity.

With N=2 — one cluster promoted and one retracted — the apparent discrimination is consistent with both *methodology works* and *methodology rationalises curatorial decisions already made*. Stronger non-flatness claims await an unfavourable test case (a cluster the catalogue expects to score high but the four-criterion audit scores low, or a randomly-sampled cluster scored blind). What the surplus-void exhibit demonstrates by retraction the introspection-architecture exhibit demonstrates by *partial* retention: the cluster earns a higher score than the surplus-void but lower than its surface count would have suggested. The non-flatness claim is provisional.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The exhibit is the Map's most direct application of the [[evidential-status-discipline|evidential-status discipline]] at the cluster level. The discipline's central rule — *a tenet may remove a defeater but must not upgrade the evidence level* — translates at the cluster level into: *the framework may identify a cluster's significance but must not upgrade its independence score*. The introspection-architecture cluster's significance for [[tenets|Tenet 1 (Dualism)]] and [[tenets|Tenet 3 (Bidirectional Interaction)]] is the framework's own move; the cluster's empirical anchoring is what the framework can earn. The cluster earns partial but real evidential weight, concentrated in the patient-population channel and the healthy-adult despite-commitments anchors, with cross-cultural support at illustrative-not-load-bearing strength and the cross-species channel partially fillable in proxy form only.

The cluster's signature — introspection delivers reconstruction rather than readout — is compatible with both dualism and physicalism. The Map's reading is that the architectural feature *fits* the dualist picture in which a non-physical witness has bounded access to some features of its substrate but not others; the materialist reading is that the architecture is itself the explanation, with no further metaphysical inference licensed. The exhibit honours the [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution void's neutrality discipline]] and does not treat the architectural finding as positive evidence for dualism; the framework-boundary disagreement is named, not adjudicated. The cluster's role in the [[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism|convergence argument for dualism]] is therefore compatibility plus boundary-marking, not positive support.

The weighting uses the *lower* (interpretive-significance) framework-independence score, not the higher (architectural-finding) one: the architectural finding's high framework-independence is what makes the empirical signature survive translation across programmes, but it does not transfer to the cluster's argumentative contribution because the convergence argument depends on the interpretive significance the framework supplies, whose framework-independence is lower by the article's own admission. The four-fold structure is significant for the [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|taxonomy of voids]]'s claim that consciousness-related voids exhibit *structured* clustering — a real architectural finding the catalogue's curation criteria did not generate — but the finding's evidential weight for the tenet-package is calibrated downward to what the independence tests can supply.

## Honest Limitations

The exhibit is a scoring artefact, not a closure. The despite-commitments / because-prediction classification depends on judgements about what the authoring frameworks predicted, and finer-grained classification by reviewers in different frameworks would shift some anchors at the margins. The patient-population channel's force depends on the dissociation being substantively cross-architectural rather than reflecting a shared deeper structure both populations possess; the defence is partial. The Buddhist *kleshas* parallel and the 2026-05-16 cross-tradition extension to Advaita, Sufi, and Stoic literatures are calibration-grade; the cross-architecture-via-LLMs scoring identified in the 2026-05-15 cross-species audit (Lindsey et al. 2025-26; Vogel's "strength but not source" finding as a structural parallel to [[source-attribution-void]]) remains the open programme.
