Per-cluster independence scoring is the open methodological programme [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|the taxonomy of voids]] names: for each void family, audit which sources supply *despite-commitments* observation (the framework does not predict the signature) versus *because-prediction* observation (it does), with patient-population, cross-species, and historically-isolated-tradition evidence as the structural defences against [[common-cause-null|the common-cause null]]. The catalogue's first worked exhibit was [[the-surplus-void]], retracted in May 2026. This is the second, scoring the four-void introspection-architecture sub-cluster against the surplus-void's profile and auditing in full the cross-species and cross-tradition channels — the two whose convergence claims are easiest to over-read. The exhibit is calibration, not proof.

## The Sub-Cluster Audited

The sub-cluster documents what the [[cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit|2026-05-15 audit]] calls *the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate* — introspection delivers reconstructed outputs whose generating substrate is structurally inaccessible. Four voids capture different faces of this architectural feature:

- [[narrative-void]] — *unobservability of storytelling*: the narrator mechanism producing autobiographical and explanatory content cannot itself be inspected.
- [[source-attribution-void]] — *origin-tag failure*: source-tags (perception versus memory, self versus other) are reconstructed, not delivered directly.
- [[noetic-feelings-void]] — *gating-signal opacity*: felt-rightness and felt-familiarity signals are produced by feed-forward heuristics consciousness cannot inspect.
- [[confabulation-void]] — *positive-content fabrication*: where direct access is missing, interpolated content fills in without feeling different from the veridical.

[[self-opacity]] is the broader methodological frame within which the cluster sits.

## The Apex's Four Criteria

[[apex/taxonomy-of-voids]] cites four criteria from [[apophatic-cartography]] (consolidated at [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria]]) for treating cognitive failure as data about genuine limits rather than current ignorance:

1. **Signature specificity** — a *specific* failure signature, not generic mystery; different voids produce different signatures.
2. **Structured persistence** — the failure persists across time and competing frameworks.
3. **Cross-observer convergence** — independent observers (traditions, methodologies, substrates) produce the same signature.
4. **Framework independence** — the failure persists when the motivating framework is abandoned (functionalism, identity theory, illusionism, global-workspace theory all leave it intact).

The voids-circularity discount operates on criterion (4) most directly: high framework-independence earns inferential force; convergence running through the framework's own definitions earns substantially less.

## Empirical Anchors

**Gazzaniga's split-brain interpreter** (Gazzaniga 1985; Gazzaniga & LeDoux 1978). Callosotomy patients produce a left-hemisphere narrative explaining behaviour the left hemisphere did not initiate — the canonical case explaining a right-hemisphere-prompted shovel selection as "for cleaning out the chicken shed." The narrator runs in real time without access to its own input. Cited by `narrative-void`, `confabulation-void`.

**Schnider's orbitofrontal reality filter** (Schnider 2003, 2008). Orbitofrontal damage produces confabulation when memories are not filtered against current-relevance; the filter is preconscious and its failure invisible to the patient. Mechanism-specifying: it identifies a substrate applying a source-tag (this memory belongs to *now* versus *not-now*). Cited by `confabulation-void` (Generation).

**Nisbett & Wilson 1977.** Subjects produced confident causal explanations for choices the manipulation showed to be wrong; the diagnosis was "little or no direct introspective access" to higher-order processes, with subjects applying "implicit, *a priori* causal theories." Cited across `narrative-void`, `source-attribution-void`, `confabulation-void`, `self-opacity`.

**Johansson & Hall 2005.** Choice blindness: when a selected card was covertly substituted, most subjects accepted the substitute and justified it. Narrowed by 2020–2021 replication distinguishing concurrent from retrospective measures, and strengthened by pupillometry showing autonomic recognition of the swap absent verbal flagging. [[topics/pupillometry-behavioural-channel|The behavioural-channel exhibit]] develops the pupillometric leg at the same *contested but real* register as the other anchors.

**Coutinho et al. 2021.** Identifies regime-conditional conditions under which felt-rightness *anti*-correlates with accuracy — confidence rising as performance degrades. Not a universal inversion. Cited by `confabulation-void` (Detection).

**Hirstein 2005** treats confabulation as the output of an editing-and-selection system whose failure modes span anosognosia, Capgras, anomia, and split-brain — convergent with Schnider at the mechanism level, broader at the population level.

**Wheatley & Haidt 2005 / Haidt 2001.** Hypnotic disgust at a word produced moral judgements with post-hoc reasons indistinguishable from non-hypnotic reasoning; moral dumbfounding subjects maintained judgements after every supporting reason was neutralised, certainty-phenomenology unaffected.

Wegner's I-Spy paradigm, Pronin's introspection illusion, and Johnson, Hashtroudi & Lindsay 1993 on source monitoring supplement without changing the population-type profile.

## Population-Type Profile

Sorting anchors by *kind* of evidence isolates which channels can or cannot discharge the common-cause null.

**Patient-population channel.** Gazzaniga split-brain, Schnider orbitofrontal, Hirstein anosognosia/Capgras. These populations' introspective architecture *differs* from neurotypical architecture — the channel's distinctive force: convergence cannot run only through shared neurotypical machinery, because callosal disconnection and orbitofrontal lesion are divergences that machinery does not share. The [[common-cause-null|common-cause null]]'s preferred test class.

**Healthy-adult experimental channel.** Nisbett-Wilson, Johansson-Hall, Coutinho, Wheatley-Haidt, Haidt, Wegner, Pronin — within-neurotypical paradigms on shared architecture. Cannot discharge a common-cause objection locating the shared upstream *in* that architecture, but supplies methodologically diverse demonstrations.

**Cross-tradition channel.** Buddhist *kleshas* supply a candidate independent tradition predating modern confabulation psychology, with Advaita, Sufi, and Stoic sources extending it at calibration-grade weight. The [full audit below](#the-cross-tradition-channel) shows historical isolation only partially satisfied, the channel scoring *low-moderate* once Neoplatonist common-cause mediation is honoured.

**Cross-species channel.** Partially fillable in proxy form, *asymmetrically across the four faces*, with the most architecturally distant evidence running through LLM introspection rather than biology. The [full audit below](#the-cross-species-channel) decomposes the partiality. Neither biological nor silicon channel discharges the strongest null; both expand methodological diversity face-asymmetrically, leaving the patient-population channel load-bearing.

## Despite-Commitments vs Because-Prediction

The [[common-cause-null|common-cause-null discipline]] classifies each anchor by whether the generating framework *predicted* the signature (because-prediction) or *did not predict* it and reported it anyway (despite-commitments); load-bearing force concentrates in despite-commitments observations.

Classification operates on the *observation at the time it was made*, not on whatever framework the same author later built to explain it. Every successful programme generates a framework accounting for its motivating findings; reclassifying a finding as because-prediction once its mature framework predicts it retrospectively would collapse the despite-commitments class. The test is whether the *signature* was anticipated by the *generating model at the time of observation*.

**Despite-commitments anchors.** Nisbett-Wilson 1977 (cognitivism did not predict it; treated as anomalous for a decade); Johansson-Hall 2005 (unpredicted by rationality-and-choice models); Coutinho 2021 (confidence-inversion contradicting monotonic accuracy-tracking); Schnider and Hirstein (patient-population dissociation no model predicted). Gazzaniga's split-brain *observations* (1978) are despite-commitments against the pre-1978 cognitivist default — even though his *interpreter framework* (1985+) was built *to account for* them.

**Because-prediction anchors.** Haidt's moral-dumbfounding and Wheatley's hypnotic-suggestion work sit within the social-intuitionist programme whose central claim *is* that moral judgement precedes justification. Gazzaniga's interpreter framework, once articulated, generated confirmed patient-population predictions. Not common-cause failures (the predictions were earned by independent pre-experimental commitments), but not despite-commitments either.

**The split, by channel.** Six observation-instances are despite-commitments, two because-prediction (borderline at Gazzaniga, Wheatley, Haidt). But the headline count is *not* the licensing count for the structural claim against the strongest null: healthy-adult paradigms cannot discharge an objection whose proposed shared upstream is neurotypical introspective architecture itself, and despite-commitments status does not rescue an anchor from an objection it is structurally unable to address. Discharge of the strongest null concentrates in the *patient-population* despite-commitments anchors — Schnider and Hirstein. Two anchors license the structural claim; four further despite-commitments observations supply methodological-diversity force in a channel that does not discharge it; two sit in the because-prediction class. The cluster licenses the structural claim where the patient-population channel licenses it, but is *not* triangulated across many traditions.

## Scoring Against the Four Criteria

**Signature specificity.** Strong. The four faces produce genuinely different positive contents — story, origin-tag, verdict-feeling, fabricated content — and the [[cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit|2026-05-15 audit]] found the differentiation substantive, not nominal. Confabulation-void's anti-correlation does not collapse into the others; the gap-preservation discipline complementary to [[apex/conjunction-coalesce|conjunction-coalesce]] confirms deliberate differentiation rather than curation bias.

**Structured persistence.** Strong on the empirical signature, with a calibration mark on theoretical persistence. Nisbett-Wilson has persisted under nearly fifty years of resolution attempts (Ericsson & Simon's 1980 protocol-analysis movement, 2010s neural-correlate work — qualifying scope, not dissolving it); Schnider's mechanism is refined but intact; the signature is stable across cognitivist, embodied-cognition, and predictive-processing programmes. Theoretical persistence is uneven: predictive-processing accounts (Clark, Hohwy) *predict* introspective limits as features of hierarchical-Bayesian inference, absorbing the signature without the void-framing.

**Cross-observer convergence.** Mixed and *face-asymmetric*. Strong within cognitive-psychology/neuropsychology; moderate at the contemplative-tradition boundary (Buddhist *kleshas*, Advaita/Sufi/Stoic at calibration-grade); weak at cross-cultural-philosophy; at cross-species, cleanly proxy-fillable for source-attribution, partially for noetic-feelings, principled-blocked for confabulation (bandwidth) and narrative (no clean analogue) — the [audit below](#the-cross-species-channel) and the [[topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests|proxy-test design audit]] make the asymmetry explicit. The cluster cannot claim full triangulation; what it has is patient-population substrate-divergence within humans plus methodological diversity within healthy-adult work, the cross-species channel contributing face-asymmetrically — the largest single calibration mark against the surface convergence claim.

**Framework independence.** Strong, with a specific exception the [[voids-circularity-discount|voids-circularity discount]] requires naming. Patient-population evidence is generated by physiology, experimental paradigms by independent protocols, and the signature itself (reconstruction-not-readout) survives translation into functionalist, identity-theoretic, illusionist, and global-workspace vocabulary. What the framework supplies is the *significance* — the move from *introspection is reconstructive* to *its reconstructive character indicates a bounded conscious witness with limited substrate access*. That second move is framework-shaped; the first is not. Framework-independence is high for the architectural finding, lower for the interpretive significance.

## The Cross-Species Channel

The cross-species channel is one of the two easiest to over-read and the patient-population channel's main competitor for load-bearing status. Worked in full, it supplies *partial methodological diversity* rather than despite-commitments triangulation.

### The Principled Obstacle

The cluster's central signature is a *report-versus-mechanism discrepancy* checkable against experimenter-controlled ground truth — Gazzaniga's narratives, Nisbett-Wilson's explanations, choice-blindness justifications. Most non-human animals produce no such report, so the discrepancy cannot be observed in its native form (Birch's *Edge of Sentience*: precautionary architecture built from proxies *because* introspection-grade self-report is unavailable). What remains is *substrate-level evidence* that a confidence-or-source-monitoring substrate — whose damage produces cluster-relevant human failures — appears preserved across rodent, primate, and corvid lineages. The inference from substrate-preservation to architectural-feature-preservation is partial.

### Substrate-Level Proxies

The companion [[topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests|proxy-tests article]] reads three proxy bodies as partial prototypes of a [[topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests#four-face-proxy-architecture|four-face proxy-test architecture]]: Crystal's rodent source-monitoring → source-attribution; Kepecs's rodent OFC and Hampton's macaque metamemory → noetic-feelings; Krupenye's chimpanzee anticipatory-looking → confabulation at narrow bandwidth; no proxy reaches narrative.

- **Rodent OFC confidence coding** (Kepecs 2008, 2014, 2020; Smith 2021). Rats compute trial-level confidence in near-threshold odour discriminations; OFC inactivation disrupts waiting-based confidence without impairing accuracy; the 2020 *Cell* paper extends it as modality- and behavior-general. *Moderate-to-high.*
- **Rodent source-monitoring lesions** (Crystal lab, 2010s). Rats remember information *source* under conditions excluding encoding-failure; medial prefrontal lesions parallel human prefrontal source-monitoring impairment. Closest analogue to the [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]]. *Moderate despite-commitments.*
- **Corvid what-where-when caching** (Clayton & Dickinson 1998; 2024-25 revisit framing the binding as source-monitoring). Avian pallium is more architecturally distant from mammalian cortex than rodent OFC is from primate prefrontal cortex. Hampton's macaque metamemory (2001, 2019) confirms the working-memory / familiarity dissociation in a primate.

A fourth proxy — mirror self-recognition (Kohda cleaner-wrasse 2019/2023/2025) — is *cluster-adjacent*: it probes self-versus-other perceptual binding, dissociable from whether process-reports are reconstructed.

### Despite-Commitments vs Because-Prediction (Cross-Species)

The four substrate observations are *despite-commitments at the substrate-extension level*: pre-2008 defaults did not predict confidence, source-tag, and feature-binding signatures would generalise across phylogenetically distant substrates. *Contested at the metacognition-grade level* by two critiques. **Le Pelley 2012** showed associative-learning models reproduce hallmark putative-metacognition patterns through reinforcement history alone — refuting the substrate-to-metacognition inference, not the substrate findings. **Carruthers** (*Opacity of Mind* 2011; 2021) reads human introspection as itself interpretive and animal metacognition as an analog-magnitude executive-engagement signal — a within-physicalist reading that *predicts* the cross-species pattern on grounds that may undercut the bounded-witness reading. Honest scoring: *partial methodological diversity*. The patient-population channel remains load-bearing.

### The Cross-Architecture Pivot {#cross-architecture-pivot}

The most architecturally distant channel currently available is not biological. LLM introspection studies — Lindsey et al. (Anthropic, 2025) on emergent introspective awareness, introspection-adapters (2026) across non-Anthropic families, Vogel 2025 "Feeling the Strength but Not the Source" — open a channel biology cannot match: (1) LLMs produce reports, so the verbal-report obstacle does not apply; (2) interpretability supplies independent ground truth via thought-injection (~20% detection, 0% false positives in Claude Opus 4/4.1; adapters extend it across four out-of-distribution families); (3) architectural distance is maximal — transformer residual streams are radically further from mammalian cortex than rodent OFC from primate prefrontal cortex.

Vogel's "strength but not source" is the most cluster-relevant artefact: LLMs detect injected-thought *strength* but not *source* — a structural parallel to the [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]] generated in silicon with interpretability fixing ground truth. *High despite-commitments by construction*: next-token prediction did not predict source-tag failure. The channel brackets phenomenal consciousness (see [[ai-consciousness|the AI-consciousness framing]]); the architectural signature may survive bracketing, but only insofar as the silicon parallel tracks the same operational structure as the biological case. *Open programme*: interpretability methods are model-dependent, the variant long-tail untested, and the inference from "fails to identify injected-thought source" to "introspection has the cluster's structural feature" carries questionable assumptions.

### Scoring the Cross-Species Channel

Against the four criteria: *signature specificity* partial (proxies match a *general* monitoring signature better than the specific report-versus-mechanism discrepancy, genuine only where a proxy maps to a face, Crystal → source-attribution); *structured persistence* strong on the substrate-extension finding; *cross-observer convergence* moderate within biology, weak as discharge of the strongest null; *framework independence* moderate, the architectural-feature inference more framework-shaped than the substrate findings, with Carruthers a within-physicalist alternative. Net — biology: *partial methodological diversity* with discounts on the substrate-to-metacognition step; LLMs: *architectural-distance triangulation*, Vogel 2025 most cluster-relevant, open programme.

## The Cross-Tradition Channel

### The Framework-Level Trap

Cross-tradition arguments commonly read framework-level predictions as independent observations. Each tradition has a soteriological doctrine whose central diagnostic is that ordinary cognition is unreliable — Advaita's *avidyā*, Sufism's *nafs ammāra*, Stoicism's passion-as-false-judgement. Citing each tradition's general claim that introspection fails is circular: the diagnostic is what the framework was built to deliver.

The [[voids-circularity-discount|voids-circularity discount]] requires architectural-specifics findings the framework did *not* predict. A claim earns architectural-specifics status only by passing all three: (1) **vocabulary-stripping survival** — still expresses a contentful structural feature once soteriological vocabulary is removed; (2) **observation-grounded origin** — supported by a tradition-internal observation report (practice manual, refinement under disagreement, case description), not doctrinal derivation; (3) **refinement rather than recapitulation** — adds structure the original diagnostic did not require. Failing any collapses the claim to because-prediction.

### Advaita Vedānta

Shankara's 8th-c. *Adhyāsa Bhāṣya* treats *adhyāsa* ("superimposition") — body-properties projected onto Ātman and vice versa — as a structural cognitive failure. Architectural specifics live in the *antaḥkaraṇa* ("inner instrument") doctrine inherited from Sāṃkhya/Yoga: a fourfold inert structure (*manas*, *buddhi*, *ahaṃkāra* the I-maker, *citta* the impression-storehouse) in which consciousness reflects (*pratibimba*). The witness accesses instrument *outputs* but not inner workings; discrimination (*viveka*) is the task *because* the architecture makes witness and instrument ordinarily indistinguishable. The *vāsanā*/*saṃskāra* doctrine adds latent dispositional tendencies operating outside awareness — the closest Advaita analogue to Nisbett & Wilson's *implicit causal theory*.

*Scoring.* The framework-level bounded-access witness is because-prediction (fails 3). The *antaḥkaraṇa* subdivision earns *moderate* despite-commitments on (2) — it appears to refine the Sāṃkhya/Yoga inheritance — and the *ahaṃkāra*-as-narrator function may survive vocabulary-stripping (parallel to the Gazzaniga interpreter). The [[research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16|Neoplatonist audit]] forecloses both prima facie independence problems: Armstrong 1936 reads early Neoplatonism as original Greek; Aklan 2018 finds Plotinian-Śaṅkara parallels "metaphorical"; the Mughal channel runs the wrong direction (Dārā Shikoh's *Sirr-i-Akbar* post-dates Shankara by ~850 years).

### Sufi Practice: Khawāṭir and Hidden Riyāʾ {#sufi-khawatir}

The Sufi *khawāṭir* taxonomy classifies incoming thoughts by source — divine, angelic, egoic, satanic — *presupposing* source is not self-evident. Strip the typology and what remains is the [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]]'s architectural claim, the same fact Johnson-Hashtroudi-Lindsay 1993 name in cognitive-psychology vocabulary. The tradition discovered it through *muḥāsaba* — al-Muḥāsibī's (d. 857 CE) self-examination programme, whose diagnostic was that pious practices can foster *riyāʾ* (hypocrisy) the agent does not see. Al-Ghazālī's 11th-c. *Iḥyāʾ* extends this into *riyāʾ khafiyy* (hidden hypocrisy) as a *structural* feature: a self-display function invisible to the agent accompanying apparently virtuous behaviour by default — the strongest cross-tradition parallel to the Wheatley-Haidt/Nisbett-Wilson rationalisation anchoring [[confabulation-void|confabulation void]]'s Generation face.

*Scoring.* *Khawāṭir* is framework-internal because-prediction (fails 3); *low*. Hidden *riyāʾ* is cleaner: earlier Sufism treated *riyāʾ* as occasional failure remediable by sincere intention, whereas Ghazālī's finding that it persists *despite* sincere intention passes all three at *moderate*. The rating softens at framing: Ghazālī's psychology is downstream of the Avicennan-Neoplatonist synthesis, and the [[research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16|Neoplatonist audit]] identifies two channels prefiguring *khawāṭir* in 9th-c. Baghdad — primarily Christian-monastic-via-Evagrius (*logismoi* + *antirrhēsis*, ~400 years of Syriac transmission); secondarily direct Arabic Plotinus via al-Kindī. The cleanest-historical-isolation attribution shifts from early-Sufi practice to Advaita.

### Stoic Propatheia {#stoic-propatheia}

Stoic theory held emotions to be *judgements*: a *phantasia* appears, the agent assents (*synkatathesis*), the assent constitutes the passion (framework-level, circular). Seneca's *propatheia* (*De Ira*) is the architecturally refined observation: involuntary affective reactions (startle, the first jolt of fear) *preceding* assent, experienced even by the sage. On Sorabji 2000's reconstruction the doctrine was *added* to handle observations the pure cognitive theory did not predict — startle forced a sub-assent layer mapping to the [[noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings void]]. Inwood and Long argue "first movements" were implicit earlier and Seneca's contribution terminological; the audit conditions on Sorabji. Cutting the other way, REBT and cognitive therapy drew explicitly on Stoicism (Robertson 2019), so modern evidence is partly downstream of Stoic categories — despite-commitments against Stoicism is *weaker* than against Buddhism or Advaita.

### Scoring the Cross-Tradition Channel

Against the four criteria: *signature specificity* moderate (doctrines map onto specific faces — *khawāṭir* → source-attribution, *propatheia* → noetic-feelings on Sorabji, hidden *riyāʾ*/*vāsanā* → confabulation Generation, *ahaṃkāra* → narrative); *structured persistence* strong (doctrines persisted under intra-tradition critique across centuries); *cross-observer convergence* *low-moderate* with differentiated Neoplatonist discounts — weak (≈none) for Stoicism (Plotinus downstream of the Stoa), weak-to-negligible for Advaita (Bréhier-Armstrong-Aklan 2018), moderate-to-substantial for Sufism via Evagrius and direct Arabic Plotinus; *framework independence* moderate (architectural-specifics survive cross-framework translation, framework-level claims earn no credit).

Net: the channel *adds* despite-commitments observations passing the test (Ghazālī's hidden *riyāʾ*; *propatheia* on Sorabji; the *antaḥkaraṇa* subdivision); it *subtracts* discounts from Pyrrho-Buddhism (Beckwith 2015 contested), the late-medieval Sufism-Vedānta synthesis, the Stoic-CBT lineage, and the Neoplatonist channel — concentrated in Advaita as cleanest-isolated and Ghazālī's hidden *riyāʾ* as cleanest single architectural-specifics finding. The [[topics/neoplatonist-common-cause-weight-for-cross-tradition-introspection-architecture-parallels|dedicated Neoplatonist audit]] supplies per-tradition detail.

### Combined Channel Verdict

Both auxiliary channels supply *calibration-grade rather than load-bearing* convergence. Neither discharges the strongest null — that the shared upstream may lie in neurotypical introspective architecture or shared framework commitments; discharge remains concentrated in the patient-population channel. What both *add* is substrate and tradition breadth (rodent OFC, corvid pallium, transformer residual streams; *riyāʾ khafiyy*, *propatheia*, *antaḥkaraṇa*) and a single converging source-attribution anchor (Vogel 2025, *khawāṭir*, Crystal's rodent source-monitoring). Shared discounts: *translation construction* (a Churchland-style reading absorbs both translations as one observation) and *framework-level absorption* (Carruthers absorbs cross-species as because-prediction; predictive-processing absorbs opacity into hierarchical-Bayesian inference).

## Comparison with the-Surplus-Void Scoring

[[the-surplus-void|The surplus void]], the catalogue's first worked exhibit, scores post-retraction (2026-05-11): signature specificity moderate; structured persistence strong (since Wallace 1870); cross-observer convergence weak (Wallace, Chalmers, Levine in continuous traditions; zombie argument and explanatory gap share lineage); framework independence weak (identification under dualism-friendly methodology). The present cluster scores higher on cross-observer convergence, substantially higher on framework independence, comparable on persistence, somewhat higher on specificity.

With N=2 — one promoted, one retracted — the discrimination is consistent with both *methodology works* and *methodology rationalises decisions already made*; stronger non-flatness claims await an unfavourable test case. The surplus-void demonstrates by retraction what this exhibit demonstrates by *partial* retention: the cluster earns a higher score than the surplus-void but lower than its surface count suggested. The non-flatness claim is provisional.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The exhibit is the Map's most direct cluster-level application of the [[evidential-status-discipline|evidential-status discipline]]. Its central rule — *a tenet may remove a defeater but must not upgrade the evidence level* — translates here into: *the framework may identify a cluster's significance but must not upgrade its independence score*. The cluster's significance for [[tenets|Tenet 1 (Dualism)]] and [[tenets|Tenet 3 (Bidirectional Interaction)]] is the framework's own move; the empirical anchoring is what it can earn. The cluster earns partial but real evidential weight, concentrated in the patient-population channel and the healthy-adult despite-commitments anchors, with cross-cultural support at illustrative strength, the cross-species channel only partially proxy-fillable, and the cross-architecture LLM evidence bracketing phenomenal consciousness.

The signature — introspection delivers reconstruction rather than readout — is compatible with both dualism and physicalism. The Map's reading is that it *fits* the dualist picture in which a non-physical witness has bounded access to some features of its substrate but not others; the materialist reading is that the architecture is itself the explanation, with no further metaphysical inference licensed. The exhibit honours the [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution void's neutrality discipline]] and does not treat the architectural finding as positive evidence for dualism; the framework-boundary disagreement is named, not adjudicated. The cluster's role in the [[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism|convergence argument for dualism]] is compatibility plus boundary-marking, not positive support.

The weighting uses the *lower* (interpretive-significance) framework-independence score, not the higher (architectural-finding) one: the convergence argument depends on the interpretive significance the framework supplies, whose independence is lower by the article's own admission. The four-fold structure is significant for the [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|taxonomy of voids]]'s claim that consciousness-related voids exhibit *structured* clustering — a real finding the catalogue's curation criteria did not generate — but evidential weight for the tenet-package is calibrated to what the independence tests can supply.

## Honest Limitations

The exhibit is a scoring artefact, not a closure. The despite-commitments / because-prediction classification depends on judgements about what frameworks predicted; finer classification would shift anchors at the margins. The patient-population channel's force depends on the dissociation being substantively cross-architectural rather than shared structure both populations possess — a partial defence.

The most consequential residual gap: whether Evagrian *logismoi* reduces to Stoicism via *antirrhēsis*, in which case the Sufi-via-Christian-monastic and modern Stoic-CBT channels share a single upstream — making Stoicism load-bearing and the cross-tradition convergence collapsing closer to "Advaita + Stoicism + downstream Stoic influences" than to independent witnesses. A defensible conservative reading — both auxiliary channels earn no upgrade over the patient-population defence — the article does not adopt but does not refute.

Translations across substrates and tradition vocabularies are *interpretive* throughout — calibrated *plausibilities*, not substrate- or tradition-internal commitments. A Churchland-style eliminativist would argue the architectural specifics are constructions of the modern source-monitoring framework projected onto both substrate data and source texts; if translation construction is heavily in play, the combined convergence may be *one* observation rather than several.
