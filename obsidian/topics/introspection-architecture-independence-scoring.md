Per-cluster independence scoring is the open methodological programme [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|the taxonomy of voids]] names: for each void family, audit which sources supply *despite-commitments* observation (the framework does not predict the signature) versus *because-prediction* observation (it does), with patient-population, cross-species, and historically-isolated-tradition evidence as the structural defences against [[common-cause-null|the common-cause null]]. The catalogue's first worked exhibit was [[the-surplus-void]], retracted in May 2026 to framework-internal-coherence calibration. The present article is the second, scoring the four-void introspection-architecture sub-cluster and comparing its profile with the surplus-void's. The exhibit is calibration, not proof.

## The Sub-Cluster Audited

The sub-cluster documents what the [[cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit|2026-05-15 audit]] calls *the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate* — introspection delivers reconstructed outputs whose generating substrate is structurally inaccessible. Four voids capture different faces of this architectural feature:

- [[narrative-void]] — *unobservability of storytelling*: the narrator mechanism producing autobiographical and explanatory content cannot itself be inspected.
- [[source-attribution-void]] — *origin-tag failure*: source-tags (perception versus memory, self versus other) are reconstructed, not delivered directly.
- [[noetic-feelings-void]] — *gating-signal opacity*: felt-rightness and felt-familiarity signals are produced by feed-forward heuristics consciousness cannot inspect.
- [[confabulation-void]] — *positive-content fabrication*: where direct access is missing, interpolated content fills in without feeling different from the veridical.

[[self-opacity]] is the broader methodological frame within which the cluster sits.

## The Apex's Four Criteria

[[apex/taxonomy-of-voids]] cites four criteria from [[apophatic-cartography]] (consolidated at [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria]]) for treating cognitive failure as data about genuine limits rather than current ignorance:

1. **Signature specificity.** A *specific* failure signature, not generic mystery — different voids produce different signatures.
2. **Structured persistence.** The failure persists across time and competing frameworks.
3. **Cross-observer convergence.** Independent observers (traditions, methodologies, substrates) produce the same signature.
4. **Framework independence.** The failure persists when the motivating framework is abandoned — functionalism, identity theory, illusionism, and global-workspace theory all leave it intact.

The voids-circularity discount operates on criterion (4) most directly: high framework-independence earns inferential force; convergence running through the framework's own definitions earns substantially less.

## Empirical Anchors

**Gazzaniga's split-brain interpreter** (Gazzaniga 1985; Gazzaniga & LeDoux 1978). Callosotomy patients shown a stimulus only to the right hemisphere produce a left-hemisphere narrative explaining behaviour the left hemisphere did not initiate. The canonical shovel/chicken example: the right hemisphere sees a snow scene and prompts the left hand to select a shovel; the left hemisphere, which selected a chicken, explains the shovel as "for cleaning out the chicken shed." The narrator runs in real time without access to its own input. Cited by `narrative-void` and `confabulation-void`.

**Schnider's orbitofrontal reality filter** (Schnider 2003, 2008). Patients with orbitofrontal damage confabulate when memories are not filtered against current-relevance status; the filter is preconscious and its failure is invisible to the patient. Mechanism-specifying rather than merely descriptive: it identifies a substrate applying a source-tag (this memory belongs to *now* versus *not-now*). Cited by `confabulation-void` (Generation face).

**Nisbett & Wilson 1977.** "Telling more than we can know" reported subjects producing confident causal explanations for choices the experimental manipulation showed to be wrong. The diagnosis: "little or no direct introspective access" to higher-order cognitive processes, with subjects applying "implicit, *a priori* causal theories" instead. Cited across `narrative-void`, `source-attribution-void`, `confabulation-void`, and `self-opacity`.

**Johansson & Hall 2005.** Choice blindness: when a selected card or photograph was covertly substituted, most subjects accepted the substitute and produced detailed justifications. Narrowed by 2020–2021 replication work distinguishing concurrent from retrospective measures, and strengthened by pupillometry showing autonomic recognition of the swap even when verbal reports do not flag it. [[topics/pupillometry-behavioural-channel|The behavioural-channel exhibit]] develops the pupillometric leg as a channel-level finding at the same *contested but real* register as the cluster's other anchors.

**Coutinho et al. 2021.** Subjective confidence partially tracks accuracy; this study identifies conditions under which felt-rightness *anti*-correlates with accuracy — confidence increases as performance degrades in the relevant regime. The anti-correlation is regime-conditional, not a universal inversion. Cited by `confabulation-void` (Detection face).

**Hirstein 2005** treats confabulation as the output of an editing-and-selection system whose failure modes appear across anosognosia, Capgras delusion, anomic responses, and split-brain — convergent with Schnider at the mechanism level, broader at the population level.

**Wheatley & Haidt 2005.** Subjects hypnotised to feel disgust at a specific word produced moral judgements about scenarios containing it, with post-hoc reasons indistinguishable from non-hypnotic moral reasoning. **Haidt 2001** moral dumbfounding: subjects maintained moral judgements about contrived scenarios after every supporting reason was neutralised; certainty-phenomenology was unaffected.

Wegner's I-Spy paradigm, Pronin's introspection illusion, and Johnson, Hashtroudi & Lindsay 1993 on source monitoring supplement without changing the population-type profile.

## Population-Type Profile

Sorting anchors by *kind* of evidence isolates which channels can or cannot discharge the common-cause null.

**Patient-population channel.** Gazzaniga split-brain, Schnider orbitofrontal patients, Hirstein's anosognosia and Capgras cases. The introspective architecture of these populations *differs* from neurotypical architecture — that is the channel's distinctive force: convergence cannot be running only through shared neurotypical machinery, because callosal disconnection and orbitofrontal lesion are divergences the standard machinery does not share. This is the [[common-cause-null|common-cause null]]'s preferred test class.

**Healthy-adult experimental channel.** Nisbett-Wilson, Johansson-Hall, Coutinho et al., Wheatley-Haidt, Haidt, Wegner, Pronin. Within-neurotypical paradigms running on shared introspective architecture. The channel cannot discharge a common-cause objection locating the shared upstream *in* that architecture itself — but supplies methodologically diverse demonstrations of the signature.

**Cross-tradition channel.** Buddhist *kleshas* on motivated reasoning supply a candidate independent tradition predating modern psychology of confabulation. Historical isolation is only *partially* satisfied: the *kleshas* developed in dialogue with Sāṃkhya, Yoga, and Brahmanical philosophies; the taxonomy was developed for practice not cognitive-architecture investigation; and the Buddhist diagnosis is anti-substantialist — afflictive states read as evidence *no fixed witness lies behind the stream*, against the cluster's *bounded-but-architecturally-real-witness* reading. Honest framing: cross-observer agreement on the empirical signature paired with metaphysical divergence on the diagnosis. Illustrative convergence, not independent confirmation.

The 2026-05-16 cross-tradition audit ([[topics/channel-audits-introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|unified channel-audits article]]; [[research/cross-tradition-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-2026-05-16|notes]]; [[research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16|Neoplatonist common-cause audit]]) extends to Advaita, Sufi, and Stoic sources at calibration-grade weight. Two architectural parallels survive vocabulary-stripping: Ghazālī's *riyāʾ khafiyy* (11th c.) maps to the confabulation void's Generation face, softened by the Avicennan-Neoplatonist faculty chain; Stoic *propatheia* (Seneca, 1st c.) maps to the noetic-feelings void via involuntary first movements forcing cognitive emotion theory to accommodate a sub-assent affective layer. Advaita's *adhyāsa* / *antaḥkaraṇa* supplies the most architecturally elaborate parallel and inverts metaphysically as the *kleshas* did. The Neoplatonist audit yields *differentiated* isolation weights: **weak** for Stoicism (Plotinus downstream of the Stoa); **weak-to-negligible** for Advaita (Bréhier-Armstrong-Hacker-Aklan 2018 — Advaita carries the cleanest defence); **moderate-to-substantial** for Sufism via Evagrian *logismoi* + *diakrisis* (4th c., Syriac transmission prefiguring *khawāṭir* by ~400 years). With Pyrrho/Buddhism (Beckwith 2015 contested) and Stoic-CBT inheritance, the channel scores *low-moderate* — a downward recalibration once broader-scope discounts surface, not load-bearing against the strongest common-cause null.

**Cross-species channel.** Partially fillable in proxy form, *asymmetrically across the four faces*. The 2026-05-15 audit ([[topics/channel-audits-introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|channel-audits article]]; [[research/cross-species-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-2026-05-15|notes]]) catalogues substrate-level proxies — Kepecs (rat OFC confidence-coding), Crystal (rodent source-monitoring), Clayton (corvid what-where-when in avian pallium), Hampton (macaque metamemory) — with Le Pelley's associative-learning critique and Carruthers's interpretive-sensory-access framework constraining despite-commitments grade. The companion [[topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests|proxy-test design audit]] decomposes the partiality: source-attribution proxies cleanly (Crystal, Clayton); noetic-feelings partially (Hampton, Kepecs, Smith); confabulation is principled-blocked at full strength by bandwidth (Krupenye's anticipatory-looking reaches it softly); narrative is principled-blocked. The most architecturally distant evidence runs through LLM introspection (Vogel 2025; Lindsey et al. 2025-26). Neither channel discharges the strongest common-cause null; both expand methodological diversity face-asymmetrically, leaving the patient-population channel load-bearing.

## Despite-Commitments vs Because-Prediction

The [[common-cause-null|common-cause-null discipline]] classifies each anchor by whether the generating framework *predicted* the signature (because-prediction) or *did not predict* it and reported it anyway (despite-commitments). The convergence's load-bearing force concentrates in despite-commitments observations.

Classification operates on the *observation at the time it was made*, not on whatever framework the same author later constructed to explain it. Every successful research programme generates a framework accounting for the findings that motivated it; reclassifying a finding as because-prediction once its mature framework predicts it retrospectively would collapse the despite-commitments class. The test is whether the *signature* was anticipated by the *generating model at the time of observation*.

**Despite-commitments anchors.** Nisbett-Wilson 1977's cognitivist framework did not predict the result; the field treated it as anomalous for a decade. Johansson-Hall 2005's result was not predicted by standard rationality-and-choice models. Coutinho et al. 2021 found confidence-inversion contradicting standard metacognition models predicting monotonic accuracy-tracking. Schnider and Hirstein were identified through patient-population dissociation no extant model predicted. Gazzaniga's split-brain *observations* (1978) are despite-commitments with respect to the pre-1978 cognitivist default — even though his *interpreter framework* (1985 onwards) was constructed *to account for* those observations.

**Because-prediction anchors.** Haidt's moral-dumbfounding and Wheatley's hypnotic-suggestion work were conducted within the social-intuitionist programme whose central claim *is* that moral judgement precedes rational justification. Gazzaniga's interpreter framework, once articulated, generated further patient-population predictions then confirmed. Not common-cause failures — the predictions were earned by independent pre-experimental commitments — but not despite-commitments either.

**The split, decomposed by channel.** Six observation-instances are despite-commitments and two are because-prediction (borderline cases at Gazzaniga, Wheatley, Haidt). But the headline count is *not* the licensing count for the structural claim against the strongest common-cause null. Healthy-adult-channel paradigms cannot discharge an objection whose proposed shared upstream is the neurotypical introspective architecture itself; despite-commitments status does not rescue an anchor from an objection it is structurally unable to address. The cluster's discharge of the strongest null concentrates in the *patient-population* despite-commitments anchors — Schnider and Hirstein. Two anchors license the structural claim against the strongest null; four further despite-commitments observations supply methodological-diversity force in a channel that does not discharge it; two sit in the because-prediction class. The cluster licenses the structural claim where the patient-population channel licenses it, but is *not* triangulated across many traditions.

## Scoring Against the Four Criteria

**Signature specificity.** Strong. The four faces produce genuinely different positive contents — story, origin-tag, verdict-feeling, fabricated content — and the [[cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit|2026-05-15 audit]] found the differentiation substantive rather than nominal. Confabulation-void's anti-correlation finding does not collapse into the others. The discipline complementary to [[apex/conjunction-coalesce|conjunction-coalesce]] (gap-preservation between deliberately distinct adjacent signatures) applies: the four faces are not coalesced, and the audit confirms deliberate differentiation rather than curation bias.

**Structured persistence.** Strong on the empirical signature, with a calibration mark on theoretical persistence. Nisbett-Wilson has persisted under nearly fifty years of attempts at resolution, including Ericsson & Simon's 1980 protocol-analysis counter-movement and 2010s neural-correlate work (qualifying scope, not dissolving it). Schnider's mechanism has been refined but not dissolved. The empirical signature is stable across cognitivist, embodied-cognition, and predictive-processing programmes. Theoretical persistence is uneven: predictive-processing accounts (Clark, Hohwy) *predict* introspective limitations as features of hierarchical-Bayesian inference rather than as architectural opacity around a bounded witness, absorbing the signature without requiring the void-framing.

**Cross-observer convergence.** Mixed and *face-asymmetric*. Strong within cognitive-psychology and neuropsychology; moderate at the contemplative-tradition boundary (Buddhist *kleshas*, with Advaita/Sufi/Stoic extension at calibration-grade weight); weak at cross-cultural-philosophy; at cross-species, partially fillable in proxy form for source-attribution (cleanly) and noetic-feelings (partially), principled-blocked at full strength for confabulation (bandwidth limit) and narrative (no clean cross-species analogue) — the [[topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests|proxy-test design audit]] makes the asymmetry explicit. The cluster cannot claim full cross-observer triangulation; what it has is patient-population substrate-divergence within humans plus methodological-diversity within healthy-adult experimentation, with the cross-species channel contributing face-asymmetrically — the largest single calibration mark against the surface convergence claim.

**Framework independence.** Strong, with a specific exception the [[voids-circularity-discount|voids-circularity discount]] requires the article to name. Patient-population evidence is generated by physiology, not the Map's framework; experimental paradigms by independent protocols; and the signature itself (reconstruction-not-readout) survives translation into functionalist, identity-theoretic, illusionist, and global-workspace vocabulary. What the framework supplies is the *significance* — the move from *introspection is reconstructive* to *introspection's reconstructive character indicates a bounded conscious witness with limited access to its substrate*. That second move is framework-shaped; the first is not. Framework-independence is therefore high for the architectural finding, lower for the interpretive significance.

## Comparison with the-Surplus-Void Scoring

[[the-surplus-void|The surplus void]] is the catalogue's first worked exhibit. On the same four criteria post-2026-05-11 retraction: signature specificity moderate; structured persistence strong (since Wallace 1870); cross-observer convergence weak (Wallace, Chalmers, Levine in philosophically continuous traditions; zombie argument and explanatory gap share methodological lineage); framework independence weak (identification under dualism-friendly methodology). The present cluster scores higher on cross-observer convergence and substantially higher on framework independence, comparable on structured persistence, somewhat higher on signature specificity.

With N=2 — one cluster promoted, one retracted — the apparent discrimination is consistent with both *methodology works* and *methodology rationalises curatorial decisions already made*. Stronger non-flatness claims await an unfavourable test case. The surplus-void exhibit demonstrates by retraction what the present one demonstrates by *partial* retention: the cluster earns a higher score than the surplus-void but lower than its surface count would have suggested. The non-flatness claim is provisional.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The exhibit is the Map's most direct application of the [[evidential-status-discipline|evidential-status discipline]] at the cluster level. The discipline's central rule — *a tenet may remove a defeater but must not upgrade the evidence level* — translates here into: *the framework may identify a cluster's significance but must not upgrade its independence score*. The cluster's significance for [[tenets|Tenet 1 (Dualism)]] and [[tenets|Tenet 3 (Bidirectional Interaction)]] is the framework's own move; the empirical anchoring is what the framework can earn. The cluster earns partial but real evidential weight, concentrated in the patient-population channel and the healthy-adult despite-commitments anchors, with cross-cultural support at illustrative strength and the cross-species channel only partially fillable in proxy form.

The signature — introspection delivers reconstruction rather than readout — is compatible with both dualism and physicalism. The Map's reading is that it *fits* the dualist picture in which a non-physical witness has bounded access to some features of its substrate but not others; the materialist reading is that the architecture is itself the explanation, with no further metaphysical inference licensed. The exhibit honours the [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution void's neutrality discipline]] and does not treat the architectural finding as positive evidence for dualism; the framework-boundary disagreement is named, not adjudicated. The cluster's role in the [[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism|convergence argument for dualism]] is compatibility plus boundary-marking, not positive support.

The weighting uses the *lower* (interpretive-significance) framework-independence score, not the higher (architectural-finding) one: the convergence argument depends on the interpretive significance the framework supplies, whose independence is lower by the article's own admission. The four-fold structure is significant for the [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|taxonomy of voids]]'s claim that consciousness-related voids exhibit *structured* clustering — a real finding the catalogue's curation criteria did not generate — but evidential weight for the tenet-package is calibrated to what the independence tests can supply.

## Honest Limitations

The exhibit is a scoring artefact, not a closure. The despite-commitments / because-prediction classification depends on judgements about what authoring frameworks predicted; finer classification would shift anchors at the margins. The patient-population channel's force depends on the dissociation being substantively cross-architectural rather than reflecting shared structure both populations possess; the defence is partial. The cross-tradition extension is calibration-grade; the [[research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16|Neoplatonist audit]] holds the channel at *low-moderate*, weighted toward Advaita (Bréhier-Armstrong-Aklan 2018) and away from early Sufism (substantial Evagrian-via-Christian-monastic discount). The cross-species channel contributes *face-asymmetrically* — strongly on source-attribution, partially on noetic-feelings, principled-blocked on confabulation and narrative — per the [[topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests|proxy-test design audit]]. The cross-architecture-via-LLMs scoring (Lindsey et al. 2025-26; Vogel's "strength but not source") remains the open programme. The most consequential residual gap: whether Evagrian *logismoi* itself reduces to Stoicism via *antirrhēsis*, in which case the Sufi-via-Christian-monastic and modern Stoic-CBT channels share a single upstream and the cross-tradition convergence collapses closer to Advaita-plus-Stoicism than to multiple independent witnesses.
