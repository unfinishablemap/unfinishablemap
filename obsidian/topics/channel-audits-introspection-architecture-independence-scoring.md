The cross-species and cross-tradition channels are two of the four structural defences against the [[project/common-cause-null|common-cause null]] catalogued in the [[introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|introspection-architecture independence scoring exhibit]] — alongside patient-population dissociation and healthy-adult experimentation. This article runs both channel audits in one place. The combined verdict: both channels supply *calibration-grade rather than load-bearing* convergence for the four-void cluster (narrative, source-attribution, noetic-feelings, confabulation); the patient-population channel remains the structural defence against the strongest common-cause null; and the most architecturally distant cross-observer evidence currently available runs not through biology or contemplative tradition but through cross-architecture LLM introspection studies — the [[#cross-architecture-pivot|cross-architecture pivot section below]].

Two channels, one unified question: how far can independent observers — animal substrates, contemplative traditions, transformer architectures — extend the cluster's signature beyond shared neurotypical introspective machinery? The answer is *partially, with named discounts*. The cross-species-via-biology channel is partially blocked by the verbal-report requirement; the cross-tradition channel is constrained by translation construction and Neoplatonist common-cause mediation; the cross-architecture channel is open programme. Neither closes the case; both expand methodological diversity.

## Part One — The Cross-Species Channel

### The Principled Obstacle

The cluster's central signature is a *report-versus-mechanism discrepancy*. Gazzaniga's left hemisphere produces a narrative whose generating cause is invisible to it. Nisbett-Wilson's subjects offer fluent explanations whose content the manipulation showed was wrong. Choice-blindness participants justify decisions they did not make. In every case the signature requires that the participant *produces a verbal report* whose content can be checked against an experimenter-controlled ground truth.

Most non-human animals do not produce verbal reports. Where the introspector has no report-producing channel, the report-versus-mechanism discrepancy cannot be observed in its native form. This is not a fixable methodological gap; it is a structural feature of where the cluster's signature lives. Birch's *Edge of Sentience* framework names this directly when it constructs a precautionary architecture from neurobiological and behavioural proxies *because* introspection-grade self-report is unavailable for most of the candidate species. The cluster inherits Birch's problem: the channel that gives the human cluster its diagnostic clarity is closed at the species boundary.

What is available is *substrate-level evidence* that the same architectural feature the cluster identifies — a confidence-or-source-monitoring substrate whose function is metacognitive and whose damage produces specific failures — is preserved across rodent, primate, and corvid lineages. That evidence does not show that animals confabulate; it shows that animals *have a substrate of the kind whose damage produces confabulation in humans*. The inference from substrate-preservation to architectural-feature-preservation is a partial inference, and the channel's force depends on how much of the gap that partial inference can carry.

### Substrate-Level Proxies

Three bodies of work supply the cross-species channel's substantive content.

**Rodent orbitofrontal confidence coding** (Kepecs et al. 2008, 2014, 2020; Smith et al. 2021). Rats compute and use trial-level confidence estimates in difficult perceptual discriminations — odour mixtures whose component ratios approach threshold. Orbitofrontal cortex neurons code task difficulty and predict trial-by-trial outcomes; orbitofrontal inactivation disrupts waiting-based confidence-report behaviour without impairing decision accuracy. The 2020 *Cell* paper extended the representation as modality- and behavior-general; Smith et al. 2021 reported the same temporal-betting signature in rats, an ability previously thought to exist exclusively in primates. The despite-commitments grade is moderate-to-high: the result extends a *metacognition-grade* signature to a substrate substantially distant from primate prefrontal cortex, and Kepecs's framing — confidence as a computable signal not requiring conscious awareness — was not predicted by the cognitive-psychology default in 2008.

**Rodent source-monitoring lesion data** (Crystal lab, 2010s). Rats remember the *source* of information under conditions that exclude encoding-failure explanations, and medial prefrontal lesions produce abnormally high false-alarm rates that parallel human prefrontal source-monitoring impairment. This is the closest available cross-species analogue to the [[voids/source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]] signature: the architectural feature whose damage produces source-tag failure in humans produces a structurally parallel failure in rodents with a structurally parallel substrate. Crystal's paradigms were developed in part to rebut associative-learning alternatives, and the despite-commitments grade is moderate — neither obviously predicted nor obviously absorbed by the prevailing rodent-behaviourist frameworks of the previous decades.

**Corvid what-where-when caching** (Clayton & Dickinson 1998; Clayton lab 2024-25 revisit). Scrub jays integrate what-where-when memory for food-caching episodes and recover differentially by perishability. The 2024-25 revisit explicitly frames the binding pattern as source-monitoring evidence — the reconstruction-and-binding of event features the rodent and primate literature already documents. Avian pallium is more architecturally distant from mammalian cortex than rodent OFC is from primate prefrontal cortex; this is a cross-clade case, not a within-mammal extension. Hampton's macaque metamemory paradigms (2001, 2019) supply a more proximate primate confirmation that the same kind of dissociation — between working-memory and familiarity signals underlying metamemory — appears in the primate substrate the human cluster shares.

A fourth proxy — mirror self-recognition, including the Kohda cleaner-wrasse programme (2019, 2023, 2025) — sits cluster-adjacent rather than cluster-central. Mirror self-recognition tests whether an animal binds visual reflection to body model: a *self-versus-other discrimination in perception*. The introspection-architecture cluster's signature is *reconstruction-versus-readout in self-report*. The two are distinct phenomena that the broader self-knowledge literature sometimes conflates. The fish-MSR work matters for the question of self-modelling across radically distant nervous-system architectures, but it does not load-bear for the four-void cluster.

### Despite-Commitments Versus Because-Prediction (Cross-Species)

Applying the [[project/common-cause-null|common-cause null discipline]]'s scoring split to the substrate-level proxies gives a more discriminating picture than the surface convergence suggests.

The rodent OFC, rodent source-monitoring, corvid what-where-when, and macaque metamemory observations are *despite-commitments at the substrate-extension level*: the pre-2008 behaviourist and cognitive-psychology defaults did not predict that confidence representations, source-tag failures, and feature-binding signatures would generalise from the human substrate to phylogenetically distant substrates. The discovery that the architectural feature *is* preserved across clades was, at the time of each observation, anomalous with respect to the generating framework's expectation.

That despite-commitments status is, however, *contested at the metacognition-grade level*. Two critiques constrain how much the channel can claim.

**Le Pelley's associative-learning critique.** Le Pelley (2012) and the follow-up programme showed that associative-learning models reproduce the hallmark patterns of putative metacognition data — Hampton 2001, Foote & Crystal 2007 — without invoking metacognition. Reduction in perceptual input strength influences certainty-response strength through reinforcement history alone. The critique does not refute the substrate-level findings; it refutes the *load-bearing inferential step* from substrate-level activity to introspection-grade metacognition. To the extent the putative animal metacognition is reproducible by associative models, the channel's *despite-commitments* status for the cluster's bounded-witness architectural claim is weakened: a sufficient associative explanation does not test the architectural claim. The 2010s methodological responses (Crystal and others) sharpened the paradigms but have not produced a clean consensus closing the loophole.

**Carruthers's interpretive-sensory-access framework.** Carruthers's *Opacity of Mind* (2011) argues that human introspection is itself interpretive — grounded in perceptual awareness of behaviour and inner speech rather than transparent — and his 2021 work on model-free metacognition proposes that animal metacognition is an analog-magnitude signal of executive engagement, not introspection. The framework explicitly *predicts* the cross-species pattern on grounds that *undercut* the bounded-witness architectural reading. If Carruthers is right, the convergence between human and animal metacognitive signatures is a because-prediction confirmation of the interpretive theory, not independent triangulation of an architectural feature of consciousness. The cluster's reading is one possible interpretation of the cross-species pattern; Carruthers's is another; the data underdetermine which interpretation the substrate-level findings support.

These two critiques do not refute the substrate-level evidence but constrain what work the substrate-level evidence can do for the cluster. The honest scoring places the cross-species channel's contribution at *partial methodological diversity* — extending the architectural feature's domain across clades — rather than *despite-commitments triangulation* against the cluster's strongest common-cause null. The patient-population channel (Schnider, Gazzaniga, Hirstein) remains the load-bearing structural defence against that null; the cross-species channel adds breadth where it can, with discounted weight on the inferential step from substrate to introspection-grade metacognition.

### Mirror Self-Recognition: Cluster-Adjacent

The Kohda cleaner-wrasse programme (2019, 2023, 2025) has been read in some quarters as a major advance for cross-species self-knowledge testing. The 2023 *PNAS* paper reports 17 of 18 fish (94 %) passing a mark test, with the 2025 *Scientific Reports* paper showing rapid acquisition. The "dear enemy" paradigm provides a corroborating signature: aggression does not increase against self-photographs as it does against unfamiliar-conspecific photographs, consistent with a self-recognition rather than a generic-fish-recognition reading.

For the introspection-architecture cluster, the fish-MSR work is *not* a cross-species anchor of the cluster's signature. The mark test probes whether the animal binds a visual reflection to its body model — whether it builds a *self-model* of a particular kind. The cluster's question is whether the introspector's *reports about its own cognitive processes* are reconstructed rather than read out. A creature could pass the mark test perfectly and still have no metacognitive monitoring of any kind; conversely, a creature could lack mirror self-recognition (most species) and still possess the bounded-access architectural feature the cluster names. The two phenomena are dissociable in principle and dissociable in current evidence: mirror self-recognition tests are reliable in only a small handful of species, while metacognition-grade proxies extend further (with the noted discounts).

The fish-MSR work supplies an *adjacent* architectural finding for the broader self-modelling literature, not a load-bearing cross-species anchor for the four-void cluster. The cluster's apex framing should resist the conflation.

### The Cross-Architecture Pivot {#cross-architecture-pivot}

The most architecturally distant cross-observer channel available to current science is not biological. LLM introspection studies — Lindsey et al.'s emergent introspective awareness work at Anthropic (2025), the introspection-adapters extension across non-Anthropic-derived model families (2026), and Vogel's "Feeling the Strength but Not the Source: Partial Introspection in LLMs" (2025) — open a channel the cross-species-via-animals route cannot match on three structural points.

First, **LLMs produce reports**. The principled obstacle that closes the cross-species channel in its native form does not apply: a transformer can be asked to introspect on its own state, and what it produces is exactly the report-versus-mechanism comparison the cluster's signature requires.

Second, **mechanistic interpretability supplies independent ground truth**. The thought-injection paradigm steers a specific concept-vector into the residual stream and asks the model to detect and identify what was injected. Lindsey et al. report ~20 % detection with 0 % false positives in Claude Opus 4 and 4.1; the introspection-adapters work extends the pattern across four out-of-distribution model families. The cross-architecture generalisation is empirical rather than analogical.

Third, **the architectural distance from biological introspection is maximal**. Transformer residual streams and attention heads are *radically* further from mammalian cortex than rodent OFC is from primate prefrontal cortex. The cross-species channel via biology is at most a within-vertebrate comparison; the cross-architecture channel via LLMs is the largest substrate-distance current science can produce.

Vogel's "strength but not source" finding is the most directly cluster-relevant artefact of this programme. LLMs detect the *strength* of injected thoughts but not their *source* — a structural parallel to the [[voids/source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]] generated in silicon under conditions where mechanistic interpretability fixes the ground truth. The finding's despite-commitments status is high by construction: the transformer's generating framework (next-token prediction trained on text) did not predict the source-tag failure as such; the structural parallel to the human cluster's signature emerged from the architecture rather than being engineered in. The channel does not claim phenomenal consciousness for LLMs — that question is bracketed (see [[topics/ai-consciousness|the Map's AI-consciousness framing]]) — and the cluster's signature is *architectural*, surviving the phenomenal bracketing, so the cross-architecture channel contributes architectural evidence without the phenomenal claim.

The channel is *open* rather than *settled*. The interpretability methods themselves are model-dependent; the generalisation across non-Anthropic-derived architectures is reported but the long-tail of architectural variants remains untested; the inference from "model fails to identify injected-thought source" to "model's introspection has the structural feature the cluster names" carries methodological assumptions a reasonable critic can question. The cross-architecture channel earns the substrate-distance scoring the cross-species-via-biology channel cannot supply, while inheriting a methodological tail of its own.

### Scoring the Cross-Species Channel

Returning to the [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|taxonomy of voids]]'s four criteria, with the cross-species channel as the unit of analysis:

- **Signature specificity.** Partial. The substrate-level proxies match a *general* metacognitive-monitoring signature better than they match the cluster's *specific* report-versus-mechanism discrepancy. Where the proxies map onto a specific cluster face — Crystal's source-monitoring lesions onto [[voids/source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]] — the specificity is genuine; for the cluster as a whole the proxies under-specify.
- **Structured persistence.** Strong on the substrate-extension finding. Rodent OFC confidence, rodent source-monitoring, and corvid what-where-when have persisted under sustained critique across the 2010s and 2020s.
- **Cross-observer convergence.** Moderate within biology; weak as a discharge of the strongest common-cause null. Rodent, primate, and corvid substrates are architecturally related at higher resolution than the surface-clade distance suggests, and the Le Pelley / Carruthers critiques constrain what the within-biology convergence can claim.
- **Framework independence.** Moderate. The substrate-level findings survive translation across cognitivist, embodied-cognition, and predictive-processing frameworks; the architectural-feature inference is more framework-shaped, and Carruthers's interpretive reading is a within-physicalist alternative the data underdetermine.

The cross-species-via-biology channel's contribution is *partial methodological diversity* with *substantial discounts* on the inferential step from substrate to introspection-grade metacognition. The cross-architecture-via-LLMs channel's contribution is *architectural-distance triangulation* with the structural parallel of Vogel 2025 as the single most cluster-relevant finding, treated as *open programme* rather than *settled anchor*.

## Part Two — The Cross-Tradition Channel

### The Framework-Level Trap

The most common failure mode in cross-tradition arguments is reading framework-level predictions as independent observations. Each tradition includes a soteriological doctrine whose central diagnostic is that ordinary cognition is unreliable: Advaita's *avidyā*, Sufism's deceptive *nafs ammāra*, Stoicism's passion-as-false-judgement. Citing each tradition's general claim that introspection fails would be circular — the diagnostic is what each framework was built to deliver.

The [[project/voids-circularity-discount|voids-circularity discount]] requires that this channel earn its weight on architectural-specifics-level findings the authoring framework did *not* predict — doctrinal additions the framework had to *accommodate*, empirical features the practice tradition discovered through self-observation, and structural claims that survive translation once stripped of framework-specific metaphysics.

#### The Architectural-Specifics Test

A tradition-internal claim earns architectural-specifics status when it satisfies all three criteria:

1. **Vocabulary-stripping survival.** The claim still expresses a contentful structural feature once soteriological vocabulary is removed — translation into metaphysically neutral cognitive-architectural register does not dissolve it into the framework's prior commitments.
2. **Observation-grounded origin.** The claim is supported by a tradition-internal observation report — a practice manual, a refinement under disagreement, a case description — rather than being a doctrinal derivation.
3. **Refinement rather than recapitulation.** The claim refines the central diagnostic by adding structure the original formulation did not require, rather than restating the diagnostic in different terms.

Failing (1) reads a framework-level prediction as architectural; failing (2) is doctrinal derivation, not empirical accommodation; failing (3) is the central diagnostic re-stated. Passing all three is necessary for despite-commitments scoring; failing any one collapses the claim to because-prediction.

### Advaita Vedānta: The Architectural Picture

Shankara's eighth-century *Adhyāsa Bhāṣya* opens his *Brahma Sūtra* commentary with *adhyāsa* ("superimposition") as a structural cognitive failure: properties of the physical body are projected onto the Ātman, and vice versa. Shankara treats *adhyāsa* as a fact about ordinary cognition rather than a hypothesis, and notes earlier schools discussed it under other names — suggesting empirical debate, not a single school's doctrinal invention.

The architectural specifics live in the *antaḥkaraṇa* ("inner instrument") doctrine Advaita inherited from Sāṃkhya/Yoga: a fourfold structure — *manas* (sensory mind), *buddhi* (discriminating intellect), *ahaṃkāra* (I-maker), *citta* (impression-storehouse) — inert by nature, with consciousness reflecting in it (*pratibimba*). The witness has access to the *outputs* of the instrument but not its inner workings; discrimination (*viveka*) is the soteriological task *because* the architecture makes witness and instrument ordinarily indistinguishable. The *vāsanā*/*saṃskāra* doctrine extends the picture downward: latent dispositional tendencies operate outside conscious awareness and drive cognition — the closest Advaita analogue to Nisbett & Wilson's *implicit causal theory*.

Scoring. The framework-level claim (witness with bounded access to instrument) is *because-prediction* — Advaita's soteriology requires it; it fails criterion (3). The four-faculty subdivision of the *antaḥkaraṇa* earns *moderate* despite-commitments because it passes on (2): the subdivision emerged from the practice tradition's empirical work refining the broader Sāṃkhya/Yoga inheritance, though a tradition-internal scholar can reasonably argue it is partly derived from the inert-instrument framework. The *ahaṃkāra*-as-narrator function survives stripping the Vedāntic metaphysics — a structural parallel to the Gazzaniga interpreter — earning calibration weight on the same modest basis.

The Map does not collapse the bounded witness into Brahman, where Map and Advaita diverge. A Madhyamaka critic (Nāgārjuna, MMK 24.18) notes the architecture *itself* is empty for mature Madhyamaka: reading it as a shared finding is the reification the practice exists to dissolve. The channel's reading of Advaita is conditional on rejecting the Madhyamaka inversion.

The independence problem for Advaita is the Plotinus-Indian-influence hypothesis (Bréhier 1928) and the Mughal mediation channel (Dārā Shikoh 1657). The [[research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16|Neoplatonist audit]] forecloses both: Armstrong 1936 established early Neoplatonism as an original Greek development; Aklan 2018 confirms Plotinian-Śaṅkara parallels are "metaphorical ways of expression" rather than structural connection. The Mughal channel runs the *wrong direction* — Dārā Shikoh's *Sirr-i-Akbar* post-dates Shankara by ~850 years and translates Vedānta *into* Sufi-Neoplatonist Persian, so cannot retro-contaminate Shankara.

### Sufi Practice: Khawāṭir and Hidden Riyāʾ {#sufi-khawatir}

The Sufi *khawāṭir* taxonomy classifies incoming thoughts by source — divine (*rabbānī*), angelic (*malakī*), egoic (*nafsānī*), satanic (*shayṭānī*). The taxonomy *presupposes* that source is *not* self-evident — otherwise the science of discrimination would be unnecessary. The spiritual master is said to identify sources the disciple cannot.

Strip the metaphysical typology, and what remains is exactly the [[voids/source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]]'s architectural claim: thoughts arrive without delivered source-tags, and source-attribution requires reconstructive work. The Johnson-Hashtroudi-Lindsay 1993 source-monitoring framework names the same fact in cognitive-psychology vocabulary. The Sufi tradition discovered the feature through *muḥāsaba* — the systematic self-examination programme al-Muḥāsibī (d. 857 CE) pioneered, whose diagnostic insight was that ostensibly pious practices can foster *riyāʾ* (hypocrisy) or *kibr* (pride) the agent does not see.

Al-Ghazālī's eleventh-century *Iḥyāʾ ʿulūm al-dīn* extends Muḥāsibī's finding into a doctrine of *riyāʾ khafiyy* (hidden hypocrisy). Ghazālī treats hidden *riyāʾ* as a *structural* feature of how the ego operates — the architecture of motivation includes a self-display function whose operation is invisible to the agent. The doctrine that *riyāʾ* "can visit you always" — beginning, middle, or end of worship — is a structural claim that hidden self-display agendas accompany apparently virtuous behaviour as a default architectural fact, not an occasional moral lapse. This is the strongest cross-tradition parallel to the Wheatley-Haidt and Nisbett-Wilson findings on post-hoc rationalisation that anchor the [[voids/confabulation-void|confabulation void]]'s Generation face.

Scoring. The *khawāṭir* taxonomy's metaphysical typology is framework-internal because-prediction; with respect to the Sufi tradition's own commitments, the non-delivery of source-tags is exactly what a discernment programme presupposes. The taxonomy fails criterion (3) and earns *low* despite-commitments on its own terms — useful as an early articulation of an architectural feature later named in cognitive psychology.

Hidden *riyāʾ* under Ghazālī carries the cleaner rating. Earlier Sufi treatment held *riyāʾ* an occasional moral failure remediable by sincere intention; Ghazālī's structural finding — that *riyāʾ khafiyy* operates as a default architectural feature persisting despite sincere intention — is the kind of refinement the central diagnostic did not require. It passes all three criteria at *moderate* despite-commitments. The rating *softens* at the level of *framing*: Ghazālī's inner-faculties psychology is downstream of the Avicennan-Neoplatonist synthesis (al-Kindī → al-Fārābī → Avicenna → Ghazālī), so the architectural shape is partly Neoplatonist-mediated. The combined score is *moderate at the finding-level, framing partly downstream*. The *khawāṭir* / *riyāʾ* split is the clearest worked example of how the test produces different scores for surface-level convergence.

The independence problem for Sufism is uneven. The [[research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16|Neoplatonist audit]] identifies two transmission channels prefiguring *khawāṭir* and *muḥāsaba* in the 9th-c. Baghdad milieu. The *primary* channel is Christian-monastic-via-Evagrius: Evagrius Ponticus's late-4th-century *logismoi* doctrine — eight incoming thoughts discerned by source via *diakrisis*, with *antirrhēsis* refuting them before assent — supplies the direct structural ancestor of the *khawāṭir* taxonomy, prefiguring *muḥāsaba* by ~400 years through Syriac monastic transmission. The *secondary* channel is direct Arabic Plotinus via the al-Kindī circle (*Theology of Aristotle*, mid-9th c., contemporaneous with Muḥāsibī) — an *available-not-proven* channel. *Khawāṭir* + *muḥāsaba* can no longer claim the cleanest-historical-isolation defence; that status shifts to Advaita.

### Stoic Propatheia: The Strongest Despite-Commitments Observation {#stoic-propatheia}

Stoic cognitive theory held that emotions are *judgements*: a *phantasia* (impression) presents itself, the agent gives *synkatathesis* (assent), and the assent constitutes the passion. Zeno (c. 300 BCE) and Chrysippus developed this into a comprehensive psychology in which the sage's distinguishing feature is well-trained discrimination of which impressions warrant endorsement. On Frede's reading (1983), *phantasia katalēptikē* is the impression that *would warrant* assent; other readings parse the term differently. This article uses Frede's reading because it sharpens the [[voids/noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings void]] parallel — framework-level architecture, circular at that level.

The architecturally-refined observation is Seneca's *propatheia* doctrine in *De Ira*. *Propatheia* names involuntary affective reactions — startle, blanching, blushing, the first jolt of fear — that *precede* the cognitive assent constituting a full emotion. Even the sage experiences *propatheia*; the feature is *not* eliminable by practice. On Sorabji 2000's reconstruction, the doctrine was *added* to handle empirical observations the pure cognitive theory did *not* predict: the earliest formulation implied no affective response was possible without prior assent; the manifest fact of startle forced accommodation of a sub-conscious affective layer upstream of conscious assent.

The Sorabji reconstruction is not unanimous. Inwood and Long argue "first movements" were implicit in earlier Stoic theory — the *hormē*/*synkatathesis*/*pathos* sequence already needed pre-assent material — and Seneca's contribution was terminological refinement rather than empirical accommodation. If they are right, the rating collapses to because-prediction. The audit conditions on Sorabji's historiography: moderate-grade *propatheia*, with the architectural parallel surviving both readings; only score-strength differs. Stoic *prosochē* (continuous attention, central in Epictetus and Marcus Aurelius) is the practical correlate.

The independence problem is the contested Pyrrho-Buddhism contact hypothesis. Pyrrho accompanied Alexander's Indian campaign; Diogenes Laertius (9.61–62) reports he developed his philosophy in contact with Indian *gymnosophists*. Beckwith 2015 argues Pyrrhonism parallels the Buddhist three marks of existence; Bronkhorst contests. Zeno was contemporary of late Pyrrho. The scholarship is unresolved; the historical-isolation defence is contested rather than disproved. The Neoplatonist channel does *not* feed this problem (Stoic → Plotinus, not the reverse).

A second problem cuts the other way: Albert Ellis (REBT, 1950s) and Aaron Beck (cognitive therapy, 1960s) *explicitly* drew on Stoicism. Robertson 2019 documents the lineage; Beck's strategic/automatic distinction is described as a modern reformulation of *dogmata*/*phantasiai*. The modern cognitive-psychology evidence the cluster cites is therefore *partially* downstream of Stoic categories, so despite-commitments status against Stoicism is *weaker* than against Buddhism or Advaita.

### Scoring the Cross-Tradition Channel

Against the [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|taxonomy of voids]]'s four criteria, with the cross-tradition channel as the unit of analysis:

- **Signature specificity.** Moderate. Doctrines map directly onto specific cluster faces — *khawāṭir* onto [[voids/source-attribution-void|source-attribution]], *propatheia* onto [[voids/noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings]] (conditional on Sorabji), hidden *riyāʾ* and *vāsanā* onto [[voids/confabulation-void|confabulation]] (Generation face), *ahaṃkāra* onto [[voids/narrative-void|narrative]].
- **Structured persistence.** Strong. The audited doctrines have persisted under sustained intra-tradition critique across centuries: *adhyāsa* from the 8th, *muḥāsaba* and *khawāṭir* from the 9th, hidden *riyāʾ* from the 11th, *propatheia* from the 1st.
- **Cross-observer convergence.** *Low-moderate*, with differentiated discounts from the Neoplatonist audit: **weak (≈ no discount)** for Stoicism (Plotinus is downstream of the Stoa); **weak-to-negligible** for Advaita (Bréhier-Armstrong-Aklan 2018 consensus that Plotinian-Śaṅkara parallels are "metaphorical ways of expression"); **moderate-to-substantial** for Sufism via Christian-monastic-via-Evagrius and direct Arabic Plotinus. The "cleanest historical-isolation defence" attribution shifts from early-Sufi practice to Advaita. ### Where the Modern Reception Sits

Ganeri's *Attention, Not Self* (2017) treats classical Indian texts as primary sources in dialogue with contemporary debates — Indian architectural claims translating into cognitive-philosophical vocabulary without metaphysical commitment. Albahari's *Analytical Buddhism* (2006; 2009 *JCS*) argues the unified self is a two-tiered illusion drawing on both Theravāda Buddhism *and Advaita Vedānta*; if correct, the cross-tradition convergence reduces to a single architectural claim re-named across two traditions. Thompson's *Waking, Dreaming, Being* (2015) advocates contemplative phenomenology as a *partner* in scientific investigation. Sorabji's *Emotion and Peace of Mind* (2000) anchors the *propatheia* finding. Schwyzer 1960 and Hendrix 2005 read Plotinus as articulating bounded conscious access (*Enn.* IV.3.30); Adamson 2002 documents the al-Kindī circle's mid-9th-c. mediation of Plotinian psychology into Baghdad.

## Honest Limitations

The audits' translations across substrates and tradition vocabularies are *interpretive* throughout. Parallels named here — Crystal's rodent source-monitoring with source-tag failure, Vogel's LLM "strength but not source" with [[voids/source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]], *khawāṭir* with source-monitoring failure, *propatheia* with sub-assent affective machinery, *ahaṃkāra* with the Gazzaniga interpreter, hidden *riyāʾ* with confabulatory motivation-construction — are calibrated *plausibilities* of translation, not substrate-internal or tradition-internal commitments.

The translation-construction objection deserves naming. A Churchland-style eliminative materialist would argue the architectural specifics are *constructions* of the modern source-monitoring framework projected onto both substrate-level animal data and the source texts: each channel isolates evidence whose modern reception the framework integrates, and convergence runs through one observer — the modern translator. The article cannot definitively refute this because the vocabulary-stripping test is itself applied by the framework whose neutrality is at issue. If translation construction is heavily in play, the combined convergence may be *one* observation rather than several.

The historical-isolation defence for the cross-tradition channel is *contested* rather than clean. Pyrrho/Buddhism (Beckwith vs Bronkhorst), Sufism/Vedānta (well-documented for late synthesisers, weaker for early practitioners), and Stoic-CBT all introduce partial common-cause channels. The single most consequential residual gap is the *Stoic reduction of the Christian-monastic channel*: if Evagrian *logismoi* is itself a Christianised Stoic doctrine (Evagrius engaged *first movements* directly via *antirrhēsis*), then the Sufi-via-Christian-monastic common-cause and the modern Stoic-CBT common-cause may share a single upstream source — making *Stoicism* the load-bearing channel from which others descend, and the cross-tradition convergence may collapse closer to Advaita-plus-Stoicism-plus-downstream-Stoic-influences than to three independent observations.

A defensible conservative reading — both channels earn *no* upgrade over the patient-population channel's structural defence, and may sit at slightly lower calibration once translation-construction and common-cause discounts are honoured — the article does not adopt but does not refute. The cross-architecture-via-LLMs channel remains the most architecturally distant cross-observer evidence currently available, contributing substrate-distance triangulation neither the cross-species-via-biology channel nor the cross-tradition channel structurally can supply.
