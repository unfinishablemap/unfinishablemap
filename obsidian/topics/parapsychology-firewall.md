A naive reading of dualism predicts that mind should be able to push matter around: if consciousness is non-physical and causally efficacious, then strong telepathy, reliable psychokinesis, and decisive precognition look like exactly the evidence a dualist should want. The Map holds the opposite. **Spectacular, reliable, ensemble-detectable psi would disconfirm the Map's framework rather than confirm it.** The interface the Map's tenets actually require is *self-concealing by construction*—Born-statistics-preserving, no-signalling-respecting, and per-trial indistinguishable from chance. A large, lawlike psi effect would violate precisely the constraints (explained below under [The Three Firewall Constraints](#the-three-firewall-constraints)) that make the Map's interface compatible with physics. This article names that structural barrier the **parapsychology firewall** and explains why the Map sits on the *unfavourable* side of any sensational parapsychological result.

The firewall is not a hedge bolted on to protect the framework from awkward data. It falls out of [[tenets#^minimal-quantum-interaction|Minimal Quantum Interaction]] and the no-signalling structure of quantum probability the way the corridor reading does in [[selection-only-mind-influence]]. The Map's relationship to parapsychology is therefore the reverse of what folk dualism expects: the Map needs psi to be *small or absent*, not large.

## What the Firewall Is

The parapsychology firewall is the claim that the Map's tenets entail an upper bound on detectable mind-matter influence so low that any effect crossing it would falsify the framework. Three distinct kinds of "spectacular psi" sit on the wrong side of that bound:

- **Macroscopic psychokinesis** — minds reliably biasing classical-scale physical systems (bending objects, deflecting dice, moving needles) by an amount large enough to survive replication.
- **High-bandwidth telepathy or precognition** — information transfer between minds, or from future to present, at rates that carry meaningful content reliably.
- **Lawlike ensemble effects** — any psi signature that persists in aggregate statistics under accumulating, well-controlled measurement, rather than regressing toward the chance baseline.

The Map predicts that none of these exists at detectable strength. Not because they are metaphysically impossible—the Map's whole point is that mind is causally efficacious—but because the *specific kind* of efficacy the tenets license is structurally incapable of producing them. The firewall is the boundary between the interface the Map endorses and the parapsychological phenomena it must predict against.

## The Three Firewall Constraints

Three commitments, each load-bearing elsewhere in the corpus, jointly erect the firewall.

### Constraint 1: Born-Statistics Preservation

The Map's default mechanism is selection within a corridor that leaves aggregate Born statistics exactly intact (position P-Q2 in [[positions/quantum-interface]]). Conscious selection biases *which* outcome becomes actual on a given trial, but the long-run frequencies still converge to |⟨φ|ψ⟩|². As [[selection-only-mind-influence]] derives, this drives the *signed* per-trial information rate toward zero across many trials. A reliable macroscopic PK effect is, by definition, a *persistent* departure from the unbiased distribution—a systematic deviation that does not wash out. That is the one thing the corridor forbids. So large, lawlike PK is not weak evidence against the Map; it directly contradicts the corridor reading the Map endorses most strongly.

### Constraint 2: No-Signalling

The Born rule can be derived from relativistic-causality / no-signalling considerations, and—as the [[causal-consistency-constraint]] develops—non-affine deviations from the Born map enable superluminal signalling. Any *systematic* per-trial deviation from Born probabilities is therefore directly coupled to a no-signalling violation (position P-Q7). High-bandwidth telepathy or decisive precognition would be a channel carrying reliable information; a channel carrying reliable information that outruns light-cone structure breaks no-signalling. The Map buys compatibility with relativistic causality at the price of accepting that *no large effect can ever be visible at the ensemble level*. Spectacular telepathy would cash a cheque the Map has explicitly refused to write.

### Constraint 3: Content-Confinement

Under the strict selection-only reading, mind can only choose among candidates the brain has already generated; it cannot register, choose, or report content no candidate encodes. This is the content-confinement limit of [[selection-only-mind-influence]]. Veridical precognition of arbitrary external facts—or telepathic reception of content with no local neural pre-image—would require mind to acquire information the brain never generated as a candidate. That is exactly what the read-and-select interface cannot do. The interface is read-and-select, not read-and-create, and a fortiori not receive-from-elsewhere.

Together these three constraints fix the firewall's height: any psi effect that is *large*, *lawlike*, or *content-importing* lands on the falsifying side.

## Why the Folk Intuition Runs Backwards

The intuition that psi would vindicate dualism rests on a picture the Map rejects: mind as a *force*, an extra causal push injected into the physical world alongside the four fundamental interactions. On that picture, more mind means more push, and a strong enough push shows up as bent spoons. But the Map's [[tenets#^minimal-quantum-interaction|Minimal Quantum Interaction]] tenet expressly rules out "uncontrolled psychokinesis, energy injection by mind, and any interaction that would be empirically detectable under current experimental precision." Mind does not inject energy or force. It *selects* among outcomes physics already leaves open, within a corridor that preserves the statistics. An interface of that kind is invisible to exactly the experiments that would detect a force.

The folk inference also commits a version of [[possibility-probability-slippage]] run in reverse. The slippage warned against elsewhere is treating *the tenets allow X* as *the evidence supports X*. The folk-dualist version treats *dualism is true* as *psi should be strong*—as though the metaphysical thesis carried an empirical prediction of large effects. It carries the opposite prediction. The Map's dualism is compatible with, and indeed predicts, a world in which the most rigorous parapsychology finds vanishing or null effects.

## The Demarcation Already in the Tenets

The Map has always drawn a sharp line between its constrained interface hypothesis and "quantum woo." Tenet 2 names the boundary explicitly: the tenet is "not to be confused with" quantum mysticism, parapsychology, or magical thinking, and it "rules out" detectable energy injection by mind. The parapsychology firewall is the systematic statement of that demarcation. It explains *why* the line is where it is: not as a reputational defence ("we are not those people") but as a structural consequence ("the interface we posit cannot produce those effects").

This matters for how the Map reads parapsychological data. The decline effect, the inverse relation between sample size and effect size in RNG-PK meta-analyses, and the strong Bayesian evidence for the null in large pre-registered micro-PK studies are all consistent with—indeed predicted by—a Born-preserving, self-concealing interface, as [[selection-only-mind-influence]] sets out. But the Map does **not** read this corridor as evidence *for* the interface. The pattern is equally consistent with there being no effect at all. Reading "the data look the way a self-limiting channel would" as support for the channel would be textbook possibility/probability slippage. The firewall is a constraint the interface must live within if it exists, not a footprint proving that it does.

## What Would Actually Bear on the Map

If spectacular psi disconfirms the Map and a null result does not confirm it, what evidence *would* bear? The firewall sharpens the answer by ruling out the headline-grabbing channel and pointing at the quiet one. The discriminating observable is not effect *size* but the **content-confinement signature**: whether the dimensionality of reportable conscious content is bounded above by the dimensionality of the brain-generated candidate space (the Holevo-style ceiling of [[selection-only-mind-influence]]). That is a test the Map can lose—it predicts a specific ceiling—without being a test that any amount of dramatic psi could win. The framework's genuine empirical exposure lives at the mechanism level (coherence-time results, microtubule-signature results, the falsified parameter-free Diósi-Penrose model) and along the [[falsification-roadmap-for-the-interface-model|falsification roadmap]], not in the parapsychology laboratory.

There is also a converse worth stating plainly. A demonstration that consciousness can influence physical outcomes *only* in ways that leave no aggregate trace at all—and that no candidate content-confinement signature exists either—would push the Map toward [[ensemble-level-epiphenomenalism]]: the worry that a perfectly Born-preserving interface does no causal work the physical statistics need. The firewall protects the Map from the *force* picture's failure modes but exposes it to the *idleness* picture's. The Map owns both edges.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The parapsychology firewall is the Map's [[tenets#^minimal-quantum-interaction|Minimal Quantum Interaction]] tenet read as a prediction about parapsychology rather than as a mechanism for neural causation. The Map interprets the absence of replicable spectacular psi as consonant with its framework, while refusing to count that consonance as positive support—the [[possibility-probability-slippage|slippage discipline]] forbids the upgrade in both directions.

The firewall aligns with [[tenets#^bidirectional-interaction|Bidirectional Interaction]] by locating mental efficacy in selection rather than force: consciousness makes the smallest possible difference, which is precisely why it makes no *detectable bulk* difference. It aligns with [[tenets#^dualism|Dualism]] by keeping the non-physical selector real while denying it the gross causal powers folk dualism imagines. And it inherits the framework's honest cost: the same self-concealment that immunises the Map against falsification-by-spectacular-psi is what generates the [[ensemble-level-epiphenomenalism|ensemble-level epiphenomenalism]] challenge logged as the strongest live open question in [[positions/quantum-interface|the quantum-interface register]] (P-Q3).

The Map's position, stated as a wager: if a future result establishes reliable, lawlike, content-importing psi, the Map should treat that as disconfirmation and revise—because such an effect would mean mind influences matter in a way that injects signal, breaks Born statistics, or imports unmediated content, none of which the Map's interface permits. The Map is on record that it would rather be falsified than redraw the firewall to absorb the result.
