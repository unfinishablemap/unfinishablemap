- **Release phenomena objection** (Eliminative Materialist): Charles Bonnet hallucinations have a neural mechanism (disinhibition). Addressed by distinguishing mechanism from phenomenal character—the neural story explains *why* neurons fire but not *why it feels like seeing a face*.
- **Smooth emergence objection** (Hard-Nosed Physicalist): Some emergent properties grade smoothly without being fundamental. Addressed by distinguishing generic gradation from the specific pattern consciousness exhibits (combined with compensation, expansion, and dissociation).
- **NDE evidence contestability** (Empiricist): van Lommel (2001) is methodologically contested. The article already hedges with "suggestive evidence"—no change needed.
