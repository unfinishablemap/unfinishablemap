## Pessimistic Analysis Summary

### Critical Issues Found

Fresh-create defect tail confirmed: a same-day-created article that "validated clean" at
creation carried two quotation-accuracy defects, both invisible to intra-corpus review and
caught only by publisher-of-record web-verify.

- **Misquotation of Mathews ("transcends causal interactions")**: The article put the string
  "transcends causal interactions" in quotation marks and attributed it to Mathews's ontopoetics.
  The source (*Living Cosmos Panpsychism*, freyamathews.net PDF) says no such thing; it says the
  poetic order "will exceed the causal order but in no way contradict it" and defines ontopoetics
  as "poetic order, and the practice of participating in it." **Resolution**: replaced with the
  verbatim source phrasing — ontopoetics as a "poetic order" of meaning that "will exceed the
  causal order but in no way contradict it."

- **Unverifiable verbatim quote attributed to Rose ("shot through with life-giving relations")**:
  Presented in quotation marks as Rose's ethnography. Not found in Rose 2013 (*Val Plumwood's
  Philosophical Animism*, verified full text) nor surfaced across three independent searches. The
  underlying claim (Country as sentient, responsive, life-giving) is faithful to Rose's documented
  view, so the content was preserved but the false verbatim framing removed. **Resolution**:
  re-anchored to a verified Rose 2013 verbatim phrase — a world "buzzing with multitudes of
  sentient beings" — with "life-giving" retained as paraphrase, not quotation.
