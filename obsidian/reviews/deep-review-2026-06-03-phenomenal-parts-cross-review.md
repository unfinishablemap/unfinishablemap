## The Conceptual Core

ChatGPT 5.5 Pro flagged a genuine cross-article tension (review §Claim 2): the
composition cluster leans on phenomenal NON-compositionality ("experience cannot
be composed"), while [[unity-of-consciousness]] treats experiences as PARTS of
larger experiences (subsumptive unity). The reviewer's worry: without an explicit
distinction the site risks saying both "experience has parts" and "experience
cannot be composed."

The reconciliation: distinguish (A) phenomenal part-whole STRUCTURE — an experience
having internal articulation, or being subsumed under a larger experience — from
(B) composition out of independently existing physical or experiential SUBJECTS
(subject-summing). Non-compositionality denies (B), not (A). Both site claims are
true once that distinction is made explicit.
