2. **Overstated empirical claim from the same source (FIXED).** The Empirical Compatibility section read: *"The COGITATE experiment (Cogitate Consortium, 2023) identified the posterior cortical hot zone as a primary locus of conscious content."* This over-claims a clean positive result. The verified finding: the study tested IIT vs GNWT and **fully confirmed neither**; conscious content was decodable from posterior cortex, but IIT's key prediction of sustained posterior synchronization was NOT supported. Calling it a clean identification of "the posterior cortical hot zone as a primary locus" misrepresents the headline result and used the wrong year. **Resolution**: rewrote to "found that conscious content was decodable from posterior cortex while fully confirming neither of the two leading physicalist-adjacent theories — a result interactionism reads as locating candidate interface regions without vindicating any materialist account," preserving the interpretive point while matching the literature.
