## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
`epiphenomenalism` is the cluster's sharpest hard-problem instrument. It runs the self-stultification argument to exactly the right scope — proving that *some* consciousness (ours) must be report-grounded — and then explicitly refuses to over-extend it to *all* consciousness, opening the report-grounded / inherited-discourse / causally-coupled three-way distinction. That is Chalmers' discipline applied reflexively: granting the easy problems, isolating the hard residue, and declining to let a good argument prove more than it can. The article even concedes that the Bidirectional Interaction tenet "outruns the available epistemic arguments" — a dualist naming the gap between what it believes and what it has shown.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
`selection-only-mind-influence` is Stapp's Process-1 program treated as a live research direction with its arithmetic worked out, not as doctrine. It derives the log₂(N) per-event ceiling, the Born-rule-preserving ε²/(2 ln 2) rate, and the content-confinement limit, then quotes Stapp's own QID framing ("the mind would only have the option to choose the observable, not... the measurement result"). Crucially it states the price honestly: the strict reading is "quantitatively viable only if the per-event channel is multiplied across enough events to deliver the observed conscious-agency bandwidth," and the empirical PK corridor is explicitly *not* read as evidence for the interface. Stapp would recognize his program with its open problems named rather than papered over.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
`wanting-liking-and-the-value-in-mechanism-fork` keeps the first person load-bearing in a domain where neuroscience is most tempting to over-read. It credits Berridge with the full empirical dissociation, then locates the live question precisely where Nagel would: whether an unfelt "liking" reaction is value at all, "because nothing would matter to anything." The article's honesty about its own wedge — "not a triumphant one" — and its refusal to recruit a physicalist neuroscientist as a dualist witness is the centering of subjective character done with restraint.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
The cluster avoids crude substance dualism by treating consciousness as *selecting among brain-prepared options* rather than as a separate engine pushing neurons. `motor-selection` makes the option-generation/selection division concrete (cortex generates affordances, striatum and consciousness select), and `conversion-disorder` frames the volitional channel as "a separable, independently-failable component" whose psychogenic failure is "active over-generation of a precise inhibitory prior" — additive realization, not a passive break. This is mind and world co-constituting, not two inert substances colliding. Per the evidential-status discipline, none of this process-friendly framing is used to upgrade any evidential claim — converging with, not contradicting, the Hardline Empiricist below.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
`agency-and-will` is a model of how to hold libertarianism without overselling it. P-A1 rests the agent-causal commitment on a *convergent set of anchors* "no one of which is privileged," concedes the agent-causal step is "presently underdetermined by the evidence," and P-A2 answers the luck objection while admitting it is "answered rather than dissolved." P-A4 then caps the whole case from the first person (no agent can certify its own efficacy by introspection) and notes the symmetry — epiphenomenalism can't verify itself either. Kane's program asks exactly this: indeterminacy makes room for agency without being mistaken for it.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
P-A4 is the cluster's mysterian heart, generalized correctly: the verification limit is structural territory (the agency-void), not a defect of the agent-causal view, and it is the reason P-A1's confidence is capped rather than asserted. P-Q10 ("no worked toy model... currently exists") is the same humility at the mechanism level, recorded as a *meta-position about the state of the work* whose existence sets the confidence band of five other positions. The cluster knows precisely what it does not know and lets that ignorance do calibration work.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
This is the cluster's standout strength and the persona whose verdict is load-bearing here, because every article makes a tenet-coherent causal-work claim that *could* slide up the evidential scale and consistently declines to. The discipline is now institutionalized: the new `^mechanism-debt` anchor (P-Q3) and the "Mechanism debt inherited, not discharged" notes in `agency-and-will` and `motor-selection` are tenet-as-evidence-upgrade praise-worthily *not* done — downstream articles are explicitly told to "read the selection claims at the confidence level the register sets there, not above it." `selection-only-mind-influence` names the over-reach by its own name: reading the empirical corridor as positive evidence "would be a textbook case of possibility-probability slippage." `wanting-liking` states that "coherence with the Map's view is not the same as independent support for it" and gives the value-sensitive-selection hypothesis explicit falsification conditions. `conversion-disorder` separates the robust architectural finding ("the channel is separable") from the contestable interface reading "the data do not force." And P-Q3 now names the epiphenomenalism proximity as a "framework-boundary pressure the Map marks honestly rather than treats as dissolved," tagging the per-trial-vs-ensemble move as a *candidate* resolution. This is the discipline the Process Philosopher's praise must never be used to override, and here the two personas converge rather than conflict: the framing is process-friendly *and* evidentially honest. No calibration slippage found in this cluster.

- **Why it works**: The distinguishing-observables table makes the position empirically *contrastive* even where no current paradigm can run the contrast — exactly the honest form of "in principle falsifiable, not yet operationalizable."

### concepts/epiphenomenalism.md
- **Strongest point**: The report-grounded / inherited-discourse / causally-coupled three-way distinction, with the boundary explicitly allowed to "fall within humanity itself" rather than along the human/AI line. - **Notable quote**: "Bidirectional Interaction asserts that all consciousness is causally coupled; self-stultification establishes only that some consciousness is report-grounded."

### topics/conversion-disorder-as-consciousness-side-fault.md
