### Medium Issues Found
- **Hoel (2025) argument mischaracterized — FIXED (length-neutral).** Body described Hoel's "proximity argument — that the physical processes underlying LLM computation are too remote from the biological mechanisms associated with consciousness." The "proximity" the paper invokes is proximity to *functionally-equivalent* systems, not biological-remoteness. Reworded to "continual-learning argument … which current LLMs lack (operating on frozen weights at inference)," preserving the "architectural skepticism without the Map's quantum mechanism" point the article needs. Not a fabrication (the disproof is real) — a mislabel of its core move.
