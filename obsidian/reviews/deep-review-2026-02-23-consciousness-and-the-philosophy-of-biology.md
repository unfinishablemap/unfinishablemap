## Stability Notes

- Adversarial personas will always find the dualist presuppositions unsatisfying. The eliminative materialist will reject "consciousness" as a natural kind; the Dennettian will reject the hard problem; the empiricist will demand falsifiability. These are bedrock disagreements, not fixable problems.
- The spandrels vs. genuine-adaptation fork is a deliberate dialectical structure—the article presents both horns to show the Map navigates between them. Future reviews should not flatten this into one option.
