## Pessimistic Analysis Summary

### Medium Issues Found

4. **Block's role functionalism lacked citation**: Attribution without specific work. **Resolution**: Added Block (1995) "On a Confusion About a Function of Consciousness" citation.

5. **Decoherence-free subspaces mentioned in opening but undeveloped**: The opening paragraph listed "decoherence-free subspaces" as a constraint but the body never discussed them. **Resolution**: Replaced with the broader "decoherence problem" in the opening, which accurately reflects the article's scope.

6. **Fullwood-Parzygnat interpretive leap unmarked**: The article's inference about consciousness presented as if deriving from the paper. **Resolution**: Added explicit note that "the authors draw no conclusions about consciousness" and marked the application as "the Map takes this as suggestive."

7. **No Many Worlds tenet conflated with consciousness-mediated selection**: The Relation to Site Perspective section presented NMW as independently supporting consciousness as selector. **Resolution**: Rewrote to explicitly note that rejecting MWI is compatible with objective collapse theories (GRW), and that the NMW tenet establishes outcome uniqueness while the Minimal Quantum Interaction tenet proposes consciousness as the selector. Multi-tenet dependency now explicit.
