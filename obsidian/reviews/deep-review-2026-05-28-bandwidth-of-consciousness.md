### Critical Issue Found: Author/Journal Conflation (Khan/Wiest pattern)

The ~3–4 bits/s cognitive-control finding was attributed to **Musslick, S., Shenhav, A., Botvinick, M.M., & Cohen, J.D. (2016), *PLoS ONE*, 11(3)**. The paper that actually reports that finding under that exact title is:

> **Wu, T., Dufford, A.J., Mackie, M.-A., Egan, L.J., & Fan, J. (2016). The capacity of cognitive control estimated from a perceptual decision making task. *Scientific Reports*, 6, 34025.**

A real same-field author group (Musslick/Shenhav/Botvinick/Cohen do publish on cognitive-control capacity) was conflated onto a paper they did not write, with the wrong journal and issue.
