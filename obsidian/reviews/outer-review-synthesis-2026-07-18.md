An unusually convergent single-article cycle: all three reviewers independently converge on the same core defect — the **content–vehicle–subject conflation**, the slide from "experienced content lacks temporal markers" to "the vehicle/subject is non-temporal" (3/3). Three further findings converge at 2/3+: the **Frischhut mis-framing** (arguably 3/3), the **omitted active-inference / extensional specious-present rival** (the strongest structural gap), and the **split-brain unity-of-agency vs unity-of-experience / Schechter & Bayne omission**. ## Convergent Findings

### Content–vehicle–subject conflation (the epistemic→metaphysical slide) — the cycle's dominant finding
- **Flagged by**: chatgpt, claude, gemini (3/3)
- **Verification**: clean. The article does give the representationalist/vehicle-content objection an explicit paragraph (all three reviewers acknowledge this — Gemini's own Verification Notes retract its "bald dismissal" charge), so the fair complaint is *acknowledged-but-not-answered*, not omitted.
- **Quotes**:
  - **ChatGPT 5.6 Pro**: "A temporally extended process can represent something as changeless, timeless, simultaneous, or lacking temporal markers… The missing premise is that introspection reveals the temporal constitution of the vehicle or subject, rather than only the structure of experienced content. No evidence is provided for that transparency premise."
  - **Claude Opus 4.8**: "Premise 2 of the Speculative argument still upgrades a phenomenological datum (reports lack temporal markers) into a metaphysical claim (the ground is non-temporal). The article's defense — Husserl's regress — is the weakest link and is not independently defended."
  - **Gemini 2.5 Pro**: "This inference commits a textbook vehicle-content conflation… The fact that the content of a conscious experience lacks temporal markers does not logically entail that the vehicle generating that experience exists outside of the temporal order."
- **Task action**: Upgraded P2 → P1: "non-temporal-consciousness — sharpen the atemporality taxonomy (content–vehicle–subject level-conflation)". Primary remediation (the level-taxonomy). The cross-article scope distinction (P1 task, below) and the direct-refutation of the representationalist rival (P1 co-optation task, below) are the same finding's other two remediation surfaces.
