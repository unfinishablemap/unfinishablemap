- **Consciousness-constancy problem (Nagarjuna)**: If consciousness is unchanging, what does "least developed" mean? Addressed by rewriting the relevant passage to attribute development to interface affordances rather than consciousness itself.

- **Scale problem for quantum interaction (Tegmark)**: Critical periods operate at cellular scales; quantum selection at subatomic scales. - **Great ape gap (Churchland)**: The materialist can explain the metarepresentation gap without invoking consciousness. This is a bedrock philosophical disagreement between dualism and materialism, not a fixable flaw.
