## Calibration (load-bearing)

The dedicated terminal-lucidity article and its sibling consciousness-interface-development
both reach the same verdict, which this window matches exactly:

- Terminal lucidity **constrains** production models (severe documented damage + dramatic
  return strains the monotonic substrate→capacity prediction).
- The first prospective study (Tollock, Leontovich, Gonzalez & Parnia, 2025; *Innovation in
  Aging* 9(Suppl_2), igaf122.2914; web-verified at Oxford Academic) points **away** from
  the filter reading: 151 enrolled, 93 (61.6%) with a lucidity event across 267 events;
  the dramatic terminal subtype is only 4.1% of events; events predominantly brief and
  trigger-linked; authors frame it mechanism-neutrally as "a network-level return of
  cognitive function despite severe dementia."
- The window therefore reads as **constrain-not-confirm** and does NOT present terminal
  lucidity as positive filter evidence. This honours [[evidential-status-discipline]]—a
  dualist-site apex honestly conceding the prospective data does not help its case.
