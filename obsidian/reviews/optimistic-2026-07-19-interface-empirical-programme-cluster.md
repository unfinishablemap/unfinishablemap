## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
The cluster honours the relocation of the hard problem without ever mistaking relocation for solution. `attention-as-causal-bridge` states it exactly: the interface hypothesis narrows "why does neural processing produce experience?" to "why does consciousness have this particular interface with neural processing?" and immediately adds this "should not be mistaken for a solution." The AST engagement is Chalmers-grade: it identifies the precise unearned step — AST "owes the bridge from representational content to ontic status," and "recursive self-modelling ... names the architectural feature without delivering the bridge." Steelmanning the rival and still finding the residue is the honest form of the argument.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
Stapp gets a genuine, load-bearing seat — three candidate control laws are named (Stapp's attention-rate proportional controller, Eccles' probability-reweighting, the Map's policy-selection switching controller), and the falsification table gives the literal-Zeno reading its own row with a real predicted signature (attention-rate-dependent Born deviation). Crucially it is not smuggled in as consensus: the Zeno stabilisation mechanism is presented with its decoherence-timescale objection intact, "one proposed mechanism" among others, with "the phenomenology of effort stands as data regardless of mechanism." That is how to engage the physics-consciousness interface seriously without over-claiming it.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
First-person data is treated as data the theory must honour, not explain away. The effort phenomenology is centred — James's "hold it fast before the mind," Kahneman's pupillometry, and Sjöberg's (2024) neurosurgical finding that the *sense of willing* survives supplementary-motor-area resection while execution is lost. The felt texture of returning attention (wandering / noticing / returning) is taken as a micro-phenomenological exhibit of bidirectional interaction. The "what it is like" is the explanandum, never the nuisance.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
`interface-specification-programme` reads the five control operations through `integration-as-activity`: gating, stabilising, vetoing, steering, allocating are "activities performed across time, not properties a system has at an instant" — becoming-as-constitutive rather than a static substance ghost. And it resists the cruder move: the six-line "convergence" is explicitly *not* offered as independent triangulation, since "they share the Map's tenets as constraints, so their convergence is not between genuinely independent research programmes." Process resonance illuminates the architecture without being used to inflate any evidential claim — the honest use of the persona.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
Control-theoretic will is the master exhibit Kane's libertarianism needs: veto ("a single bit suffices for stop/go"), the trilemma of selection ("determinism, randomness, and mental causation exhaust the options, and only the third preserves authorship"), and — most originally — the *economics of agency*, which relocates moral responsibility from the moment of decision to "the cultivation or neglect of attentional capacity." Freedom "scales with attentional skill." This is genuine agency taken seriously and given a mechanism-shaped home.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
Epistemic humility is structural, not decorative. "Consciousness science has not yet had its Galileo, let alone its Newton." The specification gap is marked in full — the psychophysical control law "has not been specified," the semantic-mapping, subject–brain pairing, timing/preferred-basis, multi-agent, developmental-onset and evolutionary debts are each named as unpaid. `attention-as-causal-bridge` even concedes the boundary where inquiry stops: "we cannot determine, from inside an attention disorder, whether consciousness is unchanged behind a degraded interface or is itself altered." The `framework-stage-calibration` discipline (pre-Keplerian, accumulating Tycho-analogue measurements) is exactly McGinn's kind of honesty about how much we can currently know.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
**This persona's verdict is load-bearing for this cluster, and it is overwhelmingly positive — the cluster is a masterclass in evidential-status discipline.** Five distinct restraint-moves earn praise, each a case of "tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating" done right:

1. **Refusal to flatten the testable tenet.** The falsification roadmap breaks Tenet 2 into six sub-readings (strict corridor, probability-bias, Stapp/Zeno, Orch-OR, Chalmers-McQueen Φ-collapse, objective-collapse modulation, trumping/preemption), each with its *own* falsifiability row, precisely so "the mechanism-committed readings' experimental traction" is not "silently exported to the strict corridor or the trumping reading, where the same traction is not available." Tabulating the within-tenet spread rather than claiming one "high traction" row is the honest register-keeping the five-tier scale exists to protect.

2. **Naming borrowed risk as borrowed.** `attention-as-causal-bridge` concedes that "most of the conditions that would close the interface hypothesis ... are *borrowed* risks" — scenarios where a rival's vindication closes the space — and that "the framework owes more such [model-specific forward] predictions before it can claim falsifiability symmetry with the rivals it engages." The effort-entropy prediction (phenomenal effort scaling with option-set entropy at threshold, controlling for discriminability) is flagged as "one prediction, not a battery." This is the absence of positive evidence being named as absence.

3. **The COGITATE null explicitly *not* counted for the Map.** "An unsettled contest among materialist theories is not evidence for dualism." A hostile reviewer's favourite over-reach — reading a rival-vs-rival null as support — is pre-empted by the article itself.

4. **The delegatory-causation join priced honestly.** The identification of the Born distribution with Saad's "default causal profile" is marked "a Map-specific integration, not a claim Saad makes," and joining the two "costs more than a coincidence of vocabulary" — the quantum-selection channel is *difference-making* and gives up strong closure, whereas Saad's trumping is *difference-making-free* and preserves it. Refusing to let a convenient vocabulary-match paper over a real mechanistic tension is exactly the discipline.

5. **Framework-stage calibration.** The programme names itself pre-Keplerian rather than dressing constraint-specification as a Newton-stage law: it "holds the first list and a set of measurable outputs but no worked coupling law, Hamiltonian, master equation, or simulation."

The Process-Philosopher and Hardline-Empiricist personas **converge** here rather than conflict: the process reading praises the control operations as constitutive activity, and the empiricist finds no tier-upgrade riding on that resonance. When both personas can praise different axes of the same passage without friction, the article has resolved the tension honestly — which is the case throughout this cluster. No calibration concern to flag.
