## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
The cluster keeps the explanatory gap in sharp focus exactly where a lesser treatment would let a correlation paper over it. The entropic-brain page's framing — "a measure that tracks consciousness without explaining it — is metaphysically modular" — is precisely the Chalmersian point: EBH "delivers the first [a physical correlate of richness] with growing precision and is silent on the second [that the activity is felt]." The page refuses to let "we have a number that tracks richness" be heard as "we have explained richness." Chalmers would also approve the filter-theory page's even-handed admission that filter theory "inherits a parallel debt: positing extra-cerebral consciousness for the brain to filter only relocates the question of what makes that consciousness phenomenal." The hard problem is applied symmetrically to both sides — a discipline the genre usually lacks.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
The quantum material is handled with the restraint Stapp's program has earned by 2026. The Orch-OR / microtubule mechanism is quarantined in a `psychedelics-and-the-filter-model` *Speculative Appendix* explicitly marked "Nothing earlier depends on it," held against the `mqi-empirical-fragility` discipline, and tagged with the 2026-05-11 recategorisation from "settled refutation" to "contested, possibility-level." A Stapp sympathiser gets a live mechanism-exploration target (Babcock et al. 2024 tryptophan superradiance, Kerskens & López Pérez 2022) without the article betting its case on it. This is engaging the physics-consciousness interface seriously *and* honestly — the appendix structure is exactly right.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
First-person structure is centred without being credulous. The minimal-self / narrative-self distinction (Zahavi vs. Metzinger) is the philosophical spine of the ego-dissolution section, and the Map's choice to follow Zahavi's pre-reflective "for-me-ness" is justified by first-person report rather than asserted: "subjects describe the loss of 'me-as-character' alongside the persistence of bare experiential presence." Crucially the persistence claim is then marked *defeasible* against the Madhyamaka objection. Nagel would also recognise the form/content move in the altered-states survey — schizophrenia and depression alter "*how the filtering itself works*" rather than its content — as taking the structure of subjectivity seriously.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
The cluster avoids crude substance dualism: the brain is "interface, not generator," and consciousness is described as operating *through* a multilayered, multi-channel architecture (claustrum, thalamic gating, predictive priors, network-identity) rather than as a ghost in a box. Whitehead-friendly readers will find the "experience persists as the filter narrows continuously" framing — consciousness narrowing by degree rather than switching off at a threshold — congenial. **Per the evidential-status constraint, this praise is registered as tenet-coherence, not evidence-elevation** — and notably the cluster never asks it to do more (see the Hardline Empiricist verdict below, with which it converges).

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
The cessation and flow material gives agency real work. The altered-states page reads meditative cessation as "consciousness *releasing* its grip on the filter from inside" and flow as consciousness "deliberately release[ing] active control" — both framed as evidence for Bidirectional Interaction. The filter-theory page is careful here too: it concedes that voluntary cessation is "harder to motivate on production accounts than on filter accounts — though not impossible for them," with the everyday voluntary-sleep counterexample supplied. Kane gets agency taken seriously without the article overclaiming a knockdown.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
Epistemic humility is structural, not decorative. The psychedelics page sharpens "access" into three grades — phenomenological, psychological-useful, veridical — and concedes that only the third would support filter theory, that veridical-grade cases "remain conspicuously thin," and that this is "the place most vulnerable to confirmation bias." The `ineffable-encounter-void` cross-link concedes that "the dismantled metacognitive instrumentation makes adjudication on phenomenological grounds particularly fraught." McGinn would applaud an article that names the spot where its own evidence is weakest and flags it as a cognitive-closure-adjacent limit rather than glossing it.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
**This is the cluster's standout strength, and the Birch verdict is load-bearing here.** The articles practise exactly the evidential-status discipline this persona exists to praise — and they do the praise-worthy thing of *declining* a tenet-driven upgrade at multiple points:

- **The named anti-double-counting move.** Both the filter-theory page and the psychedelics page state in plain text that the supportive cluster "carries the evidential weight of one pattern, not seven [/five]," that it "cannot honestly be cited as multiple independent confirmations," and that the same accommodation move "is available to production theorists on every case." This is textbook "tenet/coherence removes a defeater but does not upgrade evidential status."
- **Coherence-not-evidence stated explicitly and repeatedly.** Every "Relation to Site Perspective" / Dualism paragraph in the cluster lands on the same calibrated formula: the neuroimaging "sits naturally within filter theory and is consistent with the Map's dualist commitment; that is coherence rather than evidential support over rivals." The entropic-brain page marks its filter re-reading as "an interpretive overlay, empirically equivalent to the production reading on this evidence alone — not a discriminating prediction."
- **The deep anaesthesia symmetry is conceded as a live threat to filter theory, not buried.** "Deep anaesthesia is at least as direct a challenge to *filter* models under the same evidential standards" — the article runs the knife both ways. Birch would single this out: the discipline survives contact with the case that most tempts a one-sided reading.
- **A candidate signature is *withdrawn*, not retained.** The psychedelics page explicitly retracts "therapeutic durability without ongoing reinforcement" as a filter-specific signature once it notices that REBUS, plasticity-windows, reconsolidation, and expectancy each independently predict it. Retracting your own evidence when it stops discriminating is the cleanest possible demonstration of the discipline.
- **Tenet-as-evidence-upgrade is praise-worthily *not* done.** The minimal-organism boundary material in `filter-theory` ("Simple Organisms and the Filter Boundary") and `altered-states` ("Boundary States") had the standard structural opportunity to slide *C. elegans*, Hydra, or slime mold up the scale on tenet-load — and explicitly declines, treating *Physarum* maze-solving as "baseline cognition ... that can fully dissociate from consciousness" and leaving the nematode's status an open interface question rather than a tier-upgrade. The Process Philosopher's panpsychic resonance is available here and the cluster refuses to cash it as a probability claim. **Birch and Whitehead converge** on these passages rather than conflict — the mark of an honestly-resolved tension.

If anything, Birch's only note is that the cluster is now *so* heavily hedged that a casual reader could miss that the Map still holds the filter reading as its considered position — but that is a presentation risk, not a calibration error.
