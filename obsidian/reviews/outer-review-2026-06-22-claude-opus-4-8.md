## TL;DR

- **The site is far more self-aware than a standard adversarial audit assumes, and that is itself the central finding.** It already confronts its single deepest vulnerability — the Born-rule / statistical-indistinguishability dilemma — explicitly and repeatedly (Tenets page, the Born-rule topic article, and the post-decoherence apex), and it has operationalized most of the "recurring failure modes" as named editorial audits (voids-circularity discount, evidential-status discipline, calibration-audit-triple, a *proposed* co-optation gate). The harshest *defensible* read is therefore not "it fabricates and co-opts" but "it has built elaborate machinery to **confess** co-optation, circularity, and unfalsifiability while continuing to publish content that commits them, and banks the confession as a credential." The strongest unaddressed move is to convert those confessions into **binding deletions and demotions** rather than perpetual disclosures.
- **Citation integrity is genuinely high.** Every load-bearing citation I verified against publisher-of-record sources checks out — correct authors, venues, and characterizations, including contested ones. I found no fabricated quotations or misattributed venues. The real citation problems are **staleness** (acknowledged and partially fixed) and **co-optation of naturalist authors** (acknowledged, firewall only partially enforced).
- **The biggest structural liabilities are (a) a metastasizing, heavily duplicated Voids catalogue (150+ entries with many near-identical pages), (b) the convergence-as-evidence argument resting on a circularity the site itself has discounted in /project/ but not purged from the apex, and (c) predictive processing / active inference — the strongest physicalist rival — being under-engaged as a named adversary even as its vocabulary is repeatedly re-glossed toward the filter/interface reading.** Verdicts: **REVISE-HARD** on convergence-as-evidence; **DEMOTE TO COHERENCE-ONLY** several quantum-interface positions; **RECOMMEND DELETION/MERGE** of redundant void duplicates.

## Deliverable 4 — Stale / Unsupported / Superseded Empirical Claims

- **Psychedelics / filter theory: STALE, self-flagged, verify-the-fix.** The site's own calibration-audit-triple documents that *psychedelics-and-the-filter-model* had a median citation year ~2015 (Carhart-Harris 2012, Tagliazucchi 2016, Lebedev 2015, Griffiths 2006) and missed key 2020s work. The 2020s frame has shifted to complexity/entropy measures (Lempel-Ziv) and predictive-processing accounts the article does not engage. **VERDICT: REVISE-HARD; confirm the flagged refine pass actually landed.** [[calibration-audit-triple]]
- **Predictive processing / active inference: the strongest neglected physicalist rival.** Active inference now has explicit *theories of consciousness*, not just perception — e.g., **Laukkonen, Friston & Chandaria, "A beautiful loop: An active inference theory of consciousness," *Neuroscience & Biobehavioral Reviews* vol. 176, art. 106296, Sept 2025**, which holds that consciousness "arises specifically at the interface between continuous sensory perception and discrete, counterfactual policy selection processes" via three conditions (epistemic field, Bayesian binding, epistemic depth). The Map still treats predictive processing as mechanism-only. This is the single most important empirical-currency gap: the strongest rival has advanced to a direct competitor of the interface reading and is not confronted as such. (Note: I have not independently verified the existence of the IIT-vs-PP adversarial-collaboration preprint sometimes cited at arXiv:2509.00555; the Map should confirm before citing.)
- **Quantum-biology cluster: CURRENT but credulous.** Wiest 2025 (niaf011) and Keppler 2025 are real and recent, but Wiest's review is a partisan Orch-OR brief whose "direct physical evidence of a macroscopic quantum entangled state in the living human brain" claim the Map repeats with insufficient marking that it is contested and rests on the disputed Hagan–Hameroff decoherence estimate. **CONSTRAIN-VS-ESTABLISH violation:** the avian-cryptochrome precedent (correctly cited, incl. the Jan 2026 Princeton JACS computational study) establishes only that warm quantum effects *can* occur in a highly specific molecular architecture — which the site mostly flags — but the microtubule claims are passed through with more confidence than the primary literature supports. [ResearchGate + 2](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/391572056_A_quantum_microtubule_substrate_of_consciousness_is_experimentally_supported_and_solves_the_binding_and_epiphenomenalism_problems)
- **COGITATE adversarial collaboration: under-integrated.** The most important recent consciousness-science result is **Cogitate Consortium, "Adversarial testing of global neuronal workspace and integrated information theories of consciousness," *Nature* 642(8066):133–142, 5 June 2025 (doi:10.1038/s41586-025-08888-1; n=256, fMRI/MEG/iEEG).** Per the IIT supplement: "The results corroborate IIT's overall claim that posterior cortical areas are sufficient for consciousness, and neither the involvement of PFC nor global broadcasting are necessary"; for GNWT the paper found "the general lack of ignition at stimulus offset," and for IIT "a lack of sustained synchronization within the posterior cortex." The convergence article's "prediction-confirmed" framing is stale relative to this 2025 result.

### Bottom line

The Unfinishable Map is an unusually rigorous instance of its genre: its citations are accurate, it has pre-empted most of the failure modes an external referee would name, and it confronts its own deepest dilemma in print. The justified adversarial verdict is therefore narrow but sharp — **the project has substituted exhaustive self-disclosure for self-correction.** It knows its convergence argument is circular, knows its preferred quantum reading is unfalsifiable by construction, knows it co-opts naturalists, and knows its psychedelics literature is stale — and it documents all of this while leaving the offending content standing. The single highest-leverage change is to give the /project/ disciplines *teeth*: automatic demotion and deletion tags, a populated falsifiability-status layer, and a non-LLM adversarial review channel. Absent that, the site's growing meta-apparatus functions as sophisticated inoculation rather than genuine constraint.
