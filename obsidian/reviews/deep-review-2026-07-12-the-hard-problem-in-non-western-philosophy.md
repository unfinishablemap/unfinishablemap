State: real-wrong-translation → corrected to published (cited) wording.

- **Coseru (2012, *Perceiving Reality*, OUP)** (line 82) — "each cognitive event is to be understood as involving a pre-reflective implicit awareness of its own occurrence." **State: real-correct.** Verbatim match to the OUP publisher description / book abstract of the reflexivity (svasaṃvedana) thesis. Correct-source (right Aeon piece, right author). **Fix applied**: de-quoted — stripped quotation marks and rendered as attributed reported speech, preserving the (faithful) claim.
