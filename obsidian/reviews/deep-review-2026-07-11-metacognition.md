  accuracy; **damage impairs** explicit metacognitive judgements while leaving first-order

  1541-1543, DOI 10.1126/science.1191883; PMC3173849) shows Fleming, Weil, Nagy, Dolan & Rees

  faithful to both papers, and tighter (removed the duplicated causal clause).
