## Pessimistic Analysis Summary

### Critical Issues Found (Citation Audit)

1. **Ciaunica et al. (2022) stance/direction misattribution — FIXED.** The article stated depersonalisation is "a *down-weighting of interoceptive precision*." The cited paper (Ciaunica, Seth, Limanowski, Hesp & Friston 2022, *Consciousness and Cognition* 101, 103320) actually argues for **imbalanced precision weighting *toward* self-related priors**, with impaired attenuation of self-generated signals ("imbalanced precision weighting towards self-priors, leading to the inability to flexibly update the self- and world-models") — a precision *imbalance favouring* self-priors, not a simple down-weighting. This is a direction error on the load-bearing mechanistic variable. **Resolution**: rewrote the attribution to "an *imbalanced precision weighting toward self-related priors*, with impaired attenuation of self-generated signals"; aligned the downstream reasoning ("a disturbance in how interoceptive predictions are precision-weighted") and the convergence-paragraph descriptors ("altered interoceptive precision-weighting", "interoceptive precision-imbalance") for internal consistency. Calibration framing ("active-inference pathology rather than a channel falling silent"; "competes with a fully specified physicalist rival") preserved verbatim. Length-neutral.

2. **Wrong co-author in inline 1999 cite — FIXED.** Inline text read "(Spanos 1994; Lilienfeld, Lynn and **Kihlstrom** 1999)". The 1999 paper (Reference #15) is Lilienfeld, Lynn, **Kirsch**, Chaves, Sarbin, Ganaway & Powell — Kihlstrom is not an author. **Resolution**: corrected to "Lilienfeld, Lynn, Kirsch et al. 1999", matching the reference list.

3. **Dangling citation: Spanos 1994 — FIXED.** "Spanos 1994" was cited inline (sociocognitive/role-enactment model of DID) but had no References entry. Verified real: Spanos, N. P. (1994), "Multiple identity enactments and multiple personality disorder: A sociocognitive perspective," *Psychological Bulletin*, 116(1), 143–165, doi:10.1037/0033-2909.116.1.143. **Resolution**: added as Reference #16 (Marsh renumbered 16→17).
