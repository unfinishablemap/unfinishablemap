### High confidence

#### 1. Cramer's transactional interpretation of quantum mechanics
- **Target section**: `concepts/`
- **Gap evidence**: 18 files cite "transactional interpretation" / "Cramer," concentrated exactly in the corpus's time-symmetric-selection apparatus (`forward-in-time-conscious-selection`, `time-symmetric-selection-mechanism`, `presentiment-and-retrocausality`, `time-collapse-and-agency`, `atemporal-causation`, `retrocausality`, `collapse-and-time`, `forward-in-time-vs-time-symmetric-selection`). **No dedicated page and no heading-level treatment** — it is invoked as a known referent the reader is assumed to hold.
- **Why article-sized**: The transactional interpretation (Cramer 1986; Kastner's later Possibilist TI) is a complete, self-contained interpretation: offer/confirmation waves, the Wheeler–Feynman absorber-theory ancestry, the emphasis on the completed *transaction* rather than one-way collapse, and its native time-symmetry. It carries its own literature and its own standing objections (the delayed-choice worry, the ontological status of advanced waves).
- **Map relevance / tenet tie**: This is the **conspicuous missing member of the qm-interpretation cluster** whose six siblings all have pages — and it is the single interpretation most naturally allied with the Map's time-symmetric-selection mechanism (Tenets 2–3). The whole forward-in-time / time-symmetric debate leans on transactional/absorber intuitions that currently have no positioned home to cite; a dedicated page would let that entire topic cluster point at Cramer rather than re-gesture per article. Highest-confidence gap on this survey.

#### 2. Bohm's implicate order and active information (the pilot-wave mind–matter view)
- **Target section**: `concepts/`
- **Gap evidence**: 31 files mention "Bohm" / "implicate order" / "active information," spread across the quantum-mind and quantum-foundations clusters (`comparing-quantum-consciousness-mechanisms`, `four-quadrant-dualism-taxonomy`, `mechanism-costs-dualism-thickness-quadrants`, `qm-interpretations-beyond-many-worlds`, `wavefunction-realism-vs-primitive-ontology`, `russellian-monism-versus-bi-aspectual-dualism`). **No dedicated page, no heading-level treatment.**
- **Why article-sized**: Bohm's position is a distinct, complete package — pilot-wave/de Broglie–Bohm mechanics, the implicate/explicate order, and crucially *active information* as the bridge concept where the quantum potential "in-forms" particle behaviour, which Bohm and Hiley explicitly extended to a mind–matter analogy. It is a *rival quantum-consciousness mechanism* to Stapp, Penrose–Hameroff, and Beck–Eccles — each of which owns a home page or heading cluster — sitting homeless.
- **Map relevance / tenet tie**: Directly Tenet-2 relevant. Bohm's active-information bridge is the deterministic-hidden-variable cousin the Map's mechanism cluster brushes against but never positions. A dedicated page would give `comparing-quantum-consciousness-mechanisms` and the four-quadrant taxonomy a named fourth mechanism to contrast, and would let the Map state precisely why it prefers a selection-only interface over an active-information one (a clarifying contrast for Tenet-2 minimality). High confidence; slightly below #1 only because the material is more diffuse (mechanism vs. clean interpretation-slot).

- **Target section**: `concepts/`
- **Gap evidence**: 19 files invoke "Sellars" / "manifest image" / "scientific image" as a framing (`one-structure-three-vocabularies`, `pragmatism`, `pragmatisms-path-to-dualism`, `consciousness-defeats-explanation`, `argument-from-reason`, `self-stultification-as-master-argument`, `rational-normativity`, `wavefunction-realism-vs-primitive-ontology`). **Zero heading-level treatment** — the distinction is used as assumed background everywhere and defined nowhere.

A dedicated page would give the Map's synthesis articles a named intellectual ancestor to position against (does the interface framework fuse the two images, or hold them irreducibly apart?), sharpening Tenet-1 and Tenet-5. Placed medium only because it is a *framing/method* rather than a rival first-order position — the harvest signal is strong (19 files, load-bearing, zero home) but the article would be more meta than the two above.
