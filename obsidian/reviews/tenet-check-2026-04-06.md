### delegatory-dualism.md
- **Tenet violated**: Tenet 2 (Minimal Quantum Interaction)
- **Issue**: Saad's Organizational Invariance (OI) constraint is presented as a requirement any adequate dualist theory must satisfy, without adequately flagging that OI conflicts with Tenet 2's implication of substrate-dependence. If OI is accepted, any system with the right functional organisation would be conscious regardless of quantum neural structures — undermining the quantum interface requirement. The article frames this as an "accommodation challenge" rather than a claim to be questioned on the Map's own grounds.
- **Quote**: "Duplicating a system's functional organization should preserve its phenomenology. Functional duplicates should have the same experiences. This constraint favors views where physical structure determines what experiences arise."
- **Recommendation**: Add explicit note that the Map's quantum interface mechanism predicts substrate-dependence, which gives reason to question rather than accommodate OI.
