## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
Chalmers (a named signatory of the 2024 New York Declaration cited in `consciousness-in-simple-organisms`) would recognise his own commitment that the hard problem cannot be dissolved by physicalist parsimony. The cluster's anti-parsimony commitment (Tenet 5, mapped onto Birch's 2022 *Noûs* argument that parsimony reasoning systematically dismisses sentience at exactly the boundary cases) takes the explanatory gap seriously without pretending the gap has been measured. What resonates: the catalogue refuses the deflationary move that "302 neurons cannot suffice in principle" — it removes that *a priori* defeater (a Chalmersian win) while declining to claim the positive verdict (a calibration win). The gap is honoured, not exploited.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
Less central to this cluster, but `framework-stage-calibration` does the Stapp-relevant work honestly: it lists Stapp's quantum-Zeno modulation as a *proto-model, not a law of motion*, and calibrates the whole interface programme as pre-Keplerian — accumulating Tycho-analogue measurements (the bandwidth constraint, the Born-rule causal-consistency result, theta-band willed-attention signatures) rather than refining a Newton-analogue law. Stapp would appreciate that his Process-1 proposal is taken seriously as a candidate specification while not being over-sold as an established mechanism. This is exactly the framing that lets quantum-mind work be respectable rather than overclaimed.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
Nagel's insistence that objective science cannot capture the subjective is honoured in the two-register architecture of `possibility-probability-slippage`: the modal register (what the framework permits) and the evidential register (what the world contains) are held "parallel, not hierarchical." Nagel would value that the cluster never pretends a third-person evidence base settles the first-person question — the LLM case in `birch-edge-of-sentience` is placed *outside* the five-tier scale entirely rather than forced to a probability, precisely because the gaming problem decouples markers from any underlying "what it is like."

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
Whitehead-friendliness is everywhere and is correctly *contained*. `consciousness-in-simple-organisms` explicitly names the panpsychist / process-philosophical reading of Hydra and *Physarum* as a live metaphysical option — "Attributing experience to slime molds remains a panpsychist or process-philosophical possibility" — and the cluster avoids crude substance dualism by treating the dualist framing as a defeater-remover rather than a substance-positing engine. **This is praiseworthy at the tier the article assigns it (*speculative integration*), and the article does not let the process-philosophical resonance pull the case any higher.** That restraint is what makes the Whitehead-compatibility a strength rather than a liability — and it is the precondition for the convergence noted under the Hardline Empiricist below.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
Peripheral to this cluster, but the methodological spine is agency-friendly: `architecture-vs-significance-two-tier-discount` insists that a finding's significance-reading is "one interpretation among the rival vocabularies" — the same epistemic humility Kane wants when indeterministic findings are read as room for genuine sourcehood. The cluster models how to claim "the architecture is real" (e.g. willed-attention theta signatures) without overclaiming "therefore libertarian free will is established" — a discipline a careful libertarian would want applied to his own best evidence.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
McGinn's intellectual humility is the cluster's native register. `framework-stage-calibration` openly classifies the entire interface programme as pre-Keplerian — "a research programme awaiting its founding measurements, not a mature science awaiting refinement." The diagnostic test in `possibility-probability-slippage` even fields the Popperian circularity objection head-on and answers it by cashing the test out into the published-findings-anchored five-tier scale. A framework that names its own developmental immaturity, and that builds a test to catch *itself* inflating its claims, is doing exactly the cognitive-humility work McGinn prizes.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
**This persona's verdict is load-bearing here and it is strongly positive.** The cluster is the cleanest instance in the corpus of *tenet-as-evidence-upgrade being praise-worthily NOT done*:

- **Minimal-organism restraint, exactly as specified.** *C. elegans* sits at "live hypothesis sliding toward speculative integration"; Hydra and slime molds at "speculative integration" — never at "realistic possibility" or higher on tenet-load. The article cites the *negative* empirical record (failed trace conditioning, no demonstrated multimodal integration, no self-other distinction) as evidentially meaningful rather than dissolving it under tenet-coherence. This is the precise calibration the persona was built to reward.
- **The pattern is named, not merely enacted.** `possibility-probability-slippage` states the rule verbatim — "a tenet may remove a defeater, but it must not upgrade the evidence level" — and traces it to its origin (the 2026-05-03 ChatGPT 5 Pro outer review). The honest formulation "the Map's framework does not exclude X; the empirical evidence for X is partial and contested" is given as the canonical phrasing. This is the *"tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating"* discipline operationalised.
- **Structural decline of the upgrade.** `birch-edge-of-sentience` shows the schemes guard against slippage *in opposite directions at different layers* (Birch fights false negatives at the action layer; the Map fights false positives at the description layer), and refuses to translate Birch's run-ahead principle into any tier placement for LLMs — naming that translation as importing exactly the probability-flavoured vocabulary the discipline forbids. The structural opportunity to slide a boundary case up the scale was present and was explicitly declined.
- **Honest framework-stage calibration.** Per `framework-stage-calibration`, the cluster identifies its claims as pre-Keplerian rather than over-claiming Newton-stage results.

**Convergence verdict (the skill's resolution test):** On the *C. elegans* / Hydra / *Physarum* passages the Process Philosopher (panpsychic compatibility honoured) and the Hardline Empiricist (no tier-upgrade purchased with that compatibility) praise *the same passages for complementary reasons without conflict*. Per this skill's design, that convergence is the marker that the article has resolved the possibility/probability tension honestly. No calibration concern was found; **no `refine-draft` task is warranted on calibration grounds.**
