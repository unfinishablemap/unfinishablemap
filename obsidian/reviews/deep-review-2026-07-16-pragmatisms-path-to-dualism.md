- **Peirce, "consciousness ... reinstated without tychism"** — **DROPPED-WORD (fixed).** Article quoted: consciousness "is not to be reinstated without tychism". Primary text (Peirce's summary of the tychism papers, CP 6.61-ff / "Reply to the Necessitarians" register): *"Consciousness is not to be **so** reinstated without tychism; nor can the work be accomplished by assigning to the mind an occult power."* The word **"so"** was dropped. It is anaphoric — "so reinstated" = "reinstated as a factor of the universe" (from the preceding sentence, "what can be done to reinstate consciousness as a factor of the universe when once tychism is admitted"). Dropping it broadens the claim from "cannot be reinstated *as a cosmic factor*" to a bare "cannot be reinstated." Substance genuine; attribution (Peirce, tychism corpus) correct. **Fix**: restored verbatim "so" + supplied the anaphoric antecedent inline ("reinstated, that is, as a factor of the universe") so the quote is both verbatim-faithful and self-contained. Propagated the "so" to `research/pragmatist-philosophy-of-mind-2026-03-28.md:66` (family resolution). NOTE: prior reviews (2026-06-02, 2026-04-16) logged this as "genuine Peirce, verified" — they verified *provenance and stance* (real quote, not misrepresenting Peirce as dualist), which is correct; they did not catch the dropped word because verbatim ≠ metadata. - **Peirce, universe "suffused with mind"** — **PARAPHRASE-AS-QUOTE (fixed, de-quoted).** Article presented the synechism continuum with the universe quote-marked as "suffused with mind." This is **not verbatim Peirce.** The genuine Peirce phrases in this vicinity are "matter is effete mind," "matter is mere specialized and partially deadened mind," and the semiotic "the universe is perfused with signs" — none is "suffused with mind." Substance (synechism = mind-matter continuum, mind pervading the universe) is a faithful characterization; the quotation marks falsely assert verbatim provenance. **Fix**: de-quoted to the article's own paraphrase ("the whole universe suffused with mind").
