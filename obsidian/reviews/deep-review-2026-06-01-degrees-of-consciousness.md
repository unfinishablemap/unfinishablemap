## Pessimistic Analysis Summary

### Critical Issues Found

- **Wrong-author citation (web-verified)**: The article attributed the 2019 *Frontiers in Systems Neuroscience* paper "General Anesthesia: A Probe to Explore Consciousness" to **"Bhatt, M. B., et al. (2019)"** in both the body (Anaesthesia subsection) and the references. The actual first author is **Bonhomme** (Bonhomme, V., Staquet, C., Montupil, J., et al., *Front. Syst. Neurosci.* 13:36, 2019). Verified directly against the Frontiers/PMC record. **Resolution**: corrected to "Bonhomme et al. 2019" in the body and the full author list in the references.
  - *Why it survived 4 prior reviews*: "Bhatt MB" is a **real** author in this exact research area (the 2021 eLife anaesthetic-recovery paper, a 2024 transmissive-theory paper). So intra-corpus cross-checking — which the prior reviews relied on — confirmed the name as plausible rather than flagging it. Only live-literature verification catches it. This is the textbook ai-citation-metadata-unreliable failure mode (a wrong-but-plausible author surviving multiple cross-checks).
  - *Propagation*: the same wrong "Bhatt et al. 2019 / Frontiers" attribution appears in `topics/anaesthesia-and-the-consciousness-interface.md` (body) and two research notes (`research/anaesthetic-recovery-neuroscience-2026-03-26.md`, `research/consciousness-anesthesia-filter-theory-2026-03-20.md`).
