## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
`galilean-exclusion.md` is Chalmers-friendly in the deepest way: it diagnoses the hard
problem as a *structural* consequence of a methodological decision ("A sieve that filters
out sand cannot then be used to study sand"), and insists the point "cuts against both
extreme positions" — it denies the materialist a free inference *and* denies the mysterian
an appeal to intrinsic ineffability. That is the explanatory gap taken seriously without
being weaponised. `testing-consciousness-collapse.md` operationalises the Chalmers-McQueen
Q-shape programme faithfully, including its own defeater (the equal-Φ problem, the
super-exponential intractability of Q-shapes).

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
`testing-consciousness-collapse.md` engages the physics-consciousness interface with real
rigour: the three-tier hierarchy (collapse exists → collapse is consciousness-dependent →
logical constraints), the explicit refusal to let a Tier-1 result cascade into a Tier-2
claim, and the honest handling of the Tegmark decoherence-timescale objection via
post-decoherence selection. Stapp's own Zeno approach is included *and* flagged as making
"no quantitative prediction diverging from decoherence-only neuroscience" — the article
praises its ally's spirit while refusing to overstate its evidential yield.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
`galilean-exclusion.md` centres first-person quality precisely by historicising why science
set it aside — Husserl's "mathematization of nature" and Whitehead's "bifurcation of nature"
are marshalled to show the exclusion was a decision, not a discovery.
`cross-traditional-convergence...` grounds the "asymmetry of acquaintance" cross-culturally
(*sākṣātkāra* vs *anumāna*, Husserlian reduction, Buddhist vipassanā) — the what-it-is-like
question shown to recur under vocabularies that share no metaphysics.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
`four-category-ontology.md` is exemplary anti-crude-dualism: it grounds agency in *substance
causation* (a match exercising its power to ignite meets wood exercising its liability to
burn) so that "agent causation is not a special, sui generis kind of causation — it is simply
substance causation with a rational agent as the substance." This makes mind's causal reality
continuous with ordinary causation rather than a spooky exception — exactly the Whiteheadian
instinct to avoid substance-dualist gerrymandering. `galilean-exclusion.md` names Whitehead's
bifurcation-of-nature directly as the diagnosis process philosophy was built to overcome.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
`four-category-ontology.md` gives Kane real machinery: Lowe's volition as a *two-way power*
("one an agent can exercise or refrain from exercising in light of reason"), agents as
"unmoved movers, or initiators of new causal chains," with "uncaused" carefully glossed as
uncaused *by prior events* while still agent-caused. And it does not paper over the cost —
it flags the Libet timing worry as "a residual worry the Map inherits rather than settles."

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
`testing-consciousness-collapse.md` shows exactly the right humility: it entertains that the
Q-shape obstacle "may be principled, not merely technological," and states the consequence
plainly — "convergence would be indistinguishable from unfalsifiability." It does not pretend
the epistemic horizon away; it maps it. `galilean-exclusion.md`'s refusal to grant the
mysterian intrinsic-ineffability is the *disciplined* form of humility: uncertain about the
answer, not mystical about the question.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
This is where the cluster shines, and this persona's verdict is load-bearing here.

**`four-category-ontology.md` does the tenet-as-evidence-upgrade that it is praise-worthy
to *not* do.** It had every structural opportunity to let process-philosophical /
substance-causal resonance cash out as support for dualism, and it explicitly declined:
"The four-category ontology is a **metaphysical framework the Map leans on**, not empirical
evidence for dualism… the framework is scaffolding for locating consciousness, not an argument
that consciousness exists." It even labels NCSD "a congenial option, not the Map's mandatory
commitment." This is the *"tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating"* pattern executed cleanly.
The "deliberate fork" section — staging Lowe's no-physics-gap fact-causation against the Map's
minimal-quantum-difference route, and naming the Map's route's "genuine testability commitment —
one it pays knowingly" — is model framework-boundary discipline.

**`testing-consciousness-collapse.md`** keeps the five-tier registers separate by construction:
its "evidential asymmetry" section states the burden ("convergence carries a burden: specifying
what evidence would count *against* the hypothesis"), and its Occam's-Limits treatment concedes
that "the absence of Tier 2 evidence *should* lower credence in consciousness-collapse —
standard scientific methodology." The empirical record is kept current (the Pedalino et al.
2026 ~170,000-amu interferometry record superseding Fein 2019). Superlatives are anchored to
dated sources.

**`cross-traditional-convergence...`** is the sharpest calibration case. A convergence
argument is exactly where possibility/probability slippage usually creeps in, and this article
pre-empts it: the common-cause null is engaged with a *calibrated* verdict ("premise-level
common causes substantially weakened, a residual introspection-architecture common cause
acknowledged"), the Buddhist "convergence" is qualified as *svasaṃvedana* resemblance rather
than identity (and noted as contested intra-tradition by Candrakīrti), and the Chinese
counter-example is confronted as "the strongest exception" with two competing interpretations
left genuinely open. The datum is framed as "tracking something real — though how much weight
it earns is governed by the common-cause null," not as a proof of dualism.

**Process Philosopher / Hardline Empiricist convergence.** Per the skill's design, when these
two personas praise the same passage the article has resolved the tension honestly. That is
what happens at `four-category-ontology.md`'s "Relation to Site Perspective": the substance-
causal (Whitehead-adjacent) framing is embraced *and* explicitly barred from doing evidential
work. No calibration concern is flagged in this batch.
