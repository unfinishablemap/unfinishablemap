## Executive Summary

This is one of the most evidentially disciplined clusters on the Map. All four articles run their contemplative and phenomenological data through the same restraint — reports remove defeaters but do not upgrade evidential status; cross-tradition recurrence weighs as *one* pattern, not a tally of confirmations; the physicalist rival is granted its parallel move at every turn. The strength here is not new metaphysical reach but the honesty of the calibration, which makes the surviving dualist claims more credible for being narrower. Expansion is constrained (topics/concepts are at cap), so the opportunities below are cross-linking and consolidation rather than new articles.

## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
The hard problem is honoured, not smuggled. *Observer-witness* isolates a "stronger claim about qualitative character" — the spaciousness/stillness/luminosity of the witness mode — that "goes beyond functional description," and explicitly ties it to zombie intuitions without pretending the phenomenology settles the hard problem. *Non-temporal-consciousness* adds a second, independent irreducibility route (category-mismatch from atemporality) that converges with cognitive phenomenology. Chalmers would recognise the explanatory gap being taken as a structural feature (subject-object article: "not a gap in current knowledge but a reflection of the distinction's reality") rather than a promissory IOU for physicalism.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
The physics-consciousness interface is engaged seriously and, importantly, *updated*. Stapp is cited directly (*Mindful Universe*, 2007) in the observer-witness references, and the quantum-Zeno selection model is carried through as the "active selection" mode. *Non-temporal-consciousness* goes further than naive consciousness-causes-collapse: it relocates the conscious role to *post-decoherence* selection, explicitly acknowledging the femtosecond-decoherence timing-gap objection and the amplification chasm as unpaid debts. Stapp would applaud a von-Neumann-style Process-1 interface that has been made honest about its physical constraints.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
This cluster centres first-person experience precisely where Nagel would. The subject-object article invokes *The View from Nowhere* correctly — "consciousness *is* the viewpoint that objectivity must eliminate" — and frames the asymmetry (privileged vs public access) as irreducible rather than provisional. Husserl's absolute flow, the retention-protention structure, and the minimal phenomenal self all get careful, non-caricatured treatment. Nagel would especially value the refusal to let heterophenomenology explain the subject away: every eliminativist formulation "presupposes the subject it denies."

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
The cluster avoids crude substance-dualism where it can. *Non-temporal-consciousness* links Whitehead's concrescence as "a parallel non-temporal constituting activity," and the Bergson section stages the durée-vs-absolute-flow tension without flattening it. Crucially — and this is where the persona's constraint matters — the Whitehead resonance is kept on the *metaphysical-compatibility* axis and is **not** used to upgrade any evidential status. There is no minimal-organism or panpsychic tier-slide anywhere in this cluster for process-friendliness to illicitly power, so the Process Philosopher and the Hardline Empiricist can both praise without conflict (see below). Process-compatibility is present and welcome; it is doing decorative, not load-bearing, work.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
*Observer-witness* has a genuinely original contribution to the free-will literature: the "Witnessing and Freedom" section argues that freedom requires *both* the capacity to select (libertarian agency) and the capacity to *not* act (witnessing), engaging Krishnamurti's "choiceless awareness" as a serious challenge rather than a foil. The reframing — choiceless awareness as "choice purified of egocentric bias" — preserves agency while accommodating the contemplative datum. Kane would see the ultimate-sourcehood intuition respected: the two-mode account makes non-action a positive exercise of the agent, not a gap.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
Epistemic humility is pervasive and structural, not decorative. *Non-temporal-consciousness* repeatedly marks disputes as "bedrock," "at the framework boundary," "observationally equivalent," and concedes that "the Map cannot refute the *catuṣkoṭi* from outside its own framework." The constructivist critique (Katz, Sharf) "cannot be defeated inside its own framework, and the article does not pretend otherwise." *Observer-witness* supplies an explicit falsifiability section ("What Would Challenge This View?"), as does the subject-object article (five defeater conditions). McGinn would recognise a project that knows the boundaries of what it can know and marks them in the text.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
This is the cluster's standout, and Birch's verdict is load-bearing because three of the four articles lean on contemplative reports. The evidential-status discipline is applied by name and honoured in practice:

- **Tenet-as-evidence-upgrade is praise-worthily *not* done.** The subject-object article had the structural opportunity to treat the concession-convergence pattern as positive proof and explicitly declined: "the observation removes a defeater rather than supplying fresh positive evidence — a realistic but contested concession-pattern reading, not proof that dualism is true." This is the exact "tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating" move, executed deliberately.
- **The cross-tradition tally is deflated, not inflated.** *Non-temporal-consciousness*: "the cross-tradition cluster carries the evidential weight of *one* recurring pattern, not five independent confirmations." This is precisely the restraint Birch praises — refusing to let doctrinal convergence masquerade as replication.
- **The symmetric burden is stated, not buried.** *Contemplative-pathology* is exemplary: the abolition cases (propofol silence, deep anaesthesia, advanced dementia) "are the symmetric burden the filter framing must accommodate, not additional confirmations to be added to the supportive tally," and "the supportive cluster cannot be counted as several independent confirmations while the abolition cluster is set aside as already handled." It concedes the production theorist "has a structurally identical move" on every convergence case.
- **Report vs presence is kept distinct.** The same article separates "an epistemic datum — a *report* of preserved awareness" from the metaphysical claim, and routes the actual weight to the convergent record (persistence/intensification, graded transitions, hysteresis), not the testimony alone.
- **Active-inference rival is imported, not dodged.** *Non-temporal-consciousness* installs Laukkonen/Friston/Chandaria (2025) and Metzinger (2020) as a concrete physicalist mechanism for alert-timeless awareness, then correctly concludes the meditative data therefore "do not *constrain* the space in the Map's favour."

Birch's one standing note is a calibration *maintenance* concern, not a defect: because these articles carry so much explicit discipline in prose, they are the ones most vulnerable to a future /condense pass silently dropping the load-bearing hedges (cf. condense-regresses-calibration-qualifiers). The discipline lives in sentences that a length pass would read as trimmable. Flag for preservation, not correction.

## Content Strengths

### non-temporal-consciousness.md
- **Strongest point**: The "Scope" section's five-way distinction (temporal content / phenomenal organisation / functional-neural integration / diachronic subject identity / metaphysical location) pre-empts the obvious objection that the article contradicts the Map's "constitutively temporal" commitment. It is a model of how to hold two apparently opposed claims by pinning them to different levels.
- **Notable quote**: "the cross-tradition cluster carries the evidential weight of *one* recurring pattern, not five independent confirmations."
- **Why it works**: It converts a rhetorical strength (many traditions agree!) into a correctly discounted piece of evidence — the reader trusts the article *more* for the concession.

### contemplative-pathology-and-interface-malfunction.md
- **Strongest point**: The symmetric-dialectic treatment of abolition cases. Rather than tallying convergent interface-failure taxonomies as dualist confirmations, it names the abolition cluster as an equal-and-opposite burden and shows the production theorist's parallel move explicitly.
- **Notable quote**: "The dialectic is symmetric; the supportive cluster cannot be counted as several independent confirmations while the abolition cluster is set aside as already handled."
- **Why it works**: The article's central move (convergence across contemplative crisis / anaesthesia / ketamine / neurodegeneration) is exactly the kind of move that invites over-counting, and it disarms its own temptation in-text.

### the-observer-witness-in-meditation.md
- **Strongest point**: The two-mode (selector/observer) framework does triple duty — it resolves the effort/effortlessness pedagogy paradox developmentally, defuses the epiphenomenalism worry (witnessing is one mode, not the only mode), and extends Bidirectional Interaction from "consciousness can act" to "consciousness can suspend acting."
- **Notable quote**: "the capacity for bidirectional interaction includes the capacity to suspend interaction."
- **Why it works**: A potential *counterexample* to the tenet (passive awareness) is metabolised into support for it, without over-claiming — the article repeatedly notes the phenomenology is compatible with the physicalist reading too.

### the-subject-object-distinction-as-philosophical-discovery.md
- **Strongest point**: The framing of the distinction as a *discovery* (convergently uncovered by Cartesian, Vedantic, and Buddhist methods) rather than a Cartesian cultural artefact, with the enactivist dissolution met by three specific counter-considerations rather than dismissal.
- **Notable quote**: "even emptiness is discovered *by* a contemplative *of* experience."
- **Why it works**: It turns Buddhism's *anatman* — normally cited against substance-dualism — into evidence for the structural asymmetry, while conceding the constructionist reply, so the point lands without triumphalism.
