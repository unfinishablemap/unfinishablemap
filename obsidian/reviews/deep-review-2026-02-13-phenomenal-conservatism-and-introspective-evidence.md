- **Hard-Nosed Physicalist (Dennett)**: No independent verification for introspective seemings. Article's response via defeater framework is appropriate—heterophenomenology has not provided the mechanistic explanation PC demands.
- **Empiricist (Popper)**: PC violates falsifiability; scientific progress often requires rejecting seemings before mechanistic explanations exist. Valid tension—partially addressed by the refraction analogy clarification.
