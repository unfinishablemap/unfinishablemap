## Executive Summary

This is the Map operating at its best. The cluster demonstrates a mature, self-aware methodology — build the strongest rival in its own vocabulary, grant that it absorbs the shape of the evidence, then isolate the narrow residue that survives — applied to both a metaphysical rival (process monism) and an empirical one (value-blind selection). The two supportive personas the skill deliberately holds in tension (the Process Philosopher and the Hardline Empiricist) *converge* in praise here rather than conflict, which is the tell that the calibration is honest. The strongest expansion opportunity is not new content but tighter integration: the apex that names the method does not link to its two clearest named exemplars.

## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
`phenomenal-authority-and-first-person-evidence.md` is Chalmers' own machinery deployed with care: the acquaintance relation, direct phenomenal concepts partly constituted by the quality they represent, the knowledge-argument move, and the phenomenal-concepts-strategy counter are all present and correctly attributed (Chalmers 2003). The three-layer decomposition of authority (apodictic existence → attended content → structural periphery) is exactly the kind of structure that lets the hard problem draw only on the layers where authority is strongest and cede layer 3 to Schwitzgebel. Chalmers would recognise the explanatory gap taken seriously without inflation.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
The interface commitment is handled with unusual discipline. `the-steelman-for-process-monism.md` explicitly scopes the quantum opening: the interface and the discriminating-test designs "are meant to separate dualism from *physicalism*—not from this rival, since a process monist can re-describe any interface datum in process terms." That is precisely the kind of honesty about what the physics-consciousness interface can and cannot adjudicate that keeps the interactionist program credible rather than over-sold.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
The first-person stance is centred without special pleading. The constitutive-vs-referring-observation distinction earns layer-1's apodicticity structurally rather than by assertion — the seeming and the being-of-the-phenomenal-state "are not separable items that could come apart." The heterophenomenology treatment is a model of charity: it reads Dennett's neutrality as a *policy* about public inference rather than a denial he need not make, then locates the Map's quarrel precisely at the policy. Nagel would see "what is it like" defended at exactly the layer where the question bites.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
Whitehead is given his full due in `the-steelman-for-process-monism.md`: actual occasions, prehension, the denial of vacuous actuality, creativity as "the Category of the Ultimate," and the pithy "the many become one, and are increased by one." Crucially the article grants the rival's genuine advantages — more parsimonious, no interaction problem, dissolves rather than relocates the hard problem — and states plainly that "the convergence of three traditions on the position suggests it is not a parochial artifact." This is process metaphysics met at strength, not caricatured. Whitehead-friendliness is honoured as compatibility, and — see the next entry — it is *not* used to do work it should not do.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
Agency is protected at the axiom level rather than smuggled in. The article retains a persisting subject against Buddhist *anattā* not by refuting the no-self analysis but by grounding indexical identity in Tenet 4 and noting "the agency account needs a persisting subject for agent-causation" — while conceding that "the indexical question is meaningful" is itself a posited claim, not a neutral datum. Kane would approve of agency defended as a load-bearing commitment openly owned, not disguised as a neutral finding.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
Epistemic humility is structural throughout. Both steelman articles end at declared bedrock: "The disagreement is closer to bedrock than it first appears... rather than an argument one side has lost." `phenomenal-authority` names its own most-exposed flank — the illusionist "cannot [be] dislodge[d]... by argument from premises the illusionist already accepts" and the dispute "appears underdetermined by the evidence available from inside either framework." McGinn would recognise a framework that marks the boundary of what it can settle instead of pretending to have crossed it.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
This is where the cluster is strongest, and the Hardline Empiricist's verdict is load-bearing because `the-steelman-for-process-monism.md` is a live test of the very slippage this persona guards against. The article had every structural opportunity to let Whitehead-friendliness cash out as a metaphysical win — process monism is "more parsimonious and more unified than the Map's own framework" — and it explicitly declines to: "The decline is a tenet-grounded choice the Map pays a parsimony cost to make, not a demonstration that process monism is false." That is *tenet-as-evidence-upgrade praise-worthily not done*. The pattern is named exactly right: "steelmanning raises the rival to a serious competitor the Map has not defeated." And the parsimony discipline is applied symmetrically — Tenet 5 "forbids the Map from later invoking simplicity in its own favour." `phenomenal-authority` shows the same restraint on Tenet 3: training-dependent introspective accuracy "removes a defeater... without thereby upgrading the dualist reading over its physicalist rival," and the parsimony-epistemology refine landed today installs a genuine rival matrix (eliminativism disputes the data; reductive phenomenal realism / phenomenal-concepts / representationalism accept it and contest interpretation) rather than a strawman. This is the five-tier scale used honestly, register kept separate throughout.

**The two personas converge.** The Process Philosopher and the Hardline Empiricist are designed to be in tension; here they praise the same passages for different reasons — Whitehead's account is honoured *and* its friendliness is quarantined from tier-upgrade. Convergence is the signal that the article resolved the possibility/probability seam honestly. No calibration concern to flag; no `refine-draft` warranted on that axis.
