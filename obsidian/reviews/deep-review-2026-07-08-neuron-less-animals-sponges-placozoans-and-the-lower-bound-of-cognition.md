## Pessimistic Analysis Summary

### Critical Issues Found

- **Possibility/probability slippage in the lead (reading-(c) calibration)** — CRITICAL, fixed in place. The opening paragraph asserted the neuron-less animals are "competency without any plausible experiential interface—rich, goal-like behaviour that **no serious theory of consciousness would count as *felt*, because there is no neural substrate at all to host the feeling**." Two defects: (a) "no serious theory ... would count as felt" is factually over-broad — IIT and panpsychist theories are serious theories of consciousness that could assign non-zero experience to distributed cell coordination, so the universal quantifier is false; (b) "because there is no neural substrate at all to host the feeling" converts the point into a **proof-of-absence** causal claim (no substrate → no feeling), which presupposes substrate-necessity as fact and contradicts the article's own correctly-calibrated close (§Relation to Site Perspective: "the Map does not claim to have proved these animals are non-conscious"). This is calibration error, not bedrock disagreement — a tenet-accepting reviewer would still flag the lead as overstated relative to the careful body. Diagnostic test (would a reviewer who fully accepts the Map's tenets still flag it? yes) → correctable inside the framework → CRITICAL.
  - **Fix applied**: scoped the consensus claim to "the theories tying experience to neural organisation would not count as *felt*, because the substrate those theories require is wholly absent" (a claim about what those theories *hold*, dodging the IIT/panpsychism counterexample), and appended an explicit calibration clause: "That is a claim about what those theories hold, not a proof that nothing is felt; the point developed below is narrower and firmer—that the behaviour itself gives no purchase on the question either way." The boldface thesis was softened from "without any plausible experiential interface" to "without a localisable experiential interface" (the Tenet-3 interface-*locus* framing, which is what the Map actually claims, rather than a metaphysical absence).
