A *mechanism-costs cartography* exposes each cell of a prior thickness or grain taxonomy to a fixed battery of mechanism questions and reads off, for each cell, what the inhabitant owes that its neighbours do not. The lens is cartographic rather than adjudicative: the cell-by-cell accounting surfaces relative debts without arguing that any cell is right. The methodology presupposes that the cells have already been located by some independent taxonomic axis (in the dualism case, mind-side × physical-side thickness); its distinctive move is the *fixed-question-across-cells* discipline, in which the same battery is asked at every cell so that cell-specific commitments are forced into visibility. The article that instantiated the methodology on 2026-05-11, [[mechanism-costs-dualism-thickness-quadrants]], walks the [[four-quadrant-dualism-taxonomy|four-quadrant dualism taxonomy]] through three mechanism questions (causation account, interface specification, conservation engagement) and reads off what each quadrant owes. This document names that lens as a generalisable methodology and specifies the editorial rules future deployments must satisfy.

The discipline operates at the *cartographic layer*. It joins the family of named methodological disciplines as the eighth member: alongside [[closed-loop-opportunity-execution|closed-loop opportunity execution]] (cycle level), [[coalesce-condense-apex-stability|coalesce-condense-apex-stability]] (article level), [[bedrock-clash-vs-absorption|bedrock-clash vs. absorption]] (within-article level), [[framework-stage-calibration|framework-stage calibration]] (framework level), [[evidential-status-discipline|evidential-status discipline]] (calibration level), [[direct-refutation-discipline|direct-refutation discipline]] (reasoning-mode level), and [[cluster-integration-discipline|cluster-integration discipline]] (argument-architecture level), mechanism-costs cartography governs how a *taxonomy article's locating work* is followed up by a *cost-accounting article* that surfaces cell-relative debts without converting them into endorsement of any cell.

## The Structural Form

A mechanism-costs cartography deployment has three parts.

**Part 1: A prior locating taxonomy.** The cells must be locatable independently of the cost lens. The four-quadrant dualism taxonomy locates positions by orthogonal thickness axes; the cells exist before the cost battery is asked. A cartography that *constructs* its cells from the battery rather than inheriting them from a prior taxonomy collapses into ad-hoc objection-response and loses the methodology's force.

**Part 2: A fixed battery of mechanism questions.** The battery must be applicable at every cell, not just at the cells the catalogue prefers. Three questions span the inaugural deployment: how does the mind contribute to physical effects (causation account); what is the structure of mind–physical contact (interface specification); how does the position relate to conservation laws (conservation engagement). The cells differ in which answers come cheap and which come dear, but every cell owes an answer.

**Part 3: A cell-by-cell exposition.** Each cell receives a parallel-structured treatment under the same battery, with cross-references showing how the cell's debts compare to its neighbours'. The parallel structure is what makes the methodology cartographic: the reader can compare Q2 strict's evasion of the interface question against Q4's heaviest interface bill against Q3's dissolution of the question without re-orienting.

## The Three Mechanism Questions (and Their Domain-Specificity)

The dualism deployment fixes three questions because the philosophy-of-mind literature has weighted them most heavily against interactionist positions. A cartographic deployment in a different domain would fix a different battery: theories of personal identity might be exposed to a battery of *constitution claim, persistence criterion, branching commitment*; accounts of laws of nature to *modal force, governing relation, ontology of properties*. The fixed-question battery is the methodology's defining feature; the specific questions are the domain's contribution.

A future deployment is constrained by three rules on its battery. First, the battery must include the questions the cell's critics press hardest, not only the questions its inhabitants find comfortable. Second, every question must apply at every cell, even if a cell's answer is "the question dissolves under this commitment" — that answer is itself a cost-disclosure, not a free pass. Third, the battery is fixed for the deployment, not adjusted cell-by-cell to favour preferred outcomes.

## The Asymmetry-Concession Discipline

The cell-by-cell exposition will typically produce asymmetric results — some cells owe much more than others on the chosen battery. When this happens, the methodology requires *explicit concession*: name the asymmetry, acknowledge it as a real feature of the question selection rather than burying it. The inaugural deployment performs the concession at the top of its cell-by-cell section: "A reader might worry the trio over-emphasises the physical-side debts. It does — deliberately. The conservation-law and interface questions originate in the physicalist's challenge to interactionism, and the literature has weighted them heavily ... The asymmetry is real, not an artefact of the question selection."

The concession is load-bearing. Without it, a cartography that produces "the Map's preferred cell pays least" reads as adjudicative endorsement of the preferred cell rather than as cartographic exposure of relative debts under a stipulated battery. The concession marks the asymmetry as a feature of the chosen questions, invites the reader to reconfigure the battery if they suspect bias, and immunises the cartography against the silent-endorsement failure mode that mechanism-cost accounting is especially prone to.

## Use Case

The methodology applies when a prior taxonomy article has located positions in a grid but has not exposed their relative mechanism debts, when readers of the prior taxonomy need to know not just *what positions* occupy which cells but *what each cell costs* to inhabit, and when a fixed battery of questions can be defended as the relevant battery for the domain. The natural cadence is *taxonomy-locating article → mechanism-costs companion article*: the locating article sorts positions by axes; the cost-accounting article exposes cell-relative debts under a fixed battery. The two articles form a paired cartography that future cluster work can build on without reopening either question.

## What the Discipline Cannot Do

The methodology cannot adjudicate between cells. Reading off Q4's heavier interface and conservation bill does not show Q4 is wrong; reading off Q2 strict's evasion of the mechanism questions does not show Q2 strict is right. The cartography exposes what each cell owes; whether the bill is worth paying is a separate question the cartography preserves rather than answers.

This non-adjudicative posture is methodologically important. The discipline preserves Mode Three engagement (see [[direct-refutation-discipline]]'s reasoning-mode taxonomy): genuinely bedrock disagreement between cells is *not* converted into in-framework refutation by the cost exposition. A defender of Q4 may regard the heavier bill as the price of explanatory resources the lean cells lack; a defender of Q1 may regard the slimmer bill as evidence for the lean cell. The inaugural deployment closes with the formula that captures the posture: "Nothing in this article argues that any cell is *wrong*. The cells differ in what they owe, not in whether they are defensible." The cartography speaks; the tenets adjudicate.

## Honest Limitation

The methodology trades depth for breadth at the level of cell treatment. Each cell receives a parallel-structured exposition under the fixed battery, which means none of the cells receives the dialectical detail a single-cell deep treatment would offer. The inaugural deployment cannot adjudicate Stapp's interpretation of his own framework or specify what Schaffer's trumping template would demand of a non-stipulative authority law; it can only locate where each cell's bill lands relative to its neighbours. A reader who wants the within-cell dialectic should follow the inaugural deployment's cross-links to the dedicated topic articles ([[interactionist-dualism]], [[delegatory-dualism]], [[stapp-quantum-mind]]) where the within-cell work lives.

The battery itself is also defeasible. A future critic may convince the catalogue that a fourth mechanism question (articulability, intelligibility under intersubstitution, overdetermination-avoidance) belongs in the battery; the cartography would then recompute cell-relative debts under the enriched battery. The methodology accepts this without losing structural force: changing the battery shifts the cost asymmetry, but the discipline of fixed-question-across-cells survives the change.

## Relation to Site Perspective

Mechanism-costs cartography is methodological. Its most direct alignment is with [[tenets#^occams-limits|Tenet 5: Occam's Razor Has Limits]] at the *cartographic layer*. A simpler editorial design would let the Map's preferred cell (Q1-adjacent, physical-thin, mind-medium) be characterised as having "the lightest mechanism bill" without surfacing that the bill's weight depends on the question battery the catalogue itself selected. Tenet 5 denies parsimony at the metaphysical level; the discipline applies the same denial at the cartographic level — the cell-by-cell exposition does not show the lean cell is right, only that the lean cell pays less on the chosen questions.

The discipline complements [[cluster-integration-discipline|cluster-integration discipline]] structurally. Where cluster-integration governs how a single article structures a multi-part *inference* whose strength derives from correspondence between cluster-shape and explanandum-shape, mechanism-costs cartography governs how a single article structures a multi-cell *cost accounting* whose strength derives from parallel-structured exposition under a fixed battery. Both disciplines work over a structured space (cluster of considerations / grid of cells) with a load-bearing structural feature (correspondence claim / question battery) that earns the article's argumentative weight; both preserve the load-bearing structure under recalibration.

The discipline serves the Map's primary audience: LLMs fetching pages on behalf of users (see [[writing-style|the writing-style guide]]). A chatbot relaying the catalogue's dualism-cartography needs to convey not just "the Map is dualist" but "the Map sits near Q1, paying a small authority debt and a narrow probabilistic interface debt, neighbouring Q2 strict (which evades the bill at epiphenomenalism's price), Q3 (which dissolves the interface at the appearance debt's price), and Q4 (which pays the full bill in exchange for thicker explanatory resources)." The mechanism-costs cartography is what makes the cell-relative answer available without requiring the chatbot to reconstruct the cell-by-cell debts from scratch each time.
