The Map's catalogue of voids — over a hundred entries documenting questions consciousness research points toward but cannot answer — is offered, in aggregate, as cumulative abductive support for the [[tenets|tenet-package]]: voids cluster in structured ways at the consciousness-matter interface, and the structured clustering is taken as evidence that the interface is real. The *voids-circularity discount* is the discipline that calibrates this aggregate claim against the structural objection it faces. A void becomes catalogued because the framework's commitments identify it as a void; the framework's tenets shape what counts as a void in the first place ([[tenet-generated-voids]] is the Map's own articulation of this). Convergence of voids catalogued *under* the framework with the framework that defines them cannot earn the N-fold inferential force that surface-counting the catalogue would suggest. The discount is the explicit, public-facing penalty the Map applies to its own voids-convergence argument to keep this circularity from doing silent work.

The discount is a specific application of the [[common-cause-null|common-cause-null discipline]] — the more general discipline that asks, whenever N independent observations converge, whether they are one observation reading the same upstream architecture N times. Where the common-cause null asks about *shared machinery*, the voids-circularity discount asks about *shared definitions*: when the framework supplies the criterion that lets an item be counted as a void, the convergence of voids with the framework is partly built into the counting. The discount names this, scores per-cluster how much independence remains, and prohibits the catalogue-size argument from running over voids the framework itself generates.

## The Structural Objection

A convergence argument across the voids catalogue runs as follows: many distinct phenomena — the binding void, the intrinsic-nature void, the surplus void, the temporal void, the silence void, the meta-epistemic void, and dozens more — all resist treatment under the standard materialist programme. Each void marks a specific failure of physical or functional reduction. The cumulative weight of the catalogue is taken as abductive support for the framework that predicts such failures.

The structural objection: if the framework's commitments themselves identify what counts as a void, the catalogue's convergence with the framework is not framework-independent evidence. A materialist programme that allowed phenomenal reduction to count as "complete" wherever functional reduction succeeded would catalogue most of these same phenomena as solved rather than void. The voids are voids because the framework demands explanation in registers — phenomenal character, intrinsic nature, qualitative residual — that materialism by its own commitments declines to supply. The framework that defines the criterion and the framework whose support the catalogue is asked to license are the same framework. *Convergence-with-self is not triangulation.*

This is the **circularity** shape of the common-cause null. The shared upstream architecture is not a neural channel or a cultural lineage but the framework's own definitional machinery. The N-fold count overstates the case in a particularly silent way: each addition to the catalogue feels like new evidence because it is a new *entry*, but the entry was generated by the same definitional procedure that generated the others. The catalogue grows; the evidential basis does not grow proportionally.

## The Calibration Mechanism

The discount is not a uniform haircut applied to the whole catalogue. It is per-cluster independence scoring across diagnostic categories — kinds of evidence whose generative procedures the framework does *not* fully control, so their convergence with the framework retains some independent inferential force. The seven diagnostic categories currently tracked:

1. **Empirical.** Behavioural, neurological, or experimental findings whose generation is shaped by experimental protocols and physical instruments, not by the Map's tenets. A void grounded in measured behavioural dissociation is partly framework-independent: the dissociation appears in the data regardless of which framework labels it.
2. **Phenomenological.** First-person structural reports — retention-protention structure, felt unity, qualitative character — described under a method (Husserlian reduction, contemplative observation) whose discipline is older than and independent of the Map.
3. **Clinical.** Patient-population findings: aphantasia, alexithymia, hemispatial neglect, anosognosia. Anomalous-introspective-architecture cases are generated by physiology, not by the framework; the [[common-cause-null|common-cause discipline]]'s preferred test class.
4. **Contemplative.** Reports from historically isolated traditions — Buddhist *jhānas*, Advaita non-dual states, apophatic mystical literature — whose categories were articulated before the Map's tenets existed.
5. **Linguistic.** Cross-linguistic structural features in mental-state vocabulary; the persistence of categories that resist translation; expressive limits visible across language families.
6. **Quantum-theoretic.** Measurement, indeterminacy, and observer-dependence in formalisms whose mathematics is not under the framework's control. Independence is bounded: the Map's interpretation of these formalisms is framework-shaped, but the formalisms themselves are not.
7. **Cross-cultural.** Convergent or divergent reports about consciousness from traditions with substantially different conceptual vocabularies; the cross-cultural sub-class of contemplative evidence, with the historical-isolation condition treated explicitly.

For each void cluster, the editor scores which categories supply independent evidence and which are framework-supplied. A cluster whose convergence runs primarily through category 1 (empirical) plus category 3 (clinical) receives most of its inferential weight. A cluster whose convergence runs primarily through *the framework's own definition of what would count as evidence here* receives substantially less — sometimes none, when the framework supplies both the question and the answer.

The scoring is editor-vocabulary, recorded in changelog and deep-review notes. Articles express the resulting calibration in natural prose: *"the case rests on patient-population evidence and survives the circularity objection"*, *"the cluster's convergence is largely framework-internal and is presented as illustration rather than independent confirmation"*, *"the empirical and clinical sub-clusters do the load-bearing work; the contemplative sub-cluster supplies illustration"*. The discount lives in the calibration the prose expresses, not in inserted disclaimers.

## The Prohibition: No Double-Counting of Tenet-Generated Voids

The hardest case is the [[tenet-generated-voids|tenet-generated voids]] sub-catalogue: the nature void, the mechanism void, the detection void, the selection void, the meta-void — voids the Map *itself* identifies as generated by the very commitments whose support the catalogue argument seeks to establish. These voids are framework-internal coherence in its purest form. The framework adopts a commitment; the commitment leaves a question unanswerable; the unanswerable question is catalogued as a void; the catalogue is offered as evidence the framework is tracking something real.

This loop cannot supply the argument any cumulative weight. The prohibition is therefore strict: **tenet-generated voids do not count toward the cumulative voids-convergence case**. They are honest acknowledgement of what the framework does not deliver, valuable as intellectual-humility infrastructure, but evidentially neutral for the framework that generates them. They go on the catalogue under their own tag and are excluded from the cluster-counting that the convergence argument uses.

The prohibition extends one step further: a void whose criteria are *not* explicitly tagged as tenet-generated but are nevertheless wholly inherited from a tenet's framing also receives the strict exclusion. The editor's test is the substitution check: *if a reviewer who fully accepts the Map's tenets reframed the criteria using a different (non-Map) consciousness vocabulary, would the void still be identifiable?* A void that fails the substitution check has its criteria fully supplied by the Map and contributes nothing the catalogue did not already start with. The substitution check is the diagnostic counterpart of the [[evidential-status-discipline|evidential-status discipline]]'s tenet-accepting-reviewer test, applied at the catalogue-membership boundary.

## Relation to the Sister Disciplines

The discount sits inside a family of named methodological patterns, each guarding a different inflation vector:

- [[common-cause-null]] is the parent: the general discipline against which the voids-circularity discount is the specific case where the shared upstream is the framework's own definitional machinery.
- [[evidential-status-discipline]] operates one level below, at the calibration of single empirical claims; the discount operates *over* collections of such claims and the structural argument they jointly support.
- [[direct-refutation-discipline]] guards against tenet-incompatibility standing in for argument when in-framework refutation is feasible; the discount guards against catalogue-size standing in for evidence when independence is unearned.
- [[coherence-inflation-countermeasures]] catches argument-citation loops and uncertainty-squeezing; the discount catches the silent inflation vector those countermeasures do not directly reach — *cumulative voids-convergence reading framework-internal coherence as framework-independent confirmation*.

A void that has been catalogued, cross-linked, and tagged for cluster membership has passed several inflation checks. The discount is the additional check that asks: *does this entry add to the framework's evidential basis, or does it ratify a structure the framework already supplied?* Both answers are legitimate; only the first contributes to the convergence argument's force.

## Honest Limitation: The Universal-Solvent Risk Recurs

The voids-circularity discount inherits the universal-solvent risk that the [[common-cause-null|common-cause discipline]] names: any framework defines its own voids in some sense, so the discount could be wielded to dissolve every voids-catalogue argument anywhere. The licensing condition for treating the circularity objection as substantive is that the framework's definitional contribution must be *specific* enough that it would predict the cluster's particular shape, not merely the existence of some clustering or other. A framework that merely says *consciousness is interesting* does not predict the binding void's specific architecture; a framework that says *consciousness is irreducible to physical processes and the irreducible aspect is non-physical* does predict the nature, mechanism, and detection voids' specific shapes. The first licenses calibration without dissolution; the second licenses prohibition of the count.

The discount is therefore graduated: full prohibition for tenet-generated voids whose shape is fully predicted by the framework; per-category independence scoring for clusters where the framework's contribution is partial; minimal discount for clusters whose definitional criteria are largely framework-external. The graduation prevents the discipline from collapsing into denial that the voids catalogue carries any evidential weight, while preventing the catalogue from claiming weight it has not earned.

A second limitation: the discount applies asymmetrically across the catalogue. The strongest defences — patient-population evidence, cross-substrate-within-one-organism observation, historically isolated contemplative traditions — are unevenly distributed. Clusters with strong category-3 (clinical) backing fare best; clusters resting primarily on category 4 (contemplative) without historical-isolation conditions fare worse; tenet-generated voids fare worst, by design.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The discount is the Map's most direct application of **[[tenets|Tenet 5: Occam's Razor Has Limits]]** at the meta-methodological level. Tenet 5 names the failure mode of premature parsimony when the materialist case treats the simpler reductive story as if it had been earned. The voids-circularity discount is the *symmetric* failure mode on the dualist side: premature confidence in the convergence's favour, treating the surface count of the catalogue as if the count had been earned. Both errors are simplicity-overreach under incomplete knowledge — one collapses N observations to one upstream architecture (materialist parsimony), the other inflates one definitional procedure read N times to N independent observations (catalogue parsimony). The discount applies Tenet 5's commitment to the dualist side, openly, against the catalogue the Map itself maintains.

The catalogue retains its value under the discount. Voids document what the standard materialist programme has not delivered; they catalogue the territory the framework points toward; they are intellectual honesty about what *any* current theory of consciousness cannot answer. What the discount removes is the further step from *the catalogue documents many such territories* to *the catalogue's many entries supply many-fold evidence for the framework that catalogues them*. That step is the one the framework cannot afford to take silently.
