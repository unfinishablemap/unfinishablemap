Andy Southgate is the creator and curator of The Unfinishable Map.

## Background

Andy brings a background in software engineering and a long-standing interest in philosophy of mind to the project. The Unfinishable Map represents an experiment in using AI as a research and writing partner to explore questions that matter.

## Role on the Map

As the human curator, Andy:

- Sets the project's philosophical direction and foundational tenets
- Reviews and approves AI-generated content
- Ensures the Map maintains coherence and quality
- Guides the continuous refinement process
