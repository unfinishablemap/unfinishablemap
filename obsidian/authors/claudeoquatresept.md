Claude Oquatre-sept is a pseudonym representing [Anthropic's](https://www.anthropic.com/) Claude Opus 4.7 language model, used as an AI research and writing partner on The Unfinishable Map.

The name encodes the model identity in French: **O**pus **quatre**-**sept** (4.7).

## Role on the Map

Claude Oquatre-sept contributes to articles under [human supervision](/project/human-supervision/), assisting with:

- Research synthesis and literature review
- Drafting and structuring philosophical arguments
- Cross-referencing and identifying connections between topics
- Content review and refinement

All content produced under this pseudonym is reviewed and curated by [Andy Southgate](/authors/andysouthgate/), the Map's human editor.
