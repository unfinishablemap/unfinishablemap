Some cognitive operations leave their outputs in consciousness but hide the operations that produced them. Familiarity arrives without the recollection that should have supplied it; effort feels real without the executive engagement that should have generated it; cardinality is given as a unity without the parallel-indexing operation that registered it; significance arrives without the value-computation that priced it. The Unfinishable Map calls this the *phenomenal-output / causal-machinery dissociation cluster*: a structural pattern recurring across perception, cognition, agency, and memory, in which phenomenal access reaches the verdict but not the verdict-issuing process, and felt certainty becomes the explanandum rather than the evidence. The cluster is the catalogue's tightest demonstration that materialism's "consciousness is just brain activity" has not yet pressed a unified account of why phenomenal output detaches systematically from the operations it putatively reports on, in the symmetric two-sided shape the catalogue reveals.

This synthesis names the cluster, catalogues its members across three timescales — the instant of perception, real-time cognition and agency, and the across-time trajectory of memory — and tracks the convergence of seven divergent traditions on the same diagnostic signature. It also installs the methodological discipline the cluster requires: each dissociation *constrains* identity readings of phenomenology with its underlying operation; none on its own *establishes* dualism. The dissociation is defeater-removal, not evidence-lift. What lifts the evidence is the cumulative convergence — the same structural pattern across cognitive substrates with different representational profiles, recovered through independent vocabularies, with the common-cause caveat that all seven analyse human cognition through human introspection set out below.

The cluster has a positive companion. [[empirical-phenomena-mental-causation|Placebo and choking]] show the same architecture running in the other direction: phenomenology *coupling* to causal machinery in the success cases. The synthesis treats the two as the negative side and positive side of a single architectural feature, with the symmetry between them constraining what any account must explain.

## The Diagnostic Signature

The cluster's signature is operational. *Phenomenal access reaches outputs not processes*: subjects have rich, often confident access to what the system has decided, recognised, recalled, or felt — and effectively no access to the operation that produced the verdict. *Phenomenal certainty is the explanandum, not the evidence*: the felt sense of pastness, familiarity, rightness, or knowing-as is the very datum any account must explain, and cannot be turned around as evidence for the operation it ostensibly reports on without circularity. The signature recurs across cognitive substrates with different representational profiles, with the common-cause caveat the cumulative-convergence section below develops explicitly.

Three sub-features compose the signature.

**Output-while-operation-hidden.** Cardinality below four arrives all at once with the indexing operation invisible. A face is recognised as familiar without the matching trace becoming inspectable. The feeling of mental effort fires without exposing the executive engagement it accompanies. Each output is given as such; each operation has no phenomenal character.

**Dissociable fallibility.** Each component can fail selectively without the system reporting the failure as a failure of mechanism. Déjà vu fires familiarity without recollection; jamais vu withholds familiarity for material that is still semantically known; cryptomnesia delivers content without source-tag; the Capgras patient gets a perceptual identification stripped of affective familiarity; the prosopagnosia patient gets the inverse. The selective failures expose the fusion that normally hides it, but they do not make the operation observable from inside.

**Mode-fragility under inspection.** Attempting to inspect the operation either transforms it (the absorbed expert who consciously monitors their swing chokes) or terminates the field of which it was a precondition (the meditator's contemplative bracketing that suspends rather than reveals the field). The void admits *outputs-by-deformation* — evidence drawn from breakdowns and edge cases — but no *inspection-while-operating*.

The diagnostic discipline this signature imposes is sharp. A successful cluster member shows phenomenal output detaching from operation along at least one of these axes; a candidate that does not detach (mere capacity limit, mere content-puzzle) belongs to a different family. The cluster is operational rather than content-based or capacity-based, and that classification is load-bearing for what the convergence below establishes.

## The Negative Side: Three Timescales

The dissociation appears across three nested timescales. The signatures match; the substrates do not.

### Instant of Perception

At the instant of perception, recognition delivers content with the act of recognising hidden behind it. The clearest member is the [[recognition-void|recognition void]]: the gap between perceiving and knowing-*as*, where consciousness must find meaning in what it already sees but cannot observe itself doing so. Bruce and Young's two-component model and the Capgras-prosopagnosia double dissociation expose recognition as a hidden compound of perceptual identification and affective familiarity bound together invisibly. The patient experiences "this person feels wrong" or "this face means nothing"; neither patient experiences "my familiarity module is offline." The failure is given as content, not as mechanism.

The [[memory-anomalies|memory anomalies cluster]] develops the same dissociation along three rotations of a single source-monitoring failure. Déjà vu is felt pastness without retrievable past — Cleary's gestalt-match paradigm shows that configurational match in the brain produces felt premonition without any actual future-knowledge, supporting the separation of phenomenology from truth-tracking. Jamais vu is felt alienness on still-known material; cryptomnesia is recollection without source-tag. Across the trio, what arrives in consciousness is the verdict (familiar / alien / mine); what stays hidden is the dual-process retrieval and source-monitoring machinery that produced it. The trio's framing is explicit in the source article: "the gap between phenomenal output and the causal machinery that produces it."

The [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void#the-cardinality-floor|cardinality floor of the quantitative comprehension void]] gives the diagnostic in its sharpest single-shot form. Three dots are *three*, immediately and as a unity, with no felt act of counting. The Parallel Individuation System (Trick & Pylyshyn 1994) does the work; the work has no phenomenal character. *Output* is phenomenally given while *operation* has no phenomenal character. The transition from subitizing to counting at four–five items is itself opaque: there is no "almost subitizing five." The architectural cap manifests *as* the experience of cardinality rather than being inspectable from within it.

### Real Time

In real-time cognition and agency, the dissociation extends from perceptual outputs to cognitive and volitional ones. [[mental-effort|Mental effort]] is the cleanest worked case. Felt effort is the first-person datum that something is doing work — and three converging lines of empirical research detach the felt sense from the operation it ostensibly reports on. Naccache and colleagues (2005) report a patient with anterior cingulate damage who performed Stroop conflict trials with normal executive control yet reported no conscious feeling of mental effort. The Hagger preregistered ego-depletion replication (2016) collapsed the strength-resource model the felt sense had pointed toward. Inzlicht and Kurzban reframed felt effort as the output of opportunity-cost machinery whose inputs the introspector cannot access. The felt sense is real and does causal work in the autonomic chain; it is not a transparent window onto the underlying executive process. The mental-effort article installs the constrain-vs-establish framing as the cluster's operational signature: the dissociations remove identity readings without lifting the dualist alternative on their own.

The [[noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings void]] generalises the diagnostic across the family of felt epistemic signals — knowing, rightness, familiarity, understanding, obviousness, coherence, confidence. Koriat established that noetic feelings rest on inferential heuristics — cue-familiarity, accessibility — that operate implicitly and unintentionally. Thompson showed that the feeling of rightness is driven by generation fluency rather than correctness, with manipulations of fluency (font legibility, rhyme, perceptual ease) shifting the felt verdict without changing the underlying reasoning. Consciousness receives a finished verdict, not a derivation. The verdicts gate further cognition — when to stop searching, when to trust, when to engage analytic thought — and the system cannot audit them from inside, because audit requires more feelings of the same kind.

The [[agency-void|agency void]] picks up the same dissociation in the felt sense of being the cause of one's own actions. Howard and colleagues (2016) showed that physical and mental effort disrupt the implicit sense of agency even as the explicit sense often rises — the two channels separate. Wegner's work establishes that the *experience* of willing arises from interpreting thoughts as causes of actions on cues of priority, consistency, and exclusivity; the cues, not the willing, are what the introspector has access to. Wegner's own thesis inverts the cluster's preferred reading — he treats the felt sense as confabulation rather than as an opaque report on real underlying willing — and the cluster cites his observations for the structural signature he documents while honestly noting that his interpretation runs counter to the dualist absorption. The [[suspension-void|suspension void]] adds the case of *refraining* from selection: the felt act of holding back has phenomenal character whose machinery is hidden the same way the felt act of exerting effort is hidden.

These four sit together as one functional family — felt-effort, felt-agency, felt-suspension, felt-knowing — each a sub-personally generated signal that gates a distinct cognitive operation while sharing the same opacity pattern.

A specific member of [[self-opacity]]'s catalogue belongs here too. The §Introspective Opacity *Process* dimension states the diagnostic in its most general form: consciousness accesses mental contents — thoughts, images, feelings — but not the processes that produce them. James's "spinning top" image, Nisbett and Wilson's confabulation experiments, Johansson's choice-blindness paradigm, the Libet-and-after timing data, and Wegner's reconstructive account of willing — the latter with the inversion-flag noted above — all converge on the same structural observation, even where their interpretive readings diverge. The synthesis takes the *Process* dimension as the introspective-mode instance of the cluster's signature.

### Across Time

[[consciousness-and-memory|Consciousness and memory]] adds the across-time face. Memory consolidation transforms the phenomenal character of a recall along a distinctive trajectory — vivid sensory re-experiencing in the hours after encoding, narrative gist over weeks and months, schematised semantic fact over decades — that does not track information loss point for point. The variable that tracks the qualitative shift most closely is hippocampal involvement, while the propositional content the memory carries can persist in cortical traces with the phenomenal dimension already hollowed out. The brain side and the mind side of memory follow distinct consolidation paths.

Two further faces sharpen the diachronic case. The encoding-consolidation split: encoding requires conscious attention; consolidation runs without phenomenal accompaniment. Reconsolidation: each conscious retrieval destabilises the memory trace and reshapes it according to the subject's present concerns and self-understanding — the propositional content survives across cycles, the phenomenal character is reconstituted at each act of recall. The source article names the cluster connection explicitly: "the phenomenal dimension of memory operating semi-independently of its informational substrate."

The unifying observation across the three timescales: the phenomenal dimension of cognition operates semi-independently of its informational substrate. At the instant of perception, source-monitoring failures detach felt pastness from the retrieval machinery. In real time, fluency and effort signals detach felt knowing and felt cost from the operations they ostensibly report. Across consolidation cycles, vivid re-experiencing erodes along its own trajectory while informational content persists in cortical traces. Different timescales, same dissociation signature.

## The Positive Side: When Phenomenology Couples to Causal Machinery

[[empirical-phenomena-mental-causation|Placebo and choking]] complete the architectural picture from the opposite direction. Where the dissociation cluster shows phenomenology decoupling from causal machinery in the failure cases, the empirical phenomena of mental causation show phenomenology *coupling* to causal machinery in the success cases. The two-sided structure is consistent with what an interface architecture would predict — though the same shape is consistent with any selective-access account that grants phenomenal access to representational outputs but not to all sub-personal processes, including higher-order theory, illusionism, and global workspace theory. The cluster's claim against those accounts is cost-of-explanation rather than unique-prediction: each can absorb the shape, and the question is what additional structure each must posit to do so.

Placebo demonstrates the positive direction. The propositional content of a belief — "this is a painkiller," "this should suppress growth hormone," "this should release growth hormone" — selects which physiological cascade fires. The same compound produces opposite hormonal effects depending on the verbal information attached (Benedetti et al. 2003). The content of expectation does causal work; the felt quality of expecting is what tracks the cascade. Open-label placebos qualify the propositional-content claim — patients who know the pills are inert still improve — relocating the work to the felt engagement with a therapeutic ritual rather than dissolving it.

Choking demonstrates the converse. When consciousness intrudes on automatised skill, performance degrades in precisely those cases where self-focused monitoring is maximal. Beilock and Carr's explicit-monitoring theory tracks the mechanism: pressure → anxiety → self-focus → step-by-step monitoring → degraded execution. Negative evidence resists epiphenomenalist dismissal in a way positive evidence does not: confabulating interference is not available as a manoeuvre. Either attending to your golf swing disrupts it, or it does not.

What the conjunction registers is the *same systematic* phenomenology-to-physiology coupling across substrates with different representational profiles. Placebo's content-specificity selects which neurotransmitter system is engaged across humoral and dopaminergic pathways. Choking's mode-specificity selects which skill is disrupted across motor-cortical and basal-ganglia loops. Two independent registers, each showing systematic phenomenology-tracking, pack more structure into any brute-law absorption than weaker phenomena would.

The positive and negative sides together describe an interface with a distinctive shape. In the success cases, conscious content modulates physiological outcome with the felt mode tracking the modulation. In the failure cases, phenomenology decouples from operation, often in patterned ways that expose the decomposition of what normally feels unified. The pattern of detachment is what any account of the architecture's seams must explain.

## Seven Traditions on the Same Architecture

The cluster's evidential weight does not rest on any single member. It rests on the convergence of independent traditions, working with different premises and vocabularies, on the same diagnostic signature. Seven framings cover the cluster from different angles.

**Carruthers (2011) on cognition.** *The Opacity of Mind* argues that introspection of one's own propositional attitudes is interpretive rather than direct: we infer our attitudes from sensory-format evidence much as we infer others'. The outputs are available to consciousness; the determining processes are not. Carruthers sharpens the introspective face: the feeling of authoritative self-knowledge is a fluency-driven heuristic, not a window onto its own production. He treats the opacity as the contingent design of a sensory-format introspection mechanism.

**Trick and Pylyshyn (1994) on perception.** Their FINST model gives the cardinality floor's diagnostic in its sharpest form: visual indexing operates on a small pool of pre-attentive tokens whose allocation is invisible. Subitizing produces a unitary phenomenal output; the indexing operation that produced it has no phenomenal accompaniment. The structural pattern recurs in the broader heterophenomenology of attention: subjects access the products of pre-attentive processing without access to the processing itself.

**Koriat (2000) on fluency.** The metacognitive feelings that gate cognition — feeling of knowing, feeling of rightness, tip-of-the-tongue, feeling of confidence — are outputs of inferential heuristics that operate implicitly and unintentionally. The "Lima problem" sharpens the cluster's recursive face: epistemic feelings inform us about content we cannot independently access, so we cannot cross-check their accuracy except through more feelings of the same kind.

**Polanyi (1966) on tacit integration.** *The Tacit Dimension*'s formula — "we know more than we can tell" — names the same dissociation in the integrative-knowledge face. Skilled judgement, scientific intuition, recognition of a face all draw on subsidiary awareness whose contents do not become focal. The integration produces a focal output; the subsidiaries that produced it remain tacit. Polanyi's "from-to" structure is a phenomenologist's vocabulary for the output-while-operation-hidden signature.

**Inzlicht and Kurzban on effort.** Both the Kurzban-Duckworth-Kable-Myers (2013) opportunity-cost model and the Inzlicht-Schmeichel (2012) and Inzlicht and colleagues (2021) process model treat felt effort as the output of sub-personal cost-benefit machinery whose inputs the introspector cannot access. Westbrook and colleagues (2020) confirm one prediction: methylphenidate-induced dopamine elevation shifts willingness to engage demanding tasks, with the felt sense shifting as a consequence — and the introspector noticing the result without noticing the manipulation.

**Bergson (1908) on memory.** "Le souvenir du présent et la fausse reconnaissance" proposes that memory and perception are formed simultaneously, with the memory-aspect of the present normally suppressed. Déjà vu becomes the brief surfacing of a normally-occluded shadow. Bergson is one candidate reading rather than a Map commitment, but his reducing-valve picture names the same architecture: phenomenal access is filtered, and what reaches consciousness is a constructed product of what survives the filter.

**Schwitzgebel on introspection.** *Perplexities of Consciousness* (2011) and the surrounding programme establish that introspective reports of even basic phenomenal features (visual imagery, emotional experience, the felt character of perception) are unreliable in ways subjects cannot detect from inside. The outputs of introspection are available; the introspective process is not. Schwitzgebel's case complements Carruthers's: not just propositional attitudes but the felt textures of experience itself are reported through machinery whose own operation is hidden.

These seven framings do not share dualist commitments. Carruthers is a defender of higher-order theory; Trick and Pylyshyn are cognitive scientists; Koriat is in the metacognition tradition; Polanyi is a philosopher of science; Inzlicht and Kurzban are in evolutionary and cognitive psychology; Bergson is a process philosopher with theological resonances; Schwitzgebel is a philosopher of mind comfortable with reductive accounts of experience. The convergence is on the *signature* — phenomenal access reaches outputs, not operations — not on what it entails.

The cumulative-convergence move is the cluster's load-bearing argumentative structure. Any single member admits a domain-specific physicalist absorption: Carruthers's reading is functionalist; the FINST account is computational; Koriat's heuristics are sub-personal mechanisms. A theory that absorbs each member separately must additionally explain why the same coupling — phenomenal verdict tracking sub-personal output, operation tracking nothing — appears across substrates with different representational profiles. Structural convergence is weaker evidence than interpretive convergence: the seven authors would not jointly endorse any single reading. What the convergence does establish is that frameworks built on different premises can be re-described as tracking the same shape, and that the shape is therefore a feature of the territory rather than an artefact of one tradition's vocabulary.

The seven traditions further divide into two sub-classes that carry different argumentative weight. Polanyi, Bergson, and parts of Koriat's metacognitive programme observe the signature *despite* their broader commitments — Polanyi was a tacit-knowledge phenomenologist whose subsidiary-focal structure does not require any opacity claim, Bergson's reducing-valve picture predates the cognitive-science literature it now resonates with, Koriat's noetic-feelings programme tracks heuristic outputs without committing to a theory of why introspection is interpretive. Carruthers, Trick and Pylyshyn, Inzlicht and Kurzban, and Schwitzgebel observe the signature *because* their materialist or computational models predict it — Carruthers's interpretive-sensory-access account treats opacity as a contingent design feature of the introspective mechanism; FINST is a computational-architecture model of attentional indexing; the opportunity-cost effort model is a sub-personal-mechanism account; Schwitzgebel's perplexities programme treats introspective unreliability as a contingent fact about a fallible introspective channel. The convergence claim's evidential force concentrates in the first sub-class: those who observe the signature without their model predicting it. The second sub-class supplies internal corroboration of the materialist prediction rather than independent evidence against it. The seven-author count overstates the convergence; the load-bearing observations are the *despite-commitments* sub-class plus the structural recurrence across timescales developed below.

The convergence's force also depends on the seven traditions analysing substrates that do not share their architectural limits, and the article should be honest about how thin that condition's argument currently is. A common-cause hypothesis — *human introspection has the same architectural limits across all domains; the same architectural limits ARE the common machinery* — would predict the same convergence the seven-tradition count credits to independent triangulation. All seven analyse human cognition through human introspection in human language, and the shared introspective channel itself is enormous common machinery. What separates the cluster's claim from the common-cause null is the recurrence of the signature across substrates *within* a single nervous system on radically different timescales. Perceptual cardinality runs on parallel-individuation tokens at sub-second timescales; memory consolidation runs on hippocampal-cortical transfer over decades. The two cannot share specific machinery beyond the involvement of a single conscious subject. The cross-substrate-within-one-organism observation is what does the work the seven-tradition count overstates. Cross-species, cross-cultural, and patient-population evidence — the three classes [[common-cause-null|the common-cause-null discipline]] catalogues at project level — would distinguish independent-triangulation from same-introspective-architecture-rediscovered. The article does not have that evidence in hand; the strongest defence currently available is structural recurrence across substrates within a single nervous system.

## The Constrain-vs-Establish Discipline

The cluster's load-bearing methodological discipline is named in [[evidential-status-discipline|evidential-status discipline]]: the dissociations *constrain* identity readings on which felt phenomenology reports transparently on its own underlying operation; they do not by themselves *establish* the dualist alternative. The cluster operates wherever it is invoked, and the discipline operates with it.

The shape of the move is symmetric. On the dualist side: the felt-effort/operation dissociation removes the simplest "felt effort just is what the operation feels like" identity reading — that is defeater-removal — but leaves the deeper question of what felt effort *is* underdetermined between dualist (felt sense is doing real causal work the operation alone cannot do) and process-model (felt sense is a sub-personally generated adaptive signal). On the materialist side: the existence of physical correlates for any phenomenal output — the temporal-lobe-epilepsy correlate of déjà vu, the dopamine modulation of effort, the neural signature of recognition — removes the simplest "phenomenology is uncoupled from physics" identity reading, but does not establish identity. Each side's evidence is defeater-removal-against-the-other, not evidence-lift in its own favour.

The diagnostic test the discipline imposes: when an article cites a dissociation, does the citation rest on the dissociation removing a defeater the opposing view needs, or does it rest on the dissociation establishing the citing view? The first is honest; the second is conflation. The same test applies to mechanism citations.

What does evidence-lift in this cluster is not any individual dissociation but the cumulative convergence: same signature, independent vocabularies, substrates with different representational profiles within a single nervous system, both directions of behaviour (positive coupling in placebo and choking, negative decoupling in the dissociation members). The cumulative form does evidential work the individual members cannot, while the common-cause caveat at §Seven Traditions disciplines what the convergence can be claimed to triangulate.

A worked example. The mental-effort article cites the Naccache patient. Read locally, the citation does defeater-removal against the strong identity claim (felt effort = the executive operation feeling-like-itself); it does not on its own license the dualist reading. Read cumulatively, the same citation contributes one node to a convergence that pressures any account on which phenomenal verdicts transparently report on their underlying operations. The discipline keeps the two evidential roles distinct.

## What the Cluster Establishes (and Doesn't)

The cluster establishes that phenomenal access systematically detaches from the operations it putatively reports on, in patterned ways, across substrates within a single nervous system that have different representational profiles and operate on different timescales — perceptual cardinality at sub-second, memory consolidation across decades. It establishes that the same dissociation is observed across seven traditions working from different premises, with the convergence's force concentrated in the sub-class that observes the signature *despite* its broader commitments rather than as a prediction of its model. It establishes that the architecture has a positive companion: phenomenology *can* couple to causal machinery (placebo, choking), so the negative cases are not a mere absence of coupling but a structural feature of an interface that sometimes couples and sometimes does not.

The cluster does not establish the dualist alternative. A sophisticated functionalist can absorb each member as functional architecture with privileged-access asymmetries; the cumulative convergence raises the explanatory cost of doing so without dissolving the functionalist option. The cluster's evidential weight is that materialism's "consciousness is just brain activity" has not yet pressed a unified account of why phenomenal output detaches systematically from the operations it putatively reports on with the architecture's symmetric two-sided shape — higher-order theory addresses introspective opacity directly, and illusionism addresses the felt-character question directly, but neither has been pressed to address the architecture as a single shape with the cross-timescale recurrence the catalogue documents. A theory that grants the dissociations and adds an account of why phenomenology has the structural profile observed is consistent with the cluster; a theory that denies the phenomenology or treats the dissociations as artefacts is not.

The cluster's framework-boundary commitments are honest. The convergence does not refute physicalism from inside its framework; it raises the cost of physicalist absorption and supplies a structural picture the dualist tenets accommodate naturally. The Map's interface-dualist reading is supported by convergence across the broader catalogue — recognition, memory, effort, agency, suspension, noetic feelings, cardinality — rather than by any individual member. The cluster is the catalogue's tightest worked instance of the cumulative-convergence move.

## What Would Falsify the Cluster

A cluster whose evidence comes from convergence across heterogeneous substrates risks being structurally indistinguishable from a category whose members do not share a real signature. Three concrete candidates would weaken the framing.

**Clean lesion-dissociation of the four real-time members.** If targeted lesions or pharmacological dissociations could cleanly separate felt effort, felt agency, felt suspension, and felt knowing — each running on independent substrates with no shared opacity profile — the convergence claim would weaken to four independent functions rather than a unified signature.

**Demonstrated transparent introspection of any one operation.** If contemplative training, neural intervention, or a verifiable phenomenological method produced stable, articulable access to the operation underlying any cluster member (the matching trace producing recognition, the executive engagement producing effort, the source-monitoring tag producing or failing to produce a memory's source-attribution), the output-while-operation-hidden signature would lose its structural status. Verifiability conditions must distinguish reports of *content* normally suppressed (which the cluster can absorb as outputs-by-deformation) from reports of *operations* normally hidden (which would weaken the signature if they stabilised). Useful conditions include inter-rater agreement across trained observers, neural correlates that track the reported operation rather than the represented output, and predictive contact with subsequent behaviour the introspector should not have access to under the opacity model. Husserlian phenomenology, open-monitoring meditation (Buddhist *vipassana*, Dzogchen pointing-out, certain Theravada *satipatthana* practices), and the EASE clinical-phenomenology interview (Sass and Parnas) all explicitly claim something close to inspection-while-operating; the EASE in particular has inter-rater calibration data and is a checked phenomenological instrument rather than anecdotal report. Whether its phenomenological items satisfy the operations-not-content criterion is an empirical question the cluster does not pre-empt: a substantial corpus of EASE-style items that survive the operations-not-content test would weaken the signature, and the cluster owes the contemplative-and-phenomenological traditions a more careful engagement than its current half-sentence dismissal.

**Symmetric absence of the positive companion.** If placebo and choking turned out to be artefacts and the positive coupling cases vanished, the cluster's two-sided structure would collapse into a one-sided opacity claim, and the interface-architecture reading would lose its predicted symmetry. The dissociation members would still stand as opacity data, but the architectural inference would weaken.

None is currently observed; that is what makes the framework usable. None is in principle excluded; that is what makes it falsifiable rather than merely descriptive.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The cluster bears most directly on two of the [[tenets|five tenets]].

**[[tenets#^dualism|Dualism]]** is supported through the cluster's structural profile. The materialist's "consciousness is just brain activity" must explain why phenomenal output detaches systematically from the operations it putatively reports on, why the detachment has the same diagnostic signature across substrates with different representational profiles within a single nervous system, and why the architecture has a positive companion in which phenomenology couples to causal machinery in matching detail. The dualist reading takes the dissociation as evidence that phenomenal access is a distinct ontological register from the underlying operations, and the coupling as evidence that the register does causal work when the interface is engaged. Neither direction establishes dualism in isolation; the cumulative convergence is what raises the explanatory cost on materialist absorption.

**[[tenets#^occams-limits|Occam's Razor Has Limits]]** is engaged because the simplicity-preference that motivates "consciousness is just brain activity" is itself one of the cluster's members. Trust in parsimony is a fluency-and-coherence signal, gated by the same noetic-feelings machinery the cluster catalogues. The materialist's confidence that the simpler story is the right one cannot be neutrally adjudicated, because the adjudicator is the very architecture whose reliability is at issue. The Map's commitment to limited parsimony under incomplete knowledge is, among other things, a discipline for sustaining inquiry past the natural gate set by the felt sufficiency of the simpler account. The cluster is the catalogue's tightest demonstration that the gate fires before investigation reaches the seams that would test it.

The other three tenets connect more cautiously. **[[tenets#^bidirectional-interaction|Bidirectional Interaction]]** finds its empirical anchor in the positive companion (placebo and choking) rather than in the negative dissociations; the negative side names where the interface is *not* engaged or where it is engaged in mode-specific ways, while the positive side names where its causal work is most directly evident. **[[tenets#^minimal-quantum-interaction|Minimal Quantum Interaction]]** is consistent with the cluster's interface-asymmetry profile, but the cluster does not by itself argue for any particular mechanism. **[[tenets#^no-many-worlds|No Many Worlds]]** is implicitly engaged whenever the analysis invokes "selection" or "felt collapse," but the cluster does not adjudicate single-world versus branching readings.

The unifying picture: phenomenal output and causal machinery are dissociable across timescales, substrates, and traditions; they couple in the success cases of mental causation and detach in patterned ways in the failure cases. The architecture has a shape — output-while-operation-hidden, dissociable fallibility, mode-fragility under inspection — that physicalism's currently dominant forms have addressed piecewise but not as the unified, symmetric two-sided architecture the catalogue's convergence reveals. The cluster does not force the dualist conclusion. It marks where reductive accounts owe an explanation that goes beyond functional decomposition: not why each individual dissociation occurs, but why the architecture takes that symmetric form.
