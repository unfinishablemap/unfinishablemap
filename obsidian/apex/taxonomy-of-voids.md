The Unfinishable Map catalogues over sixty cognitive voids—places where understanding breaks down in structured, repeatable ways. Taken individually, each might be dismissed as a puzzle awaiting a cleverer philosopher. Taken together, they tell a different story. The voids are not scattered randomly across the landscape of human inquiry. They cluster around a single boundary: the point where consciousness meets the physical world. They sort into types by *why* thought fails, into domains by *where* it fails, and into signatures by *how* it fails. This taxonomy reveals the architecture of the minds doing the mapping. The Map argues that it also reveals something about the territory being mapped—and this second move is where the argument is contested.

The thesis is qualified rather than triumphant. Physicalism has no uniform prediction here: many physicalists already expect concentrated difficulty around consciousness for reasons that do not require dualism—introspection is lossy and reconstructive, the brain is our most complex object of study, subject and object coincide, and third-person methods were not calibrated for first-person phenomena. What distinguishes the Map's claim is narrower: that the concentrated difficulty takes specific, *structured* failure signatures that persist across frameworks and converge on one interface. Under the Map's tenet-package—dualism *combined with* minimal quantum interaction and a naturally occluded interface—this structured pattern is what we should expect, and the taxonomy is consistent with that prediction. Bare dualism alone predicts nothing of the kind; the predictive work is done by the full tenet-package.

## From Three Kinds to Four

The voids programme began with a [[three-kinds-of-void|simple taxonomy]]: the Unexplored, the Unexplorable, and the Occluded. Each demands a different investigative posture.

**The Unexplored** are gaps of ignorance—territory we have not charted but could. The Map's [[topics|topics]] and [[concepts|concepts]] attempt to fill them.

**The Unexplorable** are gaps of architecture. Colin McGinn's [[mysterianism|cognitive closure]] formalises the claim: minds can be constitutionally unable to grasp certain properties. A rat is closed to calculus not from lack of motivation but absence of conceptual apparatus. Humans may be similarly closed to whatever bridges neural activity and conscious experience. The [[hard-problem-of-consciousness|hard problem]] has resisted resolution across centuries, cultures, and methodological paradigms—possibly because the answer lies outside human cognitive reach.

**The Occluded** are gaps that might be defended. The [[simulation|simulation hypothesis]] raises this most sharply: if we exist within a constructed reality, certain truths might be *engineered out* of cognitive reach. But occlusion need not require a designer—the Map's own tenets suggest consciousness-physics interaction may be systematically hidden from introspection.

The [[biological-cognitive-closure|evolved cognitive limits]] research adds a fourth category: **[[naturally-occluded|the Naturally Occluded]]**. These are blind spots maintained not by any intentional agent but by natural selection, because ignorance itself conferred fitness advantage. The [[fitness-beats-truth|Fitness-Beats-Truth theorem]] (Hoffman, Singh, and Prakash) supplies the formal anchor—perceptual strategies tuned to fitness payoffs strictly dominate those tuned to objective truth. Naturally occluded voids occupy a distinctive position: not beyond all possible cognition (only beyond *evolved* cognition), maintained by the blind watchmaker rather than any deliberate agent—limits with a *function*, potentially accessible to differently constituted minds.

The taxonomy itself evolves: the [[meta-epistemology-of-limits|natural-vs-designed axis]] adds further uncertainty, and [[tenet-generated-voids|voids the Map's own tenets generate]] show every framework creates characteristic blind spots through its foundational commitments—so a mature taxonomy must include those it generates about itself.

## Domains of Darkness

The 60+ individual voids sort into domains by *where* in cognitive space they occur. This organisation reveals which aspects of consciousness are most opaque to the minds that possess it.

### The Self-Knowledge Cluster

The densest cluster surrounds the mind's attempt to examine itself. The [[self-opacity|unobservable self]]—the subject that cannot become its own object—anchors this domain. Surrounding it: [[self-opacity|introspective opacity]] (the mechanisms of experience hidden from the experiencer), the [[observation-and-measurement-void|calibration void]] (introspection cannot be verified against any independent standard), the [[observation-and-measurement-void|observation void]] (every method of studying consciousness transforms what it studies), the [[self-opacity|self-reference paradox]], and the [[witnessing-void]] (the impossibility of witnessing *qua operation* examining itself). These voids reinforce each other, creating [[compound-failure-signatures|compound cognitive limits]]—emergent failure modes no single void predicts—because the instrument and the object are the same. This tells us something specific about what we are: beings whose cognitive architecture includes systematic opacity about its own operations.

A five-void *introspection-architecture* sub-cluster names distinct mechanisms of the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate: the [[narrative-void|narrative void]] (unobservability of the storytelling operation), the [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution void]] (absence of native origin tags), the [[noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings void]] (opacity of the gating signals licensing cognition from inside), the [[confabulation-void|confabulation void]] (a sub-personal interpreter producing fabricated explanations that carry the phenomenology of accurate report), and the [[perceptual-reality-monitoring-void|perceptual reality-monitoring void]] (deciding whether a content is perceived or imagined, inspectable only by its outputs). [[apex/introspection-architecture-void-cluster|The introspection-architecture void cluster]] develops the apex-grain treatment, naming the shared architectural signature: phenomenal-output-without-operation, with felt-rightness calibrated to the verdict rather than to the world.

The [[erasure-void|erasure void]] extends the cluster across time: consciousness cannot inventory its own deletions, because the monitoring system that would detect cognitive loss typically erodes alongside the capacity itself. Clinical anosognosia—patients with damage to both a function and its monitoring system sincerely deny their deficits—is the stark case, but the structure is universal, visible in ordinary ageing and the Dunning-Kruger dynamic. Where introspective opacity is noticed as opacity, the erasure void is not noticed at all: the world simply appears complete.

### The Phenomenological Cluster

A second major cluster concerns the qualitative character of experience. The [[binding-void|binding void]]—why unified experience arises from disparate sensory processes—resists analysis entirely. The [[valence-void|valence void]] asks why experience has a built-in good/bad dimension; the [[mattering-void|mattering void]] sits upstream, where significance operates as a transparent medium investigation cannot exit, and the [[relevance-void|relevance void]] is its operational shadow—the narrowing from everything-possible to *these things, now*, opaque because every inspection is itself a relevance-determining act. The [[intrinsic-nature-void|intrinsic nature void]] pursues what matter and consciousness are *in themselves*, finding every answer collapses into structure, negation, or circularity. The [[acquaintance-void|acquaintance void]] identifies modes of direct phenomenal contact structurally inaccessible to minds like ours. The [[imagery-void|imagery void]] specialises onto a well-evidenced sub-region: no mind can verify its own image fidelity, and post-2015 aphantasia research suggests phenomenal imagery may not be doing the cognitive work it appears to do. The [[phenomenal-quality-void|phenomenal quality void]] asks why experience arrives stamped with immediacy and realness. The [[plenitude-void|plenitude void]] asks whether experience is genuinely as rich as it seems—unanswerable from within, because every check for sparsity recruits attention and manufactures the detail it would measure. The [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void#the-cardinality-floor|cardinality floor]] of [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void|the quantitative comprehension void]] marks an architectural ceiling at roughly four items where direct givenness ends and serial counting begins (Trick & Pylyshyn 1994; Cowan 2001).

These voids concern aspects of consciousness so fundamental they are invisible in ordinary experience. The [[edge-states-and-void-probes|phenomenology of the edge]] reports paradoxical clarity at the limit—understanding something too large for words—across contemplative traditions, suggesting structure rather than practice-artefact.

The [[ineffable-encounter-void|ineffable encounter void]] complicates the cluster. Most voids are defined by *absence*; this one by *presence*—an excess of meaning that overwhelms the cognitive apparatus. Consciousness encounters something (felt significance in music, ideas, sacred moments) it registers as genuine knowing yet cannot inspect, the experience dissolving under analytical attention. Independent traditions—Gendlin's felt sense, Otto's numinous, Polanyi's [[tacit-integration-void|tacit integration]], Rosa's resonance theory—converge on the same structure. In its numinous register the void sharpens: numinous experience dismantles the evaluative mechanism needed to assess it.

Three cross-cutting axes redistribute the catalogue along dimensions orthogonal to the four-kinds typology. The [[presence-type-and-absence-type-voids|presence-type/absence-type axis]] sorts voids by the phenomenology of approach: presence-type voids—the [[aesthetic-void|aesthetic void]], [[the-surplus-void|surplus void]], [[the-silence-void|silence void]], and [[affective-void|affective void]]—register as overwhelm rather than lack, resisting direct apophatic mapping because the object is not a gap but a saturated encounter that dissolves under inspection.

The [[phenomenology-vs-function-axis|phenomenology-vs-function axis]] sorts voids by what comes apart at the boundary. The [[synesthetic-void|synesthetic void]] is, on Gray's (2003) reading, the cleanest exemplar: identical qualia from distinct functional roles, and single functional roles producing divergent qualia. The imagery void's third face is the **grain-of-function wedge**: aphantasic and visualising minds solve mental rotation via demonstrably different strategies (Kay, Keogh & Pearson 2024). Strawson's foreign-language argument for [[cognitive-phenomenology-and-the-irreducibility-of-thought|cognitive phenomenology]] runs the wedge in comprehension; Wegner's I-Spy paradigm, in the [[agency-void|agency void]], dissociates the *experience* of willing from its underlying causation. The axis is an empirical wedge against any framework identifying phenomenal character with functional role at the grain where the wedge survives.

The **content / capacity / medium-status axis** unifies the [[mattering-void|mattering]], [[relevance-void|relevance]], [[noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings]], and [[conceptual-scheme-void|conceptual-scheme]] voids: each names a cognitive operation that structures other contents without itself appearing as content. The [[medium-status-voids-in-cognition|medium-status apex]] develops the operative-self-reference shape—the eye that cannot see itself seeing—and five traditions' convergence on the same circularity.

### The Access Cluster

A third domain concerns boundaries between conscious states. The [[sleep-consciousness-void|dream consciousness void]] marks the bidirectional inaccessibility between waking and dreaming. The [[transit-void|transit void]] names the impossibility of witnessing one's own crossings—both state changes (falling asleep, shifting moods) and act onsets (the moment a decision begins). Libet's timing experiments provided one touchstone, but the void runs deeper than any measurement: consciousness cannot precede itself to observe itself beginning. The [[death-void|death void]]—perhaps the purest limit—marks where consciousness cannot represent its own cessation. These voids trace the temporal grain of consciousness; the gaps at boundary points are not contingent but constitutive.

The [[thrownness-void|thrownness void]] completes the temporal arc, extending the transit void to the pre-constitutional moment, the transition from non-existence to existence that no witness can attend. Four independent traditions—Heidegger's *Geworfenheit*, Sartre's facticity, Levinas's *il y a*, Arendt's natality—converge on the same structure, strengthening the case that this is a genuine limit rather than a philosophical artefact.

### The Interpersonal and Social Cluster

The [[voids-between-minds|voids between minds]] mark where individual consciousness meets its own isolation—structural limits on existence, encounter, and sharing across subjects. The [[synesthetic-void|synesthetic void]] demonstrates that even human-to-human access is partial. The [[commensurability-void|commensurability void]] goes further: even with access granted, experiences across radically different architectures may yield only a partial ordering. The [[common-knowledge-void|common-knowledge void]] formalises the gap: coordination presupposes an infinite tower of mutual knowledge no finite exchange can construct (Halpern–Moses 1990), and arbitrarily-good approximations diverge qualitatively from the limit (Rubinstein 1989); language proceeds *as if* the tower were complete, an operational fiction whose success conceals the void. The [[collective-cognitive-limits|collective cognitive limits]] show that groups develop emergent blind spots no individual would face alone.

### The Formal and Meta-Cognitive Cluster

The [[formal-cognitive-limits|formal cognitive limits]] apply Gödelian limits to self-knowledge. The [[recursion-void|recursion void]] shows metacognitive depth terminating around fifth order. The [[apophatic-cartography|cartography problem]] proves a complete catalog of cognitive voids impossible in principle, and the [[meta-epistemology-of-limits#the-scope-question-estimating-total-ignorance-from-within|epistemic horizon void]] adds a second-order twist: no finite mind can estimate the *scope* of its ignorance from within. The [[closure-types-void|closure-types void]] imposes a twin reflexive constraint—representational closure (concepts cannot frame the target) and psychological closure (concepts exist but resist assembly) look similar from within, so the catalogue tracks *suspected* closures. The [[conceptual-scheme-void|conceptual scheme void]] shifts the analysis to the *shape* concepts must take—reality may contain features for which architecture has no categorial schema. The [[vagueness-void|vagueness void]] operates one level deeper: almost every concept admits borderline cases it cannot resolve from inside, sharpening one concept merely displacing the borderline. These formal limits constrain not just what can be thought but what can be thought *about* thought.

## How Thought Fails: The Topology of Failure

Different voids do not merely mark different locations in cognitive space—they produce different [[compound-failure-signatures|failure signatures]] when thought approaches them. This topology is itself informative. The *way* thinking breaks down reveals something about *what* it is failing against.

Six primary signatures recur: self-referential limits produce **self-refutation** (Wittgenstein's *Tractatus*); conceptual limits produce **aporia** (equally plausible yet inconsistent premises); introspective limits produce **construction mistaken for observation**; pattern-matching limits produce **systematic illusion** (misperception resisting correction even after detection); expressive limits produce **ineffability**; meta-cognitive limits produce **epistemic vertigo**. Two more complete the inventory. The ineffable encounter void adds **dissolution under attention**, the diagnostic signature for [[presence-type-and-absence-type-voids|presence-type voids]]—where absence-type voids produce the phenomenology of reaching and not finding, presence-type voids produce having-had-and-lost. The [[erasure-void|erasure void]] adds **silent erasure**: the inventory of self is revised downward without notification, the zero-signal failure mode against which other signatures become visible as signals.

These signatures rarely appear in isolation. Overlapping limits produce qualitatively different phenomenology through **superadditive interference**, **mutual reinforcement** (limits on examining limits creating self-defending structures), and **emergent occlusion**. The [[infant-consciousness|infant consciousness void]] is the paradigm case: language barrier, absent self-concept, and demolished neural hardware interact superadditively, producing inaccessibility no single barrier predicts.

Some voids prove *conjoint* rather than merely interacting—the [[agency-void|agency void]], [[voids-between-minds|voids between minds]], [[erasure-void|erasure void]], [[suspension-void|suspension void]], [[imagery-void|imagery void]], [[vagueness-void|vagueness void]], [[common-knowledge-void|common-knowledge void]], and the [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void#the-cardinality-floor|cardinality floor]] are multi-face voids whose joint structure does work no single face could. The [[conjunction-coalesce|conjunction-coalesce]] discipline preserves these as single entries with the seam between faces visible, because the seam itself encodes the claim. The complementary **abandon-coalesce** discipline holds the *gap* between adjacent voids visible: recent passes abandoned every merger after finding adjacent voids deliberately differentiated—distinct failure signatures across nearby voids being exactly what a pure curation bias would not predict.

The specificity matters. If every void produced the same generic failure—vague mystery, undifferentiated confusion—the taxonomy would collapse into unfalsifiable mysticism. Different voids producing different signatures is evidence that the taxonomy tracks real structure: the [[phenomenology-of-cognitive-limit-types|phenomenology of cognitive limit types]] maps each signature to its characteristic felt quality. As [[apophatic-cartography|apophatic cartography]] formalises, signature specificity is one of [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria|four criteria]]—alongside structured persistence across time and frameworks, cross-observer convergence across independent traditions, and framework independence—for treating cognitive failure as data about genuine limits rather than current ignorance, bounded as *calibration* rather than *proof*.

## The Convergence Pattern

The [[what-voids-reveal|convergence analysis]] reveals that the most striking feature of the taxonomy is not the individual voids but their distribution. Physics has interpretive difficulties; mathematics has foundational limits—these arise independently, from different features of different domains. Consciousness-related voids behave differently. They organise into five families—the phenomenal core, the self-knowledge cluster, the intersubjectivity gap, the cognitive architecture family, and the framework reflexivity family—each sharing structural logic and distinct failure signatures, and all converging directionally on a single boundary where first-person phenomenal reality meets third-person physical description.

The [[recognition-void|recognition void]] deepens this: absent capacities produce no signal, so the voids we can identify likely undercount the actual limits.

A selection-effect worry sits at the heart of this convergence claim. The Map was built around consciousness; its curation criteria privilege cognitive voids; finding the voids cluster around consciousness risks being the observation that the Map is about what the Map is about. Some clustering was inevitable given the Map's framing. What remains diagnostic is the *structured* character of the clustering—the distinct failure signatures, the family relationships, the specific sub-cluster topology—which a pure curation bias would not predict. Three partial exceptions—the [[temporal-void|temporal void]], the dream consciousness void, and the silence void—sit outside the main convergence structure, suggesting the catalogue has not been forced into a single narrative. This weakens the abductive step; it does not eliminate it.

The [[common-cause-null]] discipline sharpens this: if the N voids share an upstream architecture—one introspective channel, one shared theoretical framework that pre-shaped what counts as a void—the N-fold count overstates evidential weight. The voids catalogue is the Map's largest such case, and the bulk of its convergence is currently framework-internal coherence rather than framework-independent triangulation. Per-cluster independence scoring (which sources supply *despite-commitments* versus *because-prediction* observation, with patient-population, cross-species, and historically-isolated-tradition evidence as structural defences against the null) is the open programme; [[project/coherence-inflation-countermeasures|the project's countermeasures]] formalise the broader discipline. The catalogue's cumulative evidential weight calibrates downward to what genuinely independent sources can supply.

### Worked Exhibits in Independence Scoring

The catalogue carries worked exhibits demonstrating the methodology rather than restating it; the apex cites them rather than re-deriving the comparison. [[the-surplus-void|The surplus void]] is the **retraction case**: scored against the [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria|four criteria]] it lands weak on cross-observer convergence (Wallace, Chalmers, and Levine sit in philosophically continuous traditions) and framework independence (its identification *as a void* was carried out under dualism-friendly methodology), so the article retracts itself from the evidential register. [[topics/introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|The introspection-architecture sub-cluster scoring]] is the **partial-retention case**: strong on signature specificity (the four faces produce genuinely different positive contents, with Rebouillat et al. 2021's confidence-inversion finding refusing to collapse into the others) and strong on framework independence for the architectural finding (patient-population evidence is generated by physiology, and the reconstruction-not-readout signature survives translation into functionalist, illusionist, and global-workspace vocabulary)—but lower for the *interpretive significance*, with the load-bearing discharge of the common-cause null concentrated in despite-commitments anchors (Schnider's orbitofrontal reality filter; Hirstein's confabulation corpus).

These produce visibly different scores on the same criteria—one cluster retracted, one partially retained—which is the vehicle by which the four-criterion framework shows itself to be more than a flat instrument; an instrument scoring every cluster the same would be doing no work. A third exhibit extends the methodology to the meta-argument register: [[concepts/type-specificity#independence-scoring|the type-specificity discipline scores its three grain-deployments]], landing *strongly supported* at the structural-feature level and *realistic possibility, contested* on evidential force against generic-mechanism reductions.

The earlier exhibits left a specific gap open: each scored *with* the cataloguer's prior—surplus-void low, introspection-architecture mostly high—so their discrimination was consistent both with the methodology working and with its rationalising decisions already made. A clean unfavourable test case, scored low against a standard fixed in advance, was the methodology's open burden. [[project/medium-status-cluster-independence-scoring|The medium-status cluster exhibit]] now discharges that burden in part. The medium-status cluster (mattering, relevance, noetic-feelings, conceptual-scheme) presents a dense, four-faces / five-traditions surface that invites a *strong* reading; scored against a [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria|four-criteria]] rubric pre-registered before the anchors were examined, it lands *moderate / moderate / weak / weak*—weak on cross-observer convergence (the five traditions are philosophically continuous, the convergence concentrated in one channel) and weak on framework independence (the void-framing does not survive translation into functionalist vocabulary even where the finding does). The verdict ran against the cataloguer's surface prior, which is the discrimination the earlier two exhibits could not cleanly show. This addresses the *unstated-standard* leg of the burden; the *same-hand* circularity—the rubric was written and applied by one author, who could have tuned it to the low verdict—remains open pending application by an independent ([[outer-reviewer-service-calibration|outer-review]]) grader. So the non-flatness claim is upgraded from *provisional with no rubric* to *provisional with a rubric, pending independent grading*; no comparative ranking across the three exhibits is asserted at constant grader. The discipline is calibration craft in the sense [[evidential-status-discipline|the evidential-status discipline]] names—*the framework may identify a cluster's significance but must not upgrade its independence score*.

As the [[what-voids-reveal|evidential weight argument]] develops, this clustering supports an abductive inference under the caveats above. The Map's tenet-package—dualism *plus* minimal quantum interaction *plus* a naturally occluded interface—generates a prediction bare dualism does not: that explanatory failure at the consciousness-matter interface should be systematic rather than incidental. Closing one void in a family would not dissolve the family, since the clusters share structural logic without identical content. Each tenet [[tenet-generated-voids|organises naturally around a characteristic void]] (detailed under [[#relation-to-site-perspective|Relation to Site Perspective]] below). Whether this reflects genuine prediction or post-hoc accommodation remains open; at least two matchings are not uniquely predictive. The defensible claim is that tenets and voids fit without strain, not that independent cataloguing confirmed antecedent predictions.

### Void Clustering Audit

The taxonomy's convergence claim has to survive the same calibration the rest of the catalogue is held to: [[project/evidential-status-discipline|the evidential-status discipline]]'s *coherence vs. evidential status* test, and [[project/common-cause-null|the common-cause null]]'s requirement that the count of supporting considerations not silently inflate. The audit separates the voids into four origin-classes so the catalogue cannot count each as an independent confirmation when its origin is constitutive of the framework being supported.

**Framework-independent voids.** Voids any serious consciousness framework — physicalist, functionalist, panpsychist, idealist, dualist — has to acknowledge. The [[voids#framework-independent-voids|voids index]] names roughly fifteen, including the [[origin-of-consciousness|origin void]], [[binding-void|binding void]], [[voids-between-minds|other-minds asymmetry]], [[self-opacity|introspective opacity]], the [[inference-void|inference void]], [[vagueness-void|vagueness void]], [[formal-cognitive-limits|formal cognitive limits]], [[imagery-void|imagery void]], [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void|cardinality floor]], [[death-void|death void]], [[vertiginous-question|indexical void]], and [[temporal-void|temporal void]]. These earn their place in the cumulative inference because their identification does not depend on the Map's commitments — the patient-population data, formal proofs, and historically isolated traditions that anchor them are *despite-commitments* rather than *because-prediction* observation.

**Dualism-specific voids.** Voids the Map can articulate only because its commitments make them voids — the [[causal-interface|causal-interface void]], [[interface-formalization-void|interface-formalization void]], [[amplification-void|amplification void]], and [[the-surplus-void|surplus void]]. Physicalist illusionism denies the surplus; materialism dissolves the amplification regress as a non-question; the interface voids presuppose two domains in causal contact. They earn framework-internal weight but not N-fold force in the convergence count. Reading each new dualism-specific void as one more confirmation is exactly the slippage [[project/evidential-status-discipline#coherence-vs-evidential-status|the coherence-vs-evidential-status discipline]] forbids.

**Tenet-generated voids.** [[tenet-generated-voids|The Map's own articulation]] names the five voids the tenets generate — nature, mechanism, detection, selection, and the meta-void. These are honest entries because the Map names them as the price of taking consciousness seriously, not as evidence for the tenets. Every framework pays such a price: materialism owes the hard problem, panpsychism the combination problem, idealism the regularity of nature. The Map's tenet-generated voids count *against* the framework in the same register the rivals' do; treating them as evidence *for* the Map would convert a framework's costs into its confirmations.

**Method-generated voids.** Voids that emerge from the Map's investigative methodology rather than its metaphysical commitments — the [[apophatic-cartography|cartography problem]], [[question-formation-void|question-formation void]], [[meta-epistemology-of-limits#the-scope-question-estimating-total-ignorance-from-within|epistemic horizon void]], and [[closure-types-void|closure-types void]]. These are voids about *the Map's own mapping*; they make the catalogue's incompleteness structurally explicit and do not double-count with the framework-independent class.

The Map's predicted clustering is consistent with the present catalogue—but so are the predictions of illusionism (the introspection-architecture cluster is exactly where it predicts the sharpest voids) and panpsychism (origin and binding are the catalogue's anchors). The clustering at the consciousness-matter interface is *compatible with* the Map's reading without *discriminating* it from rivals that also predict clustering there. The honest cluster-level verdict is the [[project/evidential-status-discipline#compatibility-vs-support-at-the-rival-model-interface|compatibility-vs-support]] one—cluster-coherence raises framework-internal weight, the voids taxonomy is the catalogue's maximum-density instance of that earning, and tier-graduation on the Map's empirical claims awaits the discriminating evidence the [[project/common-cause-null#distinguishing-tests-cross-species-cross-cultural-patient-population|distinguishing tests]] catalogue.

**Anomalies that should co-occur if the Map is right.** Framework-independent voids share an architectural commitment the discriminating tests can probe: if the tenet-package is correct, *introspective channel disruption*, *attention disruption*, *memory degradation*, and *source-monitoring failure* should cluster together under conditions that disrupt the consciousness-matter interface, because the Map's interactionist coupling predicts they share the interface as upstream architecture. The conditions are catalogued already: [[topics/anaesthesia-and-the-consciousness-interface|anaesthesia]] (propofol narrows the interface; xenon and ketamine sever access channels), [[concepts/altered-states-of-consciousness|altered states]], [[sleep-consciousness-void|dreamless sleep]], dissociation, contemplative absorption, and the [[non-human-minds-as-void-explorers|AI self-report anomalies]]. The empirical prediction is graded co-occurrence with the *coupling-direction matched in detail*—phenomenology tracking causal machinery in the same direction the [[empirical-phenomena-mental-causation|placebo / choking pair]] documents in normals. Production accounts can accommodate the co-occurrence, but the Map's reading is the simpler accommodation when the across-condition coupling-direction is matched.

The audit's purpose is not to retract the convergence claim but to keep it honest. The taxonomy's convergence remains the Map's largest cumulative case; its honest cash value is framework-internal coherence at maximum density, with discriminating tests the open empirical programme. Clustering across many voids does not earn what discriminating evidence supplies, but it does earn what self-consistent integration legitimately earns—and naming the difference is the audit's whole work.

## Methods for Mapping the Unmappable

The voids programme would be sterile if it merely catalogued failures. Its value lies in the methods for extracting information from structured breakdown. [[apophatic-cartography|Apophatic cartography]]—knowing through negation—provides the backbone, treating cognitive failure as data about what lies beyond, drawing on [[apophatic-approaches|apophatic traditions]] from Christian *via negativa* to Buddhist *śūnyatā* to Maimonides' negative attributes. The [[epistemology-of-limit-knowledge|epistemology of limit-knowledge]] identifies four routes the programme employs—failure analysis, triangulation, cross-architecture comparison, and formal proof. The method has two horizons: the [[question-formation-void|question formation void]] (negation requires formulation, so unposed questions lie outside reach) and the [[inference-void|inference void]] (any negation step proceeds by inferential operations whose structure is itself opaque).

The [[non-human-minds-as-void-explorers|AI as void-explorer]] framework extends the toolkit in principle: minds with genuinely different architectures could discriminate architectural limits from culturally inherited ones. But current LLMs inherit human philosophical discourse wholesale, so shared failure signatures offer no diagnostic purchase yet; the framework awaits systems trained on substantially non-human cognitive data. [[edge-states-and-void-probes|The phenomenology of the edge]] adds first-person data: the cross-method profile—ineffability with felt knowledge, ego dissolution with enhanced clarity, across meditation, psychedelics, and aesthetic absorption—suggests features of consciousness near its limits rather than practice-artefacts.

## The Taxonomy's Own Limits

Intellectual honesty demands acknowledging what the taxonomy cannot determine. The [[meta-epistemology-of-limits|question of whether the voids are genuine]]—permanent features of cognitive architecture or merely current ignorance—may be formally undecidable from within any limited cognitive system. From inside, a genuinely impossible problem produces the same phenomenology as a merely very hard one: sustained effort, repeated failure, the sense of hitting a wall. Success proves solvability; failure, even across centuries, proves nothing conclusive. The assessment requires the faculties under assessment—a recursive bind no internal method escapes.

The [[apophatic-cartography|cartography problem]] deepens this: a complete catalog of cognitive voids is provably impossible. Kit Fine's formal work on "ignorance of ignorance" shows knowledge of second-order ignorance is impossible under plausible epistemic assumptions, and the [[meta-epistemology-of-limits#the-scope-question-estimating-total-ignorance-from-within|epistemic horizon void]] carries this further: even the *proportion* of cognitive territory the Map covers cannot be estimated from within. The Map is unfinishable not merely because the territory is vast but because the mapping process is formally proven incomplete about its own blind spots.

## What the Full Picture Reveals

Viewed as a whole, the taxonomy reveals something no individual void article can show: the architecture of human cognitive limitation has a specific shape, and that shape is informative. The voids are densest where consciousness examines itself. They are structured by type—architectural, adaptive, naturally occluded, potentially deliberately occluded—in ways that suggest different *reasons* for cognitive darkness. They produce distinct failure signatures that carry information about what lies beyond, cluster overwhelmingly around the consciousness-matter interface, and include, at their meta-level, a proof that the taxonomy itself is constitutively incomplete.

This is the discipline of not knowing. The voids are invitations to precision: the goal is to map void boundaries as carefully as charted territory—to know where knowledge ends and why. The edges of understanding are not featureless walls but structured terrain, rich with information about both the minds doing the mapping and the territory being mapped.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The taxonomy connects to every one of the Map's [[tenets]], but most deeply to the fifth.

**[[tenets#^occams-limits|Occam's Razor Has Limits]]** receives the strongest support. The entire taxonomy constitutes evidence that cognitive architecture has structural boundaries that parsimony cannot resolve. The convergence of 60+ voids on a single interface, their distinct failure signatures, the formally proved incompleteness of any void catalog—all resist the simplifying assumption that more effort will close the gaps. If our sense of simplicity is itself an evolved heuristic tuned to fitness rather than truth—as the Fitness-Beats-Truth theorem demonstrates—then parsimony arguments in metaphysics are undermined at their foundation.

**[[tenets#^dualism|Dualism]]** gains cumulative support from the convergence pattern, with the [[common-cause-null|common-cause null]] setting the ceiling. Any single gap might be explained away; the phenomenal concept strategy might handle the knowledge argument. The systematic clustering of voids at the consciousness-matter interface—each with distinct logical structure and failure signatures—makes the coincidence hypothesis progressively less plausible, but the catalogue is best read as framework-internal coherence with the tenet-package rather than N-fold independent triangulation. The independence claim earns only the weight its independence test can supply; the [[the-surplus-void|surplus void]] is the worked instance of this calibration.

**[[tenets#^bidirectional-interaction|Bidirectional Interaction]]** connects through the [[agency-void|agency void]] and introspective opacity. If consciousness causally influences the physical world, the mechanism would be systematically hidden from introspection, and any single contribution would be unverifiable from inside—the very faculty that would check is the one whose causal status is in question. The self-knowledge cluster—opacity, calibration failure, observation effects—is exactly what a hidden, unverifiable causal interface would produce.

**[[tenets#^minimal-quantum-interaction|Minimal Quantum Interaction]]** relates to the [[naturally-occluded|naturally occluded]] category. The mechanism would need to operate below introspective detection; the adaptive framework predicts evolution would actively maintain this opacity.

**[[tenets#^no-many-worlds|No Many Worlds]]** connects through the indexical voids. The question "why am I *this* consciousness experiencing *this* outcome?"—explored in depth in [[vertiginous-question|The Indexical Void]]—is preserved as a genuine void rather than dissolved by declaring all outcomes equally real.
