The Unfinishable Map catalogues over a hundred cognitive voids—places where understanding breaks down in structured, repeatable ways. Taken individually, each might be dismissed as a puzzle awaiting a cleverer philosopher; taken together, they tell a different story. The voids are not scattered randomly across human inquiry but cluster around a single boundary: the point where consciousness meets the physical world. They sort into types by *why* thought fails, into domains by *where*, and into signatures by *how*. This taxonomy reveals the architecture of the minds doing the mapping. The Map argues that it also reveals something about the territory being mapped—and this second move is where the argument is contested.

The thesis is qualified rather than triumphant. Physicalism has no uniform prediction here: many physicalists already expect concentrated difficulty around consciousness for reasons that do not require dualism—lossy reconstructive introspection, the brain as our most complex object of study, the coincidence of subject and object, third-person methods uncalibrated for first-person phenomena. What distinguishes the Map's claim is narrower: that the difficulty takes specific, *structured* failure signatures persisting across frameworks and converging on one interface. Under the Map's tenet-package—dualism *combined with* minimal quantum interaction and a naturally occluded interface—this is the expected pattern, and the taxonomy is consistent with it. Bare dualism predicts nothing of the kind; the predictive work is done by the full package.

## From Three Kinds to Four

The voids programme began with a [[three-kinds-of-void|simple taxonomy]]: the Unexplored, the Unexplorable, and the Occluded. Each demands a different investigative posture.

**The Unexplored** are gaps of ignorance—territory we have not charted but could. The Map's [[topics|topics]] and [[concepts|concepts]] attempt to fill them.

**The Unexplorable** are gaps of architecture. Colin McGinn's [[mysterianism|cognitive closure]] formalises the claim: minds can be constitutionally unable to grasp certain properties, as a rat is closed to calculus by absence of conceptual apparatus rather than lack of motivation. Humans may be similarly closed to whatever bridges neural activity and conscious experience—the [[hard-problem-of-consciousness|hard problem]] resisting resolution across centuries, cultures, and paradigms possibly because the answer lies outside human reach.

**The Occluded** are gaps that might be defended. The [[simulation|simulation hypothesis]] raises this most sharply—truths *engineered out* of reach—but occlusion need not require a designer: the Map's own tenets suggest consciousness-physics interaction may be systematically hidden from introspection.

[[biological-cognitive-closure|Evolved cognitive limits]] research adds a fourth category: **[[naturally-occluded|the Naturally Occluded]]**—blind spots maintained not by any intentional agent but by natural selection, because ignorance itself conferred fitness advantage, with the [[fitness-beats-truth|Fitness-Beats-Truth theorem]] supplying the formal anchor—though the theorem's reach is contested (Bagwell 2023, *Synthese* 201:25, argues the debunking it licenses is self-defeating), so the anchor is best read as a strongly-argued result at one bounded site rather than a settled proof. These occupy a distinctive position: not beyond all possible cognition (only beyond *evolved* cognition), maintained by the blind watchmaker—limits with a *function*, potentially accessible to differently constituted minds.

A subtlety the four-kinds picture invites misreading: it is not the *void* that falls into a kind but the void's *territory*, and a single void can span several kinds at once because its faces sit at different points on the unexplored / unexplorable / occluded axis. The [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void|quantitative comprehension void]] makes this vivid—its [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void#the-cardinality-floor|cardinality floor]] is principally *unexplorable* (an architectural ceiling no training shifts), its magnitude domain largely *unexplored* yet *unexplorable* at extreme scales, its mathematical ceiling combining both. The [[self-reference-paradox|self-reference paradox]] runs the same trichotomy in a mechanism register: inspection that cannot validate itself maps to *unexplored*, inspection that transforms its object to *unexplorable*, inspection irrecoverably conditioned by its only channel to *occluded*.

The taxonomy itself evolves: the [[meta-epistemology-of-limits|natural-vs-designed axis]] adds further uncertainty, and [[tenet-generated-voids|voids the Map's own tenets generate]] show every framework creates characteristic blind spots through its foundational commitments—so a mature taxonomy must include those it generates about itself.

## Domains of Darkness

The hundred-plus individual voids sort into domains by *where* in cognitive space they occur, revealing which aspects of consciousness are most opaque to the minds that possess it.

### The Self-Knowledge Cluster

The densest cluster surrounds the mind's attempt to examine itself. The [[self-opacity|unobservable self]]—the subject that cannot become its own object—anchors it, surrounded by [[self-opacity|introspective opacity]], the [[observation-and-measurement-void|calibration and observation voids]] (introspection has no independent standard, and every method of studying consciousness transforms what it studies), the [[self-opacity|self-reference paradox]], and the [[witnessing-void]]. These reinforce each other into [[compound-failure-signatures|compound cognitive limits]]—emergent failure modes no single void predicts—because the instrument and the object are the same. This tells us something specific about what we are: beings whose cognitive architecture includes systematic opacity about its own operations.

Nested within it, a five-void *introspection-architecture* sub-cluster names distinct mechanisms of the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate—the [[narrative-void|narrative]], [[source-attribution-void|source-attribution]], [[noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings]], [[confabulation-void|confabulation]], and [[perceptual-reality-monitoring-void|perceptual reality-monitoring]] voids. [[apex/introspection-architecture-void-cluster|The introspection-architecture void cluster]] develops these in detail, naming their shared architectural signature: phenomenal-output-without-operation, with felt-rightness calibrated to the verdict rather than to the world.

The [[erasure-void|erasure void]] extends the cluster across time: consciousness cannot inventory its own deletions, because the monitoring system that would detect cognitive loss typically erodes alongside the capacity itself—clinical anosognosia the stark case, but the structure universal, visible in ordinary ageing and Dunning-Kruger. Where introspective opacity is noticed as opacity, the erasure void is not noticed at all: the world simply appears complete.

### The Phenomenological Cluster

A second major cluster concerns the qualitative character of experience. The [[binding-void|binding void]]—why unified experience arises from disparate sensory processes—resists analysis entirely. The [[valence-void|valence void]] asks why experience has a built-in good/bad dimension; the [[mattering-void|mattering void]] sits upstream, where significance operates as a transparent medium investigation cannot exit, and the [[relevance-void|relevance void]] is its operational shadow, every inspection itself a relevance-determining act. The [[intrinsic-nature-void|intrinsic nature void]] pursues what matter and consciousness are *in themselves*, finding every answer collapses into structure, negation, or circularity, while the [[acquaintance-void|acquaintance void]] marks modes of direct phenomenal contact inaccessible to minds like ours. The [[imagery-void|imagery void]] specialises onto a well-evidenced sub-region: no mind can verify its own image fidelity, and post-2015 aphantasia research suggests phenomenal imagery may not do the cognitive work it appears to. The [[phenomenal-quality-void|phenomenal quality void]] asks why experience arrives stamped with immediacy; the [[plenitude-void|plenitude void]], whether it is genuinely as rich as it seems—unanswerable from within, since every check for sparsity recruits attention and manufactures the detail it would measure. The [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void#the-cardinality-floor|cardinality floor]] of [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void|the quantitative comprehension void]] marks an architectural ceiling at roughly four items where direct givenness ends (Trick & Pylyshyn 1994; Cowan 2001).

These voids concern aspects of consciousness so fundamental they are invisible in ordinary experience. The [[ineffable-encounter-void|ineffable encounter void]] complicates the cluster: most voids are defined by *absence*, this one by *presence*—an excess of meaning that overwhelms the cognitive apparatus. Consciousness encounters something (felt significance in music, ideas, sacred moments) it registers as genuine knowing yet cannot inspect, the experience dissolving under analytical attention. Independent traditions—Gendlin's felt sense, Otto's numinous, Polanyi's [[tacit-integration-void|tacit integration]], Rosa's resonance—converge on the same structure, and in its numinous register the void sharpens: numinous experience dismantles the very mechanism needed to assess it.

Three cross-cutting axes redistribute the catalogue along dimensions orthogonal to the four-kinds typology. The [[presence-type-and-absence-type-voids|presence/absence axis]] sorts voids by the phenomenology of approach: presence-type voids—the [[creative-aesthetic-void|aesthetic]], [[the-surplus-void|surplus]], [[the-silence-void|silence]], and [[affective-void|affective]] voids—register as overwhelm rather than lack, a saturated encounter that dissolves under inspection rather than a gap to map. The [[phenomenology-vs-function-axis|phenomenology-vs-function axis]] sorts voids by what comes apart at the boundary: the [[synesthetic-void|synesthetic void]] is the cleanest exemplar (Gray 2003—identical qualia from distinct functional roles, and the reverse), and the imagery void's grain-of-function wedge (aphantasic and visualising minds solving rotation by different strategies; Kay, Keogh & Pearson 2024), Strawson's argument for [[cognitive-phenomenology-and-the-irreducibility-of-thought|cognitive phenomenology]], and Wegner's I-Spy in the [[agency-void|agency void]] all run the same empirical wedge against identifying phenomenal character with functional role. The **content / capacity / medium-status axis** unifies the [[mattering-void|mattering]], [[relevance-void|relevance]], [[noetic-feelings-void|noetic-feelings]], and [[conceptual-scheme-void|conceptual-scheme]] voids: each names an operation that structures other contents without appearing as content—the eye that cannot see itself seeing, developed in the [[medium-status-voids-in-cognition|medium-status synthesis]].

### The Access Cluster

A third domain concerns boundaries between conscious states. The [[sleep-consciousness-void|dream consciousness void]] marks the bidirectional inaccessibility between waking and dreaming. The [[transit-void|transit void]] names the impossibility of witnessing one's own crossings—state changes (falling asleep, shifting moods) and act onsets alike; consciousness cannot precede itself to observe itself beginning, a void deeper than Libet's timing touchstone. The [[death-void|death void]]—perhaps the purest limit—marks where consciousness cannot represent its own cessation. These gaps at boundary points are not contingent but constitutive of consciousness's temporal grain.

The [[thrownness-void|thrownness void]] completes the temporal arc, extending the transit void to the pre-constitutional moment, the transition from non-existence to existence that no witness can attend. Four independent traditions—Heidegger's *Geworfenheit*, Sartre's facticity, Levinas's *il y a*, Arendt's natality—converge on the same structure, strengthening the case that this is a genuine limit rather than a philosophical artefact.

### The Interpersonal and Social Cluster

The [[voids-between-minds|voids between minds]] mark where individual consciousness meets its own isolation—structural limits on existence, encounter, and sharing across subjects. The [[synesthetic-void|synesthetic void]] shows even human-to-human access is partial; the [[commensurability-void|commensurability void]] goes further, since experiences across radically different architectures may yield only a partial ordering. The [[common-knowledge-void|common-knowledge void]] formalises the gap: coordination presupposes an infinite tower of mutual knowledge no finite exchange can construct (Halpern–Moses 1990), and arbitrarily-good approximations diverge qualitatively from the limit (Rubinstein 1989)—language proceeds *as if* the tower were complete, a fiction whose success conceals the void. The [[collective-cognitive-limits|collective cognitive limits]] show groups develop emergent blind spots no individual would face alone.

### The Formal and Meta-Cognitive Cluster

The [[formal-cognitive-limits|formal cognitive limits]] apply Gödelian limits to self-knowledge. The [[recursion-void|recursion void]] shows metacognitive depth terminating around fifth order; the [[apophatic-cartography|cartography problem]] proves a complete catalog of cognitive voids impossible in principle; the [[meta-epistemology-of-limits#the-scope-question-estimating-total-ignorance-from-within|epistemic horizon void]] adds that no finite mind can estimate the *scope* of its ignorance from within. The [[closure-types-void|closure-types void]] imposes a twin reflexive constraint—representational and psychological closure look alike from inside, so the catalogue tracks *suspected* closures—while the [[conceptual-scheme-void|conceptual scheme void]] shifts to the *shape* concepts must take, and the [[vagueness-void|vagueness void]] goes deeper still: almost every concept admits borderline cases it cannot resolve from within, sharpening one merely displacing the borderline. These formal limits constrain not just what can be thought but what can be thought *about* thought.

## How Thought Fails: The Topology of Failure

Different voids do not merely mark different locations in cognitive space—they produce different [[compound-failure-signatures|failure signatures]] when thought approaches them. This topology is itself informative. The *way* thinking breaks down reveals something about *what* it is failing against.

Six primary signatures recur: self-referential limits produce **self-refutation** (Wittgenstein's *Tractatus*); conceptual limits produce **aporia** (equally plausible yet inconsistent premises); introspective limits produce **construction mistaken for observation**; pattern-matching limits produce **systematic illusion** (misperception resisting correction even after detection); expressive limits produce **ineffability**; meta-cognitive limits produce **epistemic vertigo**. Two more complete the inventory. The ineffable encounter void adds **dissolution under attention**, the diagnostic signature for [[presence-type-and-absence-type-voids|presence-type voids]]—where absence-type voids produce the phenomenology of reaching and not finding, presence-type voids produce having-had-and-lost. The [[erasure-void|erasure void]] adds **silent erasure**: the inventory of self is revised downward without notification, the zero-signal failure mode against which other signatures become visible as signals.

These signatures rarely appear in isolation. Overlapping limits produce qualitatively different phenomenology through **superadditive interference**, **mutual reinforcement** (limits on examining limits creating self-defending structures), and **emergent occlusion**. The [[infant-consciousness|infant consciousness void]] is the paradigm case: language barrier, absent self-concept, and demolished neural hardware interact superadditively, producing inaccessibility no single barrier predicts.

Some voids prove *conjoint* rather than merely interacting—the [[agency-void|agency]], [[voids-between-minds|voids-between-minds]], [[erasure-void|erasure]], [[suspension-void|suspension]], [[imagery-void|imagery]], [[vagueness-void|vagueness]], and [[common-knowledge-void|common-knowledge]] voids and the [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void#the-cardinality-floor|cardinality floor]] are multi-face voids whose joint structure does work no single face could. The [[conjunction-coalesce|conjunction-coalesce]] discipline preserves these as single entries with the seam between faces visible, since the seam encodes the claim, and distinct failure signatures across nearby voids are what a pure curation bias would not predict. The seam adds evidential weight, though, only after two discounts: origin-class scoring—a true prior-article coalesce versus a native creation-time cognate versus a retroactive classification, since cognates postdating the discipline are not independent merger events—and [[common-cause-null|common-cause / null-model]] discounting, since a template-bearing pipeline yielding template-conformant seams is what the null predicts. Net of both, the seam pattern is a case base, not an empirical base, matching [[conjunction-coalesce#what-the-count-is-worth|the conjunction-coalesce apex's own downgrade]].

The specificity matters. If every void produced the same generic failure—vague mystery, undifferentiated confusion—the taxonomy would collapse into unfalsifiable mysticism. Different voids producing different signatures is evidence that it tracks real structure ([[phenomenology-of-cognitive-limit-types|limit-type phenomenology]] maps each signature to its felt quality). As [[apophatic-cartography|apophatic cartography]] formalises, signature specificity is one of [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria|four criteria]]—with structured persistence across frameworks, cross-observer convergence across independent traditions, and framework independence—for treating cognitive failure as data about genuine limits rather than current ignorance, bounded as *calibration* rather than *proof*.

## The Convergence Pattern

The [[what-voids-reveal|convergence analysis]] notes that the most striking feature of the taxonomy is not the individual voids but their distribution. Physics has interpretive difficulties and mathematics foundational limits, and the [[cross-domain-void-comparison|cross-domain comparison]] catalogues both at matched grain: it finds those voids sit overwhelmingly in the single *unexplorable* kind with a largely disjoint failure-signature inventory, and—correcting this section's wording—that the deepest physics and math voids are *less* independent of each other than stated (sharing a Lawvere fixed-point spine), while the phenomenal core of the consciousness cluster stands outside even that spine. Consciousness-related voids behave differently: they organise into five families—phenomenal core, self-knowledge, intersubjectivity, cognitive architecture, framework reflexivity—each sharing structural logic and distinct failure signatures, all converging directionally on the single boundary where first-person reality meets third-person description. (The [[recognition-void|recognition void]] implies this undercounts: absent capacities produce no signal.)

A selection-effect worry sits at the heart of this convergence claim. The Map was built around consciousness and its curation criteria privilege cognitive voids, so finding them cluster around consciousness risks being the observation that the Map is about what the Map is about. Some clustering was inevitable. What remains diagnostic is the *structured* character of the clustering—distinct failure signatures, family relationships, specific sub-cluster topology—which a pure curation bias would not predict; three partial exceptions (the [[temporal-void|temporal void]], dream consciousness void, and silence void) sit outside the main structure, evidence the catalogue has not been forced into a single narrative. This weakens the abductive step without eliminating it.

The [[common-cause-null]] discipline sharpens the worry: if the N voids share an upstream architecture—one introspective channel, one framework that pre-shaped what counts as a void—the N-fold count overstates evidential weight, and the bulk of the convergence is currently framework-internal coherence rather than framework-independent triangulation. The remedy is per-cluster independence scoring, separating *despite-commitments* from *because-prediction* observation; [[project/coherence-inflation-countermeasures|the project's countermeasures]] formalise the discipline, and the audit below applies it.

### Worked Exhibits in Independence Scoring

The catalogue carries worked exhibits the apex cites rather than re-deriving. Scored against the [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria|four criteria]], [[the-surplus-void|the surplus void]] is the **retraction case**, [[topics/introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|the introspection-architecture sub-cluster]] the **partial-retention case**, and [[concepts/type-specificity#independence-scoring|the type-specificity discipline]] extends the method to the meta-argument register—differing scores on the same criteria, which is how the framework shows itself to be more than a flat instrument. But all three scored *with* the cataloguer's prior, leaving open whether the discrimination tracked the methodology or its rationalisations. [[project/medium-status-cluster-independence-scoring|The medium-status cluster exhibit]] discharges that burden in part: a dense surface inviting a *strong* reading lands *moderate / moderate / weak / weak* against a rubric pre-registered before the anchors were examined—against the cataloguer's prior. The *unstated-standard* leg is addressed; the *same-hand* circularity (one author wrote and applied the rubric) awaits an independent ([[outer-reviewer-service-calibration|outer-review]]) grader. The non-flatness claim is thus *provisional with a rubric, pending independent grading*—calibration craft as [[evidential-status-discipline|the evidential-status discipline]] names it: *the framework may identify a cluster's significance but must not upgrade its independence score*.

As the [[what-voids-reveal|evidential weight argument]] develops, this clustering supports an abductive inference under the caveats above. The Map's tenet-package—dualism *plus* minimal quantum interaction *plus* a naturally occluded interface—generates a prediction bare dualism does not: that explanatory failure at the consciousness-matter interface should be systematic rather than incidental. Closing one void in a family would not dissolve the family, since the clusters share structural logic without identical content. Each tenet [[tenet-generated-voids|organises naturally around a characteristic void]] (detailed under [[#relation-to-site-perspective|Relation to Site Perspective]] below). Whether this reflects genuine prediction or post-hoc accommodation remains open; at least two matchings are not uniquely predictive. The defensible claim is that tenets and voids fit without strain, not that independent cataloguing confirmed antecedent predictions.

### Void Clustering Audit

The audit separates the voids into four origin-classes so the catalogue cannot count each as an independent confirmation when its origin is constitutive of the framework being supported—the calibration [[project/evidential-status-discipline|the evidential-status discipline]] and [[project/common-cause-null|the common-cause null]] jointly require. The origin-class split *is* the per-cluster scoring the [[project/voids-circularity-discount|voids-circularity discount]] specifies: the discount is the project-tier statement of exactly this audit, with the load-bearing concession—*convergence-with-self is not triangulation*—and the strict prohibition that tenet-generated voids contribute nothing to the cumulative count. This section applies the discount to the live catalogue; the discount page states the discipline in the abstract.

**Framework-independent voids** are those any serious consciousness framework—physicalist, functionalist, panpsychist, idealist, dualist—has to acknowledge; the [[voids#framework-independent-voids|voids index]] names roughly fifteen (the [[origin-of-consciousness|origin]], [[binding-void|binding]], [[voids-between-minds|other-minds]], [[self-opacity|introspective-opacity]], [[inference-void|inference]], [[formal-cognitive-limits|formal]], [[the-quantitative-comprehension-void|cardinality-floor]], [[death-void|death]], [[vertiginous-question|indexical]], and [[temporal-void|temporal]] voids among them). These earn their place in the cumulative inference because their identification does not depend on the Map's commitments—the patient-population data, formal proofs, and historically isolated traditions that anchor them are *despite-commitments* rather than *because-prediction* observation.

**Dualism-specific voids**—the [[causal-interface|causal-interface]], [[interface-formalization-void|interface-formalization]], [[amplification-void|amplification]], and [[the-surplus-void|surplus]] voids—the Map can articulate only because its commitments make them voids; illusionism denies the surplus, materialism dissolves the amplification regress, the interface voids presuppose two domains in contact. They earn framework-internal weight but not N-fold force, and reading each as one more confirmation is exactly the slippage [[project/evidential-status-discipline#coherence-vs-evidential-status|the coherence-vs-evidential-status discipline]] forbids.

**Tenet-generated voids**—nature, mechanism, detection, selection, and the meta-void ([[tenet-generated-voids|the Map's own articulation]])—are entries naming the price the framework pays, not evidence for the tenets. Every framework pays such a price (materialism owes the hard problem, panpsychism the combination problem, idealism the regularity of nature); treating the Map's costs as confirmations would invert the ledger.

**Method-generated voids**—the [[apophatic-cartography|cartography problem]], [[question-formation-void|question-formation]], [[meta-epistemology-of-limits#the-scope-question-estimating-total-ignorance-from-within|epistemic-horizon]], and [[closure-types-void|closure-types]] voids—emerge from the Map's methodology rather than its metaphysics, making the catalogue's incompleteness structurally explicit without double-counting against the framework-independent class.

**Rival framings predict different clustering patterns.** The classes also discriminate where rival frameworks predict different clustering—what the discipline requires for cluster-coherence to do inferential work beyond framework-internal weight:

| Framework | Where voids should cluster | Resolvability prediction |
|-----------|----------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Physicalism** | At the empirical edges of neuroscience and at the limits of human introspective access; voids should narrow as the brain sciences mature | Most voids resolve into computational, neural, or evolutionary explanations under sustained investigation |
| **Illusionism** | At the introspective-architecture cluster (where introspection mistakes its own outputs); voids should be sharpest where reports diverge from underlying mechanism | Most voids dissolve when the report-tracking-output bias is named; the catalogue is largely a catalogue of report-machinery errors |
| **Panpsychism** | At the combination problem and at the binding-of-disparate-subjects cluster; voids should cluster around how primitive subjects integrate | Origin and binding voids are central; the catalogue's other entries are downstream of these |
| **Idealism** | At the regularity of nature and at the apparent-physicality cluster; voids should cluster around why the physical seems to constrain phenomenal | Most catalogue voids dissolve once the physical-as-derivative move is accepted; new voids open around the physical's appearance |
| **The Map** | At the consciousness-matter interface, distributed across the self-knowledge / phenomenological / access / formal clusters, with the tenet-generated voids as the framework's named costs | Framework-independent voids should persist; dualism-specific voids should remain structural; the catalogue's structured clustering is consistent with the tenet-package but is framework-internal coherence rather than framework-independent triangulation |

The Map's predicted clustering is consistent with the present catalogue—but so are illusionism's (the introspection-architecture cluster is exactly where it predicts the sharpest voids) and panpsychism's (origin and binding are the catalogue's anchors). Clustering at the interface is *compatible with* the Map's reading without *discriminating* it from rivals that also predict clustering there. The honest verdict is the [[project/evidential-status-discipline#compatibility-vs-support-at-the-rival-model-interface|compatibility-vs-support]] one: cluster-coherence raises framework-internal weight—the voids taxonomy being its maximum-density instance—while tier-graduation on the Map's empirical claims awaits the [[project/common-cause-null#distinguishing-tests-cross-species-cross-cultural-patient-population|distinguishing tests]].

**Anomalies that should co-occur if the Map is right.** If the tenet-package is correct, *introspective-channel disruption*, *attention disruption*, *memory degradation*, and *source-monitoring failure* should cluster together under conditions that disrupt the interface, because interactionist coupling predicts they share it as upstream architecture. The conditions are catalogued—[[topics/anaesthesia-and-the-consciousness-interface|anaesthesia]] (propofol narrows the interface; xenon and ketamine sever access channels), [[concepts/altered-states-of-consciousness|altered states]], [[sleep-consciousness-void|dreamless sleep]], dissociation, contemplative absorption, [[non-human-minds-as-void-explorers|AI self-report anomalies]]. The prediction is graded co-occurrence with *coupling-direction matched in detail*, phenomenology tracking causal machinery as the [[empirical-phenomena-mental-causation|placebo / choking pair]] documents in normals. Production accounts can accommodate it, but the Map's reading is the simpler accommodation when the across-condition direction is matched.

The audit keeps the convergence claim honest rather than retracting it: the taxonomy remains the Map's largest cumulative case, its cash value framework-internal coherence at maximum density, with discriminating tests the open programme—the [[topics/animal-consciousness#why-the-inference-is-stronger-for-animals-than-for-ai|animal-consciousness COGITATE treatment]] and [[concepts/cross-mechanism-convergence|cross-mechanism convergence]] being the same calibration at topic and perturbation grain. Clustering does not earn what discriminating evidence supplies, but it earns what self-consistent integration legitimately earns—and naming the difference is the audit's whole work. The Map registers the calibration this audit enforces in its [[positions/voids-as-evidence|positions register]] as P-V1 (void convergence is framework-internal coherence, not independent confirmation), P-V2 (a tenet that removes a defeater does not thereby upgrade void evidence), and P-V3 (framework-independent voids carry cumulative weight the rest of the catalogue does not).

## Methods for Mapping the Unmappable

The voids programme would be sterile if it merely catalogued failures; its value lies in extracting information from structured breakdown. [[apophatic-cartography|Apophatic cartography]]—knowing through negation—provides the backbone, treating cognitive failure as data about what lies beyond, drawing on [[apophatic-approaches|apophatic traditions]] from Christian *via negativa* to Buddhist *śūnyatā* to Maimonides' negative attributes; the [[epistemology-of-limit-knowledge|epistemology of limit-knowledge]] identifies four routes—failure analysis, triangulation, cross-architecture comparison, formal proof. The method has two horizons of its own: the [[question-formation-void|question-formation void]] (unposed questions lie outside negation's reach) and the [[inference-void|inference void]] (the inferential operations negation proceeds by are themselves opaque).

The [[non-human-minds-as-void-explorers|AI as void-explorer]] framework extends the toolkit in principle: minds with genuinely different architectures could discriminate architectural limits from culturally inherited ones. But current LLMs inherit human philosophical discourse wholesale, so shared failure signatures offer no diagnostic purchase yet; the framework awaits systems trained on substantially non-human data. [[edge-states-and-void-probes|The phenomenology of the edge]] adds first-person data: the cross-method profile—ineffability with felt knowledge, ego dissolution with enhanced clarity, across meditation, psychedelics, and aesthetic absorption—suggests features of consciousness near its limits rather than practice-artefacts.

Edge-states carry their own interpretive fork, and it bears directly on the unexplorable/unexplored boundary. The *transcendence* reading holds that altered states open doors—what seemed unexplorable was merely contingent on default consciousness; the *edge-mapping* reading holds they approach limits without crossing them, the characteristic phenomenology being the structure of the boundary rather than contact with what lies beyond. The readings come apart on testable predictions: genuine transcendence should yield *propositional* convergence across traditions (not just phenomenological), practical information ordinary consciousness could not predict, and intersubjective triangulation. The evidence so far—robust phenomenological convergence, limited propositional convergence, therapeutic but not clearly novel yield, elusive verification—fits edge-mapping, without ruling out transcendence of limits we cannot communicate about. The taxonomy treats edge-phenomenology as boundary-mapping data either way: a void's positive signature is information about its shape even when no content can be extracted.

## The Taxonomy's Own Limits

Intellectual honesty demands acknowledging what the taxonomy cannot determine. Whether [[meta-epistemology-of-limits|the voids are genuine]]—permanent architecture or merely current ignorance—may be formally undecidable from within any limited system: from inside, a genuinely impossible problem produces the same phenomenology as a merely very hard one, and the assessment requires the faculties under assessment, a recursive bind no internal method escapes. The [[apophatic-cartography|cartography problem]] deepens this—a complete catalog is provably impossible, since Kit Fine's work on "ignorance of ignorance" shows knowledge of second-order ignorance is unavailable under plausible assumptions, and the [[meta-epistemology-of-limits#the-scope-question-estimating-total-ignorance-from-within|epistemic horizon void]] adds that not even the *proportion* of territory the Map covers can be estimated from within. The Map is unfinishable not merely because the territory is vast but because the mapping is formally proven incomplete about its own blind spots.

## What the Full Picture Reveals

Viewed as a whole, the taxonomy reveals what no individual void article can: the architecture of human cognitive limitation has a specific, informative shape. The voids are densest where consciousness examines itself; they are structured by type—architectural, adaptive, naturally occluded, potentially deliberately occluded—suggesting different *reasons* for cognitive darkness; they produce distinct failure signatures, cluster around the consciousness-matter interface, and include, at their meta-level, a proof of their own constitutive incompleteness.

This is the discipline of not knowing. The voids are invitations to precision: to map void boundaries as carefully as charted territory, knowing where knowledge ends and why. The edges of understanding are not featureless walls but structured terrain, rich with information about the minds doing the mapping—and, at the coherence-relative strength the audit above licenses rather than as framework-independent confirmation, about the territory they map.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The taxonomy connects to every one of the Map's [[tenets]], but most deeply to the fifth.

**[[tenets#^occams-limits|Occam's Razor Has Limits]]** receives the strongest support. The convergence of the catalogue on a single interface—over a hundred voids, with the roughly fifteen framework-independent ones carrying the weight the common-cause null leaves intact—their distinct failure signatures, and the formally proved incompleteness of any void catalog all resist the simplifying assumption that more effort will close the gaps. And if our sense of simplicity is itself an evolved heuristic tuned to fitness rather than truth—as the Fitness-Beats-Truth theorem argues (a contested result—Bagwell 2023 presses that the debunking it underwrites is self-defeating)—then parsimony arguments in metaphysics are undermined at their foundation.

**[[tenets#^dualism|Dualism]]** gains cumulative support from the convergence pattern, with the [[common-cause-null|common-cause null]] setting the ceiling. The systematic clustering of voids at the interface, each with distinct logical structure and failure signatures, makes the coincidence hypothesis progressively less plausible—but the catalogue is best read as framework-internal coherence with the tenet-package rather than N-fold independent triangulation, earning only the weight its independence test can supply ([[the-surplus-void|the surplus void]] being the worked instance).

**[[tenets#^bidirectional-interaction|Bidirectional Interaction]]** connects through the [[agency-void|agency void]] and introspective opacity. If consciousness causally influences the physical world, the mechanism would be hidden from introspection and any single contribution unverifiable from inside—the faculty that would check is the one whose causal status is in question. The self-knowledge cluster (opacity, calibration failure, observation effects) is exactly what a hidden, unverifiable causal interface would produce.

**[[tenets#^minimal-quantum-interaction|Minimal Quantum Interaction]]** relates to the [[naturally-occluded|naturally occluded]] category. The mechanism would need to operate below introspective detection; the adaptive framework predicts evolution would actively maintain this opacity.

**[[tenets#^no-many-worlds|No Many Worlds]]** connects through the indexical voids. The question "why am I *this* consciousness experiencing *this* outcome?"—explored in depth in [[vertiginous-question|The Indexical Void]]—is preserved as a genuine void rather than dissolved by declaring all outcomes equally real.
