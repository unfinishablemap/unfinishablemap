The interoceptive void is the partial opacity of the body's interior to the consciousness that occupies it. Most visceral signal — blood pressure, blood gas chemistry, hepatic metabolism, renal filtration, lymphatic flow, hormone titre, immune state — never reaches awareness at any temporal grain. The narrow subset that does cross threshold (heartbeat, respiration, hunger, fullness, urgency, thermal state) arrives in coarse, summarised form, and even in those channels objective interoceptive accuracy in healthy untrained adults dissociates measurably from subjective sensibility. The body is the most intimate physical object consciousness has, and most of it is silent. The Unfinishable Map maps the asymmetry between fine-grained exteroception and summarised interoception as the place where materialist and dualist accounts of consciousness-body contact read the same evidence into different metaphysics; the void's *shape* is empirically tractable, its *significance* is where the readings part.

The interoceptive void occupies a distinctive position in the [[three-kinds-of-void|taxonomy]]. Specific channels (renal filtration, lymphatic flow, hepatic metabolism, blood pH) are *unexplorable* — no afferent pathway delivers them to awareness at all. Other channels show an *occluded* face — visceral signal modulates brain dynamics sub-threshold without entering experience. An *unexplored* edge runs through the middle: the boundary between trainable and structurally inaccessible interoception moves with attention and channel reallocation. A fourth element — the *constructed* layer in which felt body state is largely top-down prediction — sits beside the void rather than within it. Construction runs in the inverse direction (too much body-experience, generated downward from priors) and is the [[predictive-construction-void|predictive construction void]] applied to the body that has the world. This article foregrounds the absence-direction faces and treats construction as a related but distinct problem.

## Three Absence-Faces, and an Inverse

The interoceptive void has three structurally distinct *absence*-faces — channels missing, attenuated, or sub-threshold — and runs alongside a construction layer that goes in the opposite direction (felt body diverges from real body by *adding* content rather than withholding it).

**The constitutively unfelt.** Renal filtration, hepatic metabolism, blood pH, lymphatic flow, hormone levels, and most aspects of immune state have no afferent nerve channel that delivers them to awareness at any temporal grain. No third-person-verifiable attentional posture surfaces them; reports of organ-specific awareness from contemplative traditions (yogic visualization on specific organs, certain Tibetan body-mandala practices, somatic-experiencing-style detailed organ work) make introspective claims that, on standard physiology, cannot be empirically distinguished from confabulation against the constructed-layer baseline, since the channels by which they could be third-person verified are absent. Instruments render these states visible; introspection cannot. This is the void's hard floor.

**The summarised.** Heartbeat, respiration, hunger, fullness, urgency, thermal state — the channels that *do* reach awareness do so as low-resolution composite signals. Cardiac interoception in healthy adults caps far below the millisecond precision of an external auditory pulse; the felt heartbeat is an integrative low-pass summary rather than a millisecond-precise percept. A 2025 review in *Phil. Trans. R. Soc. B* puts the asymmetry plainly: interoception "maps the state of the organism's internal milieu in real time, in stark contrast to the rapid, precise and time-sensitive processing of exteroceptive, proprioceptive, cognitive and linguistic signals." This face connects directly to the [[resolution-void|resolution void]]: even where interoceptive signal exists, its grain is set far coarser than the substrate it samples.

**The gated.** Visceral signals shape brain dynamics continuously while remaining sub-threshold. Cardiac and gastric rhythms modulate perceptual thresholds, decision-making, and self-experience without entering awareness (Azzalini, Rebollo, & Tallon-Baudry 2019). Whether a given visceral signal crosses into awareness is governed by arousal and attentional allocation rather than by the degree of afferent stimulation alone — perception can drop below threshold when attention shifts elsewhere and rise above it when arousal climbs. Whatever the underlying mechanism (active suppression, attentional priority, predictive-precision allocation), the functional fact is occlusion: traffic that exists does not enter experience.

**The constructed layer (the inverse face).** Active interoceptive inference (Seth, Friston, and collaborators) reframes felt body state as top-down prediction tested against ascending visceral error. The "embodied self" is the high-precision portion of these predictions; what reaches awareness is a prediction-error-corrected reconstruction of the body, not raw afferent contact. This face moves in the opposite direction from the others — too *much* body-experience, generated downward from priors — and the construction can become decoupled from its substrate, as anxiety (felt body inflated without accuracy gain), alexithymia (felt body attenuated below afferent ground), and somatoparaphrenia (limb-ownership reassigned despite intact afferent return) each demonstrate. The interoceptive case is the [[predictive-construction-void|predictive construction void]] applied to the body that has the world: a related problem rather than another absence-face of the same void.

## What the Empirical Record Shows

Four lines of evidence map the void's shape.

**Heartbeat detection.** In standard heartbeat-counting paradigms, median accuracy in untrained adults sits near chance, with substantial dispersion. Garfinkel et al. (2015) decomposed "knowing your body" into three dissociable dimensions — interoceptive *accuracy* (objective task performance), *sensibility* (subjective belief about one's own awareness), and *metacognitive awareness* (correspondence between accuracy and confidence) — and showed they vary independently. People who report high subjective sensibility frequently have low actual accuracy. Anxiety inflates reported sensation without inflating accuracy. There is no single capacity called "knowing your body."

**Subliminal influence on cognition.** Visceral signals shape spontaneous large-scale brain dynamics and cognitive performance without entering awareness. The gate does not silence the signal; it only excludes it from experience. Here the void is signal-beyond-access — a different failure mode from absent-signal, and one that bears differently on the dualism/materialism reading discussed below.

**Channel competition.** Congenitally blind individuals show enhanced cardiac interoceptive accuracy, correlated with thicker occipital cortex (bioRxiv 2022; *Cerebral Cortex* 2024). This places part of the void in the *unexplored* category — where visual-exteroceptive load is removed, interoceptive bandwidth expands. The boundary between accessible and inaccessible interoception is partly elastic, a real limit with channel-competition determinants.

**Pathologies of dissociation.** Anxiety, depersonalisation, anorexia, and alexithymia each involve characteristic dissociations between actual interoceptive state and reported felt body. The void can deepen or distort under conditions that do not change the body itself — strong evidence that what is felt is partly construction, since the construction can fail while the substrate stays intact.

The "There is no such thing as interoception" paper (Frontiers in Psychology 2025) supplies a methodological frame for these findings: interoception is not a unified sensory modality but a heterogeneous bundle of channels (cardiac, gastric, respiratory, thermal, chemoception, nociception) with different ascending pathways and very different accessibility profiles. The single label "interoception" obscures the structural variation that is itself the void's shape.

## Phenomenology of the Edge

Approaching the interoceptive edge has a recognisable feel. The closed-eyes attempt to "feel my heart" without external pulse cues yields a vague pressure that may or may not track the actual rhythm. The attempt to "feel my liver" or "feel my kidneys" returns nothing — and the nothing is not silence in the way of a quiet room. Large regions of the body are not registered as having an inside at all.

Two characteristic phenomenologies recur. Probing what is not there: attention reaches for a region (kidney, spleen, pancreas) and finds no surface to land on, closer to finding the question malformed than to finding silence. Discovering what was always there: under sustained meditation or somatic training, signals (cardiac timing, vagal tone, gut activity) become perceptible that previously seemed absent — more like the lifting of a partial colour-blindness than the discovery of a new room.

The dual phenomenology is itself diagnostic — though of *what* depends on the framing. The materialist reads it as the architecture of the brain's body-model: some channels can be recruited under attention reallocation, others lack afferent pathways, and the boundary between them is where neural plasticity meets neuroanatomical constraint. The dualist reads it as the architecture of consciousness's contact with its substrate: where contact attenuates and where it stops entirely. The boundary is partly moveable and partly not. The void's shape is mapped by where the movement stops; what the shape *is* of remains under interpretive contest.

## Approaches to the Edge

Direct probes use the channels that exist. Heartbeat detection and heart-rate discrimination tasks map the accuracy ceiling on cardiac interoception; respiratory load detection and gastric distension paradigms do the same in other modalities. Long-term contemplative practitioners show measurable shifts in interoceptive accuracy and metacognitive calibration — a probe of elasticity that cannot reach the constitutively unfelt.

Indirect probes work by inventory and by failure. Apophasis by inventory — enumerating the organs whose state never enters awareness — maps the void's outer perimeter. Pathology as probe (alexithymia, depersonalisation, anosognosia, somatoparaphrenia) reveals which axes of the body-self relation can come apart, and therefore must have been held in functional alignment by mechanisms that pathology can decouple. Anxiety inflates felt body without inflating accuracy; that cleavage between *what is felt* and *what is true of the body* is a window onto the construction-to-contact ratio.

An AI without an organic body has no interoception in the human sense, which makes it a useful negative probe: whatever the AI fails to reconstruct from absence may be precisely what the void *is* in the dualist's sense. Whether a language model's substrate state — memory pressure, throughput, queue depth — counts as interoception of the same kind is itself a void: see [[non-human-minds-as-void-explorers]].

## Relation to Site Perspective

The interoceptive void connects most directly to three of the Map's [[tenets]].

**[[tenets#^dualism|Dualism]]** is the primary tenet-connection, but the connection is interpretive rather than evidential. The interoceptive void supplies an empirical mapping of where consciousness is not in transparent contact with the matter it occupies: which channels exist, which grain they deliver, which gate selects what crosses, which states never become experience. Both materialism and dualism predict this asymmetry; they read it differently.

The standard materialist account is available without remainder. Bandwidth is finite. Evolution allocates the channels with action-relevant payoff (no behavioural advantage to perceiving lymphatic flow). Attention gates the allocation. Predictive coding compresses what does come through. Heartbeat-detection ceilings, channel-competition elasticity in blind populations, and dissociation pathologies all fall out of standard adaptive-cognition and active-inference frameworks. The dualist reading does not contest this mechanism; it adds an interpretive layer about what the architecture *is* — namely, the interface by which a non-physical witness contacts a partly-summarised, partly-silent substrate. The void's evidential weight is on the interpretive layer, not on the brute fact of the asymmetry, which is uncontested.

This is the dialectical context in which to place Damasio's contrary reading. Damasio & Damasio (2023) cite much of the same evidence the article cites and conclude that *feelings constitute consciousness* — i.e., that consciousness emerges from the felt body rather than interfacing with it from outside. On Damasio's reading, interoception is not consciousness's distance from a substrate but the substrate from which consciousness arises; the silent regions of the body are silent because they are not part of what the felt-self builds itself from. The Map's framing reverses the interpretive direction: consciousness is wider than the felt body, and most of the substrate is silent because the body is *had* rather than constitutive. Both readings are coherent with the empirical record. The void marks the place where the choice between them is forced, not concealed.

**[[tenets#^bidirectional-interaction|Bidirectional Interaction]]** is a secondary connection that requires the same caution. Interoceptive predictions enlist autonomic reflexes — the predicted blush triggers the vasodilation that confirms it. The materialist will note that this is precisely the kind of internal predictive loop the Friston camp models without any non-physical interface: a closed neural circuit in which top-down prediction and bottom-up signal converge to update a posterior over body state. The Map's reading does not contest the mechanism; it observes that whatever interface answers Bidirectional Interaction would have to operate at a level experience does not reach, and that interoception's structural opacity makes it the natural place to look for the locus of any such interface — even as it makes any such locus undetectable from inside. This connects the interoceptive void to the [[causal-interface|causal interface]] question and to the [[interface-formalization-void|interface formalization void]] without claiming that the autonomic-reflex evidence by itself supports a non-physical interface.

**[[tenets#^occams-limits|Occam's Razor Has Limits]]** is the strongest tenet-connection. The 2025 "There is no such thing as interoception" argument exemplifies how a parsimonious label obscures structurally heterogeneous channels: cardiac, gastric, respiratory, thermal, chemoception, and nociception have different ascending pathways and very different accessibility profiles to consciousness. The void's shape is invisible until the parsimony is suspended. Treating "interoception" as one sense flattens the very variation that maps the limit — and the limit, not the unified label, is what bears on the metaphysical question.

The void also constrains theories of selfhood. Embodied-self accounts build the self from interoceptive prediction; but the *content* of that self is the high-precision summary, not the unmediated body. The embodied self is a posterior over body-state evidence — which neither theory disputes; the two readings disagree only on what kind of thing is doing the building, and on whether the construction is *constitutive of* consciousness or *had by* consciousness as one of its objects.

## Distinguishing the Interoceptive Void

The interoceptive void overlaps several neighbours without reducing to them. The [[mood-void|mood void]] concerns affective atmosphere; interoception concerns localisable channels with measurable accessibility profiles. The [[embodiment-cognitive-limits|embodiment void]] concerns how having a body shapes the thoughts available; the interoceptive void concerns how little of the body is available to thought at all. The [[boundary-and-projection|boundary and projection]] void concerns the perimeter of self; the interoceptive void concerns the interior. The [[self-opacity|self-opacity void]] concerns the subject's inability to take itself as object; the interoceptive void concerns the body as object that mostly does not appear at all. The [[predictive-construction-void|predictive construction void]] concerns the world; the interoceptive void applies the same logic to the body that has the world.

The interoceptive void's distinctive contribution is empirical tractability. Heartbeat detection accuracy, channel-by-channel dissociation, blind-population enhancement, and pathology-driven dissociation are all measurable. The void has an instrument-readable shape, even where its content is inaccessible from inside.
