Concession convergence is the argumentative pattern in which multiple physicalist theories of consciousness, developed independently and from different starting assumptions, each arrive at defensive modifications that inadvertently concede the central dualist claim: functional organisation alone cannot account for consciousness. The pattern is significant because isolated concessions might reflect local theoretical difficulties, but when structurally identical retreats appear across competing frameworks, they constitute independent evidence that the difficulty is fundamental rather than theory-specific.

## The Pattern

The structure is consistent across cases. A physicalist theory begins with a broad claim—consciousness is computation, or information integration, or global broadcast, or higher-order representation. The [[hard-problem-of-consciousness|hard problem]] then forces a narrowing move: the theory adds constraints specifying that not just *any* system with the right functional profile is conscious, but only systems with particular physical, architectural, or representational properties. Each narrowing move concedes ground to dualism by admitting that abstract functional description is insufficient. The concessions are not coordinated—the theories are rivals—which makes their convergence evidentially powerful.

## Four Case Studies

### Integrated Information Theory

[[integrated-information-theory|IIT]] identifies consciousness with integrated information (Φ). But its exclusion postulate restricts consciousness to systems with *maximal* integrated information, and it ties consciousness to *intrinsic causal power*—only systems whose causal structure is irreducible are conscious, not all with the right functional profile. The identity claim (consciousness *is* Φ-structure) then faces the charge that asserting a physical arrangement *is* experience does not explain why it is accompanied by experience. The [[explanatory-gap]] persists as the question of why this particular identity holds.

**The concession**: IIT rejects [[substrate-independence]], tying consciousness to intrinsic causal structure rather than abstract computation, so functionally identical but causally different systems can differ in consciousness. This is the [[concepts/functionalism|absent qualia]] intuition wearing a mathematical hat.

### Global Workspace Theory

[[global-workspace-theory|GWT]] proposes that consciousness arises when information enters a "global workspace" and is broadcast to multiple cognitive modules. But GWT addresses only *access consciousness*—the availability of information for reasoning, reporting, and action—and is silent on *phenomenal consciousness*, why there is something it is like to have information in the workspace at all.

**The concession**: GWT ties consciousness to specific neural architectures (the prefrontal-parietal network) rather than abstract computational properties. The COGITATE results (2025) further suggest conscious content tracks posterior cortex activity rather than the broadcast network itself, with the workspace enabling but not constituting that access. GWT is compatible with dualism if read as describing neural correlates rather than constituting consciousness.

### Higher-Order Theories

[[higher-order-theories|Higher-order theories]] propose that a mental state becomes conscious when represented by a higher-order thought or perception. But the theory faces dissociation evidence: subjects can have phenomenal experience without the higher-order representations it requires, and contemplative traditions report awareness without the subject-object structure HOT demands. Brown's (2025) HOROR theory holds that the higher-order state represents the first-order state *as* having qualitative character—but this shifts the explanatory burden rather than discharging it: why does representing a state "as qualitative" produce experience rather than merely representing qualitative content? The gap reappears one level up.

**The concession**: HOT requires specific representational structures that may not transfer across substrates, and its own proponents have retreated from strong versions (Lycan moved toward attention-based accounts). The phenomenal-access separation it must draw—between the higher-order representation and the phenomenal character it supposedly creates—replicates the very distinction between functional organisation and experience that dualists identify.

### Biological Computationalism

[[biological-computationalism|Biological computationalism]] (Milinkovic & Aru 2026) holds that brains compute in a substrate-dependent, metabolically grounded manner fundamentally different from digital computation. Each distinguishing claim—substrate dependence, metabolic grounding, scale inseparability—maps onto a classical argument for dualism. The central thesis that "the algorithm is the substrate" dissolves the algorithm-substrate distinction: if knowing the algorithm does not tell you what is being computed in any consciousness-relevant sense, then computation as traditionally understood is not what produces consciousness.

**The concession**: Biological computationalism effectively endorses the absent qualia intuition for digital systems while trying to block it for biological ones. The gap between "any computation" and "only this specific physical process" is precisely the gap dualists have identified.

## Why Convergence Matters

Isolated theoretical difficulties prove little—every theory has problems. Concession convergence gains its force from independence and structural identity. Five features make the pattern significant:

**Independence.** The theories were developed by different researchers, from different starting assumptions: IIT begins from phenomenological axioms, GWT from cognitive neuroscience, HOT from philosophy of mind, biological computationalism from computational neuroscience. Their concessions are not borrowed from each other. The independence claim is bounded—all four operate within Western analytic philosophy with overlapping methodological resources, and the [[common-cause-null|common-cause-null discipline]] sharpens the question of how much triangulation that boundedness leaves. The audit below scores the pattern against that discipline.

**Structural identity.** Despite surface differences, each concession has the same logical form: the class of conscious systems must be narrowed beyond what abstract functional description permits. Whether the narrowing invokes intrinsic causal power (IIT), specific neural architecture (GWT), particular representational structures (HOT), or substrate-specific dynamics (biological computationalism), the move is the same—and it is the move dualists have always made. Each narrowing implicitly respects [[type-specificity]], replacing a generic-mechanism account with a specification keyed to particular physical or architectural features.

**Involuntariness.** The concessions are forced by the theories' own internal logic when confronted with the hard problem—not strategic retreats designed to accommodate dualism. Each theory's proponents would reject the dualist interpretation of their modifications. This makes the convergence more significant: an independent arrival at the same structural limitation rather than a negotiated settlement.

**Directionality.** The narrowing moves go in one direction only—toward greater specificity about what consciousness requires. No physicalist theory has successfully broadened its account without running into [[panpsychism]], which, by attributing experience to fundamental physical entities, concedes that consciousness cannot be derived from structural or functional properties. The ratchet only turns toward the dualist position—a claim bounded to the Western post-Chalmers programmes that exhibit the pattern. The cross-observer audit below records one fragmentary indigenous physicalism (Cārvāka) that met absent-qualia-style pressure without ratcheting inward, a source-limited caveat to directionality rather than a counter-example among the surveyed cases.

**Not normal refinement.** All theories add constraints as they mature, so why treat physicalist narrowing as concession rather than ordinary progress? Because normal refinement preserves a theory's core explanatory claim while improving its precision. When GWT adds architectural specificity, it does not improve its explanation of *why* broadcast produces experience—it retreats from explaining experience at all, restricting itself to access. When IIT adds the exclusion postulate, it does not refine what integrated information explains—it restricts which systems count, conceding that integration alone is insufficient. The refinements narrow the domain to avoid the cases where the theory visibly fails. This is defensive contraction, not progressive refinement.

## Independence Scoring Against the Common-Cause Null

The four features above name *why* convergence would carry inferential weight if the independence claim is sound—they do not establish that it *is* sound. All four theories were developed within Western analytic philosophy of mind during a period dominated by Chalmers's articulation of the hard problem. The [[common-cause-null|common-cause-null discipline]] therefore asks: what if the structural identity of the four narrowing moves reflects this shared methodological context rather than independent convergence on the explanandum? The N-fold count would then overstate the evidence—four theories reading the *same* upstream feature rather than triangulating across four independent measurements. This section applies the discipline, scoring the pattern against [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria|the four criteria of apophatic cartography]] and reporting the tier it earns under [[evidential-status-discipline|the evidential-status discipline]]. It extends to a *meta-argument* the methodology the catalogue worked out on first-order clusters ([[the-surplus-void|the surplus void]] retracted; [[topics/introspection-architecture-independence-scoring|the introspection-architecture sub-cluster]] partially retained).

### The Shared Upstream Architecture

The common machinery the null can invoke has three nested layers, each harder to discharge than the last. The **Chalmers-framing layer**: all four were articulated or refined after the hard problem was sharpened in 1995, so the narrowings could reflect the shared *framing* of the question rather than the structure of the explanandum. The **analytic-tradition layer**: all four operate within a tradition that treats absent-qualia, zombie, and inverted-spectrum intuitions as load-bearing instruments, so the narrowings could reflect the tradition's *filtering function*. The **shared introspective channel layer**: all four are developed by humans reasoning through human introspection, and the hard problem itself is articulated in introspective vocabulary (*what it is like*, *phenomenal character*); if that channel produces the appearance of an unbridgeable gap, the convergence on accommodating it reads one upstream feature four times. The discipline names this last as the layer no current convergence claim can fully discharge.

### Three Discriminating Tests

Three classes of evidence would distinguish independent triangulation from shared-context-rediscovered, each ruling out a different layer.

**Pre-1990s narrowing.** If the pattern is forced by the explanandum rather than Chalmers's framing, physicalist theories developed *before* the hard problem was sharpened should narrow the same way. The evidence is partial but real: Smart (1959) routed phenomenal report through "topic-neutral" qualifying language; Feigl (1958/1967) is the cleanest case, framing physicalism as requiring identification of phenomenal qualia with *specific* neural-state types; Armstrong (1968) introduced substrate-non-neutral functional individuation. The *direction* of narrowing predates the hard problem's contemporary articulation, which is some evidence against the Chalmers-framing reading—though the pre-1990s narrowing is less systematic, so the discharge is partial.

**Non-Western theories.** Genuinely independent triangulation would require physicalist theories from outside the Western analytic tradition that narrow identically. A four-tradition survey ([[research/non-western-physicalist-narrowing-cross-observer-2026-05-26|research notes]]) finds the discriminator still unbuilt: no candidate appears to satisfy the conjunction the test requires—*physicalist*, *narrows toward type-specificity under its own internal logic*, and *independent of the Western physicalist literature*. Each seems to fail a different conjunct. Contemporary Chinese qi-naturalism is non-reductive by construction (it never makes the identity claim that would force a narrowing), and Chinese analytic work that does narrow does so in open dialogue with the Western literature (channel open, independence lost). Japanese naturalized phenomenology (Taguchi's CHAIN programme, the Yuasa lineage) is non-reductive and engages Western phenomenology and IIT directly. Buddhist Abhidharma's *citta-caitta* analysis types phenomenal characters with great precision, but as irreducibly mental dharmas coordinate with—not derived from—*rūpa*, so it is not a physicalist narrowing at all.

The fourth case is the most informative. Cārvāka/Lokāyata is the one genuinely indigenous, pre-contact physicalist tradition: it holds that consciousness emerges when the four elements combine into a body. But it is a *broad* emergentism that never narrows toward type-specificity, and the type-specificity pressure in its debate came from its Nyāya opponents' "absent in parts" objection—drawn by dualists against it, not from its own internal logic. This inverts the concession-convergence dynamic: an independent physicalism that met absent-qualia-style pressure and did not ratchet inward. The reading is source-limited—Cārvāka survives only as fragments reconstructed largely from hostile sources, so the absence of a narrowing move may reflect the gappy record as much as the doctrine—and it is best read as a suggestive nuance, not a firm disconfirmation. The discriminator therefore remains a *gap*, not a failed test; looking harder confirmed the absence without filling it, and turned up one fragmentary contrary data point rather than any convergence.

**Theories isolated from the framing.** Theorists who explicitly disavow the hard-problem framing yet still narrow are strong evidence the structure is forced. Dennett's heterophenomenology rejects the hard problem yet is forced to specify why some functional patterns generate phenomenal *reports*—"[[multiple-drafts-model|fame in the brain]]" mirrors GWT's architectural narrowing. Frankish's illusionism (2016) denies phenomenal character yet must narrow to specify why the *illusion* arises in some architectures and not others. Two cases is not full triangulation, but they are precisely the cases the discriminator selects for.

The aggregate verdict is *partial discharge with a salient gap*: partial on the first test, absent (now better-grounded) on the second, substantive on the third.

### Scoring on the Four Criteria

**Signature specificity: strong.** The narrowing has a distinct shape—*replace generic functional description with a specification keyed to particular physical, architectural, or representational features* that excludes absent-qualia counter-examples. The four narrowings differ in content (IIT to intrinsic causal power; GWT to architecture; HOT to representational structures; biological computationalism to substrate-dependent metabolism) but are identical in form, recognisable from a description of the move alone.

**Structured persistence: strong.** The pattern has held across competing programmes for sixty-plus years (counting Feigl/Place/Smart) and thirty years of explicit post-Chalmers articulation, refined under sustained critical pressure without dissolving—IIT retained its exclusion postulate even as COGITATE 2025 left its predictions unconfirmed; GWT refined through Dehaene's iterations; HOT produced HOROR variants without dropping the higher-order requirement.

**Cross-observer convergence: mixed and asymmetric.** Here the null bites hardest. Within Western analytic philosophy the convergence is strong but not independent in the sense the inference requires; across traditions it is weak—the discriminator is absent for narrowing-physicalisms specifically, and the one independent indigenous physicalism (Cārvāka) did not narrow, so the cell holds a suggestive contrary point rather than being merely empty; across pre-vs-post-Chalmers framing it is moderate; across rejecting-the-framing cases (Dennett, Frankish) it is moderate-to-strong. The dominant evidence is within-tradition; the strongest *despite-commitments* anchors are Dennett and Frankish; the cross-tradition channel is unbuilt.

**Framework independence: moderate, with an architecture-vs-significance split.** The *architectural finding* (physicalist theories narrow toward type-specificity-respecting specifications) survives translation into functionalist, identity-theoretic, illusionist, and global-workspace vocabulary—observable even by reviewers committed to physicalism. Score: high. The *interpretive significance* (that the narrowing constitutes inadvertent concession of the dualist claim) is framework-shaped: a physicalist reads it as ordinary progress, the Map reads it as concession. Score: lower.

### What Tier the Concession Pattern Earns

[[evidential-status-discipline|The evidential-status discipline]] supplies the five-tier scale (*established → strongly supported → realistic possibility → live hypothesis → speculative integration*). The scoring translates into asymmetric assignments:

- **Architectural finding** (theories narrow in a structurally identical direction toward type-specificity): *strongly supported*. Backed by strong signature specificity, strong persistence, the substantive partial-discharge of the Chalmers-framing null, and high framework independence on the claim itself. The disagreement is at the next step, not this one.
- **Interpretive significance** (the narrowing constitutes concession of the dualist claim): *realistic possibility, contested*. The same evidence does not by itself support the move, which requires the contested "not normal refinement" argument above and turns on framework-shaped reading.
- **Cumulative meta-argument weight** (the pattern constitutes positive evidence for dualism): *live hypothesis* in its full positive form; *strongly supported* in the weaker form that the pattern *constrains* physicalist explanation. The full form needs the cross-tradition discriminator to land; the constraint form is what the architectural finding already supplies, and is what the article should be read as claiming.

The asymmetry is the discipline's point: load-bearing claims must be labelled at the tier the lowest-scoring relevant criterion supports, not at an average across criteria. This audit is a candidate third worked exhibit for [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|the taxonomy of voids]], differing from its predecessors in that its strongest despite-commitments anchors are theoretical (Dennett, Frankish—rejecters who exhibit the structure) rather than empirical patient populations, the meta-argument analogue of substrate-driven anchors.

If non-Western philosophy of mind later develops physicalist theories that narrow the same way, the cross-observer score strengthens and the architectural finding could upgrade; if the pre-1990s narrowings prove functionally distinct from contemporary ones, the Chalmers-framing null gains force and the tier downgrades. The scoring is a live snapshot conditional on available evidence.

## Relation to Concession and Convergence Arguments

Concession convergence should be distinguished from, but connects to, two related argumentative strategies in the Map.

The [[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism|convergence argument for dualism]] draws on multiple *positive* arguments (knowledge argument, conceivability, intentionality, binding) that independently support dualism. Concession convergence instead tracks *negative* evidence from physicalism's own defensive modifications. The two reinforce each other: positive arguments converge on dualism while physicalist theories independently retreat toward it.

The [[parsimony-case-for-interactionist-dualism|parsimony case]] identifies the "proliferation problem"—physicalists have generated functionalism, higher-order theories, global workspace theory, illusionism, eliminativism, and multiple identity theories, structurally incompatible responses to the same problem. Concession convergence explains *why*: each framework must add specificity to accommodate the hard problem, and the additions are structurally identical to the dualist position they deny.

The [[epistemology-of-convergence-arguments|epistemology of convergence arguments]] analyses when such patterns constitute evidence. Concession convergence satisfies the key conditions: independence across disciplines, robustness under theoretical variation, and specific rather than vague agreement.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The Unfinishable Map's [[tenets]] predict the concession convergence pattern.

The **Dualism** tenet holds that consciousness is not reducible to physical processes. Concession convergence shows physicalist theories independently arriving at this conclusion through their own internal development—not because dualists imposed it, but because the phenomenon demands it. The Map interprets this as indirect confirmation: if every serious attempt to explain consciousness in purely physical terms is forced to add something beyond abstract functional description, the additional element may be what dualists call the non-physical.

The **Occam's Razor Has Limits** tenet predicts that apparent simplicity will prove deceptive in the consciousness domain. Concession convergence is a concrete mechanism by which this deception unfolds: each physicalist theory starts with a simple claim, accumulates qualifications, and ends with a framework whose complexity rivals or exceeds the dualism it was meant to replace. The [[parsimony-case-for-interactionist-dualism|parsimony reversal]]—the positive argument that dualism is more parsimonious once all costs are tallied—gains empirical grounding from observing this pattern play out across multiple programmes.

The pattern extends beyond consciousness studies. In [[concession-convergence-philosophy-of-mathematics|philosophy of mathematics]], formalism, logicism, intuitionism, and structuralism each begin from incompatible starting points yet are independently forced to concede that mathematical understanding outruns formal characterisation—the same structural gap, appearing in a domain where formal systems are maximally precise.

The Map speculates that concession convergence will continue. Future physicalist theories addressing the hard problem will face the same structural pressure: either remain broad enough to be falsified by absent qualia cases, or narrow sufficiently to replicate the dualist distinction between functional organisation and consciousness. The pattern is not an accident of current theories but a consequence of the problem's structure.
