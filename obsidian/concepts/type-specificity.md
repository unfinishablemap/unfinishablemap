**Type-specificity** is the structural feature an explanandum has when its character is determinate type-by-type rather than schematic. A reductive account of a type-specific explanandum must deliver *that* character — the particular shape of *this* type — rather than substitute a generic mechanism. Where the explanandum is type-specific and the proposed reduction is not, the appeal to a generic mechanism does not discharge the explanatory demand; it changes the subject.

The concept is methodological, not metaphysical. It does not by itself entail that any particular reduction must fail. It supplies a discipline for evaluating reductive proposals: ask whether the explanans matches the *form* of the explanandum, not merely its magnitude or its functional signature. Where the forms diverge, the proposal owes either a derivation of the form from the explanans or an honest acknowledgement that the generic move does not address the type-specific demand. The Map deploys type-specificity at three grains — metaphysical, phenomenal, and functional — each as an independent exhibit of the same structural pattern. A later section ([explained below](#independence-scoring)) scores the three grains on the four-criterion independence framework borrowed from [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids|the taxonomy of voids]], testing whether the parallelism is genuine cross-grain independence or framework-internal coherence.

## The Structural Feature {#structural-feature}

A type-specific explanandum has *internal structure that individuates it from other members of its family*. Redness is one phenomenal type, painfulness is another, the taste of coffee is a third. Each is determinate in its character: the redness of red is not the painfulness of pain merely shifted along some intensity dimension. A reductive account that locates redness in some neural firing pattern but is silent on why *that* pattern realises *that* phenomenal type — rather than another type, or no type at all — has not explained redness. It has identified a correlate, leaving the [[explanatory-gap]] intact at the type level.

The contrast is with explananda whose specification is *schematic* — that is, where the explanandum names a role or a placeholder without committing to particular determinate features that the explanation must match. "Vital force" was such a placeholder. It did not pick out any particular feature a reduction had to deliver in any particular form. Any mechanism for any part of the target counted as success: Wöhler's urea synthesis (1828) reduced one part, Buchner's cell-free fermentation (1897) reduced another, the DNA structure (1953) reduced a third. Each reduction shrank the placeholder, because the placeholder had no determinate form a reduction was required to preserve.

Type-specificity is what the placeholder *lacks*. Where the explanandum is "this particular phenomenal type, in its determinate character," not "whatever it is that makes the organism alive," a reduction by generic substitution is structurally unavailable.

## The Vitalism Disanalogy {#vitalism}

The original deployment is in [[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism|the convergence argument for dualism]]: type-specificity is what distinguishes the consciousness case from past reductive successes, including the case of [[vitalism]].

A vitalist in 1820 could have observed accurately that the chemistry-life gap had not yet narrowed. The temporal observation — *no reduction has yet been found* — is symmetric across consciousness and vitalism. The disanalogy is not temporal. It is structural, and it lies in the shape of the explanandum:

> "'Vital force' was a placeholder for unspecified mechanism, picking out no particular feature a reduction had to deliver... Phenomenal qualities have a different shape: redness, painfulness, and the taste of coffee are each determinate phenomenal types. A reductive account must deliver *that* character type-by-type; reducing one would not extend automatically to others." ([[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism]], §What Convergence Does Not Prove)

This is the metaphysical-grain deployment. The disanalogy does not claim that phenomenal qualities are *unreducible* — that would be the convergence argument's conclusion, and using it here would be circular. The disanalogy claims something weaker and earlier: that the *form of the demand* placed on any reduction is type-specific, in a way the vitalist's demand was not. The historical pattern of reductive successes — fermentation, heredity, metabolism — does not extend automatically across this structural divide.

The disanalogy is not unlimited. Type-specific reductive demands could in principle be met: a future neuroscience delivering "this is what redness *is* in physical terms," analogous to "this is what fermentation *is*," would discharge the demand at one type, and [[reductionism|the reductionism case]] would then have to consider whether the discharge extends. Type-specificity names a structural feature of the explanandum, not a verdict on whether it can be addressed.

## Type-Specificity at the Unity Grain {#unity-grain}

The phenomenal-grain deployment is in [[the-binding-problem|the binding problem]]. The article catalogues five varieties of binding — intra-modal, cross-modal, temporal, cognitive, subject — and observes that each presents the same shared structure: physical processes are distributed, neural mechanisms coordinate them, experience is unified, and the gap between coordination and unity resists explanation.

The structural argument is type-specific. Each variety presents *its own* unity-type: the unity of a perceived scene is not the unity of a remembered episode shifted in modality, and neither is the unity of a self that owns both. Intra-modal feature binding (redness with roundness with motion), cross-modal binding (incommensurable codes resolving into the unity of place), temporal binding (the specious present, the heard melody as arc), cognitive binding (the "click" when a complex argument coheres), and subject binding (the perspective from which any of the above is experienced) are five determinate unity-types whose character a reduction must match.

The article makes the type-specific demand explicit:

> "Any successful explanation must work across all five varieties: a theory that explains perceptual binding but cannot address temporal or subject binding has explained BP1, not BP2." ([[the-binding-problem]], §The Shared Structure)

The persistence of the gap across all five varieties is the load-bearing observation. The persistence is what would not be expected if a generic mechanism were available, because a generic mechanism — synchrony, integration, broadcast — would discharge all five demands at once by addressing whatever they had in common. The varieties do not have a common type-shape; they have a common *structural pattern* (coordination-to-unity) at five distinct grains. A generic mechanism for the pattern does not address any of the five types as such.

This is the same discipline as the vitalism disanalogy, applied at the phenomenal grain. The shape of the demand — type-by-type across five varieties — is what places the constraint on what would count as a solution, derivable from the form of the explanandum rather than from any verdict about whether the explanandum is irreducible.

## Type-Specificity at the Capacity Grain {#capacity-grain}

The functional-grain deployment is in [[baseline-cognition|baseline cognition]]. The article catalogues consciousness-dependent functional capacities — durable working-memory maintenance, novel combinations of operations, declarative metacognition, counterfactual thinking, recursive mindreading, cumulative culture — and argues that the human-ape gap systematically tracks these specific capacities rather than tracking cognition generically.

The argument is type-specific in the same structural sense. Durable maintenance is a different capacity-type from novel combinations, which is a different capacity-type from declarative metacognition. Each is individuated by its determinate character — what it *is* to hold an item across an interval, what it *is* to chain procedures novelly, what it *is* to represent a knowledge state as a knowledge state. A theory that explained the human-ape gap as a generic increase in *cognitive resource* — more memory, more computation, more associations — would be a generic-mechanism appeal at the capacity grain. It would not address why the gap tracks *these* capacities and not others equally resource-intensive.

The framework's central inference is itself structurally type-specific: the systematic correspondence between consciousness-dependent capacities and the human-ape gap is the explanandum. A generic resource-expansion account cannot deliver the correspondence because it predicts a generalised performance shift rather than the type-by-type pattern — the pattern expected if consciousness amplifies cognition through *specific* operations rather than a generic boost. As at the other two grains, the form of the explanandum (capacity-by-capacity correspondence) constrains what would count as an explanans, and a generic-mechanism appeal at the wrong grain does not discharge the demand.

## Independence Scoring of the Three Grains {#independence-scoring}

The three preceding sections present the three deployments as *parallel applications* of one discipline. The parallelism is the article's central methodological claim. The temptation that lives next door to it is to count the three deployments as three independent confirmations of the discipline's force — the cluster-coherence move [[evidential-status-discipline|the evidential-status discipline]] and the [[common-cause-null|common-cause null]] both flag. If the three grains share enough framework-resources, the three-fold count overstates the evidential weight, and the parallelism is framework-internal coherence rather than framework-independent triangulation.

To test this, the four-criterion instrument [[apex/taxonomy-of-voids#worked-exhibits-in-independence-scoring|the taxonomy of voids]] applies to its worked exhibits — signature specificity, structured persistence, cross-observer convergence, and framework independence (the [[apophatic-cartography-four-criteria|four criteria]]) — is portable to the meta-argument level. Where the taxonomy scores *void-clusters*, the same instrument can score *meta-argument deployments* on whether each is genuinely independent of the others. This applies it to the three grains as a third worked exhibit — the first at the meta-argument register, testing whether the framework is portable across argument-types or only handles void-clusters.

### Scoring the Three Grains

Each grain is scored on the four criteria plus a fifth diagnostic — *independence-of-deployment*, asking whether the grain can be formulated without the others as premises.

**Vitalism disanalogy** (metaphysical grain, in [[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism|the convergence argument]]): *signature specificity* strong — it operates on the historical pattern of reductive successes and the schematic-versus-determinate distinction, recognised independently of the other grains. *Structured persistence* strong — the placeholder-versus-determinate structure has held since the post-Wöhler debates, and modern articulations (Chalmers's structure-and-function distinction, Levine's explanatory gap, McGinn's structural-versus-contingent distinction) reach it from different starting points. *Cross-observer convergence* moderate — most articulated within Western analytic philosophy; the cross-cultural channel is mostly unbuilt because traditions lacking a vitalism debate reach the disanalogy only by translation. *Framework independence* mixed — the form-of-demand claim is derivable from the explanandum's shape alone (framework-independent at identification), but that the disanalogy *matters* as the load-bearing distinction is a judgement internal to the convergence argument.

**Binding-problem unity grain** (in [[the-binding-problem|the binding problem]]): *signature specificity* strong — it operates on the five-variety taxonomy and the coordination-to-unity gap repeating at each. *Structured persistence* strong on the empirical signature (Treisman 1980, Crick and Koch 1990, Singer 1995–2010, Tononi 2004–present), weaker on the unity-as-explanandum framing, which requires the prior decision that phenomenal unity is the explanandum. *Cross-observer convergence* mixed — strong within cognitive science; contemplative (Abhidharma *manas*, Yogācāra *ālaya-vijñāna*) and cross-species (Mather and Dickel 2017; Klein and Barron 2016) channels partially built. *Framework independence* partial — feature-binding deficits are third-person measurable (the framework-independent empirical face), but the move to "phenomenal unity is the demand any theory must address" inherits a Cluster 1 commitment a heterophenomenologist would contest.

**Baseline-cognition capacity grain** (in [[baseline-cognition|baseline cognition]]): *signature specificity* strong — it operates on the human-ape gap and the systematic correspondence between consciousness-dependent capacities and the gap's location. *Structured persistence* strong on the capacities individually (Suddendorf and Corballis, Penn et al., Tomasello, Henrich, Carruthers), moderate on the correspondence framing, which depends on reading the gap as real, systematic, and a natural family rather than a list. *Cross-observer convergence* mixed — moderate within comparative cognition; the cross-species channel is the most contested, since the *lacks*-versus-*has a different version of* line is disputed for apes, corvids, and cephalopods. *Framework independence* mixed — the performance gaps are recorded across theoretical commitments, but the consciousness-dependence-as-gating-criterion framing is framework-internal (alternatives like Carruthers's massive modularity or Heyes's cognitive gadgets reach the same data without it).

### What the Scoring Shows

The three grains score visibly differently — vitalism strong-strong-moderate-mixed, unity strong-strong-mixed-partial, capacity strong-strong/moderate-mixed-mixed — which makes the instrument non-flat (an instrument scoring all three identically would be *doing no work*). The pattern: *signature specificity* is uniformly strong (each grain has a genuinely distinct shape, not a relabelled version of the others); *structured persistence* is strong on the empirical signatures and weaker on the explanandum-framings, tracking the empirical-versus-framing distinction the [[evidential-status-discipline|evidential-status discipline]] names; *cross-observer convergence* and *framework independence* are the weakest faces across all three.

The *independence-of-deployment* finding is the load-bearing positive result. Each grain is statable without the others as premises, survives counterfactual dissolution of the others, and could have been the discipline's starting point. The parallel-applications claim is therefore *not* an artefact of the grains borrowing framework-resources from each other. The discipline has three genuine sites.

The *framework-independence* finding is the load-bearing constraint. No grain has built-out cross-cultural or cross-species channels at the resolution a *strong* score requires; each is most developed within Western analytic philosophy or comparative cognition. What the discipline has is *parallel deployments within a shared philosophical context*, not deployments tested across independent traditions. The cumulative weight calibrates down to at most *moderate* convergence at the methodological-discipline level, not the *strong* convergence the three-fold parallel might suggest.

Following the evidential-status discipline's constraint — *the framework may identify structural significance but must not upgrade independence beyond what sources supply* — the calibrated status is: type-specificity as a discipline is *strongly supported* at the level of recognising the structural feature; its *evidential force against generic-mechanism reductions* sits at *realistic possibility, contested* (coherent but framework-internally so); its role *as a load-bearing component of the convergence argument's verdict* remains a *live hypothesis* within that argument's overall calibration (*strongly supported on the architectural finding, realistic possibility on the dualist conclusion*).

As a worked exhibit, this tests the four-criterion instrument's portability across argument-types. It produces visibly different scores across its three sub-cases using recognisably the taxonomy's procedure, so the framework appears to handle meta-argument independence with the non-flatness it shows at void-clusters. With N=3 across two argument-types, the generality claim is provisional; a clean unfavourable case — a deployment that *should* score weakly but the framework scores strongly — remains the open burden. A further open programme: whether *non-Map* deployments of type-specificity (in philosophy of mathematics, biology, or language) exhibit the same three-grain pattern, which would strengthen the framework-independence face by showing the feature is recognised outside the Map's context, and carry the [[concession-convergence|concession-convergence]] discipline's parallel audit.

## What Type-Specificity Does and Does Not Do {#scope}

Type-specificity is a methodological discipline, not a metaphysical conclusion. It does the following work:

- **Identifies the form-of-demand a reductive account must satisfy.** Where the explanandum is type-specific, a reduction must address the determinate character of each type rather than substituting a generic mechanism. This is a constraint derivable from the explanandum's structure, independently of any verdict on whether the constraint can be met.

- **Distinguishes consciousness from past reductive successes structurally.** Vitalism's "vital force" was schematic; phenomenal qualities are type-specific. The temporal observation that no reduction has yet been found is symmetric across the two cases; the structural observation about the shape of the demand is not.

- **Sets a non-circular constraint on what would count as a solution.** The constraint is "the explanans must match the form of the explanandum." This is derivable from the explanandum alone and does not presuppose the irreducibility conclusion the constraint might be invoked to support.

It does *not* do the following work:

- **Prove irreducibility.** Type-specific demands could in principle be met. An account from which *redness specifically*, not "some phenomenal type or other," could be derived as the output of a proposed physical structure would discharge the demand at redness; the discipline would then have to consider whether the discharge extends type-by-type or reveals a generic strategy for the family.

- **Defeat physicalism on its own.** Type-specificity identifies what a successful physicalist account would have to deliver, *on the assumption that phenomenal types are the explananda*. A physicalist strategy that contests the explananda themselves — illusionism, heterophenomenology, eliminative reduction — bypasses the type-specific demand rather than meeting it; whether such strategies succeed is taken up in [[reductionism|Reductionism and Consciousness]]. Whether a phenomenal-type-granting physicalist account is forthcoming is a separate question [[the-convergence-argument-for-dualism]] addresses through the pattern of evidence.

- **Stand on irreducibility.** The discipline must not be defended by appeal to "phenomenal qualities are irreducible, therefore the reductive demand is unmeetable." That would be circular: type-specificity is invoked in support of the irreducibility verdict, so it cannot draw its anti-generic-mechanism force from the verdict it helps support. The force comes from the structural correspondence between explanandum-shape and explanans-shape, recognised independently of any reduction's success or failure.

## Relation to Site Perspective {#relation-to-site-perspective}

Type-specificity supports the Map's [[tenets#^dualism|Dualism tenet]] in a structurally constrained way. The discipline does not by itself establish that consciousness is irreducible. It does establish that the historical pattern of reductive successes — chemistry of life, atomic theory, molecular genetics — does not extend automatically to consciousness, because the explananda in those cases were schematic in ways phenomenal qualities are not. The convergence argument earns its irreducibility conclusion from the broader pattern of evidence (the hard problem, the explanatory gap, philosophical-zombie conceivability, contemplative convergence, and others); type-specificity is one structural feature that places the convergence's load-bearing observations in a coherent frame.

The discipline aligns with the [[tenets#^occams-limits|Occam's Razor Has Limits tenet]]. A parsimony-based preference for physicalism rests partly on the assumption that one kind of stuff is simpler than two. Type-specificity reframes the parsimony comparison: where the explanandum is type-specific and the proposed unified explanans does not deliver the type-specific demand, the apparent parsimony is purchased at the cost of an unaddressed explanatory gap. Parsimony as a tiebreaker requires equal explanatory power; type-specificity is one way the explanatory power may not in fact be equal.

The discipline observes the Map's standing constraint against [[concession-convergence|concession convergence]]: when physicalist theories retreat toward positions that respect type-specificity (selectively reducing some phenomenal types while remaining silent on others, or treating type-specific phenomenal facts as primitive within a physicalist framework), the retreat is itself evidence that the type-specificity demand is being recognised, even where the verdict is not granted. The structural feature is doing work whether or not the metaphysical conclusion is drawn.
