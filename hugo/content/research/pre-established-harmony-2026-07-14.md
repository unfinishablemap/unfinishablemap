## Executive Summary

Pre-established harmony is Leibniz's positive doctrine that created substances never causally interact; God so constructs each at creation that its states unfold by its own internal law yet correspond exactly to every other substance's states. It is the purest "distinction-without-link" rival to Tenet 3 (Bidirectional Interaction): mind and body run in perfect parallel with **no** causal link at all. Research confirms it is a strong, canonical foil — but also confirms it is **already covered** across the occasionalism concept page, the interaction-problem history article, and two prior research notes, and that a dedicated page would duplicate a future parallelism page while consuming one of ~8 remaining concept slots against a standing fold directive. ## Key Sources (publisher-verified this session)

### Leibniz's Philosophy of Mind — Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy
- **URL**: https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/leibniz-mind/
- **Type**: Encyclopedia (verified verbatim via WebFetch 2026-07-14)
- **Verbatim characterization of PEH** (three theses, SEP's own numbering): "(1) no state of a created substance has as a real cause some state of another created substance (i.e. a denial of inter-substantial causality); (2) every non-initial, non-miraculous, state of a created substance has as a real cause some previous state of that very substance (i.e. an affirmation of intra-substantial causality); and (3) each created substance is programmed at creation such that all its natural states and actions are carried out in conformity with all the natural states and actions of every other created substance."
- **On mind-body specifically**: apparent mind-body causation is "the mutual conformity or coordination of mind and body ... with no interaction or divine intervention involved."
- **Efficient vs. final causes**: "at the level of bodies ... all occurs according to the laws of efficient causes; whereas with respect to perceptions and appetites ... all occurs according to the laws of final causes."
- **Is Leibniz a substance dualist?**: **No.** SEP: "He does not accept such a dualism"; Leibniz maintained "only one type of substance in the world." (This corrects the textbook filing of PEH under "non-interactionist *dualism*." PEH is a solution the dualist tradition borrows; Leibniz's own ontology is a monadology.)
- **Tenet alignment**: Conflicts with Tenet 3 (denies all creaturely mind→body causation); in tension with Tenet 2 (Minimal Quantum Interaction posits a real, if minimal, causal channel PEH denies outright).

### The two-clocks illustration — Leibniz, Postscript of a Letter to Basnage de Beauval (1696)
- **Source confirmed this session**: The canonical two-clocks passage appears in Leibniz's 1696 explanation to Henri Basnage de Beauval of his "New System of Nature" (1695) — often titled the Postscript of a Letter to Basnage de Beauval, or the "Third Explanation of the New System." Reproduced in standard anthologies: Ariew & Garber, *Leibniz: Philosophical Essays* (Hackett, 1989); Loemker, *Philosophical Papers and Letters*; Woolhouse & Francks, *Leibniz's 'New System'* (OUP, 1997).
- **The three ways two clocks can stay in agreement** (structure doubly confirmed via SEP-adjacent sources and secondary scholarship):
  1. **Mutual influence / "the way of influence"** = interactionism. Leibniz's own illustration here references Huygens's experiment of two pendulum clocks on a shared beam synchronising by transmitted vibration.
  2. **Assistance / "the way of assistance"** = occasionalism — a skilled craftsman continually adjusting the clocks to keep them in agreement (Leibniz's dig: this makes God a perpetual *Deus ex machina*, a "perpetual miracle").
  3. **Pre-established agreement / harmony / "the way of pre-established harmony"** = his own view — each clock so perfectly constructed at the outset that, following only its own laws, it agrees throughout with the other with no further intervention.
- **Purpose**: the illustration exists primarily to distinguish PEH *from occasionalism* — the point is that harmony needs neither brute influence nor perpetual divine adjustment.

Corpus articles currently paraphrase it — that remains the correct, safe treatment.

### Windowless monads — Leibniz, *Monadology* (1714), §7
- **Verified**: *Monadology* §7 states the monads "have no windows through which anything could come in or go out" (standard rendering; "Les Monades n'ont point de fenêtres"). This is the metaphysical ground of PEH: because no monad can be causally affected from outside, inter-substance causation is replaced wholesale by pre-programmed correspondence. Each monad "expresses" the whole universe from its own point of view.
- **Note**: the windowless-monads doctrine is *why* PEH is needed within Leibniz's system — it is not an independent argument so much as the same denial-of-interaction stated ontologically.

## Major Position

### Pre-Established Harmony
- **Proponent**: Leibniz (sole originator; the *term* and the positive doctrine are his).
- **Core claim**: God creates each substance so that its internal states unfold by its own law yet correspond exactly to every other substance's — apparent mind-body interaction is real *correspondence* without any real *causation*. Two clocks synchronised at manufacture, never again adjusted. All coordination is packed into a single creative act (maximally *front-loaded*).
- **Key supports**: the two-clocks trilemma (PEH is the only "way" needing neither brute influence nor perpetual miracle); the windowless-monads doctrine (*Monadology* §7); the completeness of each monad's individual concept (*Discourse on Metaphysics* §§8-9, 1686); the parallel kingdoms of efficient and final causes (bodies by efficient/mechanical law, souls by final/teleological law, never clashing).
- **Relation to Tenets**: Conflicts with **Tenet 3** (no creaturely mind→body causation at all — the purest rival). In tension with **Tenet 2** (denies the very causal channel Minimal Quantum Interaction locates). Engages **Tenet 5** (Occam cuts both ways): PEH trades a local interaction problem for a vastly larger creation-design problem — every future correspondence across all substances must be pre-computed and built in at creation. That is a parsimony *cost*, not a saving.

## Distinguishing PEH from its siblings (the coordination map the future chain must respect)

- **vs. Occasionalism**: both deny creaturely causation and route coordination through God. The difference is *when* God acts. Occasionalism = God acts at **each occasion** (continuous, moment-by-moment; "the way of assistance"). PEH = God acts **once, at creation** (front-loaded; no ongoing traffic). Leibniz's whole polemic is that occasionalism's per-occasion intervention is a "perpetual miracle" and unworthy of God. → owned by `concepts/occasionalism.md`.
- **vs. Psychophysical parallelism**: PEH is the specific **Leibnizian-theistic species** of parallelism (correlation grounded in God's pre-programming). Parallelism is the **general no-interaction structure**, with other homes: Spinoza's dual-aspect monism (one substance, two attributes, *Ethics* IIp7 — parallel *because* identical under two descriptions) and Fechner's de-theologised empirical parallelism. → **If both pages ever exist they will overlap heavily; make one page, not two.** The Spinozist version is the sharper standalone need (it shares vocabulary with the Map's bi-aspectual ontology while denying interaction), which is a further reason PEH does not independently justify its own page.
- **vs. Epiphenomenalism**: epiphenomenalism owns the causal-idleness-of-mind argument. PEH/parallelism *slide toward* epiphenomenalism (mind causally idle w.r.t. body) but reply that the mind is not *inert* — it has real intra-substantial/final-causal life. The Map treats this shared vulnerability in `concepts/epiphenomenalism.md`; do not duplicate.

## Standard Objections (all already present in the corpus)

- **"Perpetual miracle" / God-of-the-gaps** — Leibniz's own charge against occasionalism boomerangs: critics say PEH's *front-loaded* miracle is no less miraculous, just relocated to creation. Leibniz's reply: once established, the harmony is *natural* — each substance runs by its own law thereafter, so nothing miraculous recurs.
- **Collapse toward epiphenomenalism / no genuine causation** — if mind never acts on body, mental states look causally idle w.r.t. behaviour. (Shared with parallelism; developed in the epiphenomenalism article.)
- **Divine front-loading / deism worry** — packing all coordination into the initial act, with no subsequent divine involvement, edges toward a deistic clockmaker God. (A theological objection worth one sentence, not a section.)
- **Parsimony cost (Tenet 5)** — the design burden of pre-computing all correspondences across all substances is enormous; PEH does not buy simplicity, it relocates and arguably enlarges the explanatory debt.
