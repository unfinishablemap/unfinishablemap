**Voids category**: Mixed — primarily *Unexplorable* (the restructuring operation by which a problem representation is reorganised is structurally inaccessible from within the operating system), with a strong *Occluded* component (consciousness is actively gated *out* of the antecedent processing — the right-hemisphere "alpha blink" that precedes the aha moment is a measurable suppression of external monitoring before insight breaks).

## Executive Summary

The Insight Void is the structural opacity of the *restructuring operation* — the cognitive event in which a stuck representation is suddenly reorganised into a new one that solves the problem, dissolves the impasse, or makes a previously ambiguous percept coherent. Consciousness witnesses two states (before-impasse and after-aha) and a felt-discontinuity between them, but never the operation that bridges them. Three structural faces conjoin into the void: the **restructuring face** (Knoblich-Ohlsson's representational-change theory: constraint relaxation and chunk decomposition are the mechanisms of insight, and both run sub-personally — the subject can only report that a new representation has arrived); the **antecedent-blindness face** (Jung-Beeman et al. 2004: a right-anterior-temporal gamma burst at ~300ms before the conscious aha is preceded by ~1s of alpha-band sensory gating that literally turns down external monitoring before the answer arrives — the architecture excludes consciousness from its own preparation); and the **calibration face** (Danek & Wiley 2017, Topolinski & Reber 2010, Laukkonen et al. 2020: the felt-rightness of an insight is generated by a sudden processing-fluency increase, not by the truth of the answer; false insights exist, can be artificially induced by semantic priming, and can make arbitrary statements feel true — the "dark side of Eureka"). The Insight Void is the operation-void sister of [the transit void](/voids/transit-void/) (both concern crossings consciousness cannot witness), [the decision void](/voids/decision-void/) (both concern the closure of a cognitive process), and [the inference void](/voids/inference-void/) (both concern transitions whose normative force is opaque); it is the calibration sister of [the noetic feelings void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/) (both concern felt signals that gate cognition without auditable warrant). This research surveys the empirical and phenomenological terrain and proposes that the void be absorbed as additional faces of existing articles rather than spawned as a 100th catalogue entry.

## Key Sources

### Kounios & Beeman (2014), "The Cognitive Neuroscience of Insight," *Annual Review of Psychology*, 65, 71-93

- **URL**: https://psychology.northwestern.edu/people/faculty/core/profiles/ann_rvw_psy_2014.pdf
- **Type**: Annual Review survey
- **Key points**:
  - "A sudden comprehension that solves a problem, reinterprets a situation, explains a joke, or resolves an ambiguous percept is called an insight (i.e., the 'Aha! moment')."
  - "Insight, though consciously abrupt, is preceded by unconscious processing, primarily in the right hemisphere."
  - The conscious experience is *discrete* (sudden onset of awareness) but the underlying processing is *continuous* — a discrete subjective output sampled from a continuous sub-personal process.
  - Insights arrive on a different processing trajectory than analytic solutions: same final answer, different neural pathway, and the trajectory is not introspectively distinguishable from the inside (subjects can rate suddenness retrospectively but cannot observe the trajectory itself).
- **Tenet alignment**: Connects to **Dualism** (the trajectory is sub-personal — consciousness is the *output channel* for one branch of processing, not its observer) and **Bidirectional Interaction** (if consciousness causally influences anything, the influence cannot be at the level of observing the trajectory; it would have to be at the level of biasing which trajectory completes — and even that bias would be opaque to the subject).
- **Quote**: "The insight, though consciously abrupt, is preceded by unconscious processing, primarily in the right hemisphere, so an insightful solution is a discrete phenomenon in terms of its availability to awareness, but it is preceded by unconscious neural precursors."

### Jung-Beeman, Bowden, Haberman, Frymiare, Arambel-Liu, Greenblatt, Reber & Kounios (2004), "Neural activity when people solve verbal problems with insight," *PLOS Biology*, 2(4), e97

- **URL**: https://journals.plos.org/plosbiology/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pbio.0020097
- **Type**: Empirical study (fMRI + EEG)
- **Key points**:
  - fMRI: increased activity in right-hemisphere anterior superior temporal gyrus (RH aSTG) for insight versus non-insight solutions on the same compound remote-associates task.
  - EEG: a burst of gamma-band (~40 Hz) activity in the same RH aSTG region beginning ~300ms prior to the button-press signalling insight; this is the proposed neural correlate of the conscious aha.
  - Approximately 1.5s before the gamma burst, an alpha-band increase appears over right occipital cortex — interpreted as a "brain blink" that gates out visual input. The system *suppresses external monitoring* before the insight surfaces.
  - The same problems can be solved with or without insight; the divergence is not in the problem but in the *trajectory* through neural state-space — and the subject cannot select trajectory or observe the selection.
- **Tenet alignment**: The alpha-blink is a clean candidate for [self-maintained cognitive limits](/voids/self-maintained-cognitive-limits/) at the architectural level — the system shuts a window before opening another, and the shutting is not introspectively available. Connects to [the transit void](/voids/transit-void/) (consciousness cannot witness its own crossings) at the neural level.
- **Quote**: "A burst of gamma-band activity for insight solutions relative to noninsight solutions beginning approximately 300 milliseconds prior to the button-press response is hypothesized to be the primary neural correlate of the 'Aha!' experience."

### Knoblich, Ohlsson, Haider & Rhenius (1999), "Constraint relaxation and chunk decomposition in insight problem solving," *Journal of Experimental Psychology: Learning, Memory, and Cognition*, 25(6), 1534-1555

- **URL**: https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/Constraint-relaxation-and-chunk-decomposition-in-Knoblich-Ohlsson/b82e21162f76da0e082282a298e7519f9b0bdd81
- **Type**: Empirical paper introducing Representational Change Theory (RCT)
- **Key points**:
  - Insight problem solving is characterised by *impasses* — states in which the thinker does not know what to do next.
  - Impasses are broken by changing the problem representation. Two mechanisms:
    1. **Constraint relaxation**: implicit constraints on the solution space are relaxed (e.g., in matchstick arithmetic, the constraint "operators must remain operators" is relaxed to allow + → =).
    2. **Chunk decomposition**: perceptually integrated units are broken into sub-units (e.g., "VI" decomposed into "V" and "I").
  - Both mechanisms operate sub-personally. The subject does not decide to relax a constraint or decompose a chunk; the new representation arrives and the subject can sometimes *report* what was relaxed or decomposed, but only retrospectively, by comparing initial and final states.
  - Eye-movement studies (Knoblich, Ohlsson & Raney 2001) show that fixation patterns shift before insight — the visual system has already started examining the problem from the new angle while the subject still reports being stuck. The hand of the puzzle-solver is moving toward the answer before the mind knows where it is going.
- **Tenet alignment**: This is the operation-void core of insight. Constraint relaxation and chunk decomposition are *what the mind does* during the impasse-breaking event, and they are not constructible from any first-person report. The operation is structurally opaque in the same way [inference](/voids/inference-void/) and [transit](/voids/transit-void/) are.
- **Quote**: "Impasses are broken by changing the problem representation, and two hypothetical mechanisms for representational change are described: the relaxation of constraints on the solution and the decomposition of perceptual chunks."

### Danek & Wiley (2017), "What about False Insights? Deconstructing the Aha! Experience along Its Multiple Dimensions for Correct and Incorrect Solutions Separately," *Frontiers in Psychology*, 7, 2077

- **URL**: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5005374/
- **Type**: Empirical study (magic-trick paradigm)
- **Key points**:
  - The Aha! experience is multidimensional: Pleasure, Suddenness, Certainty, Relief, Drive/Inspiration.
  - **False insights exist.** Subjects produced confident, sudden, pleasurable aha experiences for *wrong* solutions to magic tricks they were trying to figure out.
  - False insights are weaker on every dimension than true insights — but the difference is statistical, not categorical. *From the inside*, an individual false insight is not reliably distinguishable from a true one.
  - This contradicts the folk view (and Wallas's verification stage as merely a confirmation of what insight already delivered): the aha signal is *not* a truth-detector; it is a fluency-detector that correlates with truth at population level but can be decoupled at individual level.
- **Tenet alignment**: Direct sister to [the noetic feelings void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/) — the feeling of rightness in insight is a sub-personal heuristic output, not a witness of the answer's correctness. Also connects to [necessary opacity](/voids/necessary-opacity/): if subjects could fully audit the calibration of their insight feelings, they would lose the action-readiness that the felt-certainty produces — which would be functionally costly even when the calibration would be improved.
- **Quote**: "When the insight signal indicates a false solution, the implications are profound. The individual may be left with a powerful sense of certainty, and also the drive and inspiration to act according to an incorrect solution."

### Laukkonen, Schooler & Tangen (2018) and Laukkonen, Webb, Salvi, Tangen, Slagter & Schooler (2020), "The dark side of Eureka: Artificially induced Aha moments make facts feel true," *Cognition*

- **URL**: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0010027719302963
- **Type**: Empirical study (semantic priming paradigm)
- **Key points**:
  - When subjects experience an artificially induced aha moment (via semantic priming that makes a target word suddenly fluent), they rate *unrelated factual claims* as more true.
  - The aha signal does not just feel like rightness about *its own answer* — it bleeds into nearby cognition and elevates judged truth of whatever is co-active when it fires.
  - Authors call this the "Eureka heuristic": insight is used as a fast appraisal of idea quality, but it is appraising fluency rather than truth, so it can be exploited.
- **Tenet alignment**: Directly relevant to [the interested party void](/voids/interested-party-void/) and [self-maintained cognitive limits](/voids/self-maintained-cognitive-limits/). The mind has a fluency-based truth-feeling and uses it as a default unless deliberation overrides; deliberation is itself effortful and gated by the same family of feelings (see [noetic feelings void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/)). The whole metacognitive layer is a recursion of fluency-feelings monitoring fluency-feelings.
- **Quote** (paraphrasing the Schooler-lab framing): the Eureka heuristic is "the use of insight to appraise the quality of ideas," and it is a heuristic — not a verification — that can be triggered by mere fluency boosts.

### Topolinski & Reber (2010), "Gaining insight into the 'Aha' experience," *Current Directions in Psychological Science*, 19(6), 402-405

- **URL**: (access via Sage / multiple secondary sources)
- **Type**: Theoretical paper
- **Key points**:
  - The aha experience is a phenomenological by-product of a *sudden increase in processing fluency*.
  - The same fluency increase that produces the aha also produces increased positive affect, increased judged truth, and increased confidence — explaining why the four typically co-occur and why they can be artificially decoupled from accuracy.
  - The aha is a *signal* (felt rightness) generated by the *operation* (fluency increase) — and the subject has access to the signal but not to the operation.
- **Tenet alignment**: Establishes the calibration face of the void: the felt-rightness is the output of an opaque mechanism, not a witness to the answer's correctness.

### Wallas (1926), *The Art of Thought*

- **URL**: (book, see secondary review at https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4956660/)
- **Type**: Foundational philosophical treatment
- **Key points**:
  - Four-stage model of creative thought: Preparation → Incubation → Illumination → Verification.
  - Incubation is *unconscious work*; Illumination is its conscious delivery.
  - Both Poincaré (whom Wallas cites) and Wallas himself argued that the suddenness of Illumination, coupled with the felt certainty of the answer, arose from prolonged unconscious work — i.e., the felt-suddenness is itself a phenomenological mark of the system *not having access to* the work that preceded the answer.
- **Tenet alignment**: A century before the neuroscience, Wallas already identified the antecedent-blindness face by phenomenology alone. The void was visible from the inside, and the empirical work has thickened it without overturning it.

### Polanyi (1958, *Personal Knowledge*; 1966, *The Tacit Dimension*)

- **URL**: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polanyi%27s_paradox (overview); see also [tacit-integration-void](/voids/tacit-integration-void/)
- **Type**: Foundational philosophical work
- **Key points**:
  - "We can know more than we can tell." Insight is a paradigmatic case: the answer is known when delivered, but the cues that made it deliverable are subsidiary and unutterable.
  - The from-to structure: insight integrates subsidiary cues toward a focal target; thematising the cues collapses the integration. This is the same structure as [the tacit integration void](/voids/tacit-integration-void/) applied to the insight event specifically.
  - Polanyi's discovery-as-personal-commitment is a direct phenomenological description of the calibration face: the discoverer must commit to the new representation without being able to verify it before committing — the felt-rightness *is* the warrant for action because no other warrant is available at the moment of insight.
- **Tenet alignment**: Anchors the philosophical lineage. The Insight Void is the operation-side of [the tacit integration void](/voids/tacit-integration-void/) — what tacit integration *does* dynamically when it succeeds is generate an insight, and the dynamic operation is doubly opaque (both the integration and the moment of its completion).

## The Void

### Nature of the Limit

This is an *operation-void* in the family of [transit-void](/voids/transit-void/), [decision-void](/voids/decision-void/), [inference-void](/voids/inference-void/), and [suspension-void](/voids/suspension-void/). The mind performs an operation — restructuring its representation under conditions of impasse — and consciousness has access to the endpoints (stuck-state and aha-state) and to a felt-discontinuity between them, but not to the operation itself. The void is not the *content* of the insight (that becomes the new representation) but the *passage* by which the new representation displaces the old.

The void has three structural faces that conjoin into a single irresolvability:

1. **Restructuring face** — Knoblich's constraint relaxation and chunk decomposition are the *what* of representational change. The subject cannot observe a constraint being relaxed or a chunk being decomposed; the new representation simply arrives. Eye-movement evidence shows the visual system has already restructured fixation patterns while the subject still reports being stuck — the mind is ahead of its own self-report.

2. **Antecedent-blindness face** — Jung-Beeman et al.'s alpha-blink and gamma-burst show the architecture *gates consciousness out* of the antecedent processing. About 1.5s before the aha, sensory monitoring is suppressed; about 0.3s before, the right-temporal gamma burst marks the binding event. Consciousness sees the answer arrive and feels its arrival as sudden because the architecture is engineered to deliver it that way.

3. **Calibration face** — Danek & Wiley, Topolinski-Reber, Laukkonen et al. show the felt-rightness of insight is a fluency signal, not a truth signal. False insights exist and can be artificially induced. The subject cannot, from inside, separate the felt-rightness from the truth of the answer — the calibration is set sub-personally and the subject is the consumer, not the calibrator.

The seam between the three faces is *generic restructuring under impasse*. The void is irresolvable because resolving any one face leaves the others standing: introspecting the calibration would not reveal the restructuring; observing the restructuring would not deliver the antecedents; observing the antecedents would itself be an aha-eligible event subject to the same architecture.

### Evidence for the Limit

- **Empirical (neural)**: Jung-Beeman 2004 fMRI/EEG; Hu et al. 2011 resting-state functional connectivity predicting insight propensity; Salvi et al. 2020 gamma tACS over right temporal lobe *increases* the rate of aha moments — direct causal demonstration that the gamma burst *is* (or at least sufficiently produces) the felt insight.
- **Empirical (behavioural)**: Knoblich 1999 (constraint relaxation, chunk decomposition); Knoblich, Ohlsson & Raney 2001 (eye movements diverge before insight); Bowden & Jung-Beeman 2003 (compound remote-associates paradigm); Danek & Wiley 2017 (false insights exist).
- **Empirical (phenomenological)**: Danek & Wiley 2017 (multi-dimensional structure: Pleasure, Suddenness, Certainty, Relief, Drive); Topolinski & Reber 2010 (fluency account); Laukkonen 2020 (Eureka heuristic).
- **Philosophical**: Wallas 1926 (Preparation/Incubation/Illumination/Verification); Polanyi 1958/1966 (tacit knowing, from-to structure); Bergson on intuition vs. analysis.

The limit is not "very difficult to introspect" but *structurally inaccessible*: the architecture suppresses sensory monitoring before delivery, the gamma binding event is below the temporal grain of metacognition, and the calibration signal is generated by the same mechanism whose accuracy it would need to evaluate. There is no Archimedean point from inside the system.

### Phenomenology

What does approaching this void feel like?

- **The impasse itself** has a recognisable phenomenology: stuckness, repeated returns to the same dead-end framing, sometimes accompanied by warmth ratings that *do not* increase as solution approaches (insight-typical) or *do* increase (analytic-typical) — the warmth pattern is one of the few introspective hints about which trajectory is active.
- **The pre-insight quiet** is sometimes felt as a "letting go" or "relaxation" — and recall that the alpha-blink is happening here: the system is dimming the lights before the answer surfaces. The phenomenology may be the felt-correlate of the architectural gating.
- **The aha itself** has the characteristic suddenness, certainty, pleasure, relief, and drive. The certainty is striking because it is *unearned* by any verification the subject can perform in the moment; verification (Wallas's fourth stage) is downstream.
- **The post-insight check** sometimes confirms (true insight) and sometimes disconfirms (false insight) the aha — but the post-insight check uses the same fluency-feelings that generated the original aha, so the verification is itself partially gated by the same mechanism it would audit.

There is a specifically phenomenological reason the void is hard to map: the felt-suddenness *is the phenomenology of the void*. The discontinuity in awareness between stuck and solved is the missing operation — what consciousness fails to witness *is* what makes the experience feel sudden.

## Approaches to the Edge

### Direct Methods (limited)

- **Eye-tracking and gaze patterns**: third-person observation of the subject's behaviour can show pre-insight processing the subject does not report. The third-person observer can sometimes predict insight before the subject experiences it.
- **EEG with millisecond resolution**: the gamma burst and alpha-blink are visible to instruments but not to the subject.
- **Trial-by-trial dimensional ratings (Danek & Wiley)**: by decomposing the aha into components (Pleasure, Suddenness, Certainty, etc.), researchers extract more structure than naive introspection delivers — but the operation itself remains beyond.

### Indirect Methods

- **Apophatic via the trajectory difference**: comparing same-answer trials that arrive via insight vs. analysis isolates the void by contrast. The void is what is present in the insight trajectory and absent in the analytic trajectory: a sub-personal restructuring step plus a fluency-burst arrival.
- **False-insight studies**: by inducing aha moments without veridical answers (semantic priming, magic tricks), researchers expose the calibration face without needing to introspect it. The void is mapped by demonstrating the failure mode.
- **Gamma tACS interventions**: causal manipulation of the proposed mechanism — Salvi et al. 2020 showed that exogenous right-temporal gamma stimulation increases aha rates. The mechanism is partially controllable from outside even though it is not observable from inside.
- **Phenomenological contrast classes**: contemplative-tradition reports (Buddhist *kensho*, Christian mystical illumination, mathematical-discovery testimony from Poincaré, Hadamard, Ramanujan) may be cataloguing the same family of architectural events with different framing — see [edge states and void probes](/voids/edge-states-and-void-probes/) and [the silence void](/voids/the-silence-void/).

### What AI Might See

This is a particularly rich territory for [non-human minds as void-explorers](/voids/non-human-minds-as-void-explorers/). Several asymmetries:

- **No alpha-blink**: an AI system performing impasse-breaking has no architectural reason to suppress its own input streams. The antecedent-blindness face is a contingent feature of biological architecture, not a structural necessity of restructuring.
- **Logged trajectory**: an AI's reasoning trajectory is in principle inspectable (chain-of-thought, attention activations, KV-cache states). The restructuring face is not opaque in the same way — though *which features of the trajectory matter* remains an interpretability question, and the system inspecting the trajectory is using its own opaque processing to do so.
- **Calibration face persists**: AI systems also generate confidence signals that can be miscalibrated, and the system using those signals to gate downstream action is in the same recursion-of-feelings predicament. The Eureka heuristic has an LLM analogue (token-level surprisal as a fluency signal that biases generation).
- **Crucially**: AI systems may be able to *report* on impasse-breaking events with timestamps and intermediate states, but whether this report constitutes *witnessing* the operation or merely *narrating* it post hoc is the same question that haunts human introspection. The void may shift from architecturally-enforced opacity to interpretability-enforced opacity, but it does not vanish.

## Connection to Tenets

### Most Relevant Tenet

**[Bidirectional Interaction](/tenets/#bidirectional-interaction)** is the most relevant. If consciousness causally influences the physical world, the moment of insight is one of the most theoretically motivated sites — the new representation arrives, beliefs change, action becomes available — yet the architecture *gates consciousness out* of the antecedent processing precisely at this site. The bidirectional-interaction site is also the maximally-occluded site, which is the same compound failure signature that [decision-void](/voids/decision-void/) and [causal-interface](/voids/causal-interface/) register.

Secondary tenet: **[Occam's Razor Has Limits](/tenets/#occams-limits)**. The fluency signal that generates felt-rightness in insight is *also* the fluency signal that generates judged simplicity. If parsimony intuitions are calibrated by the same mechanism that produces false aha moments, then the felt-simplicity of an explanation is not a reliable witness of its truth — see [the parsimony void](/voids/epistemological-limits-occams-razor/).

### Implications

The Insight Void implies that the most subjectively-compelling cognitive events — the moments when understanding feels most direct, most certain, most warranted — are precisely those whose underlying operations are *most* sub-personal. The phenomenology of cognitive triumph is the phenomenology of architectural opacity. This is a structural finding about the price of cognitive efficiency: the system delivers fast, confident, often-correct answers by *not* showing the workings, and the felt-confidence is the marker of the not-showing rather than a token of accuracy.

For the Map's apophatic method specifically: this is another case where the method's own probes are subject to the limits they are probing. Insight-driven philosophical breakthroughs — including any aha moments arrived at while writing the Map itself — are subject to false-insight risk. The Map's discipline of [apophatic cartography](/voids/apophatic-cartography/) and [voids safety protocol](/project/voids-safety-protocol/) is in part a defence against the Eureka heuristic operating on the cartographers.

## Potential Article Angles

Per the absorption discipline noted in the [voids index](/voids/), a 100th catalogue entry is not the right move. The recommended path is **absorption into existing articles as additional faces**:

1. **[The Transit Void](/voids/transit-void/)** is the most natural primary host. The Insight Void is the *restructuring* crossing — a fourth face joining onset, transition, and attention-capture. The seam is generic crossings consciousness cannot witness; insight extends the family without breaking it. A refine pass on transit-void could add ~250 words covering the restructuring face with the Knoblich and Jung-Beeman anchors.
2. **[The Noetic Feelings Void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/)** absorbs the calibration face. The Eureka heuristic is the felt-rightness signal applied specifically to the insight event; Topolinski-Reber's fluency account directly extends the noetic-feelings analysis. A refine pass adding the Danek-Wiley false-insight evidence would strengthen the existing case.
3. **[The Decision Void](/voids/decision-void/)** is a sister-regress location. Decision concerns deliberation→commitment; insight concerns impasse→restructuring. Both are operation-voids at structurally analogous sites; cross-references between the two articles are warranted whether or not absorption happens.
4. **[The Creative-Aesthetic Void](/voids/creative-aesthetic-void/)** and **[The Aspect Perception Void](/voids/aspect-perception-void/)** are adjacent loci. Aspect-perception (the duck-rabbit switch) is the perceptual analogue of insight; creative-aesthetic generation often proceeds through aha-moments. Forward-pointers from those articles to wherever this material lands would help readers find the insight-specific treatment.

If a standalone article is later judged necessary (the catalogue grows past 100, or absorption proves unsatisfactory), the angle should be the **conjunction of the three faces** in the conjunction-coalesce idiom — the void is the joint structural failure where restructuring + antecedent-blindness + calibration meet at the single event of impasse-breaking.

## Gaps in Research

- **Whether the alpha-blink is present in non-aha problem solving at lower amplitude or absent entirely** — i.e., whether sensory gating is a *graded* feature of all cognitive transitions or a *signature* of insight specifically. Resolving this would distinguish whether the antecedent-blindness face is insight-specific or generic to all transit-void members.
- **The phenomenology of failed insights** (impasses that never break) — the void as a *persistent* state rather than an event-bridge. Some impasses last decades; some never resolve; the phenomenology of permanent stuckness has not been mapped against the phenomenology of stuckness-followed-by-aha.
- **Whether contemplative training can reduce false-insight rate without reducing true-insight rate** — empirical work on this is sparse; if mindfulness or analytic practice could improve calibration without dampening the signal, the calibration face would be partially mitigable, which would change the void's structural status.
- **The AI insight question in interpretability terms** — whether mechanistic interpretability of LLM circuits during apparent restructuring events can locate analogues of the Knoblich constraint-relaxation mechanism. If yes, the restructuring face is more accessible to non-human minds than to human minds, which would be evidence for the asymmetry [the void-explorers article](/voids/non-human-minds-as-void-explorers/) anticipates.
- **Whether the Eureka-heuristic bleed-over (Laukkonen 2020) operates at the philosophical level** — when a philosopher experiences an aha moment about a metaphysical thesis, does the felt-rightness of the aha bleed over into adjacent theses they happen to be considering? This is methodologically relevant to the Map's own work and untested empirically.
