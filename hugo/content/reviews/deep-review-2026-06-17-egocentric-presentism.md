2. **Conitzer (2020), "The Personalized A-Theory…," *Dialectica* 74(1) — pages + missing DOI.** Cited **1–29** (author's preprint pagination); published article is **3–31**. Fixed pages and added DOI 10.48106/dial.v74.i1.02. (Same fix applied upstream in the source research note.)

4. **"Communicability objection" attribution — mischaracterization.** The article attributed a "communicability objection" (whether the privileged property can be coherently *stated*) to Markosian and McDaniel. Reading both reviews in full: neither frames their critique that way. McDaniel presses that the evidence supports presence being a **two-place relation, not monadic**; Markosian presses that the inside/outside intuitions can be **reconciled other ways** and that the view supplies **no mechanism** for why presence attaches to one subject. Rephrased: the communicability objection is presented as the standard (unattributed) objection, and McDaniel's and Markosian's actual arguments are now stated accurately.
