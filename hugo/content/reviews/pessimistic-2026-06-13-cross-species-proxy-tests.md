## Executive Summary

This is a strong, unusually self-disciplined article — its central move (the four-face asymmetry, two faces accessible / two principled-blocked) is honest and well-calibrated, and it pre-empts most obvious objections by foregrounding the Le Pelley and Carruthers discounts. The most serious pessimistic findings are (1) a load-bearing **epistemic/metaphysical equivocation risk** around the phrase "principled-blocked" — the article slides between *no current paradigm reaches this* (epistemic/contingent) and *the design cannot in principle reach this* (constitutive), and the stronger reading does most of the rhetorical work without an explicit bridging argument; (2) a **duplicate reference** (entries 16 and 17 are byte-identical), a mechanical defect; and (3) a possible **mode-of-engagement** softness where the Carruthers interpretive-sensory-access rival is described but not refuted, with the article's own framing leaving ambiguous whether it concedes or contests. Severity is mostly Low–Medium; this is a high-quality page.

## Critiques by Philosopher

### The Eliminative Materialist (Churchland)
The whole edifice is built on importing a *folk-psychological* taxonomy ("origin-tag failure," "verdict-feeling inversion," "fabricated content," "narrative-construction") and then hunting for animal behaviours that instantiate it. The cluster's "four faces" are not natural kinds discovered in the nervous system — they are categories of *report* inherited from human introspective vocabulary, and the article admits the proxy channel matches "a general metacognitive-monitoring signature better than the cluster's specific report-versus-mechanism discrepancy" (§"What the Proxy-Test Channel Can and Cannot Score"). That admission is fatal: if the proxy can only reach a *general* monitoring signature, then the specifically-introspective content is being supplied by the theorist, not the rat. The OFC confidence work (Kepecs) is neuroscience that will, on this view, eventually *replace* the talk of "noetic feelings" rather than vindicate it as a cross-species architectural signature.

### The Hard-Nosed Physicalist (Dennett)
The article's best instinct is heterophenomenological — it treats the wager as a behaviour requiring framework-laden interpretation rather than a transparent confidence-report — but it does not go far enough. If the human "report-versus-mechanism discrepancy" is itself just the interpreter spinning a story (which the narrative-void material elsewhere on the Map concedes), then the cross-species programme is not extending a real architectural feature into animals; it is extending an *interpretive artefact* of the human case. Carruthers' framework (cited at §"Despite-Commitments") makes exactly this point, and the article quietly grants that under Carruthers the convergence is "a because-prediction confirmation of the interpretive theory rather than independent triangulation." This is a much larger concession than the calm prose admits: it means the channel may carry *nothing* about consciousness-architecture, only about model-free magnitude signalling.

### The Quantum Skeptic (Tegmark)
Out of scope — the article makes no quantum claims and correctly stays clear of them. No attack to mount, which is itself to the article's credit.

### The Many-Worlds Defender (Deutsch)
Likewise no MWI surface. The only adjacent vulnerability: the article leans heavily on "indexical"/source-tag individuation ("was *this* memory acquired *here* or *there*"), but never defends why a source-tag discrimination is evidence of a *witnessed* origin-judgement rather than a third-person-describable discriminative capacity that an MWI-style deflation of the first-person would dissolve entirely.

### The Empiricist (Popper's Ghost)
The phrase that should worry a falsificationist is **"principled-blocked at full strength."** Narrative-construction is declared in-principle inaccessible because "without language-grade output the production cannot occur in its native form." But that is an argument from *current behavioural bandwidth*, dressed as an argument from principle. A novel coordinated-deception or multi-step planning paradigm could in future implement an interpretive sequence — the article even half-concedes this in §"Four-Face Proxy Architecture" ("the bandwidth required approaches the bandwidth at which verbal report re-becomes the cleanest tool"). So which is it: contingently unreached or constitutively unreachable? The article needs to state the *falsification condition* — what observation would convert a "principled-blocked" face to "accessible" — or admit the block is contingent. Without that, "principled-blocked" is doing unearned metaphysical work.

### The Buddhist Philosopher (Nagarjuna)
The entire project presupposes a *witness* whose bounded access produces the four-face signature — the "bounded-witness architecture" appealed to throughout. But the cluster's own evidence (confabulation, narrative-construction-without-cause) is precisely evidence that *there is no unified witness* — only a sequence of post-hoc constructions with no one home to be bounded. The article reifies a "monitoring substrate whose output drives confidence behaviour" and treats its dissociations as the *limits of a witness*; the emptier reading is that there is no witness, only the dissociations. The Map's own narrative-void material is closer to this than the proxy-test framing acknowledges.

## Critical Issues

### Issue 1: "Principled-blocked" equivocates between contingent and constitutive
- **File**: cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests.md
- **Location**: §"The Principled-Blocked Region" and §"Four-Face Proxy Architecture" (the "narrative proxy" paragraph)
- **Problem**: The article's headline finding — the four-face *asymmetry* — rests on the claim that two faces are "principled-blocked." But the supporting reasons are bandwidth/output-format reasons ("a rat cannot fabricate a *story*… it can only press or not"; "without language-grade output the production cannot occur in its native form"), which are arguments about the *current* expressive bandwidth of available behavioural channels, not about an in-principle barrier. The same paragraph then concedes the narrative face "approaches the bandwidth at which verbal report re-becomes the cleanest tool" — i.e. it is a continuum, not a wall. This is the epistemic/metaphysical equivocation pattern: an *epistemic* limit (we have no paradigm with enough behavioural bandwidth) is recruited to assert a *constitutive* one ("principled-blocked at full strength"). The load-bearing word ("principled") asserts the metaphysical reading; the evidence supports only the epistemic one.
- **Severity**: Medium
- **Recommendation**: `refine-draft` pass that either (a) supplies the bridging argument for why language-grade sentential/narrative content is *constitutively* unavailable to non-verbal channels (not merely absent from present paradigms), or (b) downgrades "principled-blocked" to "bandwidth-blocked on present designs / no current paradigm reaches this" and states the falsification condition — the kind of behavioural paradigm that would move the face from blocked to accessible. The "narrative barely or not at all" / "confabulation weakly" framing at §"The Principled-Blocked Region" close already gestures at a graded reading; the fix is to make the whole section graded-and-contingent rather than two-faces-walled-off.

## Counterarguments to Address

### "The cluster's four faces are a human-report taxonomy projected onto animals"
- **Current content says**: The four faces (source-attribution, noetic-feelings, confabulation, narrative) are an architectural signature that proxy tests can carry across the verbal-species boundary at differential strength.
- **A critic would argue**: The faces are categories of *human verbal report*; mapping animal behaviours onto them assumes the very architectural-independence the test is meant to detect. The article half-concedes this (proxies match "a general metacognitive-monitoring signature better than the cluster's specific… discrepancy").
- **Suggested response**: The article's existing per-face honesty (signature specificity "partial") is the right material — but it should state explicitly that the *general* monitoring signature, not the *specific* cluster discrepancy, is what the proxy channel mostly carries, and that this is a real limit on the channel's contribution to the cluster's case, not a hedge.

### "Principled-blocked is contingent, not principled"
- **Current content says**: Confabulation and narrative faces are "principled-blocked at full strength" for non-verbal species.
- **A critic would argue**: These are bandwidth limits of present paradigms, and the article itself says the narrative face "approaches the bandwidth at which verbal report re-becomes the cleanest tool." A future deception or multi-step-planning paradigm could reach it.
- **Suggested response**: State the falsification/upgrade condition (what paradigm would unblock a face) and downgrade "principled" to "bandwidth-blocked on present designs" unless a constitutive argument is supplied. (See Issue 1.)
