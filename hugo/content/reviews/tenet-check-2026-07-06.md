- **Tenet in tension**: 5 (Occam's Razor Has Limits), at its junction with 2 (Minimal Quantum Interaction)
- **Issue**: Several quantum-interface articles motivate *where* / *how minimally* consciousness acts partly by a least-disruption / smallest-interaction consideration. Read carelessly this is the move Tenet 5 warns against — leaning on simplicity in a domain of incomplete knowledge. The tenets page itself pre-empts the misreading: its "Minimality as empirical constraint, not truth-tracking" clause distinguishes Tenet 2's empirical-constraint minimality from the truth-tracking parsimony Tenet 5 disarms.
- **Why this is a NOTE, not a finding**: in each located instance the corpus names the tension and downgrades the move to a defeasible heuristic. The newest exemplar, [concepts/ensemble-level-epiphenomenalism.md](/concepts/ensemble-level-epiphenomenalism/), treats the strictest ("Born-preserving corridor") reading of minimality as a live structural fork with an owned empirical cost rather than a settled truth-claim: "epistemic humility about an unfalsifiable preferred answer is a virtue, but it does not supply the empirical content that answer lacks."
- **Recommendation**: No action required. Future checks should confirm new or condensed quantum-interface articles preserve this self-flagging rather than letting the minimality move read as positive evidence.
