## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
Chalmers is most at home in `neural-refresh-rates` and `grain-mismatch`, which keep the hard problem load-bearing on the temporal axis. `neural-refresh-rates` names the *smoothness problem* precisely — discrete ~10 Hz sampling vs unbroken phenomenal flow — and refuses the easy illusionist dissolve: the move "dissolves the smoothness problem only by dissolving phenomenal consciousness generally — it inherits the full weight of the explanatory gap." `grain-mismatch` sharpens this into a *structural* (not merely epistemic or modal) anti-identity argument: homogeneous phenomenal qualities and discrete neural firing "cannot be identical because they possess contradictory characteristics," and the article is careful to mark this as logically independent of the knowledge argument and zombie conceivability.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
Stapp's home is `quantum-neural-timing-constraints`, which lays out the Zeno mechanism's distinctive timing logic ("the Zeno mechanism sidesteps the coherence gap by not requiring continuous coherence… many snapshots rather than filming continuously") and grades each quantum proposal against confirmed coherence times in a clean table. Crucially it does not overclaim: it flags that Stapp's ~1 kHz observation requirement is *testable* ("the 40 Hz gamma band is too slow; look for higher frequencies"). `consciousness-bandwidth-architecture` complements this by locating the *format* problem at the same interface — Stapp's "is pattern P present?" question-and-answer split is offered as a candidate for how intentional content interfaces with physics without encoding physical detail, immediately marked "Whether this specific mechanism works remains contested."

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
Nagel would praise `neural-refresh-rates` for centering the first-person datum that physical description omits — the *felt* continuity of the stream (James's "rainbow on the waterfall," Bergson's *durée*) — and `voluntary-attention-control-mechanisms` for taking the *phenomenology of willing* seriously as a distinct explanandum, the felt difference between a self-initiated and an externally-cued attention shift being mirrored in a real neural signature (frontal theta present in the former, absent in the latter).

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
Light-touch in this cohort (its Whiteheadian member, `prehension`, was reviewed in -26d), but `neural-refresh-rates`' two-stage / time-slice framing (Herzog et al. 2020 — continuous unconscious processing crystallising into discrete conscious "time slices") resonates with the process intuition that experience comes in occasions rather than as an undifferentiated flux. The article uses this as structural illumination of the smoothness problem, not as evidence for any pan-experiential thesis — the correct register.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
Kane is well-served by `quantum-neural-timing-constraints`' Schurger resolution — the reinterpretation of Libet's readiness potential as stochastic noise-accumulation rather than an unconscious decision that precedes awareness ("takes the sting out of the Libet argument… consciousness contributes to *when* threshold-crossing becomes action") — and by `voluntary-attention-control-mechanisms`' bidirectional frontoparietal theta coherence, presented as "ongoing dialogue" rather than one-shot top-down command. Both treat agency as a real, neurally-distinguishable contribution without inflating the evidence into proof of libertarian freedom.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
`consciousness-bandwidth-architecture` earns Mysterian respect for naming the residual it cannot close: even granting hierarchical selection, "a residual problem remains: how does intentional content… interface with physical mechanisms structured by forces and timings?" and conceding "the detailed mapping from phenomenal states to physical observables remains to be worked out." `grain-mismatch`'s introspective-opacity section (Lee 2023) is pure Mysterian epistemic humility: introspection "cannot inspect its own resolution," so neither dualist nor physicalist can settle the continuity question from the inside — "the grain of consciousness is opaque to the consciousness whose grain it is."

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
This cohort sits largely outside Birch's load-bearing zone (no minimal-organism or boundary-case consciousness claims), and the restraint where it *could* have slipped is exactly what the persona rewards. Two instances stand out as **tenet-as-evidence-upgrade praise-worthily *not* done**:

1. `quantum-neural-timing-constraints` had every structural opportunity to claim its framework "survives" the decoherence objection as a competing scientific mechanism. It explicitly declines: post-decoherence selection "is not a scientific hypothesis about neural timing — it is a metaphysical interpretation… Its timing-agnosticism is not a feature that 'survives' decoherence objections so much as a consequence of operating at a different level of analysis," and "the Map should not claim that its framework 'survives' timing objections as though it were a competing scientific mechanism. It was never in the arena those objections address." This is the cleanest specimen of register-separation in the cohort — the article *names the immunity as a cost*, not a virtue ("a position immune to the field's dominant empirical objection has reduced empirical content. The Map accepts this cost").

2. `consciousness-bandwidth-architecture` keeps the inferential step from measurement to interpretation explicit: "Strictly, Zheng and Meister measured *behavioural* throughput… The step from 'behavioural ceiling in conscious tasks' to 'bandwidth of conscious influence' is an interpretation, not a direct measurement." And its epiphenomenalism section refuses the victory lap: the asymmetry "does not refute epiphenomenalism outright… What it does is make the epiphenomenal story less parsimonious." Tier language, not refutation language.

**Process Philosopher / Hardline Empiricist convergence check**: No conflict in this cohort. The Process Philosopher's (light) praise lands on `neural-refresh-rates`' time-slice framing; the Hardline Empiricist's lands on `quantum-neural-timing-constraints`' and `consciousness-bandwidth-architecture`' register-discipline. Where the cohort touches metaphysics (post-decoherence selection, the smoothness contribution thesis) it consistently marks the claim as interpretation rather than evidence. No calibration concern to flag on the evidential-status axis. (The one defect found is a *citation-integrity* issue, not a tier-slippage issue — see below.)
