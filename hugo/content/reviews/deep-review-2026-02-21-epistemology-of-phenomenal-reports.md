- **Dennett**: Heterophenomenology gives reports evidential standing *as behavioral data* — the article's critique confuses this with denying evidential standing. Addressed through more precise characterization of the methodology's non-neutral consequences.
- **Popper**: The indispensability argument risks being unfalsifiable (any challenge to reports relies on reports). Acknowledged as a genuine tension; the convergence and neurophenomenological evidence provide falsifiable predictions.
