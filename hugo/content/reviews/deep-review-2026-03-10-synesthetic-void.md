### Counterarguments Considered
- **Eliminative Materialist**: Synesthetic qualia are just neural cross-activation with no residual mystery. Response: The article's engagement with Wager's extra qualia problem and MacPherson's extraordinary feature adequately addresses this — the challenge is not that cross-activation occurs, but that the resulting phenomenal states resist functional and representational reduction.
- **Quantum Skeptic**: E-space framework is speculative and unfalsifiable. The article appropriately presents it as a conceptual tool rather than an empirical claim.
- **Empiricist**: Neonatal synesthesia hypothesis is contested. The article already acknowledges this ("The evidence remains contested—individually each piece is weak, though collectively suggestive").
- **Buddhist Philosopher**: The void framework may reify experiential boundaries that are more fluid than presented. The permeability section partly addresses this.
