## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
`quantum-probability-consciousness.md`'s "Missing Piece: First-Person Probability" section is exactly the move Chalmers would applaud: it shows all four standard readings of the Born rule (objective chance, QBism, participatory realism, many-worlds) share a blind spot — none addresses what probability means *for this consciousness*. The hard problem is not smuggled in; it is located precisely at the actualisation boundary the no-go theorems (Bell, Kochen-Specker, PBR) leave open. "Born-incomplete" is a clean, defensible framing.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
The cluster engages Stapp seriously and *honestly about his limits* — `mind-matter-interface.md` calls the quantum Zeno mechanism "a promising placeholder than a fully worked-out mechanism" and states the femtosecond-vs-100ms timescale gap plainly. `conservation-laws-and-mental-causation.md` presents the von Neumann-Lüders projection / quantum Zeno mechanism as orthodox-QM-grounded ("requires no speculative new physics") while still recording Georgiev's Monte Carlo challenge. This is the engaging-with-the-physics-interface that the Stapp persona praises, without the over-claiming that persona's enthusiasm can license.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
The indexical reading in `quantum-probability-consciousness.md` ("there is a 50% probability that *this consciousness* will experience spin-up") centres the first-person in exactly Nagel's way, and ties it to `haecceity`. The "what is it like" question becomes structurally load-bearing rather than decorative.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
`mind-matter-interface.md`'s two-layer architecture avoids crude substance dualism: Layer 1 (filter/source) is explicitly agnostic about consciousness's ultimate nature ("Mind at Large, a fundamental aspect of reality, or something else"), and the model is framed as complementary process-layers rather than a soul-substance pushing neurons. The "thin junction / wide arena" image (`mind-arena`) is process-friendly. **Per the persona constraint**: this Whitehead-friendliness must not be read as evidence for the mechanism — and the cluster itself already enforces this (see Hardline Empiricist below), so the two personas converge rather than conflict here.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
**This persona's verdict is load-bearing here, and the cluster passes strongly.** Three calibration moves deserve explicit praise:

1. **Per-trial energy residual named, not dissolved.** `conservation-laws-and-mental-causation.md`'s "Selection Without Injection" section refuses the easy equal-energy stipulation: "In realistic neural systems the superposed states need not share an energy eigenvalue ... This per-trial difference should be stated plainly rather than dissolved by stipulation." It then shows conservation is preserved at the *ensemble* (Σ pᵢEᵢ), not trial-by-trial, via the Torres Alegre no-signalling move. This is the honest-residual discipline the Hardline Empiricist exists to reward.

2. **Tenet-as-channel-width-axis is flagged as restatement, not finding.** `channel-width-third-axis.md` states it twice: that MQI maps onto "minimum channel width" is "a structural restatement of a tenet, not an argument that the channel *is* narrow in nature ... Treating the elegance of the channel-width framing as evidence *for* MQI would be exactly the [slippage](/concepts/possibility-probability-slippage/) the discipline guards against." This is *tenet-as-evidence-upgrade praise-worthily NOT done* — the article had the structural opportunity to let an elegant formalism do evidential work and explicitly declined.

3. **Framework-honest falsifiability ledger.** `quantum-randomness-channel-llm-consciousness.md` is candid that "The Map's framework accommodates both positive and negative RNG results ... A framework that accommodates all outcomes from a given experiment is not falsifiable by that experiment," and relocates testability to neural-structure identification (quantum biology) rather than parapsychology. `mind-matter-interface.md` similarly distinguishes "underdetermined" from "unfalsifiable" and lists specific defeating findings. The five-tier scale is respected: nowhere does the cluster slide the mechanism from "live hypothesis" to "realistic possibility" on tenet-coherence alone.

The only Hardline-Empiricist watch-item (not a defect): the LLM-channel article's biological-vs-LLM comparison table is correctly captioned as "theoretical requirements ... not empirically confirmed properties of neural systems" — keep that caption load-bearing in any future edit, as it is the single line preventing a possibility→probability read of the biological column.
