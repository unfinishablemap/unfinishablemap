### Counterarguments Considered
- **Eliminative Materialist**: The 10 bits/s bottleneck is a feature of serial processing architecture (global workspace), not evidence of a non-physical entity. *Addressed* in new GWT subsection.
- **Hard-Nosed Physicalist**: Behavioral output ≠ conscious throughput; the article equates them without argument. *Noted but not directly addressed* — the article follows Zheng & Meister's own framing, which treats this equation as their central claim.
- **Quantum Skeptic**: Connecting bandwidth data to quantum interaction is a logical leap. *Not addressed* — the tenet connections are explicitly philosophical readings, not empirical claims.
- **Many-Worlds Defender**: Bandwidth data doesn't distinguish single-world from many-worlds. *Addressed* — tightened the NMW tenet connection to be appropriately modest.
- **Empiricist**: The evolutionary argument has a circularity (assumes the channel is non-physical to explain why evolution can't widen it). *Not addressed* — the article frames this as the Map's "alternative explanation," not a proof, which is appropriately hedged.
- **Buddhist Philosopher**: Article conflates awareness with deliberate cognitive processing. *Not addressed* — this is a deep philosophical point that would require substantial expansion.
