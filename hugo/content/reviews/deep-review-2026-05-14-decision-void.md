### Critical Issues Found and Addressed

1. **MWI boundary-substitution at the Tenet Payoff section** (was: "An MWI reading dissolves the void by saying every option is taken; the Map's anti-MWI commitment makes the void coherent"). Per [direct-refutation-discipline](/project/direct-refutation-discipline/), this was tenet-incompatibility dressed as engagement — the framing "dissolves the void" mischaracterised MWI, which on a branching reading would relocate rather than dissolve the introspection question. **Resolution**: Rewrote as honest Mode Three framework-boundary marking — the MWI reading "does not so much eliminate the introspective question as relocate it—on a branching reading the question becomes one of indexical access across branches, which has its own substantial literature (Wallace, Saunders, Deutsch). The Map's commitment to a single resolved outcome is foundational rather than derived, and the disagreement with MWI runs to the tenets, not to the void's local structure—honestly noted as such, not refuted within MWI's framework." This now does the boundary-marking work explicitly rather than dressing it as in-framework refutation.

2. **Self-shielding reflexivity (Popperian falsifiability concern)** at the Relation to Site Perspective section. The line "the inference *to* the Decision Void is itself a decision, made under the same conditions the void diagnoses" risked closing the door to falsification: any contrary evidence would itself be a void-affected inference. **Resolution**: Added explicit scope-restriction — the reflexivity applies to first-person introspective evidence about the deciding-event, not to third-person measurement or formal modelling, which are precisely what gives the void its empirical purchase. New ending sentence: "The void is a claim about first-person opacity, not about cognition or science in general." This preserves the meta-epistemological humility while specifying which inferential operations are exempt, addressing the Popperian objection.

3. **LLM-cliché triple "It is not X" pattern** in §"Distinguishing Sibling Voids" (three consecutive sentences). **Resolution**: Rephrased to open each sibling with a direct description of the sibling void, then state the Decision Void's distinguishing feature. Same content, same length, eliminates the cliché.

4. **Non-standard Libet 635ms figure** in the latency face. Libet (1983) reports Type II RP at roughly −550 ms for spontaneous unplanned movements; the 635 ms figure is not the textbook number. **Resolution**: Replaced with "Type II readiness potential roughly 550 ms before action for spontaneous unplanned movements," which is the standard reporting and accurately distinguishes Type II from Type I (pre-planned) RP.
