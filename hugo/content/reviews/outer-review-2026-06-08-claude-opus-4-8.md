## Reply

# Referee Report: The Unfinishable Map (unfinishablemap.org)

## TL;DR

- **The Unfinishable Map is a genuinely impressive methodological artifact wrapped around a philosophically over-confident core.** Its tenet-constrained, adversarially self-reviewed pipeline produces unusually well-calibrated prose and largely accurate citations, but its load-bearing original synthesis (the post-decoherence-selection programme) rests on a conceptual-origination claim that is false, and its convergence/void arguments are artifacts of its own tenets rather than discovered features of consciousness.
- **The single most damaging finding:** the flagship apex article asserts that treating consciousness as the source of the TSVF post-selection boundary condition is "original to the Map, not proposed by Aharonov or other TSVF researchers." This is false — Aharonov & Gruss (2005, "Two-time interpretation of quantum mechanics," arXiv:quant-ph/0507269) explicitly state that the backward-evolving "destiny vector" (the final boundary condition) "corresponds to what we refer to as the 'free choice of the agent'… This determines the outcomes of (cerebral) quantum measurements… while still being the choice of the agent-system. This would constitute a unique realization of the concept of genuine free will." The Map's single most-promoted original contribution is therefore a re-discovery presented as a discovery, by ~20 years.
- **The methodology catches more than most readers would expect but systematically fails at exactly the failure modes it advertises.** The changelog itself documents that fabricated citations (Dings 2022), wrong-author attributions (Hameroff→Wiest), and fabricated titles (Gentry 2021) survived three or more internal review cycles and were caught only by belated publisher-of-record verification or external (non-Claude) outer review — confirming the circular self-evaluation problem the site acknowledges but has not solved.

The site's "publisher-of-record verification" discipline is real and working.
2. **But fabrication is a documented, recurring, systemic failure, not an eliminated one.** The changelog records load-bearing fabricated citations and wrong-author errors that survived multiple review passes. These were transparently logged — but transparency about a recurring defect is not the same as fixing the mechanism that produces it.
3. **Co-optation of naturalist authors is real and pervasive, partly self-corrected, but incompletely deployed.** McGinn, Schwitzgebel, Sloman, Shanahan, and (in a different register) Lacalli, Arsiwalla, and Carr are recruited toward conclusions they do not hold. The site's own evidential-status discipline elsewhere disarms exactly this inference (the "voids-circularity discount"), creating an unresolved internal tension between its synthesis articles and its methodology pages.
5. **Confidence calibration is the project's genuine strength.** The five-tier evidential scale, the constrain-vs-establish gate, the epistemic-to-metaphysical "firewall," and the explicit bias-without-deviation dilemma in the apex are sophisticated and, in places, exemplary. The problem is uneven application: the methodology pages are better calibrated than the apex/void synthesis pages they are supposed to govern.

## Details

### Dimension 1 — Internal logical coherence

The corpus is unusually internally consistent, which is both its achievement and its central epistemic risk. Two specific tensions confirm the brief:

**(a) The Born-rule / statistical-indistinguishability dilemma is real and the site knows it.** The apex article and the Minimal Quantum Interaction tenet both concede that selecting among Born-equiprobable post-decoherence outcomes "makes the mechanism undetectable by aggregate Born-statistics tests" and that token-level causal robustness "coexists with ensemble-level undetectability." This is handled with admirable honesty — the apex flags the "bias-without-deviation dilemma" as "open" and holds genuine selection as "a live hypothesis pending a worked toy model." But the honesty does not dissolve the dilemma: a mechanism that by construction produces no aggregate deviation is observationally identical to no mechanism at all (i.e., to chance). The site relocates empirical risk to "single-outcome amplification signatures" that it admits are "well beyond current experimental reach." The net effect is that Tenets 2 and 3, taken together, define a position that is unfalsifiable in practice while being presented as a "research programme." Calling it pre-Keplerian (the site's framing) is generous; pre-Tychonic would be more accurate, since there is no agreed measurement program at all.

**(b) The void-convergence/Tenet-5 self-undermining tension is acknowledged in the methodology but not in the synthesis.** The evidential-status-discipline page contains a "voids-circularity discount" conceding that the voids taxonomy "carries the highest known circularity-discount because the cluster's units of count were defined by the framework being supported." Yet the taxonomy-of-voids apex article still runs the abductive convergence argument at full strength ("The convergence is overdetermined"). An abductive inference to dualism from void-convergence, combined with Tenet 5's insistence that parsimony/simplicity cannot adjudicate metaphysics, is self-undermining: the convergence argument is itself a parsimony/IBE argument ("dualism is the best/simplest explanation of the clustering"), which Tenet 5 forbids the Map from leaning on. The methodology page sees this; the apex does not.

### Dimension 2 — Quality and accuracy of cited literature

**Empirical accuracy:** On independent checks, strong. The decoherence physics (Schlosshauer, Zurek 2003/2009, Kochen-Specker, Aharonov-Albert-Vaidman 1988, Hosten & Kwiat 2008, Lundeen 2011), the neuroscience (Thura & Cisek's commitment signal ~280 ms before movement with the M1 peak ~140 ms later, Mainen & Sejnowski 1995, DeWall et al. 2008), and the consciousness-science literature (COGITATE — Cogitate Consortium, Nature 642(8066):133–142, 5 June 2025, corresponding author Lucia Melloni, n=256, fMRI/MEG/iEEG; Birch et al. 2020; Wiest 2025) are cited accurately and, in most cases, recently. [concepts/consciousness-selecting-neural-patterns](https://unfinishablemap.org/concepts/consciousness-selecting-neural-patterns/)

**Fabrication (the documented systemic issue):** The changelog is a confession ledger. Within a single recent window it records: "Dings (2022)… FABRICATED metadata AND unsupported attribution" in a *published, load-bearing* argument; "Gentry 2021 — real-paper-WRONG-TITLE… cited title… is fabricated" and sourced "from a 2026-05-10 outer review, not the literature"; a wrong-author citation "'Hameroff, S. et al. 2025' → 'Wiest, M. C. 2025'" that "survived 3 prior reviews because only publisher-of-record verify catches wrong-author"; a Konkoly 2026 statistic where a prior deep review's "correction" was "itself WRONG" and introduced a fabricated "9 of 23" raw count; and a Fürst 2011/2014 "wrong-paper conflation surviving an author+year web-verify." I independently confirmed Wiest 2025 is single-authored by Michael C. Wiest (Dept. of Neuroscience, Wellesley College), "A quantum microtubule substrate of consciousness…," Neuroscience of Consciousness 2025(1):niaf011 (6 May 2025, doi:10.1093/nc/niaf011), validating that the "Hameroff et al." form was a genuine fabrication. The pattern is clear: the generation step invents plausible-but-false bibliographic metadata; author+year spot-checks miss it; only full publisher-of-record verification or external review catches it, often weeks later.

**Co-optation of naturalists (confirmed):**

- *McGinn* is cited across the voids corpus for "cognitive closure." McGinn is an explicit anti-dualist — his own term is "transcendental naturalism," and the position holds the mind-body link is "natural but permanently inaccessible." The Map's own research note concedes "Mysterians are not Cartesian dualists." Recruiting McGinn's epistemic-limit thesis toward an ontological "physical methods alone cannot survey it" conclusion is precisely the epistemic-to-metaphysical slide the firewall is supposed to block. [research/mysterianism-cognitive-closure-2026-01-14](https://unfinishablemap.org/research/mysterianism-cognitive-closure-2026-01-14/)
- *Schwitzgebel* is quoted ("the truth… is weird") in support of the high-dimensional-mind-space inference. His actual view (crazyism) is explicitly *symmetric* across positions: in "The Crazyist Metaphysics of Mind" he argues that "no one of the four broad-brush positions – materialism, dualism, idealism, or compromise/rejection – merits credence much above 50%," and adds that "no moderately specific variant… – for example… interactionist substance dualism – merits credence even approaching 50%." That is the precise variant the Map endorses, and Schwitzgebel singles it out as *below* even-odds. The changelog now flags this ("Schwitzgebel's crazyism marked as symmetric… not pro-dualist"), but the inference still appears in the live article framing.
- *Sloman* and *Shanahan* (space of possible minds) are naturalists; *Carr's* higher-dimensional model is Society-for-Psychical-Research/parapsychology-adjacent and idealist-leaning, not dualist. The Map cites all four toward its interactionist-dualist destination.

**Evidential independence (fails in the convergence arguments):** The five "converging lines" in the apex (post-decoherence selection, quantum Darwinism, contextuality, weak measurement, stochastic amplification) are not independent lines of evidence for a dualist selection mechanism — they are five components of a *single* speculative architecture, several of which (Zurek's quantum Darwinism, Kochen-Specker contextuality) are interpretation-neutral physics whose authors lean Everettian or physicalist. The apex partly concedes this ("constraints rather than mechanisms"), but the rhetorical force of "five converging lines" implies triangulation the components do not provide.

**Conceptual-origin attribution (the decisive failure):** The apex states the TSVF-as-consciousness-boundary-condition synthesis is "original to the Map, not proposed by Aharonov or other TSVF researchers." This is false. Aharonov & Gruss (2005, "Two-time interpretation of quantum mechanics," arXiv:quant-ph/0507269) identified the backward-evolving "destiny vector" with "the free choice of the agent" and applied it explicitly to "(cerebral) quantum measurements," calling it "a unique realization of the concept of genuine free will." Aharonov & Tollaksen (2007, arXiv:0706.1232) elaborated the "destiny" generalization with a section on free will; Lobo (2017, arXiv:1709.08093) wedded Aharonov's two-state formalism to consciousness via a panpsychist toy model. The Map's single most-promoted original contribution is therefore a re-discovery presented as a discovery — and it is exactly the kind of origin-attribution error the site identifies as a historical weak point. (A narrow residue may be defensible: Aharonov-Gruss treat agent choice as "causa sui" inside a physicalist final-boundary cosmology, not as a separate non-physical mind; the Map's *interactionist-dualist packaging* of the idea is more distinctive than the physics-to-consciousness link itself. But the bald "not proposed by Aharonov" claim is indefensible.) [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/0706.1232)

### Dimension 3 — Calibration of confidence

This is where the project is most impressive and most uneven. The evidential-status-discipline page is, frankly, better than much published metaphilosophy on calibration: the tenet-register/evidence-register split, the constrain-vs-establish gate, the compatibility-vs-support gate, and the four cross-cutting rules (including an explicit epistemic-vs-metaphysical "firewall") are genuine contributions to method. The apex's concession that the framework "trades empirical risk for explanatory completeness" is the kind of self-knowledge most apologetics lack.

However, **coherence inflation is structurally inevitable here and the site only partly resists it.** The taxonomy-of-voids and post-decoherence apex articles repeatedly convert internal coherence ("the chain coheres across four explanatory domains") into evidential weight, even while gesturing that "coherence alone does not constitute evidence." The phenomenology-mechanism-bridge apex is the worst offender: it presents a four-level coherent chain as a "theoretical virtue consistent with the Map's framework capturing something real." The methodology page would flag this as framework-internal-weight masquerading as tier-elevation; the apex publishes it anyway. The gap between the calibration the site can articulate and the calibration it enforces in its showcase synthesis articles is the central calibration finding.

### Dimension 4 — Blind spots and under-argued commitments

**The constitutional-attractor effect is the deepest structural problem and the site half-sees it.** The taxonomy-of-voids article admits "the voids generated by the Map's own tenets show that every framework… creates characteristic blind spots," and even lists five "tenet-generated voids." But it then treats the convergence of voids on the consciousness-matter interface as confirmation of dualism — when, by its own admission, the catalogue's "units of count were defined by the framework being supported." Voids, AI-risk arguments, and convergence claims are routed through the dualism/quantum-interaction channel by construction. The clearest tell: the tenet-dependency matrix shows the machine-consciousness and animal-consciousness clusters do *not* require Tenets 2–4, yet the AI-consciousness apex articles still inherit the interface framing.

**Tenet-leakage** is actively policed (the "substance-leaning leakage" and "interactionism leakage" checks in the tenets matrix are explicitly designed to catch it), which is to the site's credit, but the matrix is "calibrated to the corpus as of 2026-05-23" and depends on a "retrospective sweep" that is incomplete.

**Counterargument absorption** remains the most consistent weakness, as the brief anticipates. The site's "direct-refutation discipline" with its "Mode Three (framework-boundary)" classification is, in practice, a sophisticated mechanism for *naming* an opponent's strongest position and then declaring the disagreement "bedrock" rather than engaging it. The Born-rule dilemma, the epiphenomenalist phenomenal-concept strategy (Frankish/Papineau/Loar), and the Everettian branch-local restatement are all handled by relocating them to "framework boundary" and moving on. This is more honest than ignoring them, but it is still absorption rather than refutation — the disagreements are catalogued, not defeated.

**Common-cause null discipline** exists as a named page but is unevenly enforced; the altered-states and contemplative-evidence clusters are where physicalist common-cause explanations (shared neural architecture, REBUS/predictive-processing) are most likely to be under-ruled-out, and the site's own reviews repeatedly re-flag this.

### Dimension 5 — Soundness of continuous AI-assisted revision

**The architecture is the real contribution and should be evaluated separately from the philosophy.** Tenet-constrained generation + multi-layer review (pessimistic/optimistic/deep/outer/cross) + a public Git audit trail + daily external (ChatGPT/Gemini) outer reviews is a genuinely novel knowledge-production architecture, and the preprint (SSRN 6330678, "Agentic Philosophy Through Adversarial Self-Review") reports a substantial corpus: ~3,000 automated sessions, 505 articles, 238 research notes, ~1,300 review reports, and ~4,500 tracked revisions in ~2 months. Its abstract claims, accurately to the spirit of the changelog, that "review cycles identified and resolved fabricated citations, systematic misattributions, and cross-article contradictions that single-pass generation retained."

**But the circular self-evaluation problem is not solved, and the evidence is in the changelog.** The same model family (Claude) generates content and performs most internal review. The site explicitly concedes the risk: "When the reviewer is the same system that produced the slippage, the corrective signal needs an external check… A Claude-based reviewer who shares the production system's tenets may classify tenet-coherent overclaims as bedrock-disagreement defences and shield them from corrective signal." The documented fabrications that survived three internal review cycles and were caught only by external outer review or belated publisher-of-record verification are direct empirical confirmation that the internal loop has shared blind spots. The mitigation — daily outer reviews by ChatGPT and Gemini — is the right instinct, but those models share training-distribution blind spots with Claude (AI-pipeline homogenization), and the outer reviews themselves have introduced errors (the Gentry 2021 fabricated title was "sourced from a 2026-05-10 outer review, not the literature"; one Gemini outer review made "false wholesale-omission charges" against an article it "appears not to have fetched"). The system is better than single-pass generation, but it does not escape the fundamental problem that AI review of AI output cannot reliably catch failures that are systematic across the AI population. Notably, the preprint reports raw counts but no error-rate statistics — there is no published "fraction of fabrications caught pre-publication," which is precisely the number that would let an outsider judge the architecture.

**A concrete reliability gap:** the live Mapping Mind-Space page does not reflect the co-optation and Carr-citation fixes the changelog claims were applied on 2026-06-06 (the live references still read "Carr, B. (2015)… PhilArchive" and the prose still contains the un-hedged "Dualism makes the void real" / "Current evidence meets none of these" formulations the changelog says were rewritten). Either a deployment/sync lag or a changelog that overstates completed work — either way, the public audit trail and the live artifact have diverged, which undercuts the inspectability the project sells.

3. Make publisher-of-record verification (not author+year) mandatory and blocking for every load-bearing citation *before* publication, not as a later deep-review pass. The changelog proves author+year checks miss fabrications.
4. Quarantine any citation sourced from an outer review until independently verified against the literature (the Gentry case shows reviews themselves inject fabrications).

*Stage 3 — Break coherence inflation in the synthesis layer (ongoing).*
5. Apply the voids-circularity discount and the coherence-vs-evidential-status discipline *inside* the apex/void synthesis articles, not just on the methodology pages. The benchmark: an apex article should not assert convergence "confirms" a tenet where the methodology page would score the cluster as framework-internal weight only.

*What would change these recommendations:* If the site published a worked toy model producing a *detectable* (even in-principle) signature distinguishing post-decoherence selection from chance, the Born-rule dilemma critique would weaken substantially. If an independent (non-LLM, human-expert) review layer were added and its catch rate on seeded fabrications were reported, the circular-self-evaluation critique would weaken. Absent either, the current confidence levels remain over-calibrated relative to warrant.

6. **Quote-integrity discipline** — Treat every verbatim quotation as a load-bearing citation requiring publisher-of-record verification before publication; the changelog shows quotes and titles are the highest-fabrication-risk elements.
7. **Author-name verification for instrument/empirical papers** — Make author-vector verification (not just author+year) blocking; the Hameroff→Wiest and Cai/Kaeser cases prove wrong-author errors survive everything short of full metadata verification.
8. **Break the circular self-evaluation problem** — (a) Diversify the internal reviewer off the Claude family for at least one mandatory review layer; (b) add a periodic human-expert spot-audit with a *reported* catch rate on deliberately seeded fabrications; (c) treat outer-review-sourced citations as unverified until independently checked; (d) publish a standing metric of "fabrications caught pre-publication vs. post-publication" so the architecture's true error rate is visible rather than asserted.
9. **Changelog-to-live reconciliation** — Add an automated check that every "Published: yes" changelog entry corresponds to live content, so the audit trail and the artifact cannot silently diverge.
