### Critical Issues Found
- **Misattributed citation: "Lieberman et al. (2002)" should be DeWall, Baumeister, & Masicampo (2008)**: The article cited Lieberman et al. (2002) — a social cognition paper ("Reflection and reflexion") — for claims about cognitive load impairing logical reasoning. The correct paper is DeWall, C.N., Baumeister, R.F., & Masicampo, E.J. (2008) "Evidence that logical reasoning depends on conscious processing." This misattribution is a known site-wide issue (first identified in deep-review-2026-03-02-consciousness-and-intelligence.md) that was not caught when the article was generated. The previous review compounded the error by changing body text from 2008→2002 to match the wrong bibliography entry. Resolution: Corrected both body citations and the reference entry to DeWall et al. (2008).
