Chalmers would recognise this cluster as the catalogue's most disciplined treatment of his own published meta-arguments. The **convergence-argument** article's §"The Bayesian Structure" (lines 75–95) gives the Bayesian convergence-of-evidence framing Chalmers's "Consciousness and its Place in Nature" implicitly relies on — but with cluster analysis Chalmers's own work does not develop. This is Chalmersian dialectical discipline at the meta-argument level.

The **type-specificity** article gives Chalmers the methodological discipline his own work has gestured at but not articulated. The §"What Type-Specificity Does and Does Not Do" structure (lines 77–93) is exactly the kind of *bounded-claim* Chalmers asks of any non-reductive position: type-specificity identifies the form-of-demand a reductive account must satisfy, distinguishes consciousness from past reductive successes structurally, sets a non-circular constraint on what would count as a solution — and explicitly does *not* prove irreducibility, defeat physicalism on its own, or stand on irreducibility. The closing constraint at line 93 — "the discipline must not be defended by appeal to 'phenomenal qualities are irreducible, therefore the reductive demand is unmeetable'" — is the catalogue's clearest articulation of the *circularity-avoidance* discipline that Chalmers's three-step argument structure requires.

The **convergence-argument** article's §"From Irreducibility to Dualism" section (lines 131–143) gives Chalmers the route-selection move his published Type-D interactionism implicitly performs. The article explicitly stages the move: cross-traditional convergence earns *irreducibility* across rivals (panpsychism, neutral monism, idealism, Madhyamaka emptiness), but *not* dualism among them. The further selection is taken openly via the Bidirectional Interaction tenet rather than absorbed silently. This is route-selection discipline — exactly what Chalmers's published work does informally but Chalmers's critics regularly accuse him of skipping.

The **convergence-argument** article routes Nagel's question through the Bayesian-cluster discipline at lines 79–95. ### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)

This is the persona whose verdict carries the most weight for this skill, and this batch passes the calibration discipline with substantial strength to praise across multiple registers. Six points.

**(1) The vitalism disanalogy is the catalogue's load-bearing meta-argument exhibit, and it is framed structurally rather than temporally.** Both **convergence-argument** §"What Convergence Does Not Prove" (lines 145–155) and **type-specificity** §"The Vitalism Disanalogy" (lines 41–51) explicitly distinguish the *temporal* observation (no reduction has yet been found) from the *structural* observation (the form of the demand placed on any reduction is type-specific). The structural-not-temporal framing is the cleanest example in this batch of *what makes a disanalogy load-bearing*.

**(2) The convergence-argument's Bayesian cluster discipline is the catalogue's most honest weight-allocation across first-order arguments.** The §"The Bayesian Structure" subsection (lines 75–95) does *not* let the convergence's force be naively multiplicative across all eight first-order arguments in the table at lines 60–69. This is the discipline the Hardline Empiricist persona was added to reward: when the catalogue *could have* over-claimed (eight arguments converging is *much* stronger than three clusters), it explicitly refused. **(3) Concession-convergence's "not normal refinement" subsection is the cleanest dialectical-care exhibit in this batch.** The §"Not normal refinement" subsection (line 85) explicitly anticipates and addresses the most natural objection — "all theories add constraints as they mature. Newtonian mechanics added perturbation terms; evolutionary biology added genetic drift. Why treat physicalist narrowing as concession rather than ordinary scientific progress?" The reply distinguishes between *refinement that improves a theory at its central task* (normal refinement) and *retreat that narrows the domain to avoid the cases where the theory visibly fails* (defensive contraction). This is the calibration-disciplined move that lets the concession-convergence pattern *earn* its meta-argument weight rather than just *assert* it. The Hardline Empiricist persona is not in the business of taking philosophical arguments at face value when the surface form (every theory adds qualifications!) could absorb the catalogue's claim; the explicit not-normal-refinement defence is exactly what discharges the absorption objection.

**(4) Fitness-beats-truth's symmetry-objection subsection is praise-worthy *not*-done tier-upgrade.** The §"The symmetry objection" subsection (line 67) is the cleanest single-paragraph exhibit of *the move the Hardline Empiricist persona was added to praise*: "If FBT undermines parsimony arguments against dualism, does it not equally undermine the dualist's own intuitions? The FBT theorem does not *support* dualism—it removes one class of objection to it." This is *defeater-removal* explicitly distinguished from *evidence-positive support*. The Hardline Empiricist persona's load-bearing distinction — that tenet-coherence does not earn tier-upgrade — is here applied to *parsimony-defeater-coherence*: FBT removes a parsimony-based defeater but does not earn dualism a tier-upgrade. This is exactly the calibrated structural language ("removes one class of objection" rather than "supports") the persona was added to reward.

**(5) Taxonomy-of-voids' common-cause discipline is the apex application of *tenet-as-evidence-upgrade is praise-worthily not done*.** The §"The Convergence Pattern" subsection (lines 170–179) is the most disciplined treatment of the common-cause-null in this batch. The article had every structural opportunity to over-claim the convergence's evidential weight — sixty voids clustering at one interface is a *prima facie* striking pattern — but the apex explicitly notes that "the bulk of its convergence is currently framework-internal coherence rather than framework-independent triangulation" and "the catalogue's cumulative evidential weight calibrates downward to what genuinely independent sources can supply." The two worked exhibits at §"Two Worked Exhibits in Independence Scoring" (lines 180–188) — the surplus void *retracted* and the introspection-architecture sub-cluster *partially retained* — demonstrate the methodology in practice rather than declaring it in principle. The discipline-page-quality treatment of an instrument that scored *every* cluster the same would be "doing no work" (line 188) is the apex's clearest single articulation of the framework's calibration honesty.

**(6) Source-attribution-divergence's "uses and contributes to" honesty is the catalogue's clearest topic-level cluster-discipline exhibit.** The article's §"The Function-Phenomenology Wedge — Stress-Tested" closing paragraph (line 111) explicitly notes that the article both *uses* the within-species cluster (citing aphantasia and synaesthesia to discharge local-absorption) and *contributes to* it (the source-attribution case is one of the cluster's three legs).
