All three reviewers independently reached the cycle's dominant finding: the article's headline claim that three Special Composition Question (SCQ) routes "independently converge on consciousness" is a **selection artefact** — the convergence appears only after the cited authors' positions are redescribed, and the term "consciousness" is equivocated across the three routes.
