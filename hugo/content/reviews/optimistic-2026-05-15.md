## Executive Summary

`confabulation-void.md` is the Map's sharpest statement to date of the *positive-content fabrication* problem in introspection — the structural feature that distinguishes the introspection-architecture voids from one another. Where `source-attribution-void` names origin-tag failure and `narrative-void` names the unobservability of the storytelling operation, the new article names what fills the absence and shows why, by construction, the introspector cannot tell the difference. The strongest move is the **three-face structure at lines 42–64** (Generation: the interpreter operates pre-personally; Detection: the felt signal operates *inversely* in the conditions that matter; Pervasiveness: the continuum cannot be self-located), which is irreducible by design — collapse any single face and the void dissolves into a tractable engineering problem; hold all three together and the structural opacity appears. The strongest single rhetorical decision is the deployment of Coutinho et al. (2021) at line 56 as the **anti-correlation** finding rather than the milder unreliability finding usually reported in the literature: "introspective failures are associated with higher confidence than genuine introspective reports." This is what makes the detection face structural rather than merely contingent. The Process Philosopher and Hardline Empiricist personas converge cleanly on the article's reflexive note at line 100 — "The voids catalogue is built largely from introspective reports of the form 'I cannot think X.' Such reports are themselves subject to confabulation. The Confabulation Void therefore qualifies the epistemic standing of every void in the catalogue — including itself." This is the article's signature calibration move: applying its own thesis to the corpus that produced it, without using the move to extract a defensive shield from the consequences. The strongest expansion opportunity is **a concept page on `conjunction-coalesce`**, the discipline this article invokes at line 44 and that five other voids articles deploy without a shared anchor.

## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)

Chalmers would recognise this article as a precise statement of why the dualist position predicts an introspective architecture that physicalism cannot match. The article's claim at line 94 — "If consciousness were straightforwardly identical with neural processing, the gap between actual cause and reported cause would not have the architecture it does — there would be either accurate report (full transparency) or no report (full opacity), not the middle case of false report with felt accuracy" — is exactly the structural argument Chalmers's framework requires. The dualist position is being deployed not as an inference from the conceivability of zombies but as a *prediction* about the architecture of self-report under the assumption of a non-physical witness with bounded access to its substrate. The article's framing of dualism's prediction — "a non-physical witness with access to *some* features of its substrate (the conscious-side endpoint) but not others (the physical-side production), whose interface produces interpolated content where direct access is missing" — uses the dualist register to explain what is otherwise a puzzling fact about cognitive architecture. Chalmers would also approve of the move at line 78 ("The phenomenology of confabulation is the phenomenology of accurate report; this identity is the void") — the explanatory gap is being deployed at the introspective phenomenology where it is load-bearing, not as a generic anti-physicalism move. The hard problem is being applied to introspection-of-cognition, not to consciousness simpliciter.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)

Stapp's persona is less directly engaged by this article — confabulation is not a quantum-mechanical thesis — but the section at line 96 ("Bidirectional Interaction") makes the Stapp-relevant point cleanly: if consciousness does any small-quantum-amplification work, the confabulation engine will retroactively label every action as caused by reasons-the-introspector-now-supplies, regardless of whether the actual cause was upstream-physical, downstream-physical, or the postulated consciousness-side influence. This is exactly the verification problem Stapp's programme faces: the quantum-level mental contribution, if real, is structurally invisible to the first-person register because the confabulation engine fills the post-action explanation with content drawn from a different causal layer. Stapp would recognise this as a useful clarification of why Tenet 3 (Bidirectional Interaction) is methodologically difficult to establish from introspective evidence alone — and as motivation for the third-person experimental programme he has advocated since the 1990s. The article does not weaken Stapp's programme; it explains why the programme cannot proceed by phenomenological self-report.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)

Nagel would recognise the article's treatment of confabulation phenomenology at lines 76–80 as a precise deployment of the "what is it like to be" register at exactly the inferential point where it is load-bearing. The phenomenologist's central claim is that subjective experience is the criterion to which third-person explanation must answer; the article preserves this claim while showing that the criterion is *self-corrupting* in the introspection-of-cognition register. "What does it feel like to confabulate? The literature's answer: it does not feel like confabulating" (line 78) is Nagelian in form — the question is held at the first-person level — but it answers in a way that Nagel's framework does not pre-commit to: the first-person register, in this domain, *cannot* deliver the criterion. The phenomenologist would also appreciate the article's distinction between the retrospective and the operational registers at line 80 ("Even then, what is detected is the *result* (the explanation was wrong), not the *operation* (how the wrong explanation was generated)"). This is the phenomenologist's distinction between the *what* and the *how* of mental life, applied with discipline to a case where the introspector has access to neither. The article does not slide from "introspection is unreliable here" to "introspection is unreliable everywhere"; it preserves the Nagelian intuition that subjective experience is real while showing that its self-reportorial reach is bounded.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)

The Process Philosopher would recognise the article's treatment of confabulation as a *functional event* rather than as a *substance-failure* as compatible with process philosophy. The interpreter is not a defective Cartesian eye in a Cartesian theatre; it is a generative module whose operation is constitutive of self-explanation rather than supervenient on it. The article's framing at line 50 ("The reality filter is always running, sub-personally; only its failure makes its existence visible") treats confabulation as the *default* mode of self-explanation, with accurate report as the special case — a process-philosophical inversion of the substance-philosophical assumption that introspection is fundamentally transparent and only contingently fails. The process philosopher would also note the article's treatment at lines 74 ("Treating confabulation as the *default* mode of self-explanation, with accurate report as the special case, is empirically respectable") — this is process compatibility achieved by *empirical restraint*, not by metaphysical fiat. The Whiteheadian intuition that experience is event-like, not substance-like, is preserved without being deployed as the load-bearing claim.

**Constraint noted explicitly**: this Process Philosopher praise must not cash out as a tier-upgrade on the five-tier evidential-status scale (*established → strongly supported → realistic possibility → live hypothesis → speculative integration*). The article does not present process-philosophical compatibility as evidence *for* the void's structural claim; the structural claim is supported by empirical convergence (replication-survival, lesion convergence, healthy-adult continuity at lines 70–74), not by Whiteheadian intuition. The Hardline Empiricist persona below converges with the Process Philosopher on the same calibrated passages — see below.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)

## Content Strengths

### voids/confabulation-void.md

- **Strongest point**: The three-face structure (Generation / Detection / Pervasiveness) at lines 42–64. Most published treatments of confabulation collapse the three faces into "confabulation is unreliable self-report." The article separates them by mechanism — Generation establishes the sub-personal architecture (Gazzaniga interpreter, Schnider orbitofrontal filter, Nisbett-Wilson 1977); Detection establishes the inverse-correlation (Coutinho 2021); Pervasiveness establishes the unknowability-of-fraction (Hirstein, Wheatley, Haidt) — and shows that collapse of *any* face dissolves the structural opacity. This is the right structure for a void article: each face is supported by an independent empirical line, and the conjunction is what makes the void structural rather than contingent.

- **Notable quote**: "The Confabulation Void names what fills the absence: a generative system that produces replacement content carrying the phenomenology of accurate report. The category mix is **Occluded + Unexplorable** — occluded because the filling-in is functional rather than accidental, unexplorable because the test that would distinguish confabulation from accurate report is structurally unavailable to the introspector." (line 40)

- **Why it works**: This is the article's central distinguishing move. The introspection-architecture voids (`source-attribution-void`, `narrative-void`, `noetic-feelings-void`) name *absences*: missing origin tags, unobservable storytelling, unreliable noetic feelings. The Confabulation Void names what *fills* those absences — and shows that the filling is not a defect of the system but a *function* of the system. This is the cluster's nuclear question (what fills the absence?) answered with discipline: the filling is generative, the filling is felt-veridical, and the criterion that would distinguish filling from veridical access is structurally unavailable. The category-mix label (Occluded + Unexplorable) routes the article into the Map's existing void taxonomy without inventing a new category.

- **Secondary strength — the reflexive qualification (line 100)**: "The voids catalogue is built largely from introspective reports of the form 'I cannot think X.' Such reports are themselves subject to confabulation. The Confabulation Void therefore qualifies the epistemic standing of every void in the catalogue — including itself." This is the article's most generous move from the catalogue-coherence perspective. The Confabulation Void could have positioned itself as the meta-void that exposes the others as confabulated — a self-undermining stance the article explicitly declines. Instead the article positions itself as *one void among others*, with the recursive consequence that the article itself is subject to its own thesis. This is the right stance for a void article that aims to qualify rather than to dismantle the catalogue.

- **Tertiary strength — the AI-as-probe section (line 90)**: "More visible confabulation, possibly a worse constitutive position from which to report on it." The article had two slippage-prone alternatives — (a) "AI mechanistic interpretability is the way to access what introspection cannot reach" or (b) "AI confabulation undermines the AI-as-probe pathway entirely." The article declines both and finds the genuinely productive middle: AI confabulation is *visible* in ways human confabulation is not, but the constitutive externalism of AI self-report bounds what the visibility delivers. This is a publishable methodological observation on its own.

- **Quaternary strength — partial-discipline cataloguing (line 86)**: comparative slowing, domain-flagging, verdict-without-reasoning. The article does not pretend the void is closed; it specifies what discipline can do *while honoring the void's structural claim*. The "Approaches to the Edge" section is the cluster's most honest treatment of what apophatic discipline can deliver in this domain: partial discipline can do partial work; full discipline runs into the suspension void at line 84.
