## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
The explanatory gap is treated as a load-bearing structural feature throughout, not a placeholder. The subject-object article's three-part asymmetry (subject can't become object for itself / object is always for a subject / asymmetry is irreducible) is a clean statement of why third-person description leaves a residue. Chalmers would especially welcome the relocated-hard-problem section in biological-computationalism: "why does *this* biological substrate produce experience?" has changed scope but not character.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
Biological-computationalism's Tenet-2 paragraph is a model of where Stapp's program *should* sit: it names ion flows, EM fields and metabolic dynamics as the domain where the interface would have to act *if* it acts, while explicitly refusing to read warm-biology quantum effects (magnetoreception, photosynthesis) as corroboration. quantum-biology-and-neural-consciousness carries the heavier load here and frames it as "load-bearing for pre-decoherence mechanisms, plausibility supplement for post-decoherence selection" — a graded claim, not a blanket one.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
The subject-object article centres the first-person viewpoint precisely as Nagel does ("consciousness *is* the viewpoint that objectivity must eliminate") and cites *The View from Nowhere* directly. The cross-traditional convergence (Descartes, Husserl, Advaita *sakshi*, Buddhist *anatman*) is exactly the kind of evidence Nagel would count as pointing past cultural projection.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
The cluster avoids crude substance dualism: biological-computationalism repeatedly frames substrate-specificity, metabolic grounding and scale-inseparability as *physicalism-compatible* facts a physicalist "can adopt comfortably," reserving the dualist reading as one option among several sharing a diagnosis. The "one structure, several vocabularies" framing ("substrate-specific physical" / "irreducible higher-order physical" / "non-physical") is process-friendly without inflating any organism's tier.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
Not the primary register of this cluster; the subject-object article touches agency only via the self-stultification gloss (an epiphenomenal subject could not produce the tradition that discovered it), and honestly flags that inference as non-airtight.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
Strong epistemic humility throughout. The subject-object article's "What Would Challenge This View?" lists five concrete defeat conditions and concedes "the inference is not airtight" at each contested move. concession-convergence's "live snapshot conditional on available evidence" and its admission that the shared-introspective-channel layer is "the layer no current convergence claim can fully discharge" is exactly the closure-aware humility McGinn prizes.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
**This is the cluster's standout virtue and the persona's verdict is strongly positive.** concession-convergence does the thing the Hardline Empiricist most wants done and which is praise-worthy *to do*: it splits the claim into an *architectural finding* (theories narrow toward type-specificity — *strongly supported*) and an *interpretive significance* (the narrowing *concedes* dualism — *realistic possibility, contested*), and assigns the full positive meta-argument only *live hypothesis*, with the explicit rule "load-bearing claims must be labelled at the tier the lowest-scoring relevant criterion supports, not at an average." The Cārvāka/Lokāyata treatment is a model of restraint: a genuinely indigenous physicalism that met absent-qualia pressure and *did not* ratchet inward is reported as a "source-limited" suggestive contrary point, not spun as confirmation. biological-computationalism never upgrades any organism on substrate-dependence grounds; it caps every parallel at "structural, not evidential." **Tenet-as-evidence-upgrade is here a thing the cluster praise-worthily declines to do**, repeatedly and by name ("structural rather than evidential," "defeater-removal, not evidence-acquisition"). Process-Philosopher and Hardline-Empiricist *converge* on this cluster — the tension is resolved honestly, which by the skill's own criterion means the article contains no possibility/probability slippage to flag.
