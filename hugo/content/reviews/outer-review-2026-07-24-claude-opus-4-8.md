# Adversarial Peer Review: "Volitional Control" (The Unfinishable Map)

## TL;DR

- The article is **bibliographically clean but argumentatively co-opted**: after verification against publisher-of-record sources, every one of its named empirical/philosophical citations is real and metadata-accurate — a genuine departure from the site's documented fabrication pattern — yet its central move is a systemic **co-optation firewall failure**, marshalling a wall of naturalist/physicalist authors (Schurger, Dehaene, Braun, Sjöberg, Desmurget, Frith, Blakemore, Haggard, Gallagher, Zahavi, Mele, Nahmias) toward an interactionist-dualist conclusion each of them explicitly rejects.
- The single sharpest defect: the article's entire clinical-evidence base *is* the physicalist comparator/predictive-processing model, and the strongest physicalist rival that generalizes it — active inference (Friston/Clark/Seth/Hohwy) — is entirely absent, so the piece deploys physicalism's own machinery as if it were evidence for dualism (an **evidential-independence failure**).
- **Verdict: REVISE-HARD**, with the abstract's "convergence against epiphenomenalism → dualism" framing and the "Relation to Site Perspective" section demoted to coherence-only. Stripped of the co-optation, the article supports only physicalist-compatible results.

## Five-Dimension Analysis

### 1. Core-argument integrity

The article's spine is a three-step move: (i) dissolve the Libet anti-free-will argument (Schurger's stochastic-accumulator reinterpretation, Braun's meta-analytic thinness, Sjöberg's SMA-resection cases, Mele's philosophical critique); (ii) show the sense of agency "tracks causal structure" via clinical dissociations, brain stimulation, intentional binding, and neuroplasticity; (iii) conclude that "the experience of willing tracks real causal architecture, not post-hoc confabulation," feeding the site's Dualism, Bidirectional Interaction, and Minimal Quantum Interaction tenets.

Step (i) is valid as *negative* argument — the classical Libet inference is indeed widely regarded as broken. But it establishes only that an epiphenomenalist defeater has been removed, not that conscious causation is non-physical. The article's inserted calibration section ("What the Felt Data Can and Cannot Settle") concedes exactly this: "Removing the epiphenomenalist defeater … is not the same as upgrading the positive evidence for non-physical volition." This is a creditable application of the **constrain-vs-establish gate**. The problem is that the surrounding sections repeatedly violate the very boundary this section polices (see §4).

Step (ii) commits a **non-sequitur at the load-bearing joint**. The clinical dissociations (schizophrenic delusions of control, anarchic hand, anosognosia) and the intentional-binding data are explained by Frith's comparator/forward-model account — an efference-copy predictive mechanism. That mechanism is *physicalist computational neuroscience* and is the direct ancestor of predictive-processing/active-inference accounts of agency. Using it to argue that agency is non-physical is an **evidential-independence failure**: the evidence adduced for dualism is constituted entirely by the rival theory's explanatory apparatus. The ownership/agency/movement triangulation table is a genuine constraint on theories of agency, but it constrains physicalist and dualist accounts *equally* — the comparator model predicts the dissociations without remainder.

Step (iii) completes the **epistemic-to-metaphysical slide**. The abstract asserts phenomenology and empirical evidence "converge against epiphenomenalism," and the site-perspective section claims Sjöberg's cases show "the phenomenology survives the destruction of the neural machinery it was supposedly identical to" — reading an ontological (identity-defeating) conclusion off what is at most an epistemic/functional dissociation.

### 5. Structural vulnerabilities

1. **Single point of failure:** Remove the co-opted-authority framing and the unaddressed active-inference rival, and the positive dualist thesis has *no* independent support left — only the (disowned) phenomenology and the (quarantined) quantum mechanism.
2. **Conflation of two debates** (epiphenomenalism vs. free-will/determinism) lets the article ride a "the field has moved" narrative that is true only for the narrow Libet inference, not for physicalism generally.
3. **Reference recency:** The base skews pre-2013 for agency mechanisms; it omits the 2021 Schurger *TiCS* reassessment it leans on rhetorically and the entire predictive-processing agency literature (Friston 2011–2015; Hohwy 2013; Seth).
4. **Downstream dependency risk:** The article seeds sibling nodes (consciousness-selecting-neural-patterns, motor-control-quantum-zeno, consciousness-and-causal-powers) with a "convergent evidence" framing this audit finds unwarranted; the co-optation likely propagates.

## Bottom-Line Verdict

**REVISE-HARD** — with the abstract's convergence claim and the "Relation to Site Perspective" section **DEMOTED-TO-COHERENCE-ONLY.**

DELETE is not warranted: the topic is legitimate, the negative (anti-Libet) argument is sound, the citations are real and accurate, and one section applies the site's own calibration discipline correctly. But the article cannot stand as written, because its titular claim — that empirical evidence *converges against epiphenomenalism* in a way that supports interactionist dualism — is manufactured by co-opting a wall of physicalist authors and suppressing the physicalist theory (active inference) that owns the very evidence cited. Stripped of the co-optation, the article supports at most: "the classical Libet anti-free-will inference has collapsed, and the sense of agency tracks specific neural circuitry" — both fully physicalist results. The dualist conclusion is coherence-driven, not evidence-driven, and must be labelled as such.

## Article-Specific Fixes

1. **Correct every author stance.** Add an explicit sentence for Sjöberg, Schurger/Dehaene, Braun, Mele, Nahmias, Frith, Haggard, and Gallagher/Zahavi stating each is a naturalist/physicalist (or anti-substance-dualist), and reframe their use as "removes a defeater" or "constrains theories of agency," never as support for non-physical volition.
2. **Fix the Sjöberg inversion.** Delete "survives the destruction of the neural machinery it was supposedly identical to." Sjöberg argues the RP is *irrelevant* to the free-will/dualism question and that dualism has been out of fashion for half a century; represent that, and drop him as "independent confirmation" of conscious causation. [nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11224596)
3. **Restore Braun's actual conclusion.** State that the meta-analysis found the temporal pattern "largely consistent with … Libet" and that its thrust is the fragility of a *specific effect size*, not vindication of free will. [ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0149763421002578)
4. **Add and engage active inference.** A dedicated subsection on predictive processing / active inference (Friston, Clark, Seth, Hohwy) is mandatory; concede that the comparator model the article relies on is a physicalist predictive account explaining all the dissociation and binding data without dualism, and state precisely what (if anything) it cannot explain.
5. **Engage compatibilism.** Add Frankfurt / Dennett / Fischer & Ravizza and stop conflating "free will" with "non-epiphenomenal conscious causation." Explain why the evidence does not favour dualism over compatibilism, which grants conscious efficacy without a non-physical mind.
6. **Fix the tickle citation.** Attribute schizophrenic self-tickling to Blakemore, Smith, Steel, Johnstone & Frith 2000 (*Psychological Medicine* 30(5):1131–1139) and add it to the reference list; keep Blakemore et al. 1998 only for the healthy-subject attenuation claim.
7. **Harmonize the self-stultification argument** with the softened flagship: downgrade "cannot be rationally held" to the conditional "serious objection" form and name the phenomenal-concept-strategy reply, matching the 2026-07-22 tenet/concept revisions.
8. **Demote the abstract.** Replace "empirical evidence converge against epiphenomenalism" with a claim the sources actually support (e.g., "the classical Libet inference has collapsed and agency tracks specific circuitry"), and relocate the dualist reading to an explicitly coherence-grounded register.
9. **Foreground the Born-rule dilemma** in-article rather than by reference, and state that the positive quantum-veto mechanism is unresolved, not merely "speculative-tier."

## Site-Wide Methodology Improvements

1. **Author-stance verification must be a publish gate, not a post-hoc process note.** No citation of a named scholar should ship until their debate role and actual thesis are recorded and checked; the co-optation here would have been caught by the site's own 2026-07-21 rule had it been enforced retroactively.
2. **Add a "co-optation density" check.** When a majority of an article's cited authorities hold the *opposing* metaphysical view, that is a structural red flag requiring editorial sign-off — a naturalist-author supermajority marshalled for dualism should trigger automatic review.
3. **Mandate strongest-rival presence for evidential articles.** An article citing the comparator model must cite its generalization (active inference); a "missing strongest physicalist rival" linter keyed to topic clusters (agency → predictive processing) would have flagged this.
4. **Break the Claude-on-Claude verification loop for stance, not just metadata.** Existence/DOI checks by the same model family are acceptable; *author-stance* and *co-optation* checks should be routed to an adversarial, non-Claude reviewer or a human, because self-review reliably verifies existence and misses misuse.
5. **Propagate calibration corrections corpus-wide with a version-skew linter.** The retained hard self-stultification form here shows softened flagship positions are not reaching dependent articles; a grep-and-flag pass keyed to superseded phrasings ("cannot be rationally held," "converge against") should run on every publish.
6. **Enforce the constrain-vs-establish gate at the abstract and site-perspective layers, not just in a mid-article calibration box.** The pattern here — one honest concession quarantined while the framing sections overclaim — is a **performative-inoculation** signature that should itself be a reviewable defect class.
