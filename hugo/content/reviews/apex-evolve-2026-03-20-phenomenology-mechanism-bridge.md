## Differentiation from Existing Apex Articles

Three existing apex articles cover adjacent territory:

- **Consciousness and Agency (#3)**: Focuses on the metaphysical grounding of agency — agent causation, moral responsibility, counterfactual reasoning, the selector model. Starts from the philosophical problem of authorship and builds outward.
- **Phenomenology of Consciousness Doing Work (#12)**: Focuses on phenomenological evidence — the four features of effort, selection, holding, opacity. Stays within the phenomenological domain as evidence.

The Phenomenology-Mechanism Bridge is differentiated by tracing the *complete chain across all four explanatory levels* — from first-person phenomenology to neural architecture to quantum mechanism to metaphysical framework — showing how each level connects to the next. No existing apex does this. The novel contribution is the *connections between levels*, not any single level.
