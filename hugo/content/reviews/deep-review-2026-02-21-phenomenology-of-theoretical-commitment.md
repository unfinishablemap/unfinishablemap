- **Dennett-style physicalist**: "First-person authority inflation" — introspective reports about commitment may not track its underlying nature. **Assessment**: Legitimate concern, but the article's argument is about the explanatory challenge, not about introspective infallibility.
- **Empiricist**: The irreducibility claim is unfalsifiable. **Assessment**: This applies to most phenomenological claims; the article's value is in articulating the explanandum, not in providing empirical tests.
- **Buddhist**: The "committed self" may be deconstructed into momentary states. **Assessment**: Interesting alternative but not a flaw in the article's framework.
