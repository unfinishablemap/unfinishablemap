## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)

The article's signature claim — *introspection delivers reconstruction rather than readout* — sits at the structural shape of the explanatory gap rather than as a surface phenomenon. The four faces (story, origin-tag, verdict-feeling, fabricated content) are not four ways the brain reports on itself; they are four ways the report *cannot reach* the substrate that produced it. The bounded-witness reading (line 130: "a non-physical witness has bounded access to some features of its substrate but not others") is exactly the kind of architectural finding the hard problem predicts will be available when introspective access is honestly modelled: the witness can read the *output* of cognition (the verdict, the story, the felt rightness) but not the *machinery* that produced it. What resonates most is the article's refusal to slide from this architectural finding into a positive evidence claim — the signature is *compatible* with the bounded-witness picture, the framework supplies the *significance*, and the article keeps these levels separate (line 132: "compatibility plus boundary-marking, not positive support"). This is the discipline the hard problem requires once it stops being a polemic and starts being a calibration instrument: the gap is real, the gap's metaphysical diagnosis is the part the framework supplies, and the article respects the distinction. The five-tier evidential-status scale is honoured: the bounded-witness reading is correctly at *live hypothesis* / *speculative integration*, not at *realistic possibility*, despite the structural-fit being suggestive.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)

The architecture's reconstructive character is the *target shape* of the interface-with-conscious-witness picture: if conscious experience interacts with the physical at a substrate the introspective architecture cannot inspect, the architecture's failure to deliver substrate-information to the conscious side is exactly what the interface model predicts. The Stapp-friendly reading is not stated in the article — and properly so, since the article restricts itself to scoring the empirical signature — but the *room* the article leaves for that reading is generous: the architectural finding is high in framework-independence (line 116), the interpretive significance is low, and the convergence argument is bounded by the lower number. A quantum-mind theorist can take the architectural finding into the interface-model vocabulary without distorting either the architectural claim or the model. The article does not pre-empt that bridge and does not collapse it; the bridge is left to the further reading the catalogue's apex synthesis programme is being built to handle.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)

The four-face structure honours the first-person signature as the *unit of analysis*. The story-face, origin-tag-face, verdict-feeling-face, and fabricated-content-face are not four functional dissociations; they are four ways the first-person *registers* a structural feature of its own architecture. The cluster's signature is recognisable from a description of the *breakdown alone* — which is the operationalisation of signature specificity from [apophatic-cartography-four-criteria](/concepts/apophatic-cartography-four-criteria/) applied honestly. The Buddhist *kleshas* discussion is particularly Nagel-friendly: the article preserves the *what-it-is-like* of the *kleshas* observation (afflictive mental states operate without the agent's awareness — that is the phenomenology) while declining to import the tradition's metaphysical diagnosis (no fixed witness lies behind the introspective stream). The phenomenological surface convergence is real; the metaphysical diagnoses diverge; the article holds these registers separate.

The signature *is* the activity's signature, not the witness's reflective failure. A process philosopher will find the article's vocabulary congenial without finding it appropriative. **The crucial test for this persona, per the skill's explicit constraint, is whether the process-philosophical resonance is being used to upgrade an evidential-status tier — it is not.** The article's bounded-witness reading is at *live hypothesis* through-and-through; the process-philosophical compatibility is treated as removing a defeater for the metaphysical possibility of bounded conscious witnessing, not as supplying positive evidence for the cluster's framing. The article uses the named pattern *tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating* implicitly throughout the "Relation to Site Perspective" section (line 132's "compatibility plus boundary-marking, not positive support" is exactly that pattern). The two personas — Whitehead and the Hardline Empiricist below — converge in praising the same passages here; that convergence is the marker that the article has resolved the productive tension honestly.

This is the discipline's central instruction — *a tenet may remove a defeater but must not upgrade the evidence level* — operationalised at the cluster level: the framework can identify a cluster's significance; the framework must not upgrade the cluster's independence score.

3. **The cross-species channel is honestly labelled as principled-absent rather than as missing-evidence (line 94).** The article distinguishes the *principled* absence (introspection's verbal-report signature does not extend to non-verbal animals) from the *contingent* absence (a proxy test pairing behavioural-confidence against outcome-tracking could in principle deliver the signature in a species that lacks language). ## New Concept Pages Needed

- **`unfavourable-test-case`**: A concept page articulating the discipline of *seeking results the methodology was not designed to produce* as a falsification test for cluster-level calibration methodologies. The present article calls for this test (line 124); a dedicated page would generalise the concept beyond the introspection-architecture exhibit and beyond the voids catalogue, into the broader framework-stage-calibration apparatus. Audience: cataloguers (internal) and methodologically-minded external readers. Word count: ~1,800–2,200.
- **`channel-class`**: A concept page consolidating the four channels the cluster's population-type profile uses (patient-population, healthy-adult experimental, cross-tradition, cross-species), what each channel *can* establish, and what each *cannot*. Currently the four channels are named inline in the present article and inline in [common-cause-null](/project/common-cause-null/), without a dedicated home. The dedicated home would be the canonical referential anchor for future scoring exhibits and would consolidate the channel-by-channel calibration discipline.
- **`headline-versus-licensing-count`** (or a similar slug): A concept page articulating the discipline of *not letting the headline count of supporting anchors be read as the licensing count for the structural claim*. The present article performs this move at line 106 but does not name it; naming it would let future exhibits invoke the discipline by reference rather than re-deriving it.
