## 2. Terminology for the Nuclear Question

Each article describes the same architectural feature with different vocabulary. Below is the leading phrasing each article uses for the bounded-access architecture.

|---|---|
| `narrative-void` | "narrative mechanism" / "the interpreter" (Gazzaniga) / "narrator"; the framing names the *function* (storytelling) not the architecture as such |

**Diagnosis.** Each article describes one face of the same architectural fact: introspection delivers reconstructed outputs whose generating substrate is structurally inaccessible. The vocabulary is genuinely divergent at the lexical level but architecturally convergent at the structural level. The four phrasings most likely to confuse an apex reader are *narrator* (function), *witness* (passive receiver), *generator* (active filler), and *interpreter* (after-the-fact rationaliser). Each is correct for one face and misleading if it ranges over the others.

**Recommendation for apex.** The cluster should be named **the introspection-architecture sub-cluster** (this audit installs that anchor in each article). Within the apex's prose, the architectural feature should be referred to as **the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate** — neutral between *witness* and *interpreter* framings, lexically aligned with `confabulation-void`'s existing phrasing, and stable across the four faces. *Narrator*, *witness*, *generator*, and *interpreter* should be used face-locally and not as cluster-wide synonyms.

## 5. Convergences vs Divergences

### Real architectural-level convergences (apex may cite as cluster-wide claims)

1. **Introspection is reconstructive, not perceptual.** All five articles affirm this. The vocabularies differ; the architectural claim does not.
2. **Bounded access to substrate.** All five affirm that consciousness has access to some features (the conscious-side endpoint, the content, the verdict) but not others (the producing machinery, the source tag, the inferential heuristic). The vocabularies for the "bounded" framing differ; the claim is consistent.
3. **Phenomenology of accurate report is the phenomenology of inaccurate report.** All five articles converge on the claim that confabulated, source-amnesic, fluency-driven, or narratively constructed content does not feel different from veridical content. The void *signature* is the absence of a phenomenal signal where one would be needed.
4. **Better introspection cannot reach the architectural layer.** All five affirm this (most explicitly in `source-attribution-void`'s closing remark of §3 and `noetic-feelings-void`'s feed-forward opacity).
5. **Category-mix is Occluded + Unexplorable at minimum.** Universal across the five.

### Divergences at the sub-architecture level (apex should treat face-locally)

1. **What fills the void.** `narrative-void` says story; `source-attribution-void` says inferred origin; `noetic-feelings-void` says verdict-feeling; `confabulation-void` says fabricated content; `self-opacity` says first-personal content with the asymmetry-mechanism stripped out. These are four genuinely different *outputs* of the same architecture; the apex should keep the differentiation visible.
2. **Reflexive consequence framings.** `confabulation-void` foregrounds the catalogue-self-undermining problem (line 103); `noetic-feelings-void` raises the meta-epistemology tension (line 146); `self-opacity` formalises the constitutive-as-kind-not-degree response (§"Constitutive as Kind, Not as Degree"); `narrative-void` and `source-attribution-void` engage the reflexive layer more lightly. The apex should converge these into one principled treatment rather than presenting four separate concessions.
3. **Tenet routing.** All five route through Dualism, Bidirectional Interaction, and Occam's Razor Has Limits; only `self-opacity` engages Minimal Quantum Interaction directly; `narrative-void` reaches Bidirectional Interaction most strongly via the audit-of-causal-efficacy concern. The apex should adopt `self-opacity`'s tenet-routing as the spine and treat the others as specialisations.

### Genuine non-convergences (apex must not flatten)

1. **Confabulation-void's anti-correlation claim** (Coutinho 2021) does not appear in the others. The apex should attribute it specifically; it is not the cluster's general position but the *detection face's* specific finding.
2. **Self-opacity's constitutive thesis** is a stronger metaphysical commitment than the other four articles make. The apex should engage it as `self-opacity`'s reading rather than as the cluster's settled position.
3. **Source-attribution-void's neutrality between physicalism and dualism** (§"Relation to Site Perspective: Dualism") is more explicit than the other four. The apex should retain the neutrality discipline rather than overrule it for cluster-wide rhetorical force.

## 6. Recommendations for the Apex Synthesis

1. **Adopt "the introspection-architecture sub-cluster" as the canonical name** and "**the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate**" as the canonical phrasing for the architectural feature. Avoid using *narrator*, *witness*, *generator*, and *interpreter* as cluster-wide synonyms.

2. **Open with the cluster's organising structure as a sequence of architectural faces**, not as a list of independently-discovered voids: narrative-void supplies the *unobservability of storytelling*, source-attribution-void the *origin-tag failure*, noetic-feelings-void the *gating-signal opacity*, confabulation-void the *positive-content fabrication*, self-opacity the *broader bounded-knowledge frame*. The walking-order in this audit's introduction matches the apex's most coherent ordering.

3. **State the category-mix as Occluded + Unexplorable for the cluster**, with `narrative-void`'s Unexplored layer attributed to *the empirical-mechanism face* rather than as a cluster-wide property.

4. **Carry confabulation-void's anti-correlation claim as a load-bearing finding** distinguishing the detection face from generic unreliability. The apex should not flatten this back into "noetic feelings are unreliable."

5. **Route through Dualism / Bidirectional Interaction / Occam's Razor Has Limits as the tenet spine**, with Minimal Quantum Interaction reached only via `self-opacity`'s mechanism-too-fine-to-introspect argument and acknowledged as speculation.

6. **Honour `source-attribution-void`'s neutrality discipline** in the apex's Dualism section. The cluster's evidential weight for Dualism is *compatibility* and *boundary-marking*, not *positive support*. This calibration is load-bearing and should not be inflated for apex-level rhetorical effect.
