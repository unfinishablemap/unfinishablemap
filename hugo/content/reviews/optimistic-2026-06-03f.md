## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
The article keeps the explanatory gap as the explanandum at every turn: it explicitly frames phantoms as exhibiting "the standard hard problem in a setting where the failure to bridge is particularly stark," and refuses to let either the neuromatrix or predictive-processing mechanism stories count as *bridging* the gap from neurosignature activity to felt burning/itching/postural content. The shift-to-persistent-representation reading (Makin) is correctly turned into a *sharpening* of the gap (stable neural activity as the explanandum) rather than a deflation. Chalmers would recognise the gap taken seriously without inflation.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
Less central here — the article does not lean on the physics-consciousness interface. What aligns is the top-down-causation pillar: conscious visual content modulating nociception "without peripheral intervention" is the kind of mind→matter causal pathway Stapp's interactionism needs. The article wisely does *not* overclaim it (see Birch below).

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
Strong centering of first-person directness: "Phantom sensations have the same first-person directness as sensations in intact limbs," and the catalogue of phenomenology that "violates intuitive constraints" (felt position without proprioception, felt pain without nociceptive transduction at the felt site). The impossible-posture-pain passage — "the phenomenal character of *being-about-an-unrealised-bodily-state*" — is exactly the what-it's-like that objective decoding does not deliver.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
The felt body as constructed-and-persistent rather than transmitted resonates with a process reading of embodiment: the body model is an ongoing activity, not a substance relayed from the periphery. The lived/objectified-body cross-link (Leib outlasting Körper) does this gracefully. Per the standing constraint, this resonance is registered as **tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating** — it removes no defeater and upgrades no tier.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
Modest footing — the article's "conscious selection among candidate body-models does real causal work" (mirror as environmental scaffold) is the closest agency claim, and it is correctly hedged as compatible-on-both-sides rather than agency-forcing.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
Exemplary humility. The "What Would Challenge This View?" section specifies *four* concrete defeaters (complete pharmacological control, further mirror-therapy null results, predictive content-specific correlates, vindication of Price's developmental account), and item 3 explicitly disarms the unfalsifiability trap: "A bar set at *bridging* reductive accounts would be structurally unfalsifiable; evidential calibration requires specifying what *evidence* would shift the balance." This is the rare article that names the operational anchor for its own framework-boundary commitment.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
This persona's verdict is **load-bearing and strongly positive** here. The article is a model of the restraint Birch praises:
- It opens by *declining* to treat the base observation as Map-distinctive ("the convergent view of modern pain science... does not on its own embarrass twenty-first-century materialism").
- The **Common-Cause-Null Audit** section is precisely the "N observations or one observation read N ways" discipline, and concludes correctly that "the base is single; the three pillars are not three independent confirmations" — the consequence is "calibrative rather than retractive."
- It reports the findings that cut against it: Guémann et al. (2023) null on durable mirror-therapy efficacy (and *downgrades* its own claim to phenomenological modulation in response), Rajendram et al. (2022) weakening the specifically-visual claim.
- **Tenet-as-evidence-upgrade is praise-worthily NOT done**: the somatic-interface reading is explicitly flagged as "a framework-level commitment imported from that concept article, not earned by the phantom case itself... Phantoms are consistent with the interface reading; they do not independently motivate it." That is the honest move named outright.
The pattern throughout is *"compatible with both interpretations, neither forced by the data alone."* No possibility/probability slippage detected; the Process Philosopher's resonance and the Hardline Empiricist's discipline converge rather than conflict — the article has resolved the tension honestly.
