## The Inversion Identified

The commissioning brief named a synthesis the corpus implies but has not stated: AI systems are not merely possible analogues of minds but **contrast instruments** for testing claims about introspective opacity. The article states the inversion (question shifts from "is the machine like a mind?" to "what does the legible machine reveal about which of our opacities are architecturally necessary?"), then separates two competing readings of human opacity that the human case cannot pull apart on its own: opacity-because-bounded-conscious-witness vs opacity-because-bounded-self-model. AI is the substrate where these readings make different predictions because its internals are inspectable.
