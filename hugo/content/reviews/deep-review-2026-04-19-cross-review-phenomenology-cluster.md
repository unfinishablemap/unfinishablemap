- **Missing Further Reading entries for new article**: All three articles lacked an entry pointing to the new, deeper treatment. **Resolution**: Added targeted Further Reading entries to each, with brief descriptors identifying what the new article adds.

- **Frontmatter `related_articles` gaps**: None of the three articles listed the new article in their related_articles. **Resolution**: Added to each.
