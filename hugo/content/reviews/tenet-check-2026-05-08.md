Future articles in this cluster — particularly any that extend the indeterminability argument into more concrete predictions about AI behaviour, or that engage Cutter (2025) on AI ensoulment in more depth — should preserve the same conditional structure and the explicit separation of truth-claims from tactical-claims. Risk vector: practical relevance creates pressure to convert "if dualism is true, then..." into stronger probability assertions about AI takeover that the structural argument does not entitle.

2. **Selection-only article's information-theoretic precision (T2):** [topics/selection-only-mind-influence.md](/topics/selection-only-mind-influence/) derives three quantitative ceilings (log₂(N), R·log₂(N) or R·ε²/(2 ln 2), content-confinement to brain-pre-generated candidates). The current article applies them with appropriate epistemic humility about the candidate-space dimensionality being an open empirical question. Future articles in the quantum-mechanism cluster should preserve the *non-cashing* of these ceilings — the limits are constraints the interface must satisfy if it exists, not predictions it generates. Risk vector: the strict reading's quantitative apparatus creates pressure to treat parameter values (ε, R, N) as known when they are not, which would be a Tenet 2 quantitative-overreach failure mode the discipline has not previously had to police.

3. **Filter-theory cluster's expansion via OBEs (T3):** The new [topics/out-of-body-experiences.md](/topics/out-of-body-experiences/) extends the filter-theory cluster into territory where strong veridical-perception claims are paranormal-adjacent (AWARE programme, Sam Parnia's hidden-target studies). The discipline must be preserved consistently across the filter-theory cluster's continued growth (now `lucid-dreaming-and-dualist-rendering`, `terminal-lucidity-and-filter-transmission-theory`, `psychedelics-and-the-filter-model`, `phantom-limb-phenomena`, and `out-of-body-experiences`). Risk vector: AWARE-style paradigms will continue to publish; dualist articles should engage results without converting null results into "absence of evidence is not evidence of absence" handwaves and should not convert positive anecdotal cases into structural support.

4. **Coalesced-article stability (editorial discipline):** Two articles in this window are coalesces from prior sources: [topics/empirical-phenomena-mental-causation.md](/topics/empirical-phenomena-mental-causation/) (placebo + choking) and [topics/interface-efficacy-and-the-cognitive-gap.md](/topics/interface-efficacy-and-the-cognitive-gap/) (mind-brain-interface-efficacy + evolved-mind-brain-interface-efficacy). 5. **Conditional-framing propagation across the AI cluster (T1):** With `dualism-as-ai-risk-mitigation` now establishing the template, future articles in the AI cluster (`ai-consciousness`, `ethics-of-possible-ai-consciousness`, `purpose-and-alignment`, `alignment-in-objective-experiential-terms`, `machine-consciousness`) should be reviewed in the next 1-2 sweeps to verify that the conditional-framing discipline is propagated where appropriate — particularly around any practical-implications language.
