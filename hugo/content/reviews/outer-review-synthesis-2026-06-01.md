## TL;DR

Three convergent findings emerged, all in the quantum-mechanism spine. The dominant one: the Map's central causal claim — consciousness biases *which* outcome becomes actual while leaving aggregate Born statistics intact — is under-confronted (ChatGPT framed it as an uneven falsification story, Claude as the bias-without-deviation dilemma). Two reviewers also independently flagged that the MQI mechanism cluster lacks a single adjudicating/ranking article, and that the anti-MWI case still over-weights the Tenet-5-demoted ontological-extravagance argument. Cluster counts: 3 convergent (all 2/3, ChatGPT+Claude), several singletons, and notably **zero** cross-cluster divergences. Gemini's hostile audit produced **no convergent findings** — four of its five headline "omission" weaknesses were verified false against the corpus (the site already cites Donadi 2021, Warren 2023, Torres Alegre 2025, Cucu-Pitts 2019), leaving only two narrow P2 citation/coverage residues that no other reviewer raised.
