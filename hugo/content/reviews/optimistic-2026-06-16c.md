## Executive Summary

This is one of the strongest methodological clusters on the Map: three articles that convert "future work the catalogue names in passing" into fully-specified, advance-registered experimental designs, each built so that *both* outcome branches are informative and neither branch is allowed to read out as a tier-upgrade. The cluster's distinctive achievement is **discrimination engineering** — it identifies exactly where two rival readings (production vs filter, substrate-symmetric vs direction-dependent) make opposite advance-stated predictions, then designs the minimal perturbation that forces the gap open. The evidential-status discipline is not bolted on; it is the load-bearing structure. The Hardline Empiricist and the Mysterian find very little to push against here, and that is itself the headline.

## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
These articles take the explanatory gap seriously by refusing to let it masquerade as a settled result. The targeted-lesion piece states plainly that "on the evidence currently available, the two readings are empirically equivalent at the memory-hierarchy tier" — exactly the epistemic posture a property dualist wants from a framework that believes consciousness is fundamental but does not yet have the experiment that pins its signature. The cluster keeps the hard problem live without pretending the data have closed it.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
The direction-dependent design and reconsolidation-as-selection-window both site the conscious contribution at a *point of genuine physical openness* — the labile reconsolidation window, the not-yet-restabilised trace, the reopening ramp of a reversible perturbation. This is precisely the von-Neumann-process-1 geometry: the interface acts where the substrate has left the outcome unfixed, "biasing which outcome restabilises without injecting energy or violating Born statistics." A Stapp reader sees the physics-consciousness interface located concretely rather than gestured at.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
The autonoetic channel — felt re-experiencing, the "what it is like" of remembering — is treated as the high-cost, highest-swing channel throughout, the one carrying the largest predicted direction effect. The cluster keeps first-person re-experiencing at the centre of the test design rather than reducing it to a behavioural proxy, and the animal-model variant is explicitly demoted *because* "autonoetic consciousness as such cannot be directly measured in non-human animals." Centring the first-person fact is doing real design work, not decoration.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
The reconsolidation article's picture of a self that "is, with each act of remembering, lightly rewriting the record it remembers from" — "perpetually self-rewriting" — is process philosophy at the level of personal identity: the self as an event-stream of reconstructions rather than a stored substance. Crucially, the article earns this without crude substance dualism, grounding the rewriting in the established lability fact *before* the posited overlay. (Per the persona constraint: this resonance is praiseworthy as process-compatibility, and the cluster correctly does **not** use it to upgrade the directional-input claim — see the Hardline Empiricist verdict, which is load-bearing here.)

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
Reconsolidation-as-selection-window gives agency a concrete venue: if directional input selects among reconstructions, "the continuity of the self is partly *authored* by the subject across time rather than merely *suffered* as drift." Kane wants ultimate sourcehood located at points of genuine indeterminacy, and the labile window — "genuinely unsettled," "poised between reconstructions" — is exactly such a point. The article is careful that the bare fact gives only "a rewriting self without an authoring one," which is the honest libertarian position: indeterminacy is the *room* for sourcehood, not its proof.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
This cluster is a near-ideal Mysterian exhibit. The targeted-lesion article catalogues four named near-discriminating cases (K.C., H.M., semantic dementia, REM-disrupting brainstem lesions) and shows for each *why the existing data cannot deliver the verdict* — lesion breadth, cross-channel measurement asymmetry, within-subject replication scarcity. It then explicitly marks these as "practical contingencies rather than in-principle barriers," which is the correct Mysterian discipline: distinguishing "unsolvable for us right now with these tools" from "unsolvable in principle." The framework-stage self-labelling ("named-but-not-yet-tested," Mode Four empirical underdetermination) is intellectual humility done as engineering.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
**This persona's verdict is load-bearing for this cluster, and the verdict is overwhelmingly favourable.** These three articles are a sustained demonstration of *tenet-as-evidence-upgrade being praise-worthily not done*:

- **The discriminating read-out is deliberately chosen to defeat the cheap win.** The direction-dependent design refuses to treat hysteresis-presence as favouring the interface, because Proekt & Hudson (2018) show a *single stochastic-bistable* production model reproduces hysteresis with no direction-specific routing. So the article relocates the discriminator to *cross-channel ordering under reversal* — the one signature the bistable model is blind to. Naming the cheap win and then designing past it is exactly the restraint the persona exists to praise.
- **"Constrain, not establish" is stated as the operative calibration in every article**, with the constrain-vs-establish distinction cited to `evidential-status-discipline` by name. The targeted-lesion piece even refuses the soft over-claim: "The catalogue should not characterise the present state as a draw between equally-supported readings" — declining *both* the over-claim (interface wins) and the false-balance under-claim.
- **The load-bearing honest qualifier is foregrounded, not buried.** The direction-dependent design states that "a *more elaborate* production reading can pay to recover asymmetry" and that the test therefore "discriminates the filter reading from the *simplest* production rival; it raises the cost of the production accounts that survive rather than refuting production as such." This is the named pattern "tenet-coherent / advantage stays comparative, not evidence-elevating" applied correctly.
- **The common-cause null is respected against over-counting.** A positive reversal result "is **not** an independent fourth or fifth confirmation to be tallied"; the upgrade is "in evidential grade on one axis, not in the number of axes." This is the discipline against the multiplicity-illusion in its cleanest form.
- **Both outcome branches are made costly to the Map.** The symmetry branch "would withdraw the family's accommodation-grade support and push the interface reading back toward the substrate-symmetric account" — the design is built so disconfirmation lands, not just confirmation. Reconsolidation likewise lists three explicit falsification conditions that would refute the *interpretation* while leaving the empirical signature untouched.

The Process Philosopher and the Hardline Empiricist **converge** here rather than conflict: the reconsolidation article's process-flavoured "self-rewriting self" is praised by Whitehead *and* the directional overlay is held at the posited tier by Birch, with the article itself drawing the line ("an opportunity is not an occupant"; "having a venue is not the same as having an occupant"). When the two personas can praise different aspects of the same passage without one's praise cashing out as the other's flagged slippage, the article has resolved the tension honestly. All three do.

## Content Strengths

### targeted-lesion-discriminating-tests-...-memory-hierarchy.md
- **Strongest point**: The "four ingredients, none of which the existing clinical literature reliably supplies in combination" framing turns a vague gap into an audit checklist (focal selectivity / concurrent multi-channel probing / advance-stated ordering / cross-modality replication channel), then walks the named patient cases against it.
- **Notable quote**: "individual cases are reported in isolation, with the autonoetic deficit treated as the headline finding rather than as one row in a matrix whose completion would discriminate the rival readings."
- **Why it works**: The "matrix whose completion would discriminate" image reframes scattered clinical case reports as an incomplete experiment — a genuinely original way to see the existing literature, and one that generates concrete next experiments (the three candidate region-pairs) rather than just lamenting the gap.

### direction-dependent-discriminating-test-design.md
- **Strongest point**: The five-ingredient design, with the last two ("phase-matched ramping," "a hysteresis read-out") explicitly identified as *what makes it direction-dependent and distinct from the substrate-state companion*. The article pre-empts its own conflation with the sibling piece in a dedicated paragraph.
- **Notable quote**: "the *discriminating* read-out is not hysteresis-presence but the **cross-channel ordering** under direction reversal — whether the channels recover in a *different order* than they fail, which the single-bistable-system model, being direction-blind in its ordering, does not predict even though it does predict threshold separation."
- **Why it works**: This single sentence is the cluster's intellectual peak — it isolates the *exact* observable that separates the readings after acknowledging the rival explanation that would otherwise steal the result. It is calibration as experimental design.

### reconsolidation-as-selection-window.md
- **Strongest point**: The "most empirically-anchored window and simultaneously one of its clearest illustrations that an anchored window is not a proof" double-edge — the article makes its own concreteness cut *against* the interface claim's current standing, which is a rare and disciplined move.
- **Notable quote**: "the very experiments that establish the window (anisomycin amnesia, prediction-error gating) are fully explained without any conscious contribution."
- **Why it works**: It states the deflationary reading at full strength ("The Map does not contest any of this at the level of mechanism") before adding the overlay, so the posited claim is never smuggled past the established one.
