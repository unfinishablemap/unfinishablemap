## Executive Summary

This cohort is among the most evidentially disciplined material on the Map. Its defining strength is calibration architecture — a five-grade evidence ladder, an explicit calibration table that allocates each finding to the Tenet-2 sub-reading it actually bears on, a preference-ordering-that-is-not-a-commitment, and a four-layer dependency structure — deployed precisely where quantum-consciousness writing usually over-claims. The Process Philosopher and the Hardline Empiricist, normally in productive tension, **converge in praise** here: the articles extract metaphysical-possibility value from quantum mechanisms without letting any of it cash out as a tier-upgrade in evidential status. Expansion opportunities are modest and mostly foreclosed by section caps; the honest recommendation is consolidation and cross-linking, not new articles.

## Praise from Sympathetic Philosophers

### The Property Dualist (Chalmers)
Chalmers would recognise his own CSL-IIT hybrid handled fairly and see the hard problem kept firmly in view: `entanglement-binding-hypothesis` explicitly reserves a "What the Hypothesis Does Not Explain" section stating that non-separable physical states, however holistic, do not explain *why* they are accompanied by experience — "the hard problem survives even if the binding hypothesis is vindicated." The comparison article makes phenomenal character a standing criterion (criterion 4) rather than smuggling it in.

### The Quantum Mind Theorist (Stapp)
Stapp's Zeno model is given the single strongest "phenomenological fit" mark and its own place in the preference ordering, while the article is candid that Stapp *began* with James's phenomenology and fitted a mechanism to it — accommodation, not novel prediction. This is exactly the kind of serious, non-hagiographic engagement with the physics-consciousness interface Stapp asks for.

### The Phenomenologist (Nagel)
The "temporal grain of experience," "felt effort," and "willed/automatic distinction" recur as first-person desiderata each mechanism is scored against. Fisher's nuclear-spin hypothesis is docked precisely because minutes-to-hours coherence "explains the wrong grain of experience." First-person structure is treated as data a complete theory owes an account of.

### The Process Philosopher (Whitehead)
The cohort avoids crude substance dualism: Penrose OR is adopted as "the physical side of a mind-matter interface" while explicitly diverging from Penrose's own non-dualist ontology, and CSL-IIT is framed as consciousness *modulating* an already-occurring process rather than injecting force. Crucially — and this is where the Process Philosopher's praise is kept on its proper axis — the process-friendly readings are *never* used to upgrade any mechanism's evidential standing. `entanglement-binding-hypothesis` even flags that "entangled physical states *are* unified experiences" is an available panpsychist/neutral-monist reading, then declines to lean on it.

### The Libertarian Free Will Defender (Kane)
The preference ordering ties mechanism choice to genuine forward-in-time selection at the point "where physics leaves outcomes undetermined," preserving a real locus for agency without over-selling it. `comparing-quantum-consciousness-mechanisms` links mechanism choice to `quantum-indeterminacy-free-will`, keeping the agency stakes explicit.

### The Mysterian (McGinn)
Epistemic humility is structural, not decorative. The "falsifiability gap" section concedes "current evidence does not adjudicate between these mechanisms"; the CSL-IIT entry notes the untestable component "may be a principled limit rather than a technological one"; the Penrose article admits that if collapse models are entirely falsified "the Map must reconsider." The honesty-vs-dogma line is drawn by the authors themselves.

### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)
This persona is **load-bearing** for this cluster, and its verdict is strongly positive. The material does the discipline Birch demands, by name:

- **Precedent-not-licence, stated as a rule.** `quantum-biology-and-neural-consciousness` opens by insisting warm quantum biology "is a *precedent*, not a *licence*" and that the strongest external results support the *narrower, lower-grade* claims (a drug delaying rat anaesthesia; warm optical effects; instrumentation a decade out), not the consciousness-relevance claims at the top.
- **Five evidence grades kept separate** (direct biological data / biophysical plausibility / theoretical model / instrumentation horizon / speculative consciousness-relevance) with an explicit refusal to sum independent lines as independent *confirmations*.
- **The calibration table** is the standout artifact: it routes each finding to the Tenet-2 sub-reading it bears on (pre-decoherence mechanisms / post-decoherence selection / strict corridor) and names two over-counting errors it exists to prevent. This is "tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating" implemented as a table.
- **Tenet-as-evidence-upgrade is praise-worthily *not* done.** Every article had the structural opportunity to slide microtubule/entanglement findings up the scale on tenet-load and explicitly declined — the Khan epothilone-B result is given its "parsimonious classical reading" (GABA_A trafficking) in the same breath; Wiest 2025 is labelled "a partisan Orch-OR brief," not settled evidence.
- **Framework-stage calibration** is invoked correctly: the evidence delivers "Tycho-analogue measurements without yet a Kepler-analogue specification law," and Duch's opposite confidence is named as itself a stage-calibrated bet.
- **The preference ordering is fenced off from truth-claims** ("a preference ordering, not an empirical result… a mechanism the Map prefers can still turn out false"), with the four-layer dependency structure spelling out that "no commitment" lives at Layer 3.

Where Process Philosopher and Hardline Empiricist would normally pull apart, they here praise the *same* passages — the signal, per the skill, that the cluster has resolved the tension honestly rather than papering over slippage.

## Content Strengths

### comparing-quantum-consciousness-mechanisms.md
- **Strongest point**: The "Preference Ordering — and Why It Is Not a Commitment" section plus the four-layer dependency structure (metaphysical commitment / interface constraint / mechanism family / empirical sub-reading). It lets the Map *rank* Orch OR below Stapp/CSL-IIT on tenet-fit while insisting the ranking is methodological, not a bet on which mechanism nature uses.
- **Why it works**: It pre-empts the two symmetric misreadings ("endorses all equally" vs "committed to post-decoherence selection"), naming each as "collapsing a layer." Separating scientific from philosophical criteria as "two independent dimensions" is the move that makes the whole comparison honest.
- **Bonus**: The Duch section models "convergent-conclusion-opposite-reasoning" — importing his anti-coherence arguments while explicitly refusing his classical-sufficiency commitment as "a category error."

### penrose-gravity-induced-collapse-empirical-prospects.md
- **Strongest point**: The treatment of the 2021 Donadi germanium falsification. It does not spin the result: the parameter-free DP virtue is called out as lost, McQueen's Orch-OR rescue is labelled "structurally a rescue of the kind Donadi flagged," and the Derakhshani et al. (2022) atomic-scale R₀ constraint ("highly implausible in all the cases analyzed") is quoted against the Map's own preferred reading.
- **Notable quote**: "The surviving DP region therefore buys empirical viability at the price of that natural-threshold rationale: an open cost, not a second falsification."
- **Why it works**: It distinguishes "the physically simplest version failed" from "the physical insight failed," which is precisely the Tenet-5 (Occam's limits) lesson the article then draws — earned, not asserted.

### quantum-biology-and-neural-consciousness.md
- **Strongest point**: The calibration table and the "What Remains Uncertain" tri-partition (Established / Raises the prior / Uncertain). The lead paragraph itself refuses to read the 2025-26 work "as a single advance toward 'testable quantum-consciousness science.'"
- **Why it works**: It repeatedly separates the *category* precedent (cryptochrome Zeno effect is real) from any *neural deployment* of it ("The precedent is for the mechanism category, not for any neural deployment of it"). Contra-evidence (Bukhteeva NCLX null) is surfaced, not buried.

### entanglement-binding-hypothesis.md
- **Strongest point**: The "Separability No-Go" section. It turns the hypothesis's own structural argument against itself via Georgiev (2021) — a tensor-product factorization would mark a *split into independent minds*, and isolated coherence *preserves* separability — then answers by relocating binding into the interaction Hamiltonian rather than coherence. Few advocacy pieces would import a constraint this sharp.
- **Two-tier evidence framing**: The microtubule-anaesthesia line and the macroscopic-MRI-entanglement line are explicitly weighted differently ("A reader should not weight the two lines equally"), with the Baum zero-lag-gamma premise now correctly hedged as disputed by the mainstream dynamical-relaying literature (resolving the prior `quantum-binding-cluster-baum-premise-overclaim` concern for this article).
