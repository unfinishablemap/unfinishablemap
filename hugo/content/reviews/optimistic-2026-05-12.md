### The Hardline Empiricist (Birch)

This persona's praise is load-bearing for both articles because both faced structural temptation toward over-claim that they explicitly declined.

**For `mechanism-costs-cartography`, three moves warrant explicit Birch-praise.**

**First, the asymmetry-concession discipline (lines 54–58) is the structurally honest move.** The article had structural temptation: a cartography exposing relative cell-costs will typically produce "the Map's preferred cell pays least" results, and the temptation is to let that asymmetry function as silent endorsement. The article instead requires *explicit concession*: "name the asymmetry, acknowledge it as a real feature of the question selection rather than burying it." The inaugural deployment's concession formula is quoted in line 56: "A reader might worry the trio over-emphasises the physical-side debts. It does — deliberately. The conservation-law and interface questions originate in the physicalist's challenge to interactionism, and the literature has weighted them heavily." This is *tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating* at the editorial-discipline level: the methodology surfaces cell-relative debts without converting the asymmetric debts into endorsement-of-the-lean-cell. Birch's broader programme of precaution-under-uncertainty practices the same discipline at the empirical layer — name the bias in the question framing, treat the resulting asymmetry as an artefact of the framing rather than as positive evidence — and the cartography installs exactly this discipline at the editorial layer.

**Second, the non-adjudicative posture (lines 64–68) is the discipline's signature Mode-Three-preservation move.** The article explicitly forecloses the inflation-route that mechanism-cost accounting is most prone to: "The methodology cannot adjudicate between cells. Reading off Q4's heavier interface and conservation bill does not show Q4 is wrong; reading off Q2 strict's evasion of the mechanism questions does not show Q2 strict is right. The cartography exposes what each cell owes; whether the bill is worth paying is a separate question the cartography preserves rather than answers." Line 68's formula — "Nothing in this article argues that any cell is *wrong*. The cells differ in what they owe, not in whether they are defensible. The cartography speaks; the tenets adjudicate" — is the *direct-refutation discipline's* Mode-Three engagement applied at the cartographic layer. Birch's framework's central worry — that defeater-removal moves can quietly slide into evidence-positive moves — is foreclosed at the methodology level by this non-adjudicative posture. Cost-accounting *removes* the framing that the Map's preferred cell is uncosted; it does not *supply* the framing that the Map's preferred cell is established. This is the framework-stage-calibration discipline (per [framework-stage-calibration](/project/framework-stage-calibration/)) operating at exactly the layer where the cartography sits.

**Third, the §"Honest Limitation" section (lines 70–74) is the discipline's signature *self-bound* move.** The article names two limitations explicitly: (a) "The methodology trades depth for breadth at the level of cell treatment ... The inaugural deployment cannot adjudicate Stapp's interpretation of his own framework or specify what Schaffer's trumping template would demand of a non-stipulative authority law"; (b) "The battery itself is also defeasible. A future critic may convince the catalogue that a fourth mechanism question (articulability, intelligibility under intersubstitution, overdetermination-avoidance) belongs in the battery; the cartography would then recompute cell-relative debts under the enriched battery." The defeasibility-of-the-battery acknowledgement is the load-bearing Birch-praise-worthy move: the article does not pretend the question battery is interpretation-neutral or that the trio is *the* trio rather than *a* trio the literature has weighted. Naming the battery as defeasible and the methodology as resilient under battery-recalibration is the structural-honesty move that mechanism-cost accounting is most often guilty of *not* making (when researchers treat their chosen battery as canonical rather than as a stipulated framework).

**For `consciousness-and-mathematics`, four moves warrant explicit Birch-praise.**

**First, the deflationary-account-remains-live hedge (line 142) is the central inferential discipline.** The article's §"Understanding vs. Verification" had structural temptation to claim refutation of the compressed-verification deflation. The literature on chunking and pattern-recognition gives the deflationary account real bite; the article could have hidden the bite by selecting only the cases where deflation fails. Instead the article names three considerations against deflation (compression explains speed not content; understanding arrives discontinuously; the aesthetic criterion becomes mysterious) and then explicitly retreats: "The deflationary account remains a live alternative. The claim is that felt necessity is *better explained* as carrying genuine semantic content than as epiphenomenal to compression." This is the *constrain-vs-establish* discipline at exactly the load-bearing point: the article does not claim deflation is refuted; it claims dualist explanation is better-fit. Birch's framework of distinguishing *defeater-removal* from *evidence-supplied* operates here precisely: the article removes the defeater that says compression accounts for the entire phenomenon, but does not convert the defeater-removal into evidence that compression-deflation is wrong. The disagreement remains framework-level, and the article's calibration matches what the discipline supports.

**Second, the §"What Would Challenge This View?" section (lines 196–206) is the structural-falsifiability discipline at its right grain.** Each of the four falsifiers names what would have to *change structurally* — AI demonstrating *transformational* (not merely competent) mathematical creativity without phenomenal consciousness; a computational account of *aesthetic* mathematical judgment tracking depth without phenomenal experience; a *successful formalist epistemology* explaining the full range of practice including aesthetic reliability; *dissolution of the felt difference* between verification and understanding through investigation. The falsifiers are not low-bar incremental items ("show that some AI can do some maths") but structural conditions ("show that AI can do transformational maths without phenomenal consciousness"). Birch's discipline of distinguishing *strong falsifiers* from *weak ones* — and weighting the framework's commitment by the strength of its falsifiers — operates here at the right level: the article's commitment to dualism scales with the difficulty of demonstrating these structural conditions, not with the easier alternative of arguing about narrow cases. The article also names the supports honestly: "continued failure of AI to achieve transformational mathematical creativity, cross-cultural convergence on insight's distinctive phenomenal character, and successful arguments showing why understanding must be phenomenal." Each support is a *structural fact-pattern* the falsifiers' negations would establish, not a triumph-statement.

**Third, Penrose's argument is held at conditional rather than apodictic strength (lines 110–114).** Penrose's Gödelian argument is presented in the form a critic could grant: incompleteness theorems establish limits on algorithms; mathematicians *can see* these truths (which is the contested premise); the argument follows. The article *names* the critic's objection ("the argument assumes human consistency — an unproven claim") before stating the regress reply. The regress reply itself is hedged: "The regress suggests understanding cannot be captured by *any* fixed algorithm. The creative dimension deepens this." The word *suggests* is doing load-bearing work here: the regress is *suggestive* of an impossibility that is also defended by the creative-dimension consideration; the impossibility is not asserted as established. Birch's discipline applies cleanly: Penrose's argument is treated as supplying a converging consideration that *fits* with the broader case for irreducibility, not as a knock-down argument that *establishes* irreducibility. Tenet-coherent, not evidence-elevating, at the inferential register.

**Fourth, the article does not absorb cross-cultural-convergence into tier-upgrade.** The §"Sense of Discovery" subsection (line 150) notes that mathematicians overwhelmingly describe their work as finding rather than inventing; Erdős spoke of proofs being in "The Book"; even anti-Platonists use discovery terms. This convergence could be deployed as evidence that mathematical Platonism is metaphysically established. The article instead retreats: "The tension with creative generation may resolve through distinguishing levels: *frameworks* are created while *truths* are discovered." The cross-cultural convergence is treated as *establishing a phenomenology of mathematical practice* (which it does), not as *establishing Platonism* (which it doesn't). The level distinction allows discovery-language to be honest at the truth-level while admitting creative-language at the framework-level. This is the discipline's signature *honest-resolution-of-apparent-evidence-for-stronger-thesis* move at exactly the load-bearing grain.

**Tenet-as-evidence-upgrade is praise-worthily *not* done across both articles.** The cartography article had structural temptation to deploy the Process Philosopher's praise as tier-upgrade — the parallel-structure-over-structured-space argument could have been pushed from "discipline-coherent" to "metaphysics-established." The article explicitly declines (line 68: "the cartography speaks; the tenets adjudicate"). The mathematics article had structural temptation to deploy the cross-traditional-discovery convergence as tier-upgrade for Platonism specifically; the article explicitly declines (line 150 level distinction). Both articles preserve the calibration: process-philosophical resonance and cross-cultural convergence remove *defeaters* against the framework's coherence; they do not *supply evidence* that the framework's metaphysical claims are tier-upgrade-warranted. The Process Philosopher and Hardline Empiricist personas converge on both articles at exactly the moments where the articles had structural temptation to slide — convergence at the temptation-point is the strongest signal the discipline is operating.
