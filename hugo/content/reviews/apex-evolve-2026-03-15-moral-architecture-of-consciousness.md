**Narrative arc**:
1. Open with the unity thesis — consciousness as unified moral architecture
2. Walk through each of the four domains, showing how each is grounded in consciousness
3. Present the unity argument — coherence, mutual reinforcement, explanatory economy
4. Apply to concrete moral questions — animals, AI, criminal responsibility
5. Close with the open question — value-blind vs value-sensitive selection
