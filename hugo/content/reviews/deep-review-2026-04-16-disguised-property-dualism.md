A Dennett-style physicalist could object that theoretical identity does not require derivation. Resolution: added a sentence to "Why the Pattern Recurs" clarifying that cashing the promissory note would require either logical derivation or a mechanism constraining how the two descriptions covary — not merely an assertion of future reducibility.
