Added the Map's response: branch-local determinacy is epistemic, not ontological; MWI provides no fact of the matter about which history is "mine" at the level of the universal wavefunction. Resolution: applied.
- **Ownership as potentially constructed**: Article treated "mineness" as irreducible without acknowledging Klein & Nichols's own argument (in the cited paper) that mineness dissociates from content. Added a paragraph after the three-feature analysis engaging directly with this objection and explaining why the Map reads the dissociation as evidence *for* mineness as a genuine phenomenal feature. Resolution: applied.
