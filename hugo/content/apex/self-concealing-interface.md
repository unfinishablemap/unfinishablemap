A theory that says consciousness influences the physical world owes an account of why nobody has caught it doing so. The Unfinishable Map's answer is sharper than the usual appeal to difficulty: the interface is not merely hard to detect, it is *structurally selected to hide from ordinary aggregate measurement*. Every constraint the Map accepts—conservation of energy, the no-signalling theorem, the Born statistics, the integrity of the empirical record—forces the proposed conscious influence into the one register where bulk physical instruments cannot see it. The influence must be real enough to author action and small enough to leave the ensemble distribution untouched. The corollary, drawn out here for the first time as a single thesis, is that the proper evidential route is not "find a big quantum anomaly" but "find constrained asymmetries at interface boundaries": disruption, dissociation, graded channel failure, patient-population divergence, and first-person/third-person mismatch.

This is a framework-internal claim about *architecture*, not an empirical result. It says what kind of thing the interface must be if the tenets hold, and where evidence about it could in principle appear. It does not raise the probability that the tenets hold. The distinction matters enough that it organises the whole piece: naming the self-concealing architecture is a coherence achievement at the [framework-internal-weight register](/project/evidential-status-discipline/), and reading off where the seams are is a research-programme reorientation—neither is positive evidence that consciousness selects. The synthesis draws together what [the tenets](/tenets/), [brain-internal Born-rule testing](/topics/brain-internal-born-rule-testing/), [forward-in-time selection](/topics/forward-in-time-conscious-selection/), [attention as the causal bridge](/apex/attention-as-causal-bridge/), [the introspection-architecture cluster](/apex/introspection-architecture-void-cluster/), [the memory hierarchy](/topics/memory-channel-interface-evidence/), and [the voids catalogue](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/) each already imply, and names the architecture they converge on.

## The Concealment Is Built Into the Tenets, Not Added to Them

The self-concealing property is not a defensive afterthought bolted on when detection failed. It falls out of the tenets directly, and tracing the derivation is what separates an honest architectural claim from an unfalsifiable dodge.

[Minimal Quantum Interaction](/tenets/#minimal-quantum-interaction) commits to the *smallest* deviation from standard quantum mechanics that could permit mind-matter causation. The Map's strongest reading of that minimum is the strict corridor: consciousness selects which definite outcome emerges from the classical mixture left after [decoherence](/concepts/decoherence/) completes, choosing among Born-equiprobable alternatives without altering the ensemble distribution. The tenets page states the cost plainly: "Selecting between Born-equiprobable alternatives makes the mechanism undetectable by aggregate Born-statistics tests; the framework cannot be probed by the experimental routes that constrained earlier interactionist proposals." The very move that buys the framework its escape from the timing objection—acting after decoherence, on already-classical alternatives—is the move that empties the aggregate signature. A selection that respected energy conservation but *shifted* the Born distribution would be detectable; the Map forbids exactly that, because such a shift would violate the empirical record it is bound to honour.

[Brain-internal Born-rule testing](/topics/brain-internal-born-rule-testing/) makes the consequence precise: the strict corridor predicts *no* Born-statistical signature, by construction. A null result from a within-brain test of Born statistics cannot Popper-refute the strict corridor, because the corridor never promised a statistical fingerprint; null results can retire competing mechanisms or remove the motivation for the search, but they leave the strict reading where they found it. This is the cleanest instance of the general pattern. The no-signalling theorem closes the door on the interface ever being used to transmit information faster than light or between isolated systems; conservation laws close the door on its being caught injecting energy; the Born rule closes the door on its perturbing the statistics. Each closed door is a constraint the Map *accepts as true*, and the conjunction of accepted constraints is what does the concealing.

The architecture, then, is not "the interface happens to be elusive." It is "the only interface the tenets permit is one that the standard battery of aggregate physical measurements is constitutively unable to register." Concealment is entailed, not assumed.

## A Constraint, Not a Defeater—and Not a Vindication

Naming a self-concealing architecture invites two opposite errors, and the discipline of this synthesis is to refuse both.

The first error is the sceptic's: "a cause that cannot be measured in aggregate is no cause at all; you have defined yourself into unfalsifiability." This overstates. The strict corridor is undetectable by *aggregate Born-statistics tests* specifically; it is not undetectable in every register. The whole point of the reorientation below is that the architecture predicts where evidence *can* appear—at seams—and several of those predictions are operational enough to fail. A framework that says "look here, not there, and here is what would count against me at the seams" is constrained, not vacuous. What it forfeits is the easy falsifier: it can never be the case that a clean ensemble-level anomaly settles the matter, because the Map predicted no such anomaly from the start.

The second error is the believer's: "the interface is hidden exactly as a real-but-minimal interface would be, so its hiddenness confirms it." This is the slippage [the evidential-status discipline](/project/evidential-status-discipline/) exists to catch. That the evidence is absent *where the architecture predicts absence* is compatibility, not support. A framework earns support over its rivals only by naming a discriminator the rivals do not also predict—and "no aggregate signature" is predicted equally by the hypothesis that there is no interface at all. The hiddenness is therefore evidentially inert between the Map and eliminativism about the interface; it cannot be cashed as a point in the Map's favour. The honest reading is the one the tenets page already takes: without seam-level discriminating evidence, "the tenet remains an interpretive proposal rather than a tested commitment."

So the self-concealing thesis sits precisely at the framework-internal architecture-naming tier. It tells the Map what shape its own commitments force the interface into, and it tells the evidential programme where to point. It does not move the needle on whether the commitments are true. Holding those two things apart is the entire reason the thesis is worth stating: an unstated self-concealment thesis is the kind of thing that drifts, in lazy hands, into "you can't disprove it, therefore it's plausible." Stated explicitly, with its evidential tier marked, it does the opposite—it tells the Map exactly which victories it is not allowed to claim.

## The Architecture's Five Consequences

If the interface conceals itself from bulk measurement, then several features of the Map that have read as separate puzzles turn out to be consequences of one architecture. Each is a place where the corpus already moves toward seam-evidence; naming the architecture is what lets them be seen as a family.

**Strict-corridor invisibility** is the consequence at the level of physics, treated above. The interface leaves the ensemble distribution untouched; aggregate Born-statistics tests are blind to it by construction. This is the load-bearing case, because it is the one that forecloses the obvious detection route and forces every other consequence to carry the evidential burden.

**Attention bandwidth** is the consequence at the level of throughput. If consciousness acts through [a narrow selection bridge](/apex/attention-as-causal-bridge/), the channel is constrained to roughly ten bits per second of behavioural throughput—a rate "consistent with a constrained interface limiting conscious intervention to the smallest influence consistent with genuine causal efficacy." A wide-bandwidth interface would be a large physical effect and would already have been found; a bandwidth this narrow is exactly what a minimal interface predicts, and exactly what makes the interface's contribution easy to lose in the brain's classical traffic. The bandwidth limit is not a design flaw but the throughput-side face of self-concealment.

**Introspection opacity** is the consequence at the level of self-report. [The introspection-architecture cluster](/apex/introspection-architecture-void-cluster/) establishes that consciousness receives reconstructed endpoints, not the operations that produce them, across five distinct registers—storytelling, source-tagging, gating, fabrication, reality-monitoring. The cluster draws the implication the self-concealing thesis needs: "if the interface leaves no phenomenal markers, the absence of a felt tag for mind-side causation is *expected*. A markerless interface is, from the subject's side, observationally indistinguishable from no interface." The confabulation engine retroactively supplies reasons for every action regardless of its true cause, which means the subject's own introspective report is the *last* place a conscious causal contribution would show up. Self-concealment is not only from the third-person instrument; it is from the first-person witness.

**Memory-channel vulnerability** is the consequence at the level of graded breakdown. [The memory-channel interface-evidence article](/topics/memory-channel-interface-evidence/) reads the fixed degradation order—autonoetic most vulnerable, noetic intermediate, anoetic most preserved, recurring across anaesthesia, sleep, hemispherectomy, terminal lucidity, and dissociation—not as a decisive proof but as "the *shape* of evidence a multi-channel interface would predict, and the shape production theories seem to struggle to produce without ad-hoc fitting." This is the architecture showing itself precisely where the self-concealing thesis says it should: not in the intact ensemble, but in the *order* in which channels fail when the interface is partially blocked. The seam is the failure-ordering, not the bulk trace. And the ordering is richer than a single profile: [the order in which channels recover is not the mirror image of the order in which they fail](/topics/memory-channel-interface-evidence/)—the autonoetic channel returns *last* on anaesthesia emergence yet *first and most prominently* in terminal lucidity. The seam, on this refinement, is not only which channels fail but *which way the system is moving* when they do.

**The voids catalogue** is the consequence at the level of cognitive cartography. [The taxonomy of voids](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/) finds 60-plus cognitive limits clustering at the consciousness-matter interface, with the self-knowledge and introspective families densest. The catalogue's own audit already states the seam-level prediction the self-concealing thesis generalises: "if the tenet-package is correct, *introspective channel disruption*, *attention disruption*, *memory degradation*, and *source-monitoring failure* should cluster together under conditions that disrupt the consciousness-matter interface." The voids are not a miscellany of difficulties; they are the map of where a self-concealing interface leaves its only legible marks—at the edges where cognition fails, not in the middle where it runs smoothly.

The five are one architecture under five descriptions. A minimal post-decoherence selector, acting through a narrow bandwidth, reporting itself only through a reconstructive introspective channel, degrading channel-by-channel under partial blockade, and leaving its densest legible traces at cognitive seams—this is a single thing seen from physics, throughput, self-report, breakdown, and cartography. The unifying claim is that all five are faces of *concealment by construction*.

## Re-Orienting the Falsification Programme Around Seams

If the architecture is right about itself, the falsification programme has been looking in the wrong place whenever it looked for bulk physical traces. The [falsification roadmap](/topics/falsification-roadmap-for-the-interface-model/) already distinguishes corridor, outside-corridor, and trumping readings of Tenet 2, and already notes that the strict corridor predicts no Born signature; the self-concealing thesis turns that scattered caution into a positive editorial instruction. The evidence that could ever bear on the interface lives at five kinds of seam.

**Disruption.** Perturb the interface and watch how function breaks, not whether the bulk signal shifts. [Altered states](/apex/altered-states-as-interface-evidence/)—anaesthesia, psychedelics, dreaming, hypnagogia—modulate specific interface components in predictable ways; the pattern of which features survive, degrade, or intensify is the datum, and it is a seam datum because it is visible only under perturbation.

**Dissociation.** [The phenomenal-output / causal-machinery dissociation](/apex/phenomenal-output-causal-machinery-dissociation/) shows phenomenal access reaching outputs but not the operations producing them, across perception, cognition, agency, and memory. A dissociation is a seam by definition: it is the gap between two things that the smooth case presents as one. The self-concealing interface predicts that the gap is where the architecture is legible.

**Graded channel failure.** The memory hierarchy is the worked exhibit: fixed-order degradation under partial blockade. The ordering is a seam datum because no single measurement of intact memory reveals it—only the sequence of failures does. The seam has a second, sharper face: recovery does *not* simply retrace degradation in reverse. [Recovery-order asymmetry](/topics/memory-channel-interface-evidence/) makes the *direction* of interface change load-bearing—the autonoetic channel returns last under rising substrate function (anaesthesia emergence) and first under falling substrate function (terminal lucidity), a divergence a substrate-symmetric production account does not predict from a single direction-blind vulnerability profile, though one willing to add direction-specific mechanisms per case can absorb it. The signature *constrains* production rather than *establishing* the filter reading. This direction-dependence is itself a [signature family](/concepts/direction-of-interface-change/) that the graded-failure seam contains but does not exhaust: hysteresis of state transition (induction and emergence thresholds differ), recovery-order reversal across mechanisms, abrupt dissociative reconnection versus gradual onset, and direction-decoupled emergence timing all share the property that no substrate-state-at-a-time description captures them. The four are not four independent confirmations—they share one upstream architecture, so the [common-cause null](/project/common-cause-null/) applies in full and the concept node credits them as one view, not four. They are the temporal-derivative face of the same architecture the vulnerability hierarchy reads off the substrate-state face, legible only in the asymmetry between two directions, never in either state alone. The seam is sharp enough to have a design: a reversible perturbation whose up-ramp and down-ramp channel-orderings the rival readings predict *oppositely*—the [substrate-state](/topics/targeted-lesion-discriminating-tests-between-production-and-filter-readings-of-the-memory-hierarchy/) and [direction-axis](/topics/direction-dependent-discriminating-test-design/) test designs the source article develops—is the kind of seam-level discriminator that, if run, could deliver more than the constraining force the convergence currently earns, because it names a result the production reading does not equally predict.

**Patient-population divergence.** [Pharmacological dissociation](/apex/pharmacological-dissociation-as-evidence/) and the neurological and clinical dissociation literatures supply contrasts—same target / opposite outcome, different targets / convergent outcome, same behaviour / different mechanism / different phenomenology—that a normal population averages away. The divergence between populations whose introspective architecture *differs* is load-bearing precisely because it cannot be running through shared neurotypical machinery, which is the structural defence the [common-cause null](/project/common-cause-null/) demands.

**First-person/third-person mismatch.** [Forward-in-time selection](/topics/forward-in-time-conscious-selection/) names the cleanest instance: the asymmetry between the third-person measurability of a commitment-relevant signal and the first-person opacity of the closing-itself is "what an interface doing causal work at a primitive seam predicts: the cause cannot be observed from inside the system whose cause it is." Where the inside report and the outside instrument disagree, the disagreement is the data.

The reorientation is not a softening of falsifiability—it is a sharpening of it. Each seam admits genuine failure conditions. The memory hierarchy would be undercut by a population in which the degradation order reverses without an independent confounder; the recovery-order asymmetry by a focal perturbation producing a recovery sequence the filter reading forbids, or by a single substrate-state mechanism that derives both the rising-trajectory and falling-trajectory orderings without per-case additions; the dissociation cluster by clean single-mechanism derivation of all its faces; the patient-population divergences by demonstration that the contrasts run entirely through shared upstream architecture. What the programme forfeits is the fantasy of a single decisive ensemble anomaly. What it gains is a structured set of places where the architecture has committed itself to predictions it could lose—and, in the graded-failure case, a worked test design (a reversible perturbation read on both its up-ramp and its down-ramp) where the rival readings have already been pinned to opposite orderings, which is what moves a seam from constraining to potentially discriminating.

A caution belongs here, on the Map's own discipline. Seam evidence is, almost without exception, *constraining* rather than *establishing* evidence: a dissociation removes the simplest identity reading without by itself installing the interactionist one, because production accounts can usually accommodate any single seam at the cost of an extra mechanism or a parameter set after the fact. The force is cumulative and it is bounded. Worse, the seams share an upstream architecture—the same interface—so the [common-cause null](/project/common-cause-null/) applies in full: counting disruption, dissociation, graded failure, population divergence, and first-person/third-person mismatch as five independent confirmations would inflate the weight, because if the Map is right they are five views of one thing. The honest accounting credits the convergence at the framework-internal-weight register and reserves tier-elevation for the seams that supply a discriminator the rivals do not equally predict. The reorientation tells the programme where to look; it does not tell it that what it finds there will be more than the discipline allows.

## What the Architecture Does Not Settle

The self-concealing interface is an account of *where* and *how invisibly* consciousness acts. It is silent on *why one outcome rather than another*. [The selection-criterion trilemma](/topics/forward-in-time-conscious-selection/) is the gap it leaves open: if consciousness selects for internal reasons the dualism collapses into functionalism; if it selects randomly it contributes nothing; if it selects by a non-reducible phenomenal preference, an unanalysed primitive sits at the centre. The Map's route is the third, and [the selection-criterion problem](/concepts/selection-criterion-problem/) develops what that primitive owes. The self-concealing thesis and the selection-criterion problem are complementary open structures: one names the architecture's invisibility, the other names the primitive doing the selecting inside it. Neither dissolves the other, and a reader should not mistake the architectural picture for a mechanism of valuation it does not supply.

Nor does the thesis settle the deeper metaphysics. That the interface is markerless from inside and statistics-neutral from outside is *what dualism would predict*; it is not evidence that dualism is true, because eliminativism about the interface predicts the same absences. The architecture is the shape the Map's commitments force; the case for those commitments is made elsewhere, on grounds the self-concealing thesis neither strengthens nor weakens.

## The Practical Ontology the Architecture Names

The self-concealing interface is also a portrait of the kind of mind the Map is committed to. It is not a Cartesian controller with transparent access to its own operations, not a panpsychist ground present everywhere in the physical, not an epiphenomenal witness causally idle. It is a subject with bounded access, acting through a narrow selection channel prepared by brain architecture, whose every causal contribution is by construction invisible to aggregate physical measurement and unmarked in first-person report. The interface hides from the instrument and from the self alike. Naming the architecture is, in part, naming this practical ontology—so that the Map's live position can be distinguished from the broader dualisms it is often confused with.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The synthesis is an architecture-naming exercise across three tenets; its evidential standing is framework-internal coherence, not empirical support.

**[Dualism](/tenets/#dualism)**: The self-concealing interface is the architecture dualism predicts once minimality and the empirical constraints are taken seriously—a non-physical contribution real enough to author action and small enough to vanish into the classical record. The compatibility is structural fit between prediction and the shape of the corpus, not direct evidence; physicalist accounts can read each seam without the dualist remainder.

**[Minimal Quantum Interaction](/tenets/#minimal-quantum-interaction)**: The concealment is *entailed* by this tenet. The strict corridor's neutrality on Born statistics is not a bug to be explained away but the defining property the tenet's minimality requires, and the source of the architecture's invisibility to aggregate tests.

**[Bidirectional Interaction](/tenets/#bidirectional-interaction)**: If consciousness causally influences the physical, the influence's markerlessness from inside is exactly what the introspection-architecture cluster predicts. The evidential case for downward causation must therefore be made through statistical, behavioural, and instrumental traces at seams—never through a felt tag of authorship, which a self-concealing interface guarantees will be absent or confabulated.

The thesis honours the [evidential-status discipline](/project/evidential-status-discipline/) throughout: it names an architecture and reorients a programme, both of which earn framework-internal weight; it claims no tier-elevation for the tenets, because the interface's hiddenness is compatible with the Map's reading and with the denial of any interface at all. The achievement is precise self-knowledge about where the Map's own commitments permit evidence to live—and where, by the Map's own construction, no evidence ever will.
