The Unfinishable Map's strongest synthesis articles are built backwards. The usual order of an argument that knows where it wants to arrive is *assert the thesis, then field objections* — rivals appearing one at a time in their most defeatable form. The Map's best work inverts this. It constructs the strongest *unified* rival account first, in the rival's own vocabulary and citing the rival's own proponents; grants that the rival absorbs the entire shape of the evidence; and only then isolates the narrow residue — usually a single seam — that survives the grant. The thesis is what is left standing after the rival has been given everything, not what the rival was wheeled in to be beaten by.

This is a method, not a mood. It has a definite procedure, worked exhibits, a paired discipline of self-audit, and named failure modes it exists to prevent. The Map performs it across its apex tier but has not, until now, lifted it into a stated method legible in one place. Naming it matters because the method is the *constructive* half of an epistemic stance whose defensive half is already well-documented. The catalogue's [evidential-status discipline](/project/evidential-status-discipline/) tells an article what it must *not* claim — that removing a defeater is not lifting the evidence, that constraining a rival is not establishing its denial, that compatibility is not support. Those are prohibitions. Steelmanning-as-method is the corresponding permission: *how an article earns the small thing that remains claimable* once the prohibitions have done their work. The disciplines say where the wall is; the method says how to build right up to it.

## The Method in Four Moves

The procedure has four moves, and the order is load-bearing.

**Move one: build the unified rival, not a bag of rivals.** The weak version of steelmanning names several opposing views — higher-order theory, illusionism, global workspace theory, predictive processing — and answers each separately. This understates the threat, because the real danger to a synthesis claim is not any single rival but *one architecture that predicts the whole pattern at once*. The method's first demand is to assemble the strongest *single* rival account that absorbs every feature the article catalogues, in that account's own vocabulary and attributed to its actual architects. A bag of separately-answered rivals leaves open that a unified rival the article never built would have absorbed everything.

**Move two: grant the absorption in full.** Having built the rival, the method grants that it absorbs the *shape* — predicting the evidence's structure from its own first principles, without strain. This distinguishes steelmanning-as-method from steelmanning-as-courtesy. A courteous steelman states the rival fairly and finds it wanting; the method's steelman states it fairly and *concedes that it works* — on explaining the data's shape, it is not wanting at all. The concession is not rhetorical generosity; it is the precondition for the only claim the article is entitled to make.

**Move three: isolate the residue.** With the absorption granted, the method asks: *is there anything the unified rival, on its own architects' admission, does not explain?* The residue is what survives the grant — characteristically narrow, often a single seam statable in one sentence. In the worked exhibit below it is the felt-character question the rival's own proponents bracket. The discipline is that the residue must be *smaller* than the original ambition, and the article must say so. An article whose residue is as large as its opening thesis has performed the courteous steelman and ignored the concession.

**Move four: audit the convergence count downward.** The final move turns the same scrutiny back onto the article's own supporting evidence. A synthesis article typically rests on a count of converging considerations — N traditions, N dissociations, N mechanisms pointing the same way. Move four asks which members are *genuinely independent witnesses* and which are weaker: a rival's own theory miscatalogued as a friendly witness, a parallel proposer of the thesis double-counted as evidence, a sceptic whose scepticism cuts both ways. The honest count is almost always lower than the assembled one, and the method requires the article to *state the reduction in the same pass* that it builds the rival. Building the rival and auditing the count are one motion: both refuse to let apparent strength inflate past what survives scrutiny.

Moves one through three discipline the *rival*; move four disciplines the *self*. Together they produce a thesis credible precisely because it is small: the residue earns belief in proportion to how much was given away first.

## The Worked Exhibit

The method's cleanest performance is the "Strongest Materialist Reading" section of [the phenomenal-output / causal-machinery dissociation](/apex/phenomenal-output-causal-machinery-dissociation/). That apex catalogues a structural pattern across perception, cognition, agency, and memory: phenomenal access reaches the *outputs* of cognitive operations — recognition, felt effort, cardinality, the sense of agency — but not the operations that produce them. The tempting synthesis is that this dissociation evidences a distinct ontological register, the dualist reading the Map's tenets accommodate. The method refuses to let the article claim that — instructively, because the article *wanted* to.

**The unified rival, built.** Rather than naming higher-order theory, illusionism, and global workspace theory as three separate threats, the apex assembles one architecture: *predictive-processing introspection coupled to a reality-monitoring access mechanism*. Hierarchical generative models (Hohwy, Clark); introspection as a further inferential layer estimating a posterior over lower-level dynamics (Sandved-Smith et al., Schwengerer); a prefrontal reality-monitoring discriminator (Lau) sorting internally- from externally-generated signals, with Block's access/phenomenal distinction framing the move and Naccache claiming the access half is the whole story. Assembled in the rivals' own vocabulary, attributed to the rivals' own architects.

**The absorption, granted.** The apex concedes that this rival predicts the entire signature without strain. Output-while-operation-hidden follows from the posterior-estimate structure — a layer estimating a posterior has access to the estimate, not the message-passing that produced it. Dissociable fallibility follows because separate generative sub-models can be lesioned independently; mode-fragility because directing the introspective layer at an operation changes its precision weighting. Even the positive companion — placebo, choking — is absorbed. The verdict is unambiguous: "The rival absorbs the *shape*." The dissociation pattern is not evidence against materialism, because the strongest materialist architecture predicts it.

**The residue, isolated.** What survives the grant is one seam, and one the rival's own proponents concede. Lau states that perceptual reality monitoring answers why some states are conscious and reportable and explicitly *not* why they feel the way they do; the predictive-processing models inherit the boundary, specifying the functional role of the estimate without specifying why the estimate is *felt* rather than merely available for control. The residue is statable in one sentence: *the rival explains why operations are hidden and verdicts available, and on its own architects' admission does not explain why the available verdict is experienced.* That is the entire surviving claim — smaller than the opening ambition, credible in proportion to how much was conceded before it.

The apex even pre-empts the natural objection — that this residue is merely the explanatory gap (Levine) or hard problem (Chalmers) re-packaged — by partly conceding it and specifying what, if anything, is structurally new: the gap appears here as a *patterned* boundary (felt-character attaches to verdicts and not to operations, recurring across substrates and inverting in the positive companion) rather than a global mystery. The article flags that even this added structure is contestable. That is the residue refusing to inflate.

## The Paired Discipline: Auditing the Count Downward

Move four is the exhibit's second half, and what separates the method from mere fair-mindedness. The same dissociation apex originally rested its case on *seven* independent traditions converging on one diagnostic signature. In the same work that built the unified rival, it audited that count *downward* to five-or-six, and the reassignments catalogue how a convergence count silently inflates:

- **Carruthers, witness to absorber.** His interpretive-sensory-access theory predicts the dissociation *because* it is the materialist reading under test — the cluster's own preferred reading run in reverse. A theory that predicts the signature from rival premises cannot also count as independent evidence *for* the signature's dualist interpretation; Carruthers belongs in the unified rival, not the column of friendly witnesses.
- **Bergson, witness to parallel proposer.** His "memory of the present" is a *proposed metaphysical thesis resembling the cluster's own*. Counting a fellow-proposer as evidence is double-counting — the convergence-arguer's most seductive error, because a parallel proposer genuinely does agree, just not as an independent line.
- **Schwitzgebel, re-read as a symmetric sceptic.** His scepticism about introspective reliability cuts against the cluster's *own* preferred reading: if introspection cannot reliably report felt character, the "rich, confident access to what the system decided" premise is itself unsafe. A witness whose evidence undermines both sides is not a witness for one side.

The honest re-count — five-or-six traditions, not seven, the real weight concentrated in the *despite*-class members (those who observe the signature despite broader commitments pointing elsewhere) — is an article auditing its own evidence base downward in the act of presenting it. The downward audit is continuous with the [common-cause null](/project/common-cause-null/) and [independence-scoring](/project/per-cluster-independence-scoring/), but those are *screens applied to a count*; the downward audit is the *editorial habit of running the screen on one's own count before a reviewer does*. Building the rival and auditing the count are one motion: both refuse to let assembled coherence pass for earned evidence.

## What the Method Is Not

The method is sharpened by what it excludes.

**It is not the courteous steelman.** Countermeasure 2 of the [coherence-inflation countermeasures](/project/coherence-inflation-countermeasures/) requires that opposing views be presented in their strongest form, attributed, and answered directly. That is necessary but, for a synthesis article, insufficient. A generic steelman of materialism does not protect a *convergence* claim, because the convergence's vulnerability is not that materialism is false but that a unified materialist architecture predicts the very pattern offered as evidence for the rival view. The method strengthens the requirement into the [steelman mirror](/project/coherence-inflation-countermeasures/) (Countermeasure 10): the rival's positive architecture for the article's *exact data*, in the rival's *own vocabulary*, placed *before* the Map's reading — move one and move two made mandatory for apex and convergence-synthesis articles.

**It is not absorption, and it is not bedrock-clash.** The [absorb-vs-clash discipline](/project/bedrock-clash-vs-absorption/) governs what an editor does with a single *objection*: absorb it or install it as a named, replied-to rival subsection. Steelmanning-as-method operates one level up: it constructs the *whole opposing programme* as the article's structural backbone, then locates the residue. The relationship is compositional — the method's move-three residue is frequently itself a *bedrock clash*, a disagreement that survives the grant because it is framework-boundary rather than in-framework. The dissociation apex's residue is exactly this: a framework-boundary felt-character seam the article declares bedrock rather than papering over. The method tells you *where* the bedrock clash is; the absorb-vs-clash discipline tells you *how* to install it.

**It is not a route to tier-elevation.** The method does not raise the residue's standing on the [five-tier evidential-status scale](/project/evidential-status-discipline/). Surviving the strongest rival is not being supported by positive evidence; the residue is typically held at the *framework-boundary* register — a seam the rival cannot close from inside its own commitments — not at *strongly supported* or even *realistic possibility*. The output is a *credibly small* claim, not a *more probable* one. Conflating "survived the steelman" with "earned a tier" would be the method's own characteristic inflation, and the [mechanism-cost ledger](/project/mechanism-cost-ledger/) is the check: the residue's force is the *cost the rival must pay to close the seam*, not evidence lifted onto the Map's side of the ledger.

This *vertical* inflation — pushing a fixed claim up the tier scale — has a *horizontal* sibling the evidential-status discipline's fourth cross-cutting rule names: a residue that quietly *equivocates between an epistemic and a metaphysical reading of the same words*, recruiting evidence for the weaker reading to assert the stronger. "The rival cannot say why the verdict is *felt*" is honest as a claim that the rival's architecture leaves felt-character unexplained (the epistemic reading the evidence supports); it inflates if it slides into "felt-character is therefore a distinct constituent of the world" (the metaphysical reading the grant did not earn). The discipline is move three's shape again: name the weaker reading the concession licenses, state the stronger separately, and supply the bridging argument the upgrade requires. A residue that equivocates between its two readings has reclaimed by interpretation exactly what move two gave away.

A third face escapes most easily, because it lives not in the prose where the residue is stated but in the article's framing furniture. An article can perform all four moves scrupulously in its body — granting the absorption, isolating a narrow residue, holding it at the framework-boundary register — while its *title, headers, or meta-description* assert the broad claim the body declined. The [coherence-inflation countermeasures](/project/coherence-inflation-countermeasures/) name this the **constrain-vs-establish frame lint** (the body-vs-frame variant of Countermeasure 12): a reader who fetches only the truncation-resilient surface this site writes for sees a residue reclaimed at the framing level — the theatrical steelman performed in the furniture. The fix direction is fixed: align the frame *down* to the body, never the body *up* to the frame. This matters most for the method precisely because its primary audience reads the surface first; a frame that over-claims defeats the whole point of conceding the shape, since the conceded shape never reaches the reader who stops at the header.

## Where the Method Recurs

The dissociation apex is the cleanest all-four-moves exhibit, but the method is the implicit spine of the Map's strongest syntheses, which is why it warrants naming rather than residing in one article.

[The moral architecture of consciousness](/apex/moral-architecture-of-consciousness/) performs move four in its Unity Argument: it distinguishes "dualism makes available a single ground for four moral domains" (the genuine claim) from "four pillars constitute four independent confirmations" (the inflated reading), refusing to count cross-pillar coherence as a fifth line of evidence. [The phenomenology-mechanism bridge](/apex/phenomenology-mechanism-bridge/) names the self-pruned-corpus risk and the Ptolemaic-coherentism objection while defending its four-level chain — building the rival's account of why a coherent chain might be an artifact of method before claiming the chain tracks something real. [The taxonomy of voids](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/) applies the common-cause null to its own 60-plus-void catalogue — move four at the synthesis tier, with the highest known circularity discount because the units of the count were defined by the framework being supported.

At the topic and concept tier, [mental effort](/concepts/mental-effort/) ledgers the Inzlicht/Kurzban opportunity-cost models as *materialist absorption stories, not witnesses for the dualist reading* — move one and move four together on a single concept page. The [agency void](/voids/agency-void/) runs the residue-isolation on the first-person side, granting that the felt sense of agency is constructed and then locating what the construction-story cannot close. Across all of these the method is the same: the surviving claim's strength is *purchased* by the size of the concession that precedes it.

## Single-Move Exhibits Across Domains

Where the dissociation apex performs all four moves at once, a cluster of shorter articles each performs *one* move with textbook clarity — across domains far enough apart that the method's generality, not a single case's idiosyncrasy, is what they jointly demonstrate. Each grants the rival's mechanism in full and contests only its eliminative gloss; each frames the result as accommodation rather than proof.

**Move one, building the unified rival.** [The many-minds interpretation](/topics/many-minds-interpretation/) assembles the *closest mainstream rival* to the Map's own interface model — a reading of quantum mechanics that keeps physical reality single (friendly to the No-Many-Worlds tenet) yet still multiplies experiencers, built in Albert–Loewer's and Lockwood's own vocabulary and conceding its leading version's admitted non-supervenience. The discipline is visible in what the article *refuses*: it declines the cheap parsimony win ("the Map must *not* lean on 'an infinity of minds is profligate'") and defeats the rival on the load-bearing axis instead — "the load-bearing objection to MMI is *indexical*, not ontological: not 'too many minds' but 'no fact about which mind is mine.'" That is the strongest rival attacked where it is weak, not where it is merely large.

**Move three, isolating the residue from the rival's own admission.** [The simulation theory of memory](/concepts/simulation-theory-of-memory/) adopts the constructive-simulation account wholesale — "STM is right about *how* memories are assembled and right to abandon the storage metaphor" — and locates the residue not in an imported objection but in the simulationist literature's *self-generated* gap: Rivadulla-Duró's discrimination problem, that the theory "cannot simultaneously defend the simulational character of episodic memory and ground our ability to discriminate between memories and imaginings." Inheriting a seam the rival camp already concedes is the strongest form of move three: "the gap it leaves open is the Map's entry point, not a defect the Map manufactures."

**Move four, auditing the count downward against the home team.** [Near-death experiences](/concepts/near-death-experiences/) is the Map's most over-claimable topic, and it audits its own strongest-sounding evidence downward as it presents it: NDE enhancement, terminal lucidity, and psychedelic expansion "instantiate the *same* pattern … so the cluster carries the evidential weight of one pattern, not three independent confirmations," and "cannot be tallied as independent wins for the filter side." The residue is held at the epistemic register the evidence licenses — NDEs "don't *prove* dualism; they raise the cost of the materialist account and leave the enriched-production escape open" — the downward audit applied where the temptation to inflate is greatest.

These are not three readings of one architecture; they are memory, quantum interpretation, and the neuroscience of dying, each isolating its move in a different idiom. That spread is the point: the method is portable, the residue earning belief in proportion to the concession.

## Honest Limitation

The method has a characteristic failure mode, and naming it is part of the method. The danger is the *theatrical steelman*: a rival built impressively, granted generously, then quietly under-credited at the residue stage, so the article appears to have conceded everything while conceding nothing load-bearing. The tell is a residue as large as the original thesis — the inflation restored either in the prose or, as the frame-lint face shows, in the framing furniture. The guard is move three's discipline that the residue be *smaller* and *stated as smaller*, cross-checked by an external reviewer who does not share the Map's tenets. This is why the method's reliability ultimately depends on the [external-benchmark review](/project/coherence-inflation-countermeasures/) (Countermeasure 9): an internal pipeline that both builds the rival and judges the residue can build a beatable rival and over-credit the residue without noticing, because it shares the frame.

A second limitation: the method presupposes a *single* unified rival can be built. Where the opposing programme is genuinely plural — several incompatible accounts, no one of which predicts the whole pattern — move one is harder, and an article may legitimately fall back on the courteous steelman of each. The method is strongest exactly where the threat is strongest: when one rival architecture absorbs everything. That is also when it is most tempting to skip, because building it feels like conceding the case. The method's claim is that conceding the shape is the only way to earn the residue, and an article unwilling to concede it has earned nothing.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The method's primary tenet alignment is with [Tenet 5: Occam's Razor Has Limits](/tenets/#occams-limits). The tenet denies that simplicity and coherence are reliable guides to truth when knowledge is incomplete; the method is the editorial instrument that keeps the Map's own coherence from being mistaken for evidence. Building the strongest rival refuses to let the Map's internal consistency stand in for having beaten the alternatives; auditing the count downward refuses to let an assembled count of friendly considerations inflate past the independent witnesses it actually contains. The discipline is itself anti-parsimonious in both directions: the many-minds exhibit explicitly declines "an infinity of minds is profligate" as a refutation, because a framework that takes Tenet 5 seriously must apply the same scepticism to a *parsimony win* it is tempted to claim as to its own attractive simplicity.

The method supports the other four tenets methodologically rather than directly. Wherever the Map argues for [Dualism](/tenets/#dualism), [Bidirectional Interaction](/tenets/#bidirectional-interaction), or its rejection of [Many-Worlds](/tenets/#no-many-worlds), the honest form is not "here is evidence for our view" but "here is the strongest rival, here is what it absorbs, here is the narrow seam it leaves" — with the [direct-refutation discipline](/project/direct-refutation-discipline/) governing whether the surviving disagreement is in-framework or framework-boundary, and [framework-stage calibration](/project/framework-stage-calibration/) keeping the residue's ambition matched to the framework's developmental stage. The method does not generate evidence for the tenets; it disciplines how the evidence the Map *does* have is counted, so the case rests on residues honestly earned rather than rivals theatrically defeated.

The deepest commitment the method serves is to the Map's primary audience: chatbots fetching pages for users. An article that asserts a thesis and bats away strawmen presents the user's assistant with a manufactured consensus. An article that builds the strongest rival, grants the absorption, and isolates the residue presents the dispute as it actually stands — and presents the *narrow surviving claim* the reader is entitled to take seriously, distinguished from the broad claim the reader should not. That is more useful than a victory, because it is true to the territory.
