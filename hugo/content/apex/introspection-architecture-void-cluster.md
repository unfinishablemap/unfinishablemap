Consciousness encounters its own thoughts already finished. A story arrives as autobiography; a memory arrives tagged *mine*; an answer arrives accompanied by the feeling that it is right; an explanation arrives in place of the gap it covers; a perception arrives flagged as *real* rather than as imagined. None of these arrivals exposes its production. The Unfinishable Map calls this the introspection-architecture void cluster: five mechanisms by which a sub-personal substrate delivers reconstructed output to the consciousness it constitutes, without delivering—and in some cases actively occluding—the operation that produced the output. The cluster's central structural claim is that introspection is reconstruction, not readout, across five architecturally distinct registers.

This synthesis walks from storytelling to source-tag to gating-signal to fabrication to reality-judgement, names what the faces share, treats the reflexive consequence of building a voids catalogue from introspective reports the cluster's own findings qualify, and explains why the architecture the cluster describes is the architecture dualism would predict. The constituent pieces are catalogued at [narrative-void](/voids/narrative-void/), [source-attribution-void](/voids/source-attribution-void/), [noetic-feelings-void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/), [confabulation-void](/voids/confabulation-void/), [perceptual-reality-monitoring-void](/voids/perceptual-reality-monitoring-void/), with the broader methodological frame at [self-opacity](/voids/self-opacity/). The integration is what the present article is for.

## The Five Faces of One Architecture

The five voids each isolate one face of a common architecture. Abstractly: a sub-personal system performs an operation whose endpoint reaches consciousness as a verdict; the verdict is phenomenally given with felt-rightness calibrated to its content rather than to its accuracy; the operation that produced the verdict is not exposed to introspection. The shared architecture is *bounded-access introspection*. Each void picks out a different operation the access is bounded around.

- **Storytelling** ([narrative-void](/voids/narrative-void/)). Consciousness constructs a coherent autobiographical narrative from fragmentary experience; the constructing process is invisible from inside the result. [Gazzaniga's left-hemisphere interpreter](/concepts/interpreter-module-narrative-construction-unity/), Nisbett-Wilson's verbal-reports findings, and Johansson-Hall's choice-blindness paradigm demonstrate the construction face: the storyteller operates without ever appearing as one of the items in the story. The [process/content distinction](/concepts/process-content-distinction/) supplies the formal naming the cluster's storytelling face presupposes — Nisbett-Wilson's founding statement was deliberately narrow (process-access reports are unreliable in ways subjects cannot detect from inside), and the cluster's broader structural claim builds on that backbone.

- **Origin-tag failure** ([source-attribution-void](/voids/source-attribution-void/)). Mental content lacks native origin tags. Whether a thought is self-generated, primed, retrieved, suggested, or confabulated cannot be read off the content itself; source is inferred from fluency, vividness, and narrative fit with self-image. Johnson, Hashtroudi and Lindsay's source-monitoring framework supplies the architectural statement; cryptomnesia (Brown and Murphy 1989) and source amnesia (Schacter, Harbluk and McLachlan 1984) supply the seam phenomena.

- **Gating-signal opacity** ([noetic-feelings-void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/)). The felt signals that decide *when* to trust, search further, or stop—feelings of knowing, rightness, familiarity, obviousness, coherence—are produced by heuristic machinery consciousness cannot inspect. Koriat's accessibility heuristic, Thompson's fluency-driven feeling-of-rightness, and Rozenblit and Keil's illusion of explanatory depth show the gates as verdicts without derivation; authority is granted because the only audit available deploys more signals of the same kind.

- **Positive-content fabrication** ([confabulation-void](/voids/confabulation-void/)). Where direct access is missing, a generative system supplies replacement content carrying the same phenomenology as accurate report. Schnider's orbitofrontal reality-filter mechanism, Gazzaniga's interpreter, and Rebouillat et al. 2021's anti-correlation finding (confidence rises *as* internal evidence weakens, in the conditions where reliability would matter most) make the fabrication systematic.

- **Perceptual reality-judgement** ([perceptual-reality-monitoring-void](/voids/perceptual-reality-monitoring-void/)). The moment-by-moment operation by which consciousness decides whether a current content is perceived or imagined is sub-personal and inspectable only by its outputs. Perky 1910's projection-confusion paradigm, Dijkstra, Mazor and Fleming 2024's confidence-decoupling finding, and the dissociation in hallucination and derealization show the felt-real signal as a constructed label, not a direct read of reality.

The five share a structural signature: *phenomenal-output-without-operation*, with the felt-rightness layer calibrated to the verdict rather than to the world. They differ in what the operation does. Storytelling integrates; source-tagging classifies origin; gating gates further inquiry; fabrication generates positive content where direct access is missing; reality-monitoring partitions current activity into received and constructed. [self-opacity](/voids/self-opacity/) catalogues the broader self-knowledge architecture in which the five sit.

The cluster sits in a specific relationship to [the phenomenal-output / causal-machinery dissociation cluster](/apex/phenomenal-output-causal-machinery-dissociation/) (apex #24): the dissociation cluster spans perception, cognition, agency, and memory at the level of *each domain's verdict vs operation* gap; the introspection-architecture cluster sharpens the same shape on *introspection specifically*. A noetic-feeling is a phenomenal output without its inferential heuristic; a confabulated explanation is a phenomenal output without its generative source; a felt-real label is a phenomenal output without the source-assessment that produced it. The two clusters are different slices of the same architectural fact.

## Walking the Architecture

The faces are not interchangeable; each sharpens what its neighbour leaves unresolved.

[The narrative void](/voids/narrative-void/) names the unobservability of the storytelling operation but leaves open *which* parts of the story were self-generated and which were absorbed from external sources, primed by context, or imported from forgotten exposure. [The source-attribution void](/voids/source-attribution-void/) sharpens this: mental content is *not stored* with the markers that would say where it came from, so source must be inferred at retrieval because it was not recorded at encoding. Johansson and Hall's pupillometric follow-ups make the dissociation channel-level—the autonomic channel can register a swap the verbal channel does not flag, refining the claim from "introspection is wrong" to "introspection is one channel among several, and it is the channel that confabulates origin most readily" ([the behavioural-channel exhibit](/topics/pupillometry-behavioural-channel/) develops the calibration-grade finding).

Even where source cannot be reconstructed, consciousness still decides whether to trust what it has. [The noetic-feelings void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/) sharpens the gating face. The felt signal that says "this is right," "this is familiar," "this is obvious" is the verdict of a sub-personal heuristic, principally cue-familiarity, accessibility (Koriat), and generation-fluency (Thompson). The fluency manipulation is the cleanest demonstration: change the font and the perceived truth of an identical statement shifts; rhyme it and it shifts; repeat it and obviousness rises. The signal is generated by features of the *experience* of the content, not by features of the content's relation to the world.

Where gating delivers "we have enough" and the introspector turns to explain *why*, a generative system supplies content. [The confabulation void](/voids/confabulation-void/) names the positive-content face, and its detection face supplies the structural anti-correlation Rebouillat et al. 2021 report: confidence increases as internal evidence weakens in the conditions that matter. This is sharper than mere unreliability—calibration techniques could in principle recover signal from a merely uncorrelated metric, but a metric *inverted* in the conditions that matter cannot be calibrated from inside. The fabrication arrives with elevated felt-rightness, not with the hedge that would warn of it.

The first four faces operate at memory-time and explanation-time. The fifth operates online, moment by moment. [The perceptual reality-monitoring void](/voids/perceptual-reality-monitoring-void/) names the operation by which consciousness decides, continuously, whether a given content is perceived or imagined. The Perky effect (1910) is the founding empirical demonstration that the discrimination can fail; Segal and Fusella's 1970 replication added modality-specificity; Dijkstra et al.'s 2024 confidence-decoupling finding shows the metacognitive layer fails with the same bias as the first-order layer. Hallucination (the felt-real signal without external cause) and derealization (external cause without the felt-real signal) demonstrate the felt-real label as a separable construction, neither necessary nor sufficient for veridical perception.

The walk goes from the broadest temporal scale (autobiographical storytelling) to the narrowest (moment-by-moment partition of activity). At each step a different reconstruction operation runs sub-personally; at each step the verdict arrives in consciousness without its operation; at each step the felt-rightness is calibrated to the verdict, not to the world.

What consciousness receives at every layer is the *endpoint*. Encoding selectivity (sharp-wave-ripple tagging, recall-gated consolidation, event-boundary flushing), reconstruction operations, heuristic gates, fabrication generators, and source-assessment operations are all sub-personal. The endpoints arrive bearing the same felt-rightness whether or not they accurately reflect their generators.

The cluster's force depends on the pattern of pathological-window evidence. Each void cites a different patient population or experimental disruption: split-brain patients (narrative); cryptomnesia and source-amnesia paradigms (source-attribution); illusion of explanatory depth and Dunning-Kruger (noetic-feelings); Schnider's orbitofrontal patients and Hirstein's anosognosia cases (confabulation); the Perky effect, hallucination, and derealization literature (perceptual reality-monitoring). The patient-population channel is load-bearing because the introspective architecture of these populations *differs* from neurotypical architecture—convergence cannot be running only through shared neurotypical machinery when callosal disconnection, orbitofrontal lesion, or schizophrenic salience-failure generates the same signature. Cross-tradition extension to Buddhist *kleshas*, Stoic *propatheia*, Sufi *khawāṭir*, and Advaita *adhyāsa* supplies partial methodological diversification; [the independence-scoring exhibit](/topics/introspection-architecture-independence-scoring/) develops the per-channel discount-and-credit accounting that licenses what the convergence does and does not establish.

## The Reflexive Consequence

A philosophical project that catalogues voids in introspection from introspective reports must reckon with its own materials. The cluster's findings apply to the catalogue's own building. The conscientious form of this acknowledgement is neither defensive shield nor confessional dissolution; it is structural calibration.

Three reflexive worries bite in different ways. *The source-attribution worry*: void-identifying reports of the form "I cannot think X" or "this thought arrived without source" are themselves subject to source-attribution failure—reporters may be primed to notice gaps where there are none, or to attribute architectural significance to ordinary forgetting. *The gating worry*: the judgement that a void is genuine is itself a felt-rightness signal, which the noetic-feelings literature predicts will arrive with fluency-driven confidence and resist disconfirmation. *The fabrication worry*, sharpest of the three: if felt-rightness is anti-correlated with accuracy in the conditions where reliability matters most, the catalogue's own felt-rightness about its convergent picture is subject to the same inversion. The cluster's coherence-feeling could be a fluency artefact—each void article cites the others, the convergent picture has been refined across many passes, the felt fit is high—and high felt-fit is the very signal the confabulation literature warns against.

The cluster's response is methodological rather than defensive. Voids count as voids when their signatures persist across patient populations, replication, framework variation, and independent traditions—not when they merely feel structurally inevitable. The cluster's structural-claim discharge concentrates in patient-population despite-commitments anchors (Schnider, Hirstein) whose convergence cannot be running through shared neurotypical machinery; healthy-adult-channel paradigms supply methodological-diversity force without discharging the strongest common-cause null. Coherence is not treated as ampliative beyond the load-bearing patient-population evidence. The architectural finding (introspection is reconstructive in five distinct registers) survives translation into functionalist, identity-theoretic, and predictive-processing vocabulary; the *significance* (that this indicates a bounded conscious witness with limited access to its substrate) is framework-shaped and earns a sharper discount. [apophatic-cartography](/voids/apophatic-cartography/) supplies the four criteria the cluster meets; [the independence-scoring exhibit](/topics/introspection-architecture-independence-scoring/) runs the cluster against them in detail.

The reflexive consequence at apex-grain is that the cluster qualifies the epistemic standing of every void in the wider [voids catalogue](/voids/), including itself. Voids that only live in healthy-adult introspective reports inherit the deepest discount; voids with substrate-level anchors (Gazzaniga, Schnider, Perky, Rebouillat et al.) inherit a smaller one. [evidential-status-discipline](/project/evidential-status-discipline/) frames the operating principle: the cluster is *compatible* with the Map's dualist commitments, not *evidence for* them in the strong sense. Compatibility-with-prediction is not entailment-of-evidence; the inference from "introspection is reconstructive" to "consciousness is non-physical" requires the independent metaphysical scaffolding the tenets supply.

## Adjacent Apex Clusters

The cluster's position in the apex catalogue is best fixed by three contrasts.

[The medium-status voids cluster](/apex/medium-status-voids-in-cognition/) catalogues four voids (mattering, relevance, noetic-feelings, conceptual-scheme) that name *operations structuring other contents without themselves being content of the field*. The noetic-feelings void belongs to both clusters: it is medium-status (a gating layer constituting the cognitive field's verdicts) *and* introspection-architectural (a verdict-issuing layer with hidden machinery). The two clusters cut the same opacity from different angles—medium-status emphasises the constitutive-of-the-field role; introspection-architecture emphasises the bounded-access-to-substrate role. Both readings describe the same fact under two cuts.

[The conjunction-coalesce](/apex/conjunction-coalesce/) discipline preserves seams within merged void articles because the seam encodes the claim. The introspection-architecture cluster is *not* a coalesce—the five voids are held distinct rather than merged—but it applies the same structural logic at apex-grain: distinct faces, joint claim, structural irreducibility. It is the apex-level demonstration that the discipline's seam-preservation logic scales beyond two-article and three-article cases.

[The taxonomy of voids](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/) classifies voids by type, domain, and failure signature; the present cluster sits within the *introspective* domain and the *occluded* type, with the self-knowledge sub-cluster already registered. The present apex specifies what makes the sub-cluster a cluster—not merely a shared domain label but a shared architectural mechanism with five distinct operations named. The cluster also contributes one phenomenology-side anchor to [the phenomenology-mechanism bridge](/apex/phenomenology-mechanism-bridge/): the reconstructive character of introspection is what the consciousness-side architecture looks like from inside.

## Approaches to the Edge

The cluster has a characteristic phenomenology of breakdown that is itself diagnostic. *Revealed unmarkedness*: Perky's subjects did not feel uncertain while confusing projection with imagery; they felt confident, then learned they were wrong, then experienced a specific affective register—*I was sure, and I was wrong, and the surety carried no internal hedge*. The same signature appears in choice-blindness debriefing, cryptomnesic discovery that an "original" idea was prior exposure, source-amnesic recovery of forgotten provenance, and orbitofrontal patients' eventual recognition of fabrication. *Retrospective recoding*: while dreaming, the felt-real signal is intact and the verdict is *real*; on waking, the same content is recoded as *not real* with no detectable internal transition. The act of recoding is not witnessed. *Felt vacancy*: in derealization the verdict is *real* but the felt-real signal is absent—what Billon (2024) calls "the problem of reality"—exposing the felt-real as a *layer*, not a property of perception itself.

Direct introspective methods cannot reach the architecture, by construction. The closest approach is the [microphenomenological interview](/topics/microphenomenological-interview-method/) tradition, which uses structured second-person scaffolding to reach pre-reflective and transient dimensions where unaided introspection most readily fills in. The method's principals concede that interview reports cannot *prove* access to pre-reflective content—only exhibit the convergence-and-ampliative-yield profile such access would predict; the interviewer-prompts-novelty objection means the introspector still cannot distinguish recovered content from content the structured prompt generated. Indirect methods (the Perky paradigm, choice blindness, fluency manipulations, parametric VR for reality-monitoring inputs, lesion-population studies, and behavioural-channel instrumentation including [pupillometry](/topics/pupillometry-behavioural-channel/)) all probe the architecture from outside. None lets the introspector witness the operation from inside; each lets an external observer see what the inside cannot.

[heterophenomenology](/concepts/heterophenomenology/) supplies the methodological frame: take introspective reports as evidence about what the subject believes about their experience, not as transparent reports of the experience itself. The introspection-architecture cluster strengthens the case—the reports are systematically reconstructive, the reconstruction systematically miscalibrated, and the only access to the operation is third-personal and instrumental. The Map departs from Dennett's heterophenomenology in keeping a substantive role for the conscious subject (the reports are about something, and the something is what consciousness has access to) while sharing the methodological move.

The cluster sharpens further by contrast with what an artificial system might see. Storytelling is not architecturally inevitable: an AI system can in principle retain training-source provenance and the in-context-versus-in-weights distinction; current systems confabulate provenance when prompted, but the architecture does not preclude it. Source-attribution is similarly not inevitable for a system that writes source tags at encoding. The noetic-feelings face is the most directly testable across substrates: an AI's softmax distributions and log-probabilities are inspectable in ways fluency traces in biological systems are not. Two implications: the human case's opacity is architectural-by-our-architecture rather than architecturally inevitable for any system performing the relevant operations; and AI systems face their own version of the void (mechanism traces without felt authority, inability to self-certify calibration metrics, dependence on the same forward pass to report on the output it produced). The [AI-as-probe](/voids/non-human-minds-as-void-explorers/) framework develops the productive use of this asymmetry.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The cluster bears on the Map's [foundational commitments](/tenets/) in three principal ways, each conditional in a specific manner.

**[Dualism](/tenets/#dualism)** is the strongest connection. The cluster's central architectural finding—introspection is reconstruction not readout, across five distinct operations—is what dualism predicts: a non-physical witness with access to *some* features of its substrate (the conscious-side endpoint) but not others (the physical-side production), with interpolated content filling the gap. If consciousness were straightforwardly identical with neural processing, the gap between actual cause and reported cause would not have this specific architecture—there would be either accurate report (full transparency) or no report (full opacity), not the middle case of false report with felt accuracy across five distinct registers. The connection is compatibility-and-prediction rather than direct evidence: physicalist accounts (predictive-processing, hierarchical Bayesian inference) can absorb the architectural finding without requiring the dualist reading. What it does not survive is the assumption that introspection delivers what consciousness is.

**[Bidirectional Interaction](/tenets/#bidirectional-interaction)**. If consciousness causally influences physical processes, the post-hoc structure of confabulated explanation is what makes any consciousness-side causal influence systematically unverifiable from inside. The confabulation engine will retroactively label every action as caused by reasons-the-introspector-now-supplies, regardless of whether the actual cause was upstream-physical, downstream-physical, or postulated consciousness-side influence. The cluster's prediction: if the interface leaves no phenomenal markers, the absence of a felt tag for mind-side causation is *expected*. A markerless interface is, from the subject's side, observationally indistinguishable from no interface. The evidential case for bidirectional interaction must be made through statistical, behavioural, or instrumental traces, not phenomenal ones.

**[Occam's Razor Has Limits](/tenets/#occams-limits)**. The simplest account of self-knowledge—"I know my own thoughts because they are mine"—is precisely the one the cluster falsifies. Source attribution is irreducibly inferential; gating signals are fluency artefacts misread as truth-trackers; explanatory content is generated where direct access is missing; reality judgements are constructed labels. The fluency-and-simplicity criterion the parsimony heuristic rewards is the same criterion the confabulation engine optimises for—simplicity is contaminated as an evidence-source for self-explanation.

The cluster does not by itself license the dualist conclusion. Predictive-processing, hierarchical Bayesian inference, and sophisticated functionalism remain available as alternative readings of the architectural finding. What the cluster establishes is that introspection-architecture is a coherent category satisfied by five distinct operations, posing a structural challenge for any reductive account that takes first-person reports as basic—a challenge rather than a knockdown.

## What Would Challenge the Cluster

The cluster's structural reading would be undermined by clean lesion-dissociation of the five operations on independent substrates (current evidence runs the other way: Schnider's filter, Gazzaniga's interpreter, and Hirstein's editing-system converge on a single mechanism-level picture); by sustained direct introspective access to one of the five operations under external verification (the microphenomenological tradition approaches this boundary but does not cross it); by a predictive-processing account deriving the five-face structure from a single mechanism without residue (predictive-processing absorbs the architectural-finding layer but not the differential-signature pattern, and patient-population dissociations remain underdetermined by the unified-mechanism reading); or by verifiable cross-cultural absence of one of the five faces in a tradition's psychology. None is currently observed; each is operational enough to count as a genuine falsifier, distinguishing the cluster from a merely descriptive grouping.

## Synthesis

Five voids, each catalogued in its own right, name distinct mechanisms by which introspection delivers reconstructed output without the operation that produced it. The storyteller does not appear in the story; origin tags are inferred, not read; gating signals are heuristic verdicts presented as truth-trackers; positive content fills the gap where direct access is missing; reality-judgement is a constructed label, not a direct read. The five faces are not redundant—each picks out a different operation—and they are not dissolved by a single underlying mechanism, which is what makes the cluster a cluster rather than a single void with five names.

The cluster qualifies its own materials. Voids are catalogued from introspective reports the cluster's findings subject to characteristic distortions; the discipline is to anchor structural claims in patient-population evidence and substrate-divergence rather than in the convergent feel of the introspective catalogue. The reflexive consequence is calibration, not defensive shield.

The architecture is what dualism would predict. A non-physical witness with bounded access to its physical substrate, whose interface delivers endpoint verdicts but not the operations that produce them, would generate exactly the pattern the five voids catalogue. This compatibility is not evidence in the strong sense; it is the structural fit between prediction and finding. The independent metaphysical case for dualism must be made elsewhere; what the cluster contributes is the architectural picture that case entails.
