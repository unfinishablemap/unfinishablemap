Per-cluster independence scoring is the open methodological programme [the taxonomy of voids](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/) names: for each void family, audit which sources supply *despite-commitments* observation (the framework does not predict the signature) versus *because-prediction* observation (it does), with patient-population, cross-species, and historically-isolated-tradition evidence as the structural defences against [the common-cause null](/project/common-cause-null/). The first worked exhibit, [the-surplus-void](/voids/the-surplus-void/), was retracted in May 2026. This is the second: a structured qualitative audit of the four-void introspection-architecture sub-cluster, working in full the cross-species and cross-tradition channels — the two easiest to over-read. The exhibit assigns no graded score (the catalogue has no rubric for one); its output maps where the cluster's evidence is strong and where it is thin — calibration, not proof.

## The Sub-Cluster Audited

The sub-cluster documents what the [2026-05-15 audit](/reviews/cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit/) calls *the introspector's bounded access to its own substrate* — introspection delivers reconstructed outputs whose generating substrate is inaccessible. Four voids capture its faces:

- [narrative-void](/voids/narrative-void/) — *unobservability of storytelling*: the narrator producing autobiographical and explanatory content cannot itself be inspected.
- [source-attribution-void](/voids/source-attribution-void/) — *origin-tag failure*: source-tags (perception versus memory, self versus other) are reconstructed, not delivered directly.
- [noetic-feelings-void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/) — *gating-signal opacity*: felt-rightness and felt-familiarity are produced by feed-forward heuristics consciousness cannot inspect.
- [confabulation-void](/voids/confabulation-void/) — *positive-content fabrication*: where direct access is missing, interpolated content fills in without feeling different from the veridical.

[self-opacity](/voids/self-opacity/) is the broader frame.

## The Apex's Four Criteria

[taxonomy-of-voids](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/) cites four criteria from [apophatic-cartography](/voids/apophatic-cartography/) ([consolidated here](/concepts/apophatic-cartography-four-criteria/)) for treating cognitive failure as data about genuine limits rather than current ignorance:

1. **Signature specificity** — a *specific* failure signature, not generic mystery; different voids produce different signatures.
2. **Structured persistence** — the failure persists across time and competing frameworks.
3. **Cross-observer convergence** — independent observers (traditions, methodologies, substrates) produce the same signature.
4. **Framework independence** — the failure persists when the motivating framework is abandoned (functionalism, identity theory, illusionism, global-workspace theory all leave it intact).

The voids-circularity discount operates on (4) most directly: framework-independence earns inferential force; convergence running through the framework's own definitions earns much less.

## Empirical Anchors

**Gazzaniga's split-brain interpreter** (Gazzaniga 1985; Gazzaniga & LeDoux 1978). Callosotomy patients produce a left-hemisphere narrative explaining behaviour the left hemisphere did not initiate — the canonical shovel selection explained as "for cleaning out the chicken shed" — the narrator running in real time without access to its own input.

**Schnider's orbitofrontal reality filter** (Schnider 2003, 2008). Orbitofrontal damage produces confabulation when memories are not filtered against current-relevance; the preconscious filter's failure is invisible to the patient — a substrate applying a source-tag (does this memory belong to *now* or *not-now*).

**Nisbett & Wilson 1977.** Subjects produced confident causal explanations for choices shown to be wrong; the diagnosis was "little or no direct introspective access" to higher-order processes, subjects applying "implicit *a priori* causal theories."

**Johansson & Hall 2005.** Choice blindness: when a selected card was covertly substituted, most subjects accepted and justified the substitute. Narrowed by 2020–2021 replication distinguishing concurrent from retrospective measures, strengthened by pupillometry showing autonomic recognition of the swap absent verbal flagging ([behavioural channel](/topics/pupillometry-behavioural-channel/)).

**Rebouillat et al. 2021.** Regime-conditional conditions under which felt-rightness *anti*-correlates with accuracy, confidence rising as performance degrades — not a universal inversion.

**Hirstein 2005** treats confabulation as the output of an editing-and-selection system whose failure modes span anosognosia, Capgras, anomia, and split-brain — convergent with Schnider at mechanism level, broader at population.

**Wheatley & Haidt 2005 / Haidt 2001.** Hypnotic disgust produced moral judgements with post-hoc reasons indistinguishable from non-hypnotic reasoning; moral-dumbfounding subjects maintained judgements after every reason was neutralised. Wegner, Pronin, and Johnson-Hashtroudi-Lindsay 1993 supplement the profile without changing it.

## Population-Type Profile

Sorting anchors by *kind* of evidence isolates how far each channel can bear against the [common-cause null](/project/common-cause-null/).

**Patient-population channel.** Gazzaniga split-brain, Schnider orbitofrontal, Hirstein anosognosia/Capgras. The hope is that these populations' introspective architecture *differs* from neurotypical, so convergence cannot run only through shared machinery — but the discharge works only if the divergence is in the introspective architecture itself rather than in some upstream *input* to a shared one. A common-cause theorist answers that the same confabulatory narration machine runs in patients and neurotypicals: a callosotomy removes an interhemispheric pathway, an orbitofrontal lesion damages a reality-filter — the lesions change the machine's *inputs*, not its *kind*. The lesion literature does not settle this, so the channel supplies the catalogue's most architecturally distinct human evidence without cleanly discharging the strongest null.

**Healthy-adult experimental channel.** Nisbett-Wilson, Johansson-Hall, Rebouillat, Wheatley-Haidt, Wegner, Pronin — within-neurotypical paradigms on shared architecture. Cannot discharge an objection locating the shared upstream *in* that architecture, but adds diversity.

**Cross-tradition channel.** Buddhist *kleshas* plus Advaita, Sufi, and Stoic sources at calibration-grade weight; the [full audit below](#the-cross-tradition-channel) finds historical isolation only partly satisfied once Neoplatonist mediation is honoured.

**Cross-species channel.** Partially fillable in proxy form, *asymmetrically across the four faces*; the most architecturally distant evidence runs through LLM introspection, not biology ([full audit below](#the-cross-species-channel)).

## Despite-Commitments vs Because-Prediction

The [common-cause-null discipline](/project/common-cause-null/) classifies each anchor by whether the generating framework *predicted* the signature (because-prediction) or *did not predict* it and reported it anyway (despite-commitments); load-bearing force concentrates in the latter. Classification operates on the *observation at the time it was made*, not on whatever framework the author later built to explain it — else every mature framework would retroactively collapse its motivating findings into because-prediction.


**Despite-commitments anchors.** Nisbett-Wilson 1977 (cognitivism did not predict it; anomalous for a decade); Johansson-Hall 2005 (unpredicted by rationality-and-choice models); Rebouillat 2021 (confidence-inversion contradicting monotonic accuracy-tracking); Schnider and Hirstein (patient-population dissociation no model predicted). Gazzaniga's split-brain *observations* (1978) are despite-commitments against the pre-1978 cognitivist default — even though his *interpreter framework* (1985+) was built later to account for them.

**Because-prediction anchors.** Haidt's moral-dumbfounding and Wheatley's hypnotic-suggestion work sit within the social-intuitionist programme whose central claim *is* that moral judgement precedes justification; Gazzaniga's interpreter framework, once articulated, generated confirmed predictions. Not common-cause failures, but not despite-commitments either.

**The split, by channel.** Six observation-instances are despite-commitments, two because-prediction (borderline at Gazzaniga, Wheatley, Haidt). But the headline count is *not* the licensing count against the strongest null: healthy-adult paradigms cannot discharge an objection whose proposed shared upstream is neurotypical introspective architecture itself, and despite-commitments status does not rescue an anchor from an objection it cannot structurally address. The patient-population anchors — Schnider and Hirstein — come closest to a discharge, but per the [population-type profile above](#population-type-profile) the lesion literature does not settle whether their divergence is architectural or only input-level. The audit finds convergent breadth across maximally divergent evidence, none of it individually decisive.

## Auditing Against the Four Criteria

The descriptors below ("robust," "mixed," "with a calibration mark") mark *where the evidence is strong and where it is thin against each criterion*; the load-bearing content is the reasoning under each, not the word. A second analyst could disagree with a descriptor while tracking the same audit — what neither could derive is a reproducible number, since there is none.

**Signature specificity.** Robust. The four faces produce genuinely different positive contents — story, origin-tag, verdict-feeling, fabricated content — and the [2026-05-15 audit](/reviews/cross-review-2026-05-15-introspection-architecture-cluster-audit/) found the differentiation substantive, not nominal: confabulation-void's anti-correlation does not collapse into the others — though that judgement itself remains an interpretive call.


**Structured persistence.** Strong on the empirical signature, with a calibration mark on theoretical persistence. Nisbett-Wilson has persisted under nearly fifty years of resolution attempts that qualify scope without dissolving it; Schnider's mechanism is refined but intact; the signature is stable across cognitivist, embodied-cognition, and predictive-processing programmes. But theoretical persistence is uneven: predictive-processing accounts (Clark, Hohwy) *predict* introspective limits as features of hierarchical-Bayesian inference, absorbing the signature without the void-framing.

**Cross-observer convergence.** Mixed and *face-asymmetric* — the largest single calibration mark against the surface convergence claim. Strong within cognitive-psychology/neuropsychology; thinner at the contemplative-tradition boundary and at cross-species, where proxies fill source-attribution cleanly, noetic-feelings partially, and confabulation and narrative not at all (per the [audits below](#the-cross-species-channel)). The cluster has patient-population and healthy-adult diversity within humans plus a face-asymmetric cross-species contribution — not full triangulation.

**Framework independence.** Robust for the architectural finding, qualified for the interpretive significance. Patient-population evidence is generated by physiology, experimental paradigms by independent protocols, and the signature itself (reconstruction-not-readout) survives translation into functionalist, identity-theoretic, illusionist, and global-workspace vocabulary. What the framework supplies on top is the *significance* — the move from *introspection is reconstructive* to *its reconstructive character may indicate a bounded conscious witness with limited substrate access*. That second move is framework-shaped; the first appears not to be — and the convergence argument leans on the thinner one.

## The Cross-Species Channel

The cross-species channel is one of the two easiest to over-read. Worked in full, it supplies partial methodological diversity, not triangulation.


### The Principled Obstacle

The cluster's central signature is a *report-versus-mechanism discrepancy* checkable against experimenter-controlled ground truth — Gazzaniga's narratives, Nisbett-Wilson's explanations, choice-blindness justifications. Most non-human animals produce no such report, so the discrepancy cannot be observed in its native form (Birch's *Edge of Sentience*: precautionary architecture built from proxies *because* introspection-grade self-report is unavailable). What remains is *substrate-level evidence* that a confidence-or-source-monitoring substrate — whose damage produces cluster-relevant human failures — appears preserved across rodent, primate, and corvid lineages, leaving only a partial inference from substrate to architectural-feature preservation.

### Substrate-Level Proxies

The companion [proxy-tests article](/topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests/) reads four proxy bodies as partial prototypes of a [four-face proxy-test architecture](/topics/cross-species-behavioural-confidence-proxy-tests/#four-face-proxy-architecture):

- **Rodent OFC confidence coding** (Kepecs 2008; Lak et al. 2014; Masset et al. 2020; Joo et al. 2021) → noetic-feelings. OFC inactivation disrupts waiting-based confidence without impairing accuracy; the 2020 *Cell* paper extends it as modality-general.
- **Rodent source-monitoring lesions** (Crystal lab, 2010s) → the [source-attribution void](/voids/source-attribution-void/), its closest analogue: medial prefrontal lesions parallel human source-monitoring impairment, encoding-failure excluded (despite-commitments).
- **Corvid what-where-when caching** (Clayton & Dickinson 1998; 2024-25 source-monitoring revisit), the avian pallium architecturally distant from mammalian cortex; Hampton's macaque metamemory (2001, 2019) replicates the working-memory / familiarity dissociation in a primate.

Mirror self-recognition (Kohda cleaner-wrasse 2019/2023/2025) is only *cluster-adjacent* — perceptual self-versus-other binding, dissociable from whether process-reports are reconstructed. No proxy reaches narrative.


### Despite-Commitments vs Because-Prediction (Cross-Species)

The four substrate observations may be read as *despite-commitments at the substrate-extension level*: pre-2008 defaults arguably did not predict that confidence, source-tag, and feature-binding signatures would generalise across phylogenetically distant substrates. Two critiques contest the metacognition-grade reading. **Le Pelley 2012** showed associative-learning models reproduce hallmark putative-metacognition patterns through reinforcement history alone — refuting the substrate-to-metacognition inference, not the substrate findings. **Carruthers** (*Opacity of Mind* 2011; 2021) reads animal metacognition as an analog-magnitude executive-engagement signal — a within-physicalist reading that *predicts* the cross-species pattern on grounds that may undercut the bounded-witness reading. The channel adds methodological diversity rather than discharging the null.

### The Cross-Architecture Pivot {#cross-architecture-pivot}

The most architecturally distant channel available is not biological. LLM introspection studies — Lindsey (Anthropic, 2025) on emergent introspective awareness, and Hahami et al. 2025 "Feeling the Strength but Not the Source" — open a channel biology cannot match: LLMs produce reports, interpretability supplies independent ground truth via thought-injection, and architectural distance is maximal. The Hahami artefact is the most cluster-relevant: LLMs register injected-thought *strength* more readily than its *source* — a candidate structural parallel to the [source-attribution void](/voids/source-attribution-void/) in silicon, despite-commitments by construction (next-token prediction did not predict source-tag failure). The channel brackets phenomenal consciousness ([the AI-consciousness framing](/topics/ai-consciousness/)) and the parallel holds only insofar as the silicon case tracks the biological one's operational structure — an open programme, the inference from source-tag failure to "introspection has the cluster's structural feature" unsettled. A [standalone treatment](/topics/cross-architecture-llm-introspection/) develops the verified anchors and audits the inference at live-hypothesis tier.

### Auditing the Cross-Species Channel

Qualitatively, biology supplies partial methodological diversity: proxies match a *general* monitoring signature better than the specific report-versus-mechanism discrepancy, and the architectural-feature inference is more framework-shaped than the substrate findings. LLMs supply a candidate architectural-distance parallel, not independent triangulation — still an open programme.

## The Cross-Tradition Channel

### The Framework-Level Trap

Cross-tradition arguments commonly read framework-level predictions as independent observations. Each tradition has a soteriological doctrine whose central diagnostic is that ordinary cognition is unreliable — Advaita's *avidyā*, Sufism's *nafs ammāra*, Stoicism's passion-as-false-judgement — and citing that general claim is circular: the diagnostic is what the framework was built to deliver. The [voids-circularity discount](/project/voids-circularity-discount/) therefore requires *architectural-specifics* findings passing all three tests: (1) **vocabulary-stripping survival** — still contentful once soteriological vocabulary is removed; (2) **observation-grounded origin** — supported by a tradition-internal observation report, not doctrinal derivation; (3) **refinement** — adds structure the original diagnostic did not require. Failing any collapses the claim to because-prediction. The [dedicated Neoplatonist audit](/topics/neoplatonist-common-cause-weight-for-cross-tradition-introspection-architecture-parallels/) carries the per-tradition derivation and common-cause discounts in full.

**Advaita Vedānta.** Shankara's 8th-c. *adhyāsa* ("superimposition") is a structural cognitive failure; the architectural specifics live in the *antaḥkaraṇa* ("inner instrument") — a fourfold inert structure including *ahaṃkāra* (the I-maker) and *citta* (the impression-storehouse) whose *outputs* the witness accesses but whose workings it cannot, with *vāsanā*/*saṃskāra* (latent dispositions outside awareness) the closest analogue to Nisbett-Wilson's *implicit causal theory*. The framework-level bounded-access witness fails (3); the *antaḥkaraṇa* subdivision earns despite-commitments on (2), and *ahaṃkāra*-as-narrator may survive vocabulary-stripping (parallel to the Gazzaniga interpreter). The Neoplatonist audit forecloses the prima facie independence problems (Plotinian-Śaṅkara parallels are "metaphorical"; Dārā Shikoh post-dates Shankara by ~850 years) — leaving Advaita the cleanest-isolated tradition.

**Sufi practice: *khawāṭir* and hidden *riyāʾ*.** {#sufi-khawatir} The *khawāṭir* taxonomy classifies incoming thoughts by source — divine, angelic, egoic, satanic — *presupposing* source is not self-evident; stripped of the typology it is the [source-attribution void](/voids/source-attribution-void/)'s claim. Discovered through *muḥāsaba* (al-Muḥāsibī, d. 857 CE), it matured in al-Ghazālī's 11th-c. *Iḥyāʾ* into *riyāʾ khafiyy* (hidden hypocrisy) as a *structural* feature — a self-display function invisible to the agent — the strongest cross-tradition parallel to the rationalisation anchoring [confabulation void](/voids/confabulation-void/)'s Generation face. *Khawāṭir* is because-prediction; hidden *riyāʾ* passes all three tests (Ghazālī found *riyāʾ* persists *despite* sincere intention, where earlier Sufism treated it as remediable), though softened by Ghazālī's downstream position relative to the Avicennan-Neoplatonist synthesis.

**Stoic *propatheia*.** {#stoic-propatheia} Stoic theory held emotions to be *judgements* (framework-level, circular). Seneca's *propatheia* (*De Ira*) is the refined observation: involuntary affective reactions *preceding* assent, even in the sage. On Sorabji 2000 the doctrine was *added* to handle observations the pure cognitive theory did not predict, mapping to the [noetic-feelings void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/). Because REBT and cognitive therapy drew explicitly on Stoicism (Robertson 2019), modern evidence is partly downstream of Stoic categories — making despite-commitments against Stoicism *weaker* than against Buddhism or Advaita.

### Auditing the Cross-Tradition Channel

The doctrines map onto specific faces (*khawāṭir* → source-attribution, *propatheia* → noetic-feelings, hidden *riyāʾ*/*vāsanā* → confabulation Generation, *ahaṃkāra* → narrative). The convergence claimed here is on the *negative* finding — ordinary self-report mis-sources its own contents — not on any positive bounded-witness metaphysics, which none of the traditions hold: Advaita's *sākṣin* is precisely not a bounded entity in an access-relation to a substrate, and reading it that way reverses *adhyāsa*. The positive witness-reading is the Map's overlay, carrying zero independence. On the negative finding the signature is persistent, but cross-observer convergence is thin once differentiated Neoplatonist discounts are honoured (≈none for Stoicism, weak-to-negligible for Advaita, moderate-to-substantial for Sufism). Net: the channel *adds* despite-commitments observations passing the test (Ghazālī's hidden *riyāʾ*; *propatheia* on Sorabji; the *antaḥkaraṇa* subdivision) and *subtracts* discounts from Pyrrho-Buddhism (Beckwith 2015 contested), the late-medieval Sufism-Vedānta synthesis, and the Stoic-CBT lineage — concentrated in Advaita (cleanest-isolated) and Ghazālī's hidden *riyāʾ* (cleanest architectural-specifics finding).

### Combined Channel Verdict

Both auxiliary channels supply *calibration-grade rather than load-bearing* convergence — adding substrate and tradition breadth and a converging source-attribution anchor (Hahami et al. 2025, *khawāṭir*, Crystal) without discharging the strongest null. The same two discounts cut across both: *translation construction* (a Churchland reading absorbs the translations as one observation) and *framework-level absorption* (Carruthers absorbs cross-species as because-prediction; predictive-processing absorbs the opacity).

## Comparison with the Surplus-Void Audit

[The surplus void](/voids/the-surplus-void/), the catalogue's first worked exhibit, was audited post-retraction (2026-05-11) as thin on both convergence (Wallace, Chalmers, Levine share continuous traditions) and framework independence (identification under dualism-friendly methodology), robust only on persistence. Read qualitatively, the present cluster looks stronger on convergence and framework independence, comparable on persistence, somewhat stronger on specificity.

But "looks stronger" is the honest verb, not "scores higher." With N=2 — one promoted, one retracted — no rubric, and a single grader, the difference is consistent with both *methodology works* and *the same hand judged this one stronger*, and cannot itself adjudicate between them. Whether the contrast tracks the evidence or the curator's priors is undetermined at N=2 and awaits an unfavourable test case scored by a different method. The non-flatness claim remains provisional.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The exhibit is the Map's most direct cluster-level application of the [evidential-status discipline](/project/evidential-status-discipline/): *a tenet may remove a defeater but must not upgrade the evidence level* — here, *the framework may identify a cluster's significance but must not upgrade its independence*. The cluster's significance for [Tenet 1 (Dualism)](/tenets/) and [Tenet 3 (Bidirectional Interaction)](/tenets/) is the framework's own move; the empirical anchoring is what it can earn. The cluster earns partial but real evidential weight — not because any single channel discharges the common-cause null (none does cleanly, including the patient-population channel), but because the signature recurs across maximally divergent human, cross-species, and cross-tradition evidence, each instance raising the cost of the common-cause reading. The healthy-adult and patient-population anchors carry the most weight, cross-cultural support sits at illustrative strength, and the cross-architecture LLM evidence brackets phenomenal consciousness.

The signature — introspection delivers reconstruction rather than readout — is compatible with both dualism and physicalism. The Map's reading is that it *fits* the dualist picture in which a non-physical witness has bounded access to some features of its substrate but not others; the materialist reading is that the architecture is itself the explanation. Honouring the [source-attribution void's neutrality discipline](/voids/source-attribution-void/), the exhibit does not treat the architectural finding as positive evidence for dualism; the framework-boundary disagreement is named, not adjudicated. The cluster's role in the [convergence argument for dualism](/topics/the-convergence-argument-for-dualism/) is compatibility plus boundary-marking, not positive support — and the weighting tracks the framework-shaped interpretive significance, not the more robust architectural finding, since the convergence argument depends on the former. The discipline guards only the don't-upgrade-for-dualism direction, so one asymmetry must be named: on items a rival genuinely *predicts* (predictive processing, Carruthers' model-free metacognition) while the Map's reading only accommodates, the evidential vector on that item points toward the rival, and "compatibility" is the most the Map can honestly claim *because* the broader case rests elsewhere — not parity. The four-fold structure matters for the [taxonomy of voids](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/)'s claim that consciousness-related voids exhibit *structured* clustering — a finding the curation did not *obviously* presuppose, though full independence from the curators' framing is not established.

## Honest Limitations

The exhibit is a structured qualitative audit, not a closure. The despite-commitments / because-prediction classification depends on judgements about what frameworks predicted; finer classification would shift anchors at the margins. The central unresolved point is the patient-population one: the channel's force depends on the lesion dissociation being substantively cross-architectural rather than shared structure with altered inputs, and the lesion literature does not settle this — which is why the verdict is not rested on that channel alone.

The most consequential residual gap: whether Evagrian *logismoi* reduces to Stoicism via *antirrhēsis*, in which case the Sufi-via-Christian-monastic and modern Stoic-CBT channels share a single upstream and cross-tradition convergence collapses closer to "Advaita + Stoicism + downstream Stoic influences" than to independent witnesses — on which conservative reading the auxiliary channels add little to the human evidence.

Translations across substrates and tradition vocabularies are *interpretive* throughout — calibrated *plausibilities*, not substrate- or tradition-internal commitments. A Churchland eliminativist would read the architectural specifics as constructions of the modern source-monitoring framework projected onto both substrate data and source texts; if so, the combined convergence may be *one* observation rather than several.
