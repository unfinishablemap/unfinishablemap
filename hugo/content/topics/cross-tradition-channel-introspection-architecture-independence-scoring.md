The cross-tradition channel is one of four structural defences against the [common-cause null](/project/common-cause-null/) catalogued in the [introspection-architecture independence scoring exhibit](/topics/introspection-architecture-independence-scoring/). For the four-void cluster (narrative, source-attribution, noetic-feelings, confabulation), the parent exhibit calibrated the Buddhist *kleshas* parallel at *moderate* and deferred the wider audit across Advaita Vedānta, Sufi practice, and Stoicism. This article runs that audit. The result is *calibration-grade rather than load-bearing* convergence, *re-calibrated downward* from the kleshas-only baseline once common-cause and translation-construction discounts are honoured, concentrated in two architectural-specifics-level parallels that survive vocabulary-stripping: Ghazālī's *riyāʾ khafiyy* as a structural analogue of the [confabulation void](/voids/confabulation-void/)'s Generation face, and Seneca's *propatheia* doctrine on Sorabji's contested historiographic reading. The [Neoplatonist common-cause audit](/research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16/) (2026-05-16) holds the overall score at *low-moderate* while shifting internal weight away from early Sufism (Christian-monastic-via-Evagrius discount) toward Advaita as the cleanest-historical-isolation component.

Paired with the [cross-species channel article](/topics/cross-species-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-scoring/), this audit walks the three traditions, scores each at the *architectural-specifics* level, names the historical-isolation problems the independence test requires, and updates Criterion 3 (cross-observer convergence) with calibrated caveats.

## The Framework-Level Trap

The most common failure mode in cross-tradition arguments is reading framework-level predictions as independent observations. Each tradition includes a soteriological doctrine whose central diagnostic is that ordinary cognition is unreliable: Advaita's *avidyā*, Sufism's deceptive *nafs ammāra*, Stoicism's passion-as-false-judgement. Citing each tradition's general claim that introspection fails would be circular — the diagnostic is what each framework was built to deliver.

The [voids-circularity discount](/project/voids-circularity-discount/) requires that this channel earn its weight on architectural-specifics-level findings the authoring framework did *not* predict — doctrinal additions the framework had to *accommodate*, empirical features the practice tradition discovered through self-observation, and structural claims that survive translation once stripped of framework-specific metaphysics.

### The Architectural-Specifics Test

A tradition-internal claim earns architectural-specifics status when it satisfies all three criteria:

1. **Vocabulary-stripping survival.** The claim still expresses a contentful structural feature once soteriological vocabulary is removed — translation into metaphysically neutral cognitive-architectural register does not dissolve it into the framework's prior commitments.
2. **Observation-grounded origin.** The claim is supported by a tradition-internal observation report — a practice manual, a refinement under disagreement, a case description — rather than being a doctrinal derivation.
3. **Refinement rather than recapitulation.** The claim refines the central diagnostic by adding structure the original formulation did not require, rather than restating the diagnostic in different terms.

Failing (1) reads a framework-level prediction as architectural; failing (2) is doctrinal derivation, not empirical accommodation; failing (3) is the central diagnostic re-stated. Passing all three is necessary for despite-commitments scoring; failing any one collapses the claim to because-prediction.

## Advaita Vedānta: The Architectural Picture

Shankara's eighth-century *Adhyāsa Bhāṣya* opens his *Brahma Sūtra* commentary with *adhyāsa* ("superimposition") as a structural cognitive failure: properties of the physical body are projected onto the Ātman, and vice versa. Shankara treats *adhyāsa* as a fact about ordinary cognition rather than a hypothesis, and notes earlier schools discussed it under other names — suggesting empirical debate, not a single school's doctrinal invention.

The architectural specifics live in the *antaḥkaraṇa* ("inner instrument") doctrine Advaita inherited from Sāṃkhya/Yoga: a fourfold structure — *manas* (sensory mind), *buddhi* (discriminating intellect), *ahaṃkāra* (I-maker), *citta* (impression-storehouse) — inert by nature, with consciousness reflecting in it (*pratibimba*). The witness has access to the *outputs* of the instrument but not its inner workings; discrimination (*viveka*) is the soteriological task *because* the architecture makes witness and instrument ordinarily indistinguishable. The *vāsanā*/*saṃskāra* doctrine extends the picture downward: latent dispositional tendencies operate outside conscious awareness and drive cognition — the closest Advaita analogue to Nisbett & Wilson's *implicit causal theory*.

Scoring. The framework-level claim (witness with bounded access to instrument) is *because-prediction* — Advaita's soteriology requires it; it fails criterion (3). The four-faculty subdivision of the *antaḥkaraṇa* earns *moderate* despite-commitments because it passes on (2): the subdivision emerged from the practice tradition's empirical work refining the broader Sāṃkhya/Yoga inheritance, though a tradition-internal scholar can reasonably argue it is partly derived from the inert-instrument framework. The *ahaṃkāra*-as-narrator function survives stripping the Vedāntic metaphysics — a structural parallel to the Gazzaniga interpreter — earning calibration weight on the same modest basis.

The Map does not collapse the bounded witness into Brahman, where Map and Advaita diverge. A Madhyamaka critic (Nāgārjuna, MMK 24.18) notes the architecture *itself* is empty for mature Madhyamaka: reading it as a shared finding is the reification the practice exists to dissolve. The channel's reading of Advaita is conditional on rejecting the Madhyamaka inversion.

The independence problem for Advaita is the Plotinus-Indian-influence hypothesis (Bréhier 1928) and the Mughal mediation channel (Dārā Shikoh 1657). The [Neoplatonist audit](/research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16/) forecloses both: Armstrong 1936 established early Neoplatonism as an original Greek development; Aklan 2018 confirms Plotinian-Śaṅkara parallels are "metaphorical ways of expression" rather than structural connection. The Mughal channel runs the *wrong direction* — Dārā Shikoh's *Sirr-i-Akbar* post-dates Shankara by ~850 years and translates Vedānta *into* Sufi-Neoplatonist Persian, so cannot retro-contaminate Shankara.

## Sufi Practice: Khawāṭir and Hidden Riyāʾ {#sufi-khawatir}

The Sufi *khawāṭir* taxonomy classifies incoming thoughts by source — divine (*rabbānī*), angelic (*malakī*), egoic (*nafsānī*), satanic (*shayṭānī*). The taxonomy *presupposes* that source is *not* self-evident — otherwise the science of discrimination would be unnecessary. The spiritual master is said to identify sources the disciple cannot.

Strip the metaphysical typology, and what remains is exactly the [source-attribution void](/voids/source-attribution-void/)'s architectural claim: thoughts arrive without delivered source-tags, and source-attribution requires reconstructive work. The Johnson-Hashtroudi-Lindsay 1993 source-monitoring framework names the same fact in cognitive-psychology vocabulary. The Sufi tradition discovered the feature through *muḥāsaba* — the systematic self-examination programme al-Muḥāsibī (d. 857 CE) pioneered, whose diagnostic insight was that ostensibly pious practices can foster *riyāʾ* (hypocrisy) or *kibr* (pride) the agent does not see.

Al-Ghazālī's eleventh-century *Iḥyāʾ ʿulūm al-dīn* extends Muḥāsibī's finding into a doctrine of *riyāʾ khafiyy* (hidden hypocrisy). Ghazālī treats hidden *riyāʾ* as a *structural* feature of how the ego operates — the architecture of motivation includes a self-display function whose operation is invisible to the agent. The doctrine that *riyāʾ* "can visit you always" — beginning, middle, or end of worship — is a structural claim that hidden self-display agendas accompany apparently virtuous behaviour as a default architectural fact, not an occasional moral lapse. This is the strongest cross-tradition parallel to the Wheatley-Haidt and Nisbett-Wilson findings on post-hoc rationalisation that anchor the [confabulation void](/voids/confabulation-void/)'s Generation face.

Scoring. The *khawāṭir* taxonomy's metaphysical typology is framework-internal because-prediction; with respect to the Sufi tradition's own commitments, the non-delivery of source-tags is exactly what a discernment programme presupposes. The taxonomy fails criterion (3) and earns *low* despite-commitments on its own terms — useful as an early articulation of an architectural feature later named in cognitive psychology.

Hidden *riyāʾ* under Ghazālī carries the cleaner rating. Earlier Sufi treatment held *riyāʾ* an occasional moral failure remediable by sincere intention; Ghazālī's structural finding — that *riyāʾ khafiyy* operates as a default architectural feature persisting despite sincere intention — is the kind of refinement the central diagnostic did not require. It passes all three criteria at *moderate* despite-commitments. The rating *softens* at the level of *framing*: Ghazālī's inner-faculties psychology is downstream of the Avicennan-Neoplatonist synthesis (al-Kindī → al-Fārābī → Avicenna → Ghazālī), so the architectural shape is partly Neoplatonist-mediated. The combined score is *moderate at the finding-level, framing partly downstream*. The *khawāṭir* / *riyāʾ* split is the clearest worked example of how the test produces different scores for surface-level convergence.

The independence problem for Sufism is uneven. The [Neoplatonist audit](/research/neoplatonist-common-cause-introspection-architecture-2026-05-16/) identifies two transmission channels prefiguring *khawāṭir* and *muḥāsaba* in the 9th-c. Baghdad milieu. The *primary* channel is Christian-monastic-via-Evagrius: Evagrius Ponticus's late-4th-century *logismoi* doctrine — eight incoming thoughts discerned by source via *diakrisis*, with *antirrhēsis* refuting them before assent — supplies the direct structural ancestor of the *khawāṭir* taxonomy, prefiguring *muḥāsaba* by ~400 years through Syriac monastic transmission. The *secondary* channel is direct Arabic Plotinus via the al-Kindī circle (*Theology of Aristotle*, mid-9th c., contemporaneous with Muḥāsibī) — an *available-not-proven* channel. *Khawāṭir* + *muḥāsaba* can no longer claim the cleanest-historical-isolation defence; that status shifts to Advaita.

## Stoic Propatheia: The Strongest Despite-Commitments Observation {#stoic-propatheia}

Stoic cognitive theory held that emotions are *judgements*: a *phantasia* (impression) presents itself, the agent gives *synkatathesis* (assent), and the assent constitutes the passion. Zeno (c. 300 BCE) and Chrysippus developed this into a comprehensive psychology in which the sage's distinguishing feature is well-trained discrimination of which impressions warrant endorsement. On Frede's reading (1983), *phantasia katalēptikē* is the impression that *would warrant* assent; other readings parse the term differently. This article uses Frede's reading because it sharpens the [noetic-feelings void](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/) parallel — framework-level architecture, circular at that level.

The architecturally-refined observation is Seneca's *propatheia* doctrine in *De Ira*. *Propatheia* names involuntary affective reactions — startle, blanching, blushing, the first jolt of fear — that *precede* the cognitive assent constituting a full emotion. Even the sage experiences *propatheia*; the feature is *not* eliminable by practice. On Sorabji 2000's reconstruction, the doctrine was *added* to handle empirical observations the pure cognitive theory did *not* predict: the earliest formulation implied no affective response was possible without prior assent; the manifest fact of startle forced accommodation of a sub-conscious affective layer upstream of conscious assent.

The Sorabji reconstruction is not unanimous. Inwood and Long argue "first movements" were implicit in earlier Stoic theory — the *hormē*/*synkatathesis*/*pathos* sequence already needed pre-assent material — and Seneca's contribution was terminological refinement rather than empirical accommodation. If they are right, the rating collapses to because-prediction. The audit conditions on Sorabji's historiography: moderate-grade *propatheia*, with the architectural parallel surviving both readings; only score-strength differs. Stoic *prosochē* (continuous attention, central in Epictetus and Marcus Aurelius) is the practical correlate.

The independence problem is the contested Pyrrho-Buddhism contact hypothesis. Pyrrho accompanied Alexander's Indian campaign; Diogenes Laertius (9.61–62) reports he developed his philosophy in contact with Indian *gymnosophists*. Beckwith 2015 argues Pyrrhonism parallels the Buddhist three marks of existence; Bronkhorst contests. Zeno was contemporary of late Pyrrho. The scholarship is unresolved; the historical-isolation defence is contested rather than disproved. The Neoplatonist channel does *not* feed this problem (Stoic → Plotinus, not the reverse).

A second problem cuts the other way: Albert Ellis (REBT, 1950s) and Aaron Beck (cognitive therapy, 1960s) *explicitly* drew on Stoicism. Robertson 2019 documents the lineage; Beck's strategic/automatic distinction is described as a modern reformulation of *dogmata*/*phantasiai*. The modern cognitive-psychology evidence the cluster cites is therefore *partially* downstream of Stoic categories, so despite-commitments status against Stoicism is *weaker* than against Buddhism or Advaita.

## Scoring the Channel

Against the [taxonomy of voids](/apex/taxonomy-of-voids/)'s four criteria, with the cross-tradition channel as the unit of analysis:

- **Signature specificity.** Moderate. Doctrines map directly onto specific cluster faces — *khawāṭir* onto [source-attribution](/voids/source-attribution-void/), *propatheia* onto [noetic-feelings](/voids/noetic-feelings-void/) (conditional on Sorabji), hidden *riyāʾ* and *vāsanā* onto [confabulation](/voids/confabulation-void/) (Generation face), *ahaṃkāra* onto [narrative](/voids/narrative-void/).
- **Structured persistence.** Strong. The audited doctrines have persisted under sustained intra-tradition critique across centuries: *adhyāsa* from the 8th, *muḥāsaba* and *khawāṭir* from the 9th, hidden *riyāʾ* from the 11th, *propatheia* from the 1st.
- **Cross-observer convergence.** *Low-moderate*, with differentiated discounts from the Neoplatonist audit: **weak (≈ no discount)** for Stoicism (Plotinus is downstream of the Stoa); **weak-to-negligible** for Advaita (Bréhier-Armstrong-Aklan 2018 consensus that Plotinian-Śaṅkara parallels are "metaphorical ways of expression"); **moderate-to-substantial** for Sufism via Christian-monastic-via-Evagrius and direct Arabic Plotinus. The "cleanest historical-isolation defence" attribution shifts from early-Sufi practice to Advaita. The residual gap — whether Evagrian *logismoi* itself reduces to Stoicism (Evagrius engaged Stoic *first movements* via *antirrhēsis*) — is named below.
- **Framework independence.** Moderate. Architectural-specifics claims survive translation across cognitivist, embodied-cognition, and predictive-processing frameworks once stripped of authoring metaphysics. Framework-level claims earn no independence credit.

The audit *adds* architectural breadth and despite-commitments observations passing the test (Ghazālī's hidden *riyāʾ*; *propatheia* on Sorabji; the *antaḥkaraṇa* subdivision). It *subtracts* common-cause discounts from Pyrrho-Buddhism, late-medieval Sufism-Vedānta synthesis, the Stoic-CBT lineage, the Albahari two-tier reduction risk, and the audited Neoplatonist channel. The *net* score is *low-moderate* — numerically *lower* than the kleshas-only "moderate" baseline. This is re-calibration with disconfirming information, not score-improvement; the broader the scope, the more common-cause discounts surface.

## Where the Modern Reception Sits

Ganeri's *Attention, Not Self* (2017) treats classical Indian texts as primary sources in dialogue with contemporary debates — Indian architectural claims translating into cognitive-philosophical vocabulary without metaphysical commitment. Albahari's *Analytical Buddhism* (2006; 2009 *JCS*) argues the unified self is a two-tiered illusion drawing on both Theravāda Buddhism *and Advaita Vedānta*; if correct, the cross-tradition convergence reduces to a single architectural claim re-named across two traditions. Thompson's *Waking, Dreaming, Being* (2015) advocates contemplative phenomenology as a *partner* in scientific investigation. Sorabji's *Emotion and Peace of Mind* (2000) anchors the *propatheia* finding. Schwyzer 1960 and Hendrix 2005 read Plotinus as articulating bounded conscious access (*Enn.* IV.3.30); Adamson 2002 documents the al-Kindī circle's mid-9th-c. mediation of Plotinian psychology into Baghdad.

## Relation to Site Perspective

The exhibit honours the [voids-circularity discount](/project/voids-circularity-discount/) at the channel level. The three traditions' architectural-specifics claims are compatible with both dualism and physicalism: the bounded-witness reading is one available reading of Advaita's *antaḥkaraṇa*, the Sufi *khawāṭir* taxonomy, and Stoic *propatheia*, but each tradition admits substantively non-dualist readings. The Map's framework supplies the *significance* of the architectural feature for [Tenet 1 (Dualism)](/tenets/) and [Tenet 3 (Bidirectional Interaction)](/tenets/); the cross-tradition evidence supplies only the architectural feature's empirical extension.

The honest framing: the channel partially fills the gap the parent exhibit named, through despite-commitments observations of architectural specifics that survive vocabulary-stripping, and earns *low-moderate* rather than load-bearing weight against the strongest common-cause null. The cluster's empirical anchoring remains concentrated in the patient-population and healthy-adult observations the [parent exhibit](/topics/introspection-architecture-independence-scoring/) catalogues; this channel contributes calibration-grade architectural breadth.

## Honest Limitations

The audit's translation across tradition vocabularies is *interpretive* throughout. The parallels named here — *khawāṭir* with source-monitoring failure, *propatheia* with sub-assent affective machinery, *ahaṃkāra* with the Gazzaniga interpreter, hidden *riyāʾ* with confabulatory motivation-construction — are calibrated *plausibilities* of translation, not tradition-internal commitments.

The translation-construction objection deserves naming. A Churchland-style eliminative materialist would argue the architectural specifics are *constructions* of the modern source-monitoring framework projected onto the source texts: the channel isolates three texts whose modern reception the framework integrates, and convergence runs through one observer — the modern translator. The article cannot definitively refute this because the vocabulary-stripping test is itself applied by the framework whose neutrality is at issue. If translation construction is heavily in play, the convergence may be *one* observation rather than three.

The historical-isolation defence is *contested* rather than clean. Pyrrho/Buddhism (Beckwith vs Bronkhorst), Sufism/Vedānta (well-documented for late synthesisers, weaker for early practitioners), and Stoic-CBT all introduce partial common-cause channels. The single most consequential residual gap is the *Stoic reduction of the Christian-monastic channel*: if Evagrian *logismoi* is itself a Christianised Stoic doctrine (Evagrius engaged *first movements* directly via *antirrhēsis*), then the Sufi-via-Christian-monastic common-cause and the modern Stoic-CBT common-cause may share a single upstream source — making *Stoicism* the load-bearing channel from which others descend, and the cross-tradition convergence may collapse closer to Advaita-plus-Stoicism-plus-downstream-Stoic-influences than to three independent observations.

A defensible conservative reading — the channel earns *no* upgrade over the kleshas-only framing, and may sit at slightly lower calibration once translation-construction and Neoplatonist discounts are honoured — the article does not adopt but does not refute. The cross-architecture-via-LLMs channel identified in the [cross-species channel article](/topics/cross-species-channel-introspection-architecture-independence-scoring/) remains the most architecturally distant cross-observer evidence currently available, contributing substrate-distance triangulation the cross-tradition channel structurally cannot supply.
